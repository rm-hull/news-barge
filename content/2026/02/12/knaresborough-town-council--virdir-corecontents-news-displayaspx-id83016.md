# Annual Town Meeting

            *Published: 12 February 2026*

            The Annual Town Meeting will be held in the Assembly Hall, Knaresborough House on Monday 2 March 2026 at 7pm.

 

Come and hear the Mayor of Knaresborough Cllr Helen Westmancoat give a report outlining the council's work, and a presentation from North Yorkshire Council's Regeneration Team focusing on the Town Investment Plan for Knaresborough.
