Harrogate Town Council has been notified by North Yorkshire Council of a number of upcoming utility improvement schemes scheduled to take place across Harrogate’s highway network throughout 2026.

As Harrogate continues to experience increased demand on its road infrastructure, North Yorkshire Council’s Highways Team is working closely with utility providers to coordinate planned works and minimise disruption. We are being kept updated on project timelines and activity to ensure clear and consistent communication with Harrogate residents.

Harrogate Town Council will share further updates with the community as the year progresses as and when we receive these from North Yorkshire Council, with the next round of information expected in March/April.

In the meantime, if you require location‑specific or real‑time details of works in your area, these can be accessed via the Causeway one.network mapping tool.

You can also report highways issues to North Yorkshire Council via the following link: Report highways Issues to North Yorkshire Council – NY Highways
