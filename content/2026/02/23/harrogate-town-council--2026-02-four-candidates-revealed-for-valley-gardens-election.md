As four candidates have been validly nominated for the vacancy in Valley Gardens Ward, the election is contested and a poll will take place on 19th March 2026.

The official Statement of Persons Nominated and Notice of Poll are available to view below.

Electors will be sent a polling card with details of how and where to vote, but are reminded that:

- You must be registered to vote.
- Accepted photographic identification is required at polling stations.
- Postal and proxy vote applications must be received by the deadlines set by North Yorkshire Council Electoral Services.

Further information can be obtained from:

 North Yorkshire Council – Electoral Services

 Civic Centre, St Lukes Avenue, Harrogate, HG1 2AE
