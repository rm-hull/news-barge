Tom Martin elected as new councillor for Valley Gardens ward.

Harrogate Town Council has been notified by North Yorkshire Council of the results of the poll for the election of a Town Councillor for the Valley Gardens Ward.

Thomas James Martin (known as Tom), representing the Conservative Party, has been elected.

Further information will be provided on our website regarding Councillor Martin once it is available.
