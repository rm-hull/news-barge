Councillor Poskitt has served the Harrogate community for many years and brings a wealth of professional and civic experience to the role. A local GP with over 25 years’ experience, she has demonstrated a strong commitment to public service, community wellbeing, and partnership working across the town.

### A Strong Commitment to Community

Known locally for both her professional expertise and her creativity, Councillor Poskitt has actively supported a wide range of local initiatives. Her work includes fundraising for community causes, promoting active travel, engaging with residents and championing health and wellbeing across Harrogate.

In support of her nomination, Councillor Poskitt shared a personal and creative reflection highlighting her enthusiasm for the role and her experience in public speaking, community engagement, and representing organisations at events.

### The Role of the Town Mayor

The Town Mayor holds a unique and important position within the community, serving both as the civic figurehead of the town and as Chair of the Town Council. In this dual role, the Mayor represents Harrogate at official events, supports local organisations, and promotes the town and its interests.

As Chair of the Council, the Mayor is responsible for presiding over meetings, ensuring they are conducted fairly and in accordance with the council’s standing orders, and supporting effective decision-making. The role combines civic leadership with responsibility for maintaining good governance and representing the views of the community.

### Looking Ahead

Speaking ahead of the Annual Meeting, the outgoing Mayor, Councillor Chris Aldred, said:

“Councillor Poskitt brings dedication, compassion and a strong track record of public service. Her unique blend of professional experience, community engagement and personal creativity reflects the values of Harrogate, and I look forward to handing the baton over so she can continue representing the town in the year ahead.”

Subject to her formal election, Councillor Poskitt will serve as Mayor of Harrogate for the 2026/27 civic year.
