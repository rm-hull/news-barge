# King George V Playing Field Public Consultation

            *Published: 30 April 2026*

            Improvements to King George V playing field in Knaresborough.  Help us plan improvements to King George V playing field in Knaresborough.

 

North Yorkshire Council in collaboration with Knaresborough Town Council, are working to develop a comprehensive plan to improve this much-loved local green space for the community.


This public consultation will gather opinions from residents and users of the recreation area helping the council to understand what improvements are needed.

The consultation report will be used to inform the final proposals.
