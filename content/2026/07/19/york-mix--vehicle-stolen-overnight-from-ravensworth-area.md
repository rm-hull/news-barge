Between the hours of 03:20 and 04:20 the morning of Wednesday 15th July 2026 a Polaris Ranger 1000 was stolen from a property in the vicinity of Ravensworth.

The vehicle was displaying the registration NU71 AVX, and was of a similar style to the vehicle in this picture.

We believe a vehicle involved in the theft was a Citroën Berlingo Silver in colour and the two vehicle travelled in the direction of Hutton Magna / Ovington on the Durham border.

If you have any information that could help locate the whereabouts of these vehicles, any relevant CCTV or can identify anyone involved please contact North Yorkshire Police via 101 quoting crime reference 12260134343.
