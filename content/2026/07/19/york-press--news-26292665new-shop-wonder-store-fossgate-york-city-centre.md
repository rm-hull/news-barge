## New shop prepares to open in York city centre

Fossgate, where the shop will be opening its doors *(Image: Alice Kavanagh)>*

A NEW shop is taking shape and preparing to open in York city centre.

Wonder – a new lifestyle, homeware, and gift shop – is currently being fitted out at 24 Fossgate with a fresh red paint job and decals with the shop name and a sun logo appearing above the door.

The shop will be opening its doors in the former vegan Remedy café, which suddenly closed its doors in 2025 – something due to debts of more than £60,000 owed by the business.

Whilst details of Wonder are not yet known, the inside of the shop looks to be bright and airy with big windows showing a light wood interior with lots of shelving to display products.

An Instagram account has also been set up that says: “Coming soon to York - a homeware, gift and lifestyle store…watch this space.”
