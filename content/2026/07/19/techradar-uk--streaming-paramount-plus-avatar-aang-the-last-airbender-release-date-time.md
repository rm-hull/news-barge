2026 is giving us Aang squared. Not only did Netflix give us *Avatar: The Last Airbender* season 2 in late June, but now Paramount+ returns to its popular animated franchise with*Avatar Aang: The Last Airbender*.

The title is only slightly changed, but the difference between the two stories is huge. In the latter, it's been 13 years since the gang defeated Lord Ozai and the Fire Nation, and attentions have turned to a new airbender called Tagah.

Together, Aang and Tagah embark on a global quest to find the relic before a group called The Denied. But, will they succeed — and when does*Avatar Aang: The Last Airbender* arrive on Paramount+?

## What time can I watch Avatar Aang: The Last Airbender on Paramount+?

![Avatar Aang: The Last Airbender | Official Movie Trailer | Paramount+ | Avatar - YouTube](https://img.youtube.com/vi/7b20GRFZBFE/maxresdefault.jpg) 

If you avoided seeing*Avatar Aang: The Last Airbender* when it was leaked in full earlier this year, you'll be able to catch it on one of the world's best streaming services on**July 25, 2026.**

Like the streamer's other releases, such as *The Madison* and*Landman*, we can expect this Paramount movie to arrive at 12am PT. Here's when it will be released in other nations globally:

- **US**– 12am PT / 3am ET
- **Canada**– 12am PT / 3am ET
- **UK**– 8am BST
- **India**– 1:30pm IST
- **Singapore**– 4pm SGT
- **Australia**– 7pm AEDT
- **New Zealand**– 9pm NZDT

## When do new episodes of Avatar Aang: The Last Airbender come out?

 ![Aang, Katara, Sokka and Zuko look at something offscreen](https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/K4Do3wCyuSufrAhGvvsgGG.jpg) 


Unlike the original series, *Avatar Aang: The Last Airbender* is actually a feature-length film. Clocking in with a runtime of 99 minutes, that's roughly the equivalent of four episodes of the original series.

For those who want to watch it on the big screen, it's also getting a theatrical release. However, only select theaters in New York and Los Angeles will be showing it between July 24 and 30. You'll need to be quick, then, to secure your tickets to experience it during its limited run in cinemas.
