A charity shop in Acomb is celebrating 15 years of fundraising.

Martin House Children’s Hospice charity shop first opened its doors in 2011 and has raised hundreds of thousands of pounds to support children and families across Yorkshire.

Carol Atkinson, shop manager, said: "It's hard to believe it's been 15 years.

"We've built such a wonderful community around the shop and I'm incredibly proud of everything we've achieved together.

"Every donation, every purchase and every hour our volunteers give helps make a real difference to the children and families supported by Martin House."

Over the past 15 years, annual sales have grown by more than 32 per cent, rising from £62,067 in the first full financial year to more than £100,000 in 2025/26.

Stephanie Rimmington, head of retail at Martin House, said: "We'd like to thank everyone who has supported the Acomb shop over the past 15 years."

For more information, visit: [https://www.martinhouse.org.uk/](https://www.martinhouse.org.uk/)
