## Inside mid-century styled home for sale near York - with stunning countryside views

Inside the three-bedroom detached bungalow for sale in Main Street, Newton On Derwent, for £425,000 *(Image: Hudson Moody/Zoopla)*

A mid-century styled bungalow has hit the market east of York with stunning countryside views.

The three-bedroom detached property is for sale in Main Street, Newton On Derwent, for £425,000.


Hudson Moody, which is marketing the house, said it is a “beautifully presented property with stunning views over open countryside”.

Inside, an entrance hall leads to the currently mid-century designed living dining room.

There is a modern kitchen featuring a double oven, spaces for a fridge and washing machine – as well as views out to the Wolds.
