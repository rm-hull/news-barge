# One dead and 13 injured as Russia pounds Kyiv with ballistic missiles

- A significant Russian aerial assault on Kyiv resulted in at least one fatality and 13 injuries, involving ballistic missile strikes on residential and industrial areas.
- The attack, which commenced at 1.30 am local time, was one of the largest in recent weeks, causing fires across five districts of the Ukrainian capital.
- This assault followed Ukrainian drone strikes that killed at least nine people and injured 60 at two major warehouses belonging to the Russian online retailer Wildberries.
- The targeted Wildberries facilities were located in Kotovsk, Tambov region, and Elektrostal, situated near Moscow.
- Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky stated that these strikes aimed at "significant logistical facilities," alleging that Wildberries supplied equipment to the Russian military.
