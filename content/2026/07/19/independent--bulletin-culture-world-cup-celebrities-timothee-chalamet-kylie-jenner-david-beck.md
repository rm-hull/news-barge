# All the famous faces at World Cup final from Timothee Chalamet and Kylie Jenner to David Beckham

The World Cup final in New Jersey has attracted a significant number of celebrities and sports legends.

The closing ceremony featured performances by Robbie Williams, Nicole Scherzinger, and Italian singer-songwriter Laura Pausini.

Other big stars performed, including Justin Bieber, Madonna, BTS, Sharkira and Post Malone.

Other notable attendees were closing ceremony guest Tom Cruise, Sir David and Victoria Beckham, Ronaldinho Gaúcho and Ronaldo Nazário.

President Donald Trump is attending with First Lady Melania Trump and will present the trophy, resulting in heightened security measures.
