THREE vehicles have been stolen from a garage in a York suburb.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a moped and two bicycles were stolen from a garage on Clifton earlier this month.

The theft took place between July 6 and July 13 and saw a royal blue Suzuki CS50 with a registration of A369 FDX and two black Giant bikes stolen.

“No matter how insignificant it may seem, the information you provide could prove valuable to our enquiries,” said a spokesperson for the force.

They added that they are asking residents to review any CCTV, doorbell camera, or dashcam footage and to get in touch if they saw the vehicle or any suspicious activity in the area.

If you have any information that could assist the investigation, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and quote the crime reference number 12260136398.
