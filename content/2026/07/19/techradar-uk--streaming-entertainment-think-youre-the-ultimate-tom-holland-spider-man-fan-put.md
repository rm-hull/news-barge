2026 is a big year for Tom Holland. The popular actor has already appeared in one of this year's most anticipated new movies in Christopher Nolan's *The Odyssey*, and he'll soon be wearing his iconic superhero spandex once more when* Spider-Man: Brand New Day* releases later this month.

Starring in two of 2026's biggest films would be worth cheering about in itself. However, this year also sees Holland celebrate the 10th anniversary of his debut in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), which happened in 2016's *Captain America: Civil War*. Hey, I wasn't lying when I said 2026 was a big year for the A-lister.

With six MCU apperances already under his belt, Holland is now a certifiable stalwart of Marvel's cinematic juggernaut — so much so, in fact, that I believe some of you consider yourselves to be the biggest Tom Holland Spider-Man superfan.

Well, now is the time to put your superhero skills to the test. Below, I've rounded up 35 questions — some are way tougher than others — about Holland's wallcrawling career so far, including a query or five about *Spider-Man 4*.

So, what are you waiting for? Load up your web shooters and swing on over to the forthcoming quiz, and see if you have what it takes to be crowned the ultimate Holland Spider-Fan. Good luck!

For more on the forthcoming Marvel Phase 6 film, find out everything we know so far about *Spider-Man: Brand New Day*. Keep your eyes fixed on TechRadar later in July, too, for even more coverage of the hotly-anticipated movie.
