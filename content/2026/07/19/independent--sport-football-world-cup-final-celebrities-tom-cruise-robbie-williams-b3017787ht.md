Celebrities and sport legends are out in full force for the World Cup final as Spain face off against Argentina in New Jersey.

The event kicked off with a closing ceremony, which saw Robbie Williams perform the official Fifa anthem, *Desire*, alongside Pussycat Doll Nicole Scherzinger and Italian singer-songwriter Laura Pausini.

Tom Cruise gave a passionate speech after Streamer IShowSpeed sang his song *Champions* and Post Malone performed with rapper Swae Lee.

![Canada's Prime Minister Mark Carney, Mexico's President Claudia Sheinbaum, first lady Melania Trump and President Donald Trump attend the final](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/19/20/50/AP26200707287585.jpg)

![Ronaldinho and Ronaldo with Madonna](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/19/20/31/01KXY00KSBP0JNSW6M4FCGP974.jpg)

Comedian Will Ferrell was spotted in the crowd along with *Dune* star Timothee Chalamet and his girlfriend, social media star Kylie Jenner, and singer Pharrell Williams.

Sir David and Victoria Beckham were also in the crowd for the final, along with Ronaldinho Gaúcho and Ronaldo Nazário with Madonna, Mick Jagger, Jon Hamm and Jason Sudeikis.

President Donald Trump is attending with First Lady Melania Trump and will present the trophy to the winning team. That led to increased security measures at the game, with Secret Service on hand for additional screening.

A huge list of celebrities, athletes, and public figures was released as potential attendees for the event.
