# Spain v Argentina lineup: Scaloni makes three changes for World Cup final

- Spain and Argentina’s lineups for the 2026 Fifa World Cup Final have been confirmed.
- Spain‘s side remains unchanged from the one that beat France in the semis, meaning Lamine Yamal and Pedro Porro were in fact fit to start, while Pedri is benched again. Argentina made three changes with Rodrigo De Paul, Nico González and Gonzalo Montiel set to kick off for the defending champions.
- Argentina XI: E. Martinez; Montiel, Romero, Lis. Martinez, Tagliafico; Gonzalez, Enzo, De Paul, Mac Allister; Álvarez, Messi.
- Subs: Musso, Rulli, Senesi, Otamendi, Medina, Molina, Paredes, Barco, Lo Celso, Palacios, Almada, Simeone, Paz, Lopez, Martinez.
- Spain XI: Simon, Porro, Cubarsi, Laporte, Cucurella; Rodri; Olmo, Fabian; Yamal, Oyarzabal, Baena.
- Subs: Raya, Garcia, Pubill, Grimaldo, Garcia, Llorente, Merino, Gavi, Zubimendi, Pedri, Torres, Pino, Williams. Munoz, Iglesias.
