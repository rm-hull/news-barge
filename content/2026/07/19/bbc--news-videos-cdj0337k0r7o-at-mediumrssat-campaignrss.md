# 'A 10-goal thriller': England fans react to 6-4 win over France

England supporters in Miami have been celebrating a 6-4 victory over France, which secured the team's best World Cup result since 1966.

The nation was left disappointed on Wednesday when the Three Lions lost their semi-final with Argentina, failing to secure a spot in the final on Sunday.

Fans who stayed with the team were treated to a thrilling 10-goal match.

England dominated the first half to lead 4-0, but France's resurgence in the second half saw England edge out a 6-4 victory.
