## Much-loved North Yorkshire agriculture show closes its doors

Pig Young handlers 5-8 years winner Henrietta Barker aged 9 from Pudsey. *(Image: Provided)*

THIS year's Great Yorkshire Show has come to a close.

Taking place at the Great Yorkshire Showground, Railway Road, Harrogate, the 167th edition of the nation's best loved agriculture show has come to a close after an exciting four days that saw everything from spoon carving to a royal take over the role of president.
