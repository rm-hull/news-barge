International pop powerhouse CMAT delivered one of the stand-out shows of the summer on a sensational night at the Scarborough Open Air Theatre.

The chart-topping singer-songwriter – aka Ciara Mary-Alice Thompson –gave her adoring fans two hours of hits from her debut album *If My Wife New I’d Be Dead,* follow up * Crazymad, For Me *and massively successful Ivor Novello–winning* EURO-COUNTRY*.

Opening the show with* Jamie Oliver Petrol Station, * CMAT declared: “Scarbados, I said Scarbados! My name is CMAT, this is the very sexy CMAT and we have a great show for you tonight.”

The Irish star, and self-proclaimed Dunboyne Diana in celebration of her hometown, then wasted no time performing fan favourites *When A Good Man Cries, Running/Planning, Have Fun! * before the Yorkshire coast venue united as one for viral hit *Take A Sexy Picture Of Me.*

CMAT welcomed young fan Monty from Hartlepool to join her on stage to play guitar during* Euro-Country * before ending the night on* I Wanna Be A Cowboy, Baby * and* Stay For Something * where the star jumped into the crowd to create a Scarborough catwalk for a brilliant finale.

Singer songwriter Katy J Pearson opened the night with a superb set.
