# Moment fans in Madrid celebrate World Cup win

Thousands of fans watched Spain's World Cup victory at the Plaza de Colón in central Madrid on late Sunday night.

The BBC's Guy Hedgecoe was there as people cheered, sang and celebrated after counting down the last seconds of the match against Argentina, following a tense play of extra time.
