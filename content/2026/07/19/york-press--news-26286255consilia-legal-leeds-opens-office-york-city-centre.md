A Leeds law firm has expanded into York as part of its wider growth in Yorkshire.

Consilia Legal has opened a dedicated family law and mediation office in the city to strengthen its presence across North and East Yorkshire, including the Yorkshire coast.

The new riverside office at East Coast House, 25 Skeldergate, will be led by York-born family lawyer, mediator, and collaborative law specialist Sam Sinclair.

Sam specialises in out-of-court settlements for clients with complex financial arrangements.

Ms Sinclair said: "York is home for me, so opening Consilia Legal’s office here is particularly special.

"Many clients want to resolve family issues privately and constructively, especially where there are businesses, property, pensions, or wider family interests involved.

"My aim is to help people move forward with clarity, dignity and as much control over the process as possible."

The expansion broadens Consilia Legal’s reach across Leeds, Harrogate, York, Scarborough, Bridlington, and the coast, with a focus on private dispute resolution in family law.

It also aims to enhance the firm’s work with business owners, professional advisers, and families in need of specialist legal or mediation support.

Ms Sinclair joins existing team lead Laura Clapton, a family mediator and trained child consultant, supporting clients through out-of-court solutions that prioritise constructive outcomes and include children’s perspectives where appropriate.

The team handles cases involving business interests, trusts, property portfolios, pensions, and overseas assets, as well as mediation for couples seeking to reach agreement outside court.

Ms Sinclair said: "The new office will allow us to support more clients in York, Scarborough, Bridlington and across the wider region, while working closely with trusted professional advisers where families need joined-up guidance."

Laura Clapton, director at Consilia Legal, said: "Opening in York is a significant milestone for the firm and a natural next step in our growth across Yorkshire.

"Sam has an outstanding reputation for combining technical expertise with a calm, practical, and empathetic approach."

Ms Clapton said the expansion reflects growing client demand for confidential, strategic legal advice that avoids court proceedings wherever possible.

She said: "We are seeing increasing demand for specialist family law advice from clients who value discretion, careful planning, and alternatives to court.

"The York office builds on our Leeds roots and established presence in Harrogate and London, while making our services more accessible to clients across North and East Yorkshire and the coast."

Consilia Legal was founded in 2014 and provides boutique, partner-led advice in family and employment law, specialising in complex financial and family matters that require a careful, confidential approach.
