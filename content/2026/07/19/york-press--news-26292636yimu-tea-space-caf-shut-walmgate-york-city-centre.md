A YORK café has shut after less than a year in the city centre.

Yimu (一木) Tea Space – which is located at 55 Walmgate – looks to have closed its doors after the space has been completely cleared, with the exception of one Chinese lantern.

As reported by The Press last September prior to opening, the shop - which was previously Lawton Henry of York Hairdressing - said it is ‘dedicated to promoting Chinese tea culture’.

It offered traditional Chinese teas, modern tea drinks, and premium coffee – including speciality beans sourced from Yunnan province – alongside a range of experiences and tea ceremonies.

“It is a place where tea, coffee, and cultural experiences meet – and we’d love you to join us,” said a notice placed on the door.

It is unknown whether there are any future plans for the site, but Yimu have been contacted for comment and when we recieve their reply, we will update.
