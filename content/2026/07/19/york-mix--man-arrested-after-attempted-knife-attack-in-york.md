A man tried to attack a person with a knife in a York suburb.

It happened at about **5.10pm yesterday**(Saturday) in** Birch Park, Huntington**, near to the Royal Mail depot.

Police say a bald, shirtless male attempted to attack another person with a knife.

**Birch Park, Huntington**

Residents reported a large police response on Birch Park.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “After an extensive search a male has been detained and arrested however the knife has not been located.

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/york-birch-park-royal-mail-delivery-office-gsv.webp)

“It appears the knife is likely to have been dumped somewhere around Byland Avenue, Middleham Avenue, Monkton Road or possibly within the nearby beck or surrounding vegetation.

“If any residents sight a discarded or hidden knife described to have a long silver blade please contact North Yorkshire Police immediately.”
