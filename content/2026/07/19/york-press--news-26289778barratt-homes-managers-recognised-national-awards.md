## Two East Yorkshire site managers win prestigious housebuilding awards

James Walker, based at the Stewarts Reach site in Pocklington *(Image: Barratt Homes)*

Two site managers in East Yorkshire have been recognised for their work.

Jay Kerman and James Walker from Barratt Homes received the Pride in the Job Quality Award from the National House Building Council.

Mr Kerman said: "I am very proud of myself the team around me.

"I have a fantastic team; I know that we are all on the same page when it comes to ensuring consistent quality and customer satisfaction."

This is Mr Kerman’s sixth award at Barratt Homes’ Mortimer Park development.

Mr Walker, based at the Stewarts Reach site in Pocklington, is celebrating his first Pride in the Job award.

He said: "It is brilliant to have won my first NHBC award.

"It is a great achievement for myself and everyone involved at Stewarts Reach."

In total, Barratt Homes’ parent company Barratt Redrow Plc has won 122 Pride in the Job awards this year.

This marks the 22nd consecutive year it has led the industry.
