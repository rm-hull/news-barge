An insurance company has been recognised for its outstanding customer care.

The Insurance Emporium, based in York, won the Customer Care Award at the 2026 British Insurance Awards.

The ceremony took place at the Royal Albert Hall in London on July 8.

Francis Martin, chief executive officer at The Insurance Emporium, said: "To be recognised for our customer care is an incredible honour.

"This award reflects the commitment of our people to delivering outstanding service every day and demonstrates that customer care remains at the heart of everything we do."

Hosted by comedian Alan Carr, the event brought together 1,400 people to celebrate success in the insurance sector.

The Insurance Emporium was shortlisted alongside other major insurers, including Aviva (Home Claims and SME Underinsurance), Covéa Insurance, Cover-More Europe, and Got You Covered.

Jason Milner, chief experience officer at The Insurance Emporium, said: "What makes me especially proud of this award is that it isn't recognition for one project or one team.

"It’s a recognition of the culture we've built together – one where we genuinely care about doing the right thing for our customers and for each other."

The York-based insurer has also earned Feefo's Platinum Trusted Service Award 2026 and Best Claims Service at the Moneyfactscompare.co.uk Awards 2026.

The British Insurance Award is the latest in a string of honours.

Earlier this month, The Insurance Emporium received a Golden Moments Award from York Cares for Sustained Support for Collection Campaigns.

The award recognises consistent contributions to the community through volunteering, fundraising, and charitable efforts.

Throughout the year, staff have supported donation drives for The Hygiene Bank and York Cares' Easter and Christmas appeals.

These campaigns have supported a range of community initiatives delivered by York Cares.
