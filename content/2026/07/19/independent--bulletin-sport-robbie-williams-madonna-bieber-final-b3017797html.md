# BTS, Madonna and Muppets take World Cup stage in Super Bowl style halftime show

![Madonna accompanied by Brazilian icons Ronaldinho and Ronaldo during the first ever World Cup final halftime show](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/19/20/18/Screenshot-2026-07-19-at-1-23-36-PM.png)

- The World Cup closing ceremony featured performances from British singer Robbie Williams, Jennifer Hudson, and Post Malone, alongside a motivational speech by Tom Cruise.
- Robbie Williams performed his FIFA anthem 'Desire' with Nicole Scherzinger and Italian singer Laura Pausini, while Post Malone debuted a new song and sang 'Sunflower' with Swae Lee.
- The Super Bowl style halftime show was headlined by Madonna, who opened with her hit 'Music' and was joined by Brazilian football legends Ronaldo and Ronaldinho.
- Other major acts during the halftime show included K-pop sensation BTS, Justin Bieber, and a joint performance by Shakira and Burna Boy of their anthem 'Dai Dai'.
- Venezuelan conductor Gustavo Dudamel led an orchestra, and the halftime spectacle, curated by Coldplay's Chris Martin, concluded with elementary school students from Staten Island.
