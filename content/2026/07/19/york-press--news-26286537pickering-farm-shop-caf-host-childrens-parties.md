A Pickering Farm Shop and Café has added children’s parties to its range of attractions which include Pick Your Own, a miniature railway and a children’s playground.

‘Parties in the Barn’ will be available at Cedarbarn Farm Shop and Café throughout the summer and are suitable for children under 10.

Cedarbarn’s assistant general manager, Lauren Turnbull, said: "People have been asking us if we could do children’s parties for a while, so we decided to give them a go."

“We have upgraded our Dutch barn to make it suitable for all year round events and are looking forward to welcoming children to our unique parties.”

The ‘Party Barn’ holds up to 20 children and partygoers will receive bespoke party invitations, exclusive use of the barn for 2.5 hours, a ride on the miniature railway (grown-ups go free), a party tea, which includes a choice of sandwiches plus a Lottie Shaw’s train-shaped ginger biscuit and use of the farm-inspired playground.

Party places cost £15 per child and are available between 10.30am and 1pm or 2pm and 4.30pm at weekends between July 18 and September 20.

The barn is also available to hire for other events for £15 per hour. For more information or to book, please call 01751 475614 or contact the farm shop through the Cedar Barn Farmshop website.
