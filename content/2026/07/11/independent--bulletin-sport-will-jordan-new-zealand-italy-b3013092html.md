# Will Jordan makes All Blacks history as New Zealand thrash Italy

- Will Jordan became the All Blacks' all-time leading try scorer, surpassing Doug Howlett's record with his 50th Test try.
- The 28-year-old achieved this milestone by scoring a hat-trick against Italy in his 56th Test match.
- Jordan's hat-trick contributed to New Zealand's 47-17 victory over Italy in Wellington.
- Other All Blacks try scorers in the match included Sam Darry, Cam Roigard, Ethan de Groot, and Tupou Vaai.
- Italy's Niccolo Cannone received a red card for a second-half headbutt during the game.
