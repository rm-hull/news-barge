A POLICE incident has happened at a popular DIY shop in North Yorkshire.

North Yorkshire Police has launched a CCTV appeal following the incident at B&Q in Seamer Road, Scarborough on March 27 between 3.10pm and 3.30pm.

  


A force spokesperson said: "We would like to speak to them, as they may have information that could help our investigation."

  Police said they would like to speak to the men pictured in the CCTV appeal, as they could help the investigation 

*(Image: North Yorkshire Police)*

They added: "If you can help our appeal, please email [steven.holloway@northyorkshire.police.uk.](mailto:steven.holloway@northyorkshire.police.uk.)

"If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

"Please quote reference 12260090280."
