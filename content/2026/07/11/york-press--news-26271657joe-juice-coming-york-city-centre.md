## 'Global lifestyle' brand eyes York site

The inside of a Joe & The Juice store *(Image: Joe & The Juice)*

Search the Public Notice PortalA GLOBAL health and lifestyle brand has lodged plans for a site in York.

Plans lodged with City of York Council would see Joe & The Juice open a store in St Sampson’s Square.

The Danish company’s application stated the juice, coffee and sandwich seller would occupy the ground, first and second floors of what is currently the Size? footwear and clothes shop.

Size? remained open in St Sampson’s Square as of Friday, July 10.

The plans would see the building converted to house the venue which would be able to seat 65 people, 29 on the ground floor and 36 on the first.

Applicants stated the conversion would result in a cleaner, more refined interior while preserving the building’s historic character.

Joe & The Juice was founded in Copenhagen in 2002 and now has more 500 stores across Europe, the United States, Middle East, Africa and Asia.

It is known for its freshly-prepared juices, shakes, coffee and sandwiches, according to its website.

The website stated: “Joe & The Juice has grown into a global lifestyle and hospitality brand with more than 500 stores worldwide.”

The company announced plans to accelerate its ongoing global expansion following the publication of its financial results for 2025 in April.

Its plans for York stated: “By virtue of the proposed development, the external enhancements will facilitate the upgrade of the site for the operation of Joe &The Juice.

“Joe & The Juice are a prominent juice bar and café known by many people around the world for exceptional smoothies, juices and food.

“Allowing Joe & The Juice to operate in the space will bring increased employment and opportunities for local people and continued social and economic regeneration of the city.”
