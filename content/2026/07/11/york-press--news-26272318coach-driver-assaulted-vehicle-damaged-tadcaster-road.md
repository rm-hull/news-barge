## Police launch investigation into assault of driver along busy main road in York

Police are investigating the alleged assault of a coach driver in Tadcaster Road on June 11 *(Image: North Yorkshire Police)*

A coach driver was assaulted during an incident that also left their vehicle damaged along a main road in York.

The alleged attack happened in Tadcaster Road at 8.30am on Thursday (June 11).

Police are now appealing for witnesses and information to assist with their investigation.

  


A spokesman for North Yorkshire Police said: "We believe the person in the image will have information that could help our investigation."

  North Yorkshire Police has released CCTV images of a man officers would like to speak to following the incident 

*(Image: North Yorkshire Police)*

They added: "If you have any information that could assist our investigation, please contact us online in the first instance: Email: PC Bailey-Lane on [Clair.Bailey-Lane@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:Clair.Bailey-Lane@northyorkshire.police.uk)

"Live chat: Available via our website, Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm

"If you are unable to use these options, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101."

Information can also be given anonymously through Crimestoppers.

Please quote reference 12260111824 when passing on information.
