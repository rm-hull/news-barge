# South Africa star dies weeks after World Cup exit

- South African footballer Jayden Adams has died at the age of 25.
- The Mamelodi Sundowns midfielder recently represented Bafana Bafana at the World Cup 2026, helping them reach the knock-out stage for the first time in their history.
- Adams played all three group games for his country in the tournament, starting against Mexico and featuring as a substitute in two other matches.
- Details surrounding his passing remain unclear, with the South African Football Players Union expressing its devastation at the untimely loss.
- A bright talent, Adams had collected nine caps and scored two goals for Bafana Bafana, and had recently lost his grandmother two weeks prior.
