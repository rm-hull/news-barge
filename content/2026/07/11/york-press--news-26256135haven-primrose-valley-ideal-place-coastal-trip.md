## Coastal holiday park in Yorkshire with one of the best seaside arcades

Haven Primrose Valley holiday park is the ideal place for a family trip. *(Image: Tripadvisor)*

A huge holiday park found along the Yorkshire coast could be a great option for a family staycation this year.

The Haven Primrose Valley holiday park was recently named as having one of the best seaside arcades in Yorkshire and has plenty of other activities to get involved in.

You don’t have to spend all of your time at the holiday park, as it is extremely close to both Filey and Hunmanby beach for a day trip.

The site has plenty of outdoor things to do for children, such as den building, pond dipping, and kayaking.

  The holiday park has a range of activities from two pools, kayaking, and workshops. 

*(Image: Tripadvisor)*

## What can you do at the Haven Primrose Valley holiday park?

The holiday park has both an indoor and outdoor pool with lots of slides and inflatables to use.

There are loads of sessions for creative activities like pottery, bracelet making, and sand art, as well as more adventurous options like rock climbing and the aerial adventure course.

With 32 games ranging across seven different categories, the holiday park’s arcade was revealed as one of the best in Yorkshire and will provide families with hours of fun.

The holiday park’s website said: "Primrose Valley Holiday Park is one of our biggest - and every last inch is brimming with family fun.


"Kids and grown-ups alike will love our indoor swimming pool, packed with splashing features including a multi-lane water slide.

"If you prefer to take it outdoors there’s the heated pool and direct access to a sandy beach, or thrill seekers can also tackle our outdoor Aerial Adventure course.

"Fantastic Filey is a short drive or stroll north and is brilliant for a day out off park.

"And there are plenty of fantastic food and drink options once you are back on site, including our very own Slim Chickens restaurant, and a J D Wetherspoon pub."

## What do visitors think of Haven Primrose Valley Holiday Park?

One reviewer on Tripadvisor said: "We went Friday to Monday and we stayed at 8 puffin and it was honestly amazing, the caravan was a gold caravan it was clean and tidy, the view was incredible, it was lovely just to wake up and step onto the decking and just enjoy the sea, it was a quiet area as well, lots to do for kids, we went in term time so was a lot quieter, we cannot wait to book again."

Another person shared: "Although we didn't pay for any passes we could see there was still plenty of things to do for families.

"The caravan was spotless and all the staff were friendly and helpful."
