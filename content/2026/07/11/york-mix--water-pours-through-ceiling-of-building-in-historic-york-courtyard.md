Firefighters were called out after an incident in an historic corner of York.

They went to **Coffee Yard**, off Stonegate, at 12.51am this morning.

Coffee Yard is home to both the medieval townhouse Barley Hall and The Langton café bar.

#### Coffee Yard, York

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “A crew from York responded to reports of water coming through the ceiling and affecting the electrics of a building.

“Crews isolated the water and advice was given.”
