# Which England players are on a yellow card? The stars keen to avoid semi-final ban

- England secured a 3-2 victory over Mexico in the last 16, but face significant disciplinary concerns for their World Cup quarter-final against Norway.
- Defender Jarell Quansah received a straight red card for serious foul play and will be suspended for both the quarter-final and a potential semi-final match.
- Jude Bellingham, Declan Rice, Marc Guehi, Nico O’Reilly, and Jordan Henderson are all one yellow card away from a suspension, having accumulated one booking each in the knockout stages.
- Players who receive two yellow cards across the last 32, last 16, and quarter-finals will incur a one-match ban.
- Yellow cards reset after the group stage and again after the quarter-finals, meaning a booking in the semi-final would not rule a player out of the final.
