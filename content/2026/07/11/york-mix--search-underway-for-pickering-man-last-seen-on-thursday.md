Police have launched a search for a Pickering man who has gone missing.

Christopher Clark, 49, was last seen in Helmsley church at 12.30pm on Thursday (9 July).

He is described as a white man, about 5ft 6in tall and is of a slim build.

Christopher has brown wavy shoulder length hair and may be wearing navy blue/khaki shorts, a brown T-shirt and black and grey Merrell walking shoes.

He was also carrying a black Osprey rucksack, which is believed to have a black North Face shell jacket, and a dark blue Rab puffer jacket.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Christopher is a keen walker and has a knowledge of the local area.

“If you see Christopher, please call 999. If you have any other information that could help us find them, please call us on 101 and select option 1 to speak to our control room.”
