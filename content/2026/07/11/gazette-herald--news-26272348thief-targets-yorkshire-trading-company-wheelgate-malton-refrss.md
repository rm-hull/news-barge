A THIEF has targeted a popular discount shop in North Yorkshire.

The incident happened at the Yorkshire Trading Company in Wheelgate, Malton on June 21.

North Yorkshire Police is urging the public to get in touch if they recognise the man in CCTV, as he may have information that could assist the investigation.

  


A spokesperson said: “Please email [Ben.Hepworth@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:Ben.Hepworth@northyorkshire.police.uk) if you can help.

“Alternatively, you can visit our website, select ‘Tell us about’, then choose ‘An existing case or report’ or contact us through "Live Chat" via our website, Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.

“If you are unable to use these options, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101 or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.”
