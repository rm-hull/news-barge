York City will play Peterborough United in a special testimonial match for Paddy McLaughlin.

Taking place on Tuesday, July 28, at 7.30pm, the fixture will be the final event of Paddy's testimonial year.

Paddy McLaughlin's Testimonial Committee has confirmed the match will be the last opportunity for him to wear a York City shirt, marking his decade of service to the club.

Fans attending the match will have the chance to bid on commemorative shirts worn by the team.

The proceeds will support Paddy's testimonial year and his chosen charity, Menfulness.

Supporters can already purchase tickets for the match.

More information about sponsorship opportunities, such as becoming the front-of-shirt sponsor for the game, can be obtained through the email address [paddy.testimonial@gmail.com.](mailto:paddy.testimonial@gmail.com.)
