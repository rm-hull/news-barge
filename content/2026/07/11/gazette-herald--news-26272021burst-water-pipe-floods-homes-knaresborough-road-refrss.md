A BURST water pipe has flooded homes along a main road in North Yorkshire.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service were called to Knaresborough Road in Harrogate shortly before 12am on Saturday (July 11).

Two fire engines from Harrogate were used to redirect water away from affected properties, with crews using a pumps to lower water levels before the arrival of Yorkshire Water.

  


North Yorkshire Police and North Yorkshire Highways were also in attendance.

Knaresborough Road was closed as a result.

A spokesperson for North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said: “The incident was left with Yorkshire Water who, upon their arrival, isolated the water.”
