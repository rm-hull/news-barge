Police are investigating an assault on a main York road.

It happened at 8.30am on **Thursday11 June** on** Tadcaster Road**.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Damage was caused to a coach and the driver was assaulted.

“We believe the person in the image will have information that could help our investigation.

“If you have any information that could assist our investigation, please contact us online in the first instance.”
