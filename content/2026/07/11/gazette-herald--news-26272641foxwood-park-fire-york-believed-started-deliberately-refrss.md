A FIRE in a York park is believed to have been started deliberately.

Firefighters from Acomb were called to Foxwood Park, in Bellhouse Way, at 1.50pm on Saturday (July 11).

  


On arrival, the grassland fire measured five metres by two metres, North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said.

The crew successfully extinguished the blaze, which was thought to have started deliberately.
