An evening of music and open-air theatre is set to light up the stage this summer.

Members of the Sing Space Musical Theatre Choirs from Malton and York will perform Musicals by Moonlight at Scampston Hall Gardens on Sunday, July 19.

The outdoor concert will feature songs from some of musical theatre’s most popular shows, creating a spectacular outdoor experience for audiences beneath the summer sky.

James Aconley, choir leader, said: "The most special thing about Sing Space is seeing the community that has grown around it.

"We have people who joined because they loved musicals but never imagined they would stand on a stage performing to an audience.

  The choir is made up of a mix of seasoned performers and those new to the stage 

*(Image: Sing Space Musical Theatre Choir)*

"Watching their confidence grow and seeing everyone support each other is what makes this so rewarding."

The choir is made up of a mix of seasoned performers and those new to the stage, many of whom have found renewed confidence and connection through singing.

Mr Aconley added: "Musicals by Moonlight is a celebration of everything that makes community music so powerful – bringing people together, creating friendships and giving everyone the chance to be part of something truly special."

Guests are encouraged to bring a picnic and enjoy the evening in the gardens.

Tickets are available at www.tickettailor.com/events/maltonmusicaltheatrechoir/2226072
