EMERGENCY services have been called to a crash on a main road in North Yorkshire.

The A59 York Road, near Green Hammerton, is expected to be closed for some time, North Yorkshire Police has said.

  


It comes after reports of a crash shortly after 5pm on Saturday (July 11).

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Motorists are advised to divert through Kirk Hammerton or find other alternative routes in the meantime.”

At 5.24pm, an update from AA Traffic read that traffic was "very slow", with the road partially blocked.
