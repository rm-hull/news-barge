FIRE crews have been called to a historic York city centre street.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service was brought to Coffee Yard at 12.51am on Saturday (July 11).

  


A service spokesperson said: “A crew from York responded to reports of water coming through the ceiling and affecting the electrics of a building.

“Crews isolated the water and advice was given.”
