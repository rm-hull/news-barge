# Sunday’s sport: What’s on and where can I watch it?

- Argentina vs Switzerland is due to kick off at 2am BST on Sunday; viewers in the United Kingdom can watch the match live on ITV, with coverage on the channel from 1.15am BST.
- Alexander Zverev and Jannik Sinner are set to battle it out on Centre Court for the Wimbledon men’s singles title; the Wimbledon action is on BBC Two at 11am, BBC One at 1.05pm and 4pm and TNT Sports 2 at 3.30pm.
- The Tour de France heads for the hills with a brutal stage nine, featuring Tadej Pogacar and Jonas Vingegaard; TNT Sports 1 has the coverage, beginning at 12pm.
- England Women continue their historic first Test at Lord’s on its third day, with Sophie Ecclestone becoming England’s all-time leading wicket taker; coverage starts from 10.30am on Sky Sports Cricket
- Golf fans have plenty to watch, including the Genesis Scottish Open, the PGA Tour’s ISCO Championship, and the LPGA Tour’s Amundi Evian Championship; coverage will be on Sky Sports Golf throughout the day.
