# BBC or ITV? Where can I watch England v Norway today?

- England are scheduled to play Norway in the World Cup 2026 quarter-finals in Miami.
- The match will take place at the Hard Rock Stadium on Saturday 11 July, with a**10pm BST** kick-off; viewers in the United Kingdom can watch the game live on ITV1 from**8:45pm BST**, or stream it via ITVX.
- England progressed after a hard-fought victory against co-hosts Mexico, while Norway secured their place by defeating Brazil.
- Both teams have player fitness concerns, with Norway battling a virus among several players and England monitoring Reece James's hamstring issue, while Jordan Henderson is out with an arm injury.
- The winner will face either Argentina or Switzerland in Atlanta at **8pm BST (3pm ET) ,** with the World Cup final set for 19 July at**8pm BST (3pm ET).**
