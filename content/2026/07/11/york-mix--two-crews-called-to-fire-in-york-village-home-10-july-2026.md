York and Tadcaster fire crews were called to an incident in **Copmanthorpe** last night (Friday).

They went to a home on **Moor Lane** at**6.17pm**.

A tumble dryer had caught fire. Everyone was safely out of the property before the firefighters arrived.

#### Moor Lane, Copmanthorpe

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “The tumble dryer fire was out on arrival and removed to open air and inspected by fire crews using a thermal imaging camera. Advice was given to the occupant.”
