Police have launched an investigation after the alleged assault of a woman by a man in York.

The incident happened close to Acomb Funeral Care in Gale Lane between 1.45am and 1.55am on Sunday (July 5).

The victim was allegedly punched in the head without warning or reason by a man, North Yorkshire Police said.

  


The suspect is described as white, of a slim build and in his 60s.

At the time of the incident, he was wearing a cap, a checked shirt with a white t-shirt underneath and was possibly wearing glasses.

A force spokesperson said: “If anyone recognizes this description or lives in the area and has CCTV, witnessed the incident or was driving in the area or has information that could assist our investigation, please contact us.

“You can do this online in the first instance by emailing [ben.robinson-brockhill@northyorkshirepolice.uk](mailto:ben.robinson-brockhill@northyorkshirepolice.uk), or by visiting our website, selecting ‘Tell us About’, the choosing ‘An existing case or report’”

  The police has a live chat available on its website, which is active from Monday to Friday from 9am till 5pm.

  If you are unable to use these options, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Please quote reference 12260126195 when providing information.
