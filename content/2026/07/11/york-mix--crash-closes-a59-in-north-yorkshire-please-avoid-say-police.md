Emergency services are at the scene of a crash on the A59.

It’s near to Green Hammerton.

The road is closed both ways. Drivers are reporting standstill traffic.

#### Crash location

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said at 5.02pm: “The road is expected to be closed for some time and motorists are advised to instead divert through Kirk Hammerton or find other alternative routes in the meantime.”
