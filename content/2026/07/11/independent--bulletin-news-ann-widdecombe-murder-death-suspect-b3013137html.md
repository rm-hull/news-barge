# Police share new theory on Ann Widdecombe’s death as murder investigation continues

- Former MP and MEP Ann Widdecombe was found dead at her home in Haytor, Dartmoor, on Thursday after sustaining serious injuries.
- Police believe Miss Widdecombe was attacked around 12:30 pm on Wednesday, nearly 24 hours before her body was discovered.
- Authorities are hunting for a white male suspect, treating her death as suspicious but stating there is no wider risk to the public.
- A 26-year-old man arrested on suspicion of murder was released from custody on Saturday.
- Devon and Cornwall Police are continuing their investigation, appealing for public information, and urging against speculation.
