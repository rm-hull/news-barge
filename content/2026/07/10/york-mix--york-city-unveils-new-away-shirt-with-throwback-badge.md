Come on you yellows!

This is York City’s away shirt for their first season back in the Football League.

Here’s what the club is saying about quite a dramatic new look.

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/york-city-2026-27-away-kit-3.webp)

“It has been 10 years since we had a yellow away kit, so we have brought one back for our travels.

“Our sunflower gold yellow kit features a navy pinstripe as well as a York City Longboat to the back neck, which also features in our National League winning kit from last season.

“The kit also sees us replace our crest with the YCFC script logo brought back from the mid 1970’s to give it a unique look and feel.

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/york-city-2026-27-away-kit-2.webp)

“The ribbed collar features a yellow pinstripe, and the side collar and side panels are done in a different technical fabric for increased breathability.

“The Yorkshire rose and Hummel authenticity patch features on the hem.

“The kit is also available in long sleeve and a women’s first team female fit.”

This new away shirt will go on sale in-store and online from**Thursday**.
