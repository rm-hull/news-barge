A FIRE is currently burning in a field in North Yorkshire.

Fire crews have been called to the incident in Common Lane, Ulleskelf, near Tascaster, which involves a tractor and a baler.

  


At 12.41pm on Friday (July 10), a North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service spokesperson said the fire from the equipment has since spread to the field, and a "significant" amount of smoke may affect visibility on nearby roads.

Firefighters are working to create a fire break and prevent the fire from spreading further.

People are advised to avoid the area, with residents asked to close their windows and doors.
