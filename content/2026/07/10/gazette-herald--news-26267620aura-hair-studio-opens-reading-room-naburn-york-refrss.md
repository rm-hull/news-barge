A NEW hair salon is bringing fresh style and convenience right to the heart of the community.

Naburn Parish Council has expressed its delight after a new hairdressers has opened its doors in the Reading Room - saving residents from having to travel into the centre of York.

Aura Hair Studio - run by Aliki Wigget, a professional hairdresser with an established mobile business - has opened in the Reading Room on Front Street, Naburn, which is offering hairdressing services for men and women on Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays, and alternate Saturdays.

A Naburn Parish Council spokesman said: "The Parish Council is delighted to announce that the new hairdressing salon is now open in the Reading Room on Front Street.

"This will be Aliki’s first permanent, dedicated salon but she already has a loyal customer base from her mobile hairdressing business.

"The Parish Council are confident that the business will be financially sustainable and will provide an excellent service for the village residents long term. Please support Aliki - and also save yourself a journey into town!"

Aura Hair Studio can be found on Facebook and Instagram under Aura Hair Studio By Aliki.
