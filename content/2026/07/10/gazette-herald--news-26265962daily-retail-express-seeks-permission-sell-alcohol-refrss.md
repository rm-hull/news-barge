Search the Public Notice PortalA convenience shop is seeking permission to sell alcohol.

Daily Retail Express, in Easingwold, has put forward an application to North Yorkshire Council under the Licensing Act 2003.

The application, submitted by Thurairasa Rasamohan, seeks permission for the off-premises sale of alcohol every day of the week, from 7am to 11pm.

The application can be inspected at North Yorkshire Council, County Hall, Boroughbridge Road, Northallerton.

People have until July 29 to submit their thoughts on the proposal.

Submissions can be sent in writing to [licensing.west@northyorks.gov.uk](mailto:licensing.west@northyorks.gov.uk) or by post to North Yorkshire Council at County Hall.

The council has stated that it is a criminal offence to knowingly or recklessly provide false information in connection with an application.

Anyone found guilty of this can be fined an unlimited amount.

The proposed hours mirror those of many other small retailers across the area.
