Here are the latest planning applications submitted to North Yorkshire Council for the region.

An application has been approved for tree works at York House in Claxton.

The works include the removal of one cherry plum tree and the reduction of a laburnum’s crown to three metres and lateral spread by 1.5 metres.

Similar reduction will be made to a Pride of India tree along the public highway and over the driveway.

A single-storey side extension and patio enlargement have been approved at Manor Farm in Broughton.

Ref. No: ZE26/00366/HOUSE | Received: Mon 27 Apr 2026 | Validated: Fri 01 May 2026 | Status: Approved

Springfield House in Gate Helmsley will be converted from a residential property to offices following approval.

Ref. No: ZE26/00301/FUL | Received: Tue 07 Apr 2026 | Validated: Wed 13 May 2026 | Status: Approved

Replacement UPVC windows and doors at The Mill Barn in Kirkby Mills have received approval.

Ref. No: ZE26/00137/HOUSE | Received: Wed 18 Feb 2026 | Validated: Mon 20 Apr 2026 | Status: Approved

A single-storey extension, along with changes to the external floor slab, windows, and doors, has been approved at The Hall in Kirkham Abbey.

The application includes the demolition of a porch and removal of a generator and enclosure.
