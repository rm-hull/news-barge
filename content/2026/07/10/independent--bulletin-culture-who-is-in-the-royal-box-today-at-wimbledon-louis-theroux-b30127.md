# Wimbledon Royal Box: Who’s made an appearance today?

- Wimbledon's Men's Singles semi-finals were held on Day 12, Friday 10 July, on Centre Court.
- The matches included British hope Arthur Fery against world No 3 Alexander Zverev, and world No 1 Jannik Sinner facing Novak Djokovic.
- The event drew a host of celebrities to the Royal Box, such as **Hugh Laurie**,** Louis Theroux**, and fashion icon** Anna Wintour**.
- Other notable attendees included Liverpool footballer **Virgil Van Dijk**, actors** Martin FreemanBenedict Cumberbatch**, and comedian** Sandi Toksvig**.
- Tennis legend **Pat Cash** and ice dancing star**Sir Christopher Dean** were also among the famous faces watching the semi-final action.
