# The all-time World Cup top scorers as Mbappé closes in on record

- **Lionel**-** Messi**- Argentina’s captain has already scored 8 goals in the tournament, bringing his all-time World Cup total to** 21**.
- The 38-year-old surpassed the record previously held by Germany's **Miroslav Klose**.
- Meanwhile,**Kylian Mbappé** is hot on Messi’s heels for the top spot, having also surpassed Klose with a total of 20**goals**.
- As such, the overall top scorers in Fifa Men’s World Cup history are now as follows: Lionel Messi (21), Kylian Mbappé (20), Miroslav Klose (16), Brazil’s Ronaldo (15), Harry Kane (14) and Gerd Müller (14).
