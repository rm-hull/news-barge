Crews have been called to a fire at a property in North Yorkshire.

Four fire crews were called to the blaze in Finkle Hill, Sherburn in Elmet near Selby, shortly after 9.30am on Friday (July 10).

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said the crews used a hose reel jet and breathing apparatus to extinguish the fire.

In a statement, the fire service said: “Crews from Tadcaster, Acomb, Garforth and Castleford (West Yorkshire Fire Service) responded to a fire in a property.

“Crews used two breathing apparatus and a hose reel jet to extinguish.”
