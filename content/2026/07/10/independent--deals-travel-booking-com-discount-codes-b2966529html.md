## Ways to save at Booking.com in July

### 1. **Use Genius loyalty discounts**

Booking.com’s free “Genius” loyalty programme is one of the easiest ways to enjoy ongoing savings and travel perks. The more you book, the bigger the discounts:

- **Level 1:** 10 per cent off selected stays
- **Level 2:** 10-15 per cent off selected stays
- **Level 3:** 10-20 per cent off selected stays

These discounts are applied automatically to the room price before taxes and charges.

You can also save on car hire through the programme:

- **Level 1:** 10 per cent off rental cars
- **Level 2:** 10–15 per cent off rental cars
- **Level 3:** Up to 15 per cent off rental cars

Car hire discounts apply to the base rental cost only.

Other Genius membership perks include:

- Flight price alerts (Level 1)
- Free breakfast at selected stays (Level 2)
- Free room upgrades with selected stays (Level 2)
- Priority customer support with live agents (Level 3)

## Booking.com FAQs

### How do I apply my Booking.com discount code?

Most deals are applied automatically at checkout, however, if you do have a Booking.com promo code, here is how to redeem it:

- Click the “see code” button to reveal and copy the voucher code.
- Head to your Booking.com payment page.
- Locate the “Do you have a promo code” box and paste it here.
- Your booking total will auto-adjust once applied to reflect your discount.

### When is the cheapest time to book on Booking.com?

Hotel prices are usually lower during off-peak travel periods and midweek stays. Booking several months ahead can help secure lower prices for summer holidays and school breaks, while flexible travellers may find bigger discounts through last-minute deals.

### Does Booking.com offer an NHS discount?

### Can I combine Booking.com offers?

You can only apply one Booking.com discount code at a time, however, you can often stack these with site-wide sales or hotel deals to maximise your savings. Discount codes may only be valid on selected hotels, so be sure to check the terms and conditions before you complete your booking.

### Is there a Booking.com student discount?

Unfortunately, Booking.com student discounts are rare, and there are no offers right now.

### When is the best time to book on Booking.com?

Prices on Booking.com vary depending on demand, season and destination. Generally, if you follow the below travel booking tips, you’ll be most likely to get the lowest price:

- Book early for peak travel periods such as summer and school holidays
- Look for last-minute discounts if your plans are flexible
- Shop during major sale events such as Black Friday and January sales

### Does Booking.com do Black Friday sales?

Booking.com does take part in Black Friday sales, but you can usually secure even bigger savings during “Travel Tuesday” which is typically the Tuesday after Black Friday and Cyber Monday. Last year, there were up to 35 per cent off bookings.
