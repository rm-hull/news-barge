A carefully chosen shade sail can be the focal point of your garden. While parasols are great for covering bistro sets and loungers, these larger coverings can turn whole areas of your outdoor space into a shaded sanctuary, offering protection from the sun’s rays in summer and beyond.

The best shade sails provide UV protection, water-resistance and weatherproof qualities. They should also be breathable, so that you don’t feel like you’re baking on the garden sofa.

Whether you need ample space where little ones will be covered, or to keep guests cool while serving food straight from the barbecue, there are options for every preference. My top pick is the Kookaburra 3.6m triangle shade sail, which gave me shelter from rain as well as the sun.
