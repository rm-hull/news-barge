POLICE are appealing for information after counterfeit £20 notes were passed in the doorstep sale of a £500 mobile phone in a York suburb.

North Yorkshire Police said the incident happened around 2.20pm on June 12 in Osbaldwick Lane, where a man responded to a Facebook Marketplace advert and handed over £500 in fake £20 notes for the device.

  


The force has released an image of a man it said may have information which may be able to help its officers investigation into the offence.

Anyone with information is asked to contact police quoting reference 12260108676.
