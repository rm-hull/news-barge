# World Cup results: France cruise past Morocco as Mbappe matches Messi

- France secured a 2-0 victory over Morocco in Boston, progressing to the 2026 World Cup semi-finals.
- Kylian Mbappe and Ousmane Dembele scored in the second half, with Mbappe also missing a first-half penalty.
- Mbappe's goal was his eighth of the tournament, drawing him level with Lionel Messi in the scoring charts.
- Morocco were eliminated by France for the second consecutive World Cup, having also been knocked out by them in 2022.
- France will now to go to play the winner of Spain v Belgium in the semi-finals – that game takes place this evening.
