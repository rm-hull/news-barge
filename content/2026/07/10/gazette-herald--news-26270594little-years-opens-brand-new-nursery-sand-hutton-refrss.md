A new nursery is set to open its doors in North Yorkshire.

Little Years Sand Hutton, on the York Biotech Campus, will celebrate its opening this month with a summer fun day on Friday (August 1).

The day will introduce the brand-new nursery to local families.

  


A spokesperson for the nursery said: "Families are invited to enjoy the nursery's fun and welcoming atmosphere and discover a uniquely family-led approach to early years education."

The new facility, just off the A64, features connected playrooms for children of all ages, a large outdoor space with access to nearby woodland, a child-sized working kitchen for "hands-on" learning and a dedicated area for parents.

It will offer flexible full and term-time care for youngsters aged 0 to five between 7.30am till 6pm.

The Little Years team say a mixed-age approach to learning mirrors family life, and would help children build their confidence and friendships.

A spokesperson said: "Families are warmly invited to join the Family Fun Day to experience the nursery for themselves.

"Those who would like an earlier look can book a private show round by emailing [sandhutton@littleyearsnursery.co.uk.](mailto:sandhutton@littleyearsnursery.co.uk.)"

It comes as plans are underway to build the company's sixth nursery at Monks Cross, which is set to open in January 2027.
