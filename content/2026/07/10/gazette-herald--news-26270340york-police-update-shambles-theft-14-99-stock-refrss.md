NORTH Yorkshire Police has issued an update following the theft of £14.99 of stock from a shop in one of York's most visited and photographed streets.

The theft took place in Shambles on Monday, June 8.

  


A force spokesperson said it carried out an investigation and would welcome information from anybody who has yet to come forward.

Information can be emailed to [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk) or by calling 101 and asking for PC 1488 Bell, the force said.

Please quote reference 12260107605 when providing information.
