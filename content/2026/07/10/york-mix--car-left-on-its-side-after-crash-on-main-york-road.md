A car was left on its side after a crash in York today (Friday).

Witnesses described it as a two-car collision.

It’s on Water Lane near its junction with Lancaster Way. The crash happened on the roundabout.

#### Crash location

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/car-overturns-water-lane-lancaster-way-york-lc.webp)

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/car-overturns-water-lane-lancaster-2-way-york-dn.webp)

The road is still open and traffic is running as normal.

One person was taken to hospital but they’re not believed to be seriously injured.
