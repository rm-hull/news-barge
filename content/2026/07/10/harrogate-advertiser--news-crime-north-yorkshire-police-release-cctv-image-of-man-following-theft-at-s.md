# North Yorkshire Police release CCTV image of man following theft at shop in Harrogate

## North Yorkshire Police has issued a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to following a theft at a shop in Harrogate.

The incident happened on June 22, at Cotswold Outdoor on West Park, and involved the theft of clothing.

A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: “Please contact us if you recognise the male pictured in the CCTV image, as he may have information that will assist our investigation.”
