# King reunites with Archie and Lilibet after Meghan Markle brings children to UK during Prince Harry trip

- The King has been reunited with his grandchildren, Archie and Lilibet, after the Duke and Duchess of Sussex visited him at his Highgrove country estate.
- Charles welcomed Meghan and Harry’s children, aged seven and five, to his Gloucestershire residence during the afternoon, following days of speculation.
- It marks their first in-person meeting with their grandfather since Queen Elizabeth II’s Platinum Jubilee celebrations in 2022.
- The Queen, who has a private home near the King’s property, is also understood to have been present.
- Harry arrived in London on Monday without his family after they pulled out of the visit to the capital because of security concerns. However, it appears worries around safety may have been mitigated.
