## Major clothing retailer announces its arrival into centre of York

The Animal store at 7 Davygate *(Image: Darren Greenwood)*

A major clothing retailer has announced its arrival in York.

UK lifestyle brand Animal has opened at 7 Davygate, where sister company Mountain Warehouse used to be, before it moved to a larger site in the street.

Animal says the opening marks a significant step in it’s evolution, blending contemporary design with the brand’s coastal heritage and celebrating a lifestyle that’s active, outdoor, and travel ready.

Eight new jobs have been created in the area as a result of the opening.

From its beginnings in 1987, Animal says has evolved into a lifestyle brand for those who live life outdoors.

The new York store promises timeless, functional pieces designed for every journey, from the coast to the city and everywhere in between.

Shantelle Charters, Animal’s Chief Commercial Officer, said: “York perfectly captures what Animal stands for - it’s creative, active, and always outdoors.”

  Some of the clothes Animal sells 

*(Image: Pic supplied)*

“Our new store is designed to celebrate that spirit, offering a space where people can find inspiration for their next adventure, whether that’s a weekend away, an outdoor walk or exploring their own city.”

Inside the new store, visitors are promised a clean, modern space set over two floors with milky-pale walls and matte black fixtures that provide a crisp canvas for the latest collections.

The space is also brought to life by Animal’s signature mannequins, each featuring a bold geometric animal head design, an owl, a fox, and a hare, instantly drawing the eye and creating a memorable visual experience.

Visitors will be able to browse a versatile range of womenswear, menswear, kidswear, footwear, beachwear, and travel accessories designed to suit every kind of adventure and everyday lifestyle, the retailer added.
