# Belgium star suffers injury in warm-up before World Cup quarter-final with Spain

- Belgium were forced to make a last-minute change to their starting lineup for the World Cup quarter-final against Spain.
- Captain Youri Tielemans sustained an injury during the warm-up session before the match.
- Tielemans, who was set to start alongside Nicolas Raskin, was replaced by Club Brugge midfielder Hans Vanaken.
- The alteration to the team occurred approximately 12 minutes prior to the scheduled kick-off.
- The Belgian team confirmed the change via their social media channels, stating Tielemans' replacement by Vanaken due to injury.
