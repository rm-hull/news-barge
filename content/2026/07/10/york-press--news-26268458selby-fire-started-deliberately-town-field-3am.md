ARSONISTS have deliberately sparked an early morning fire in a North Yorkshire town field.

The county's fire and rescue service said a crew from Selby was called to a fire just after 3am this morning (Friday, July 10).

  


A service spokesperson said an abandoned motorcycle was "well alight" as firefighters arrived.

The crew used knapsack sprayers, buckets of water and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the blaze.
