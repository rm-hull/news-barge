A site manager has been recognised for his leadership on a housing development.

Karl Laverick, senior site manager at Bellway Homes’ Penny Way development in Snaith, has received the National House Building Council’s (NHBC) Pride in the Job Quality Award for his work at the site.

The award marks his third win, having previously been honoured for his work at Bellway’s Ambler’s Grange site in Pocklington.

Melanie Smith, sales director at Bellway Yorkshire, said: "This award is a representation of the hard work, professionalism and total commitment to driving standards that align with the Bellway vision."

The NHBC-run awards scheme is the only UK-wide competition in the housebuilding industry to recognise site managers who deliver to the highest possible standard.

Mr Laverick said: "This award doesn’t celebrate just my work, it celebrates the team that work with me every day to ensure the highest standards are met."

Penny Way offers homes ranging from two to five bedrooms.
