# World Cup today: Spain face Belgium – but who would the winner play in the semi-finals?

- Spain and Belgium will compete in the World Cup 2026 quarter-finals in Los Angeles today, with the victor progressing to face France in the semi-finals.
- The match is scheduled for an **8pm BST (3pm ET) kick-off** with both the European teams very familiar to each other.
- Spain's squad faces potential injuries to Nico Williams and Yeremy Pino, while Belgium will be without Amadou Onana, though Jeremy Doku and Kevin De Bruyne may return to their starting XI.
- In other World Cup news, England faces potential double injury and illness setbacks ahead of their quarter-final against Norway on Saturday.
- Marc Guehi is a doubt due to a sore hamstring, having missed recent training sessions while Declan Rice is ill.
