A THIEF has targeted a popular outdoor retailer in North Yorkshire.

North Yorkshire Police has launched an investigation into an alleged theft in Cotswold Outdoors in Harrogate, where items of clothing were reported stolen on June 22.

Officers have published a CCTV image of a man the force would like to speak to, as he may have information that will assist the investigation.

  


A police spokesperson said: “Please email PC1488 Bell on [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk), or visit our website, select ‘Tell us about’, then choose ‘An existing case or report’ if you have any information.

“Alternatively, live chat is available via our website, Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.

“If you are unable to use these options, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101 or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

“Please quote reference 12260116236 when providing information.”
