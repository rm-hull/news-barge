A North Yorkshire-based whisky distillery is marking a milestone with a limited-edition release.

Spirit of Yorkshire, based in Hunmanby, is celebrating 10 years in business with its first 10-year-old age statement whisky, the Filey Bay 10 Year Old Single Cask 'Decade of Distilling.'

David Thompson, co-founder of Spirit of Yorkshire, said: "Be the first to both try and take home our ‘Decade of Distilling’ Single Cask, the first 10-year-old age-statement Yorkshire whisky.

"This single cask whisky was distilled and casked in summer 2016.

"Presented in a special box and only available to people attending the Masterclass, it shows how our distillery style has matured over the past ten years.

"It is light, fruity and complex, with flavours of barley sugar, biscuit, citrus and caramel."

Only 192 bottles will be released, exclusively for masterclass attendees during the distillery’s open weekend on August 1 and August 2.

Tickets for the masterclass are available at: [https://bit.ly/DecadeofDistillingMasterclass](https://bit.ly/DecadeofDistillingMasterclass)
