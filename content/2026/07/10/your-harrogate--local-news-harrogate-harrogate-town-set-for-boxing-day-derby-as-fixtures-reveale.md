                                    Harrogate Town supporters can start planning for the new National League season - with a Boxing Day derby against FC Halifax Town among the standout dates.

The National League fixture list has been revealed today (Friday) and will see Simon Weaver's side begin their campaign at home to Solihull Moors on Saturday 8th August, before travelling to AFC Fylde a week later.

One of the most eye-catching fixtures comes on Boxing Day when Town make the short trip to face FC Halifax Town, with the reverse fixture at The Exercise Stadium taking place on Good Friday.

Other notable matches include clashes with fellow former EFL clubs Carlisle United, Barrow, Forest Green Rovers and Southend United as Harrogate look to make an immediate return to League Two.

Town's final league match of the regular season will see them travel to Worthing on Saturday 24th April.
