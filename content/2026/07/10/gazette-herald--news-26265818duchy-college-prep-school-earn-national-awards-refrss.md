A school in Harrogate has been commended in two categories at a national awards scheme.

Duchy College, formerly Harrogate Ladies’ College, was recognised in the Independent Schools of the Year Awards, receiving a commendation in the Regional Independent School of the Year: North category.

Duchy College Prep, formerly Highfield Prep, was also commended in the Independent Prep School of the Year category.

Joanna Fox, principal of Duchy College, said: "To gain recognition in these national awards is a fantastic achievement for our family of schools.

"We are exceptionally proud to represent our region in the North, with our pupils achieving some outstanding accolades in the past 12 months."

Pupils have enjoyed notable success, including three winners at the TeenTech Finals and the senior netball team reaching the finals of the Sisters N Sport Seniors Trophy.

The Chapel Choir also reached the Grand Final of the National Choir Competition.

Duchy College Prep is the only commended prep school in North Yorkshire.

James Savile, head of Duchy College Prep, said: "Our prep school has delivered outstanding outcomes for our pupils this year and are worthy recipients of a commendation."

The awards celebrate excellence and student experience in the independent education sector, with winners to be announced in October.
