A MAN is in police custody in after being arrested for allegedly "going into people's gardens and interfering with vehicles" in a York suburb.

North Yorkshire Police said the arrest took place following alleged incidents of suspicious behaviour reported around 5am on Friday (July 10) morning near Rowan Avenue in New Earswick.

  



The force has launched an appeal for information that could support its investigation and in addition a spokesperson said: "We are also asking anyone who may have witnessed a man acting suspiciously to please contact us."
