# World Cup final tickets suddenly made available by Fifa for thousands of dollars

- Fifa unexpectedly released nearly 1,200 high-priced tickets for the World Cup final on July 19 at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey, despite previously indicating the event was sold out.
- The last-minute sales included 1,178 Category 2 seats, priced at $7,380 each, distributed across five sections of the top deck along the sidelines.
- For a more premium experience, Fifa also offered 68 front Category 1 tickets in the lower deck, with prices ranging from $19,995 to $32,970.
- Exclusive hospitality packages in the Trophy Lounge and Trophy Lounge+ sections, which include food and drinks, were also made available at $34,500 and $32,500 respectively.
- On Fifa's official marketplace, resale tickets for the final were listed with a wide price range, from $7,440.50 to nearly $11.5 million.
