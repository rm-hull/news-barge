## Top rated 5-star B&B comes to market in popular East Coast resort for £635,000

The B&B comes with stunning sea views *(Image: Pic supplied)*

A highly-rated five-star bed and breakfast has gone on the market in Scarborough.

Specialist business property adviser Christie & Co has been instructed to market The Helaina in Blenheim Terrace with an asking price of £635,000 for the freehold.

Comprising 10 double en suite rooms, a guest lounge and breakfast room on the ground floor and set in an elegant 5 storey building, the agents say the hotel is tastefully presented throughout with the Penthouse Suite and The Premium Double on the top floor being outstanding examples of luxury accommodation.

There is also a spacious and very pleasant owner’s apartment on the lower ground floor with sitting room, kitchen, bathroom, office and bedroom.

The agents say the property also boasts excellent guest reviews and ratings and a Visit England 5 Star accreditation.

TripAdvisor awards The Helaina 4.8 stars out of five, based on 701 reviews, ranking it 30th out of 167 Scarborough B&Bs and Inns.

Booking.Com awards 9.5 out of ten, branding the property ‘exceptional.’

  A most lovely interior 

*(Image: Pic supplied)*

Christie & Co says The Helaina enjoys a wonderful cliff top position which affords spectacular coastal views along the North Bay Beach and the historic Scarborough Castle.

It is just a short walk into the town centre and all its amenities and tourist attractions, including both North and South Bay.

Owners, Tom and Jill Wilson, have operated the business for more than 20 years but have decided to sell in order to focus on their hobbies and interests.

They said, “After 21 wonderful years at The Helaina, we are ready to have more time to pursue our interests of surfing, hiking, mountain biking, art and travel. We take great pride in having created a beautiful B&B with both a strong repeat business and a healthy flow of many new guests.”

  A well appointed room with a view of the sea 

*(Image: Pic supplied)*

Mark Worley, Director – Hotels at Christie & Co, who is managing the sale process, said: “If anyone is considering purchasing a bed and breakfast business, then The Helaina is surely a perfect choice. It is beautifully presented with great reviews and reputation and is set in an amazing location, ideal for tourism and leisure trade.

“There is substantial living accommodation included and the business posts substantial profits derived from just 8 months trade, allowing the owners four months holiday every year.”

For more information, visit the Christie & co website.
