                                    A Bilton woman has become the twelfth person to claim a full tank of free fuel.

Our Free Fuel Friday competition returned thanks to O'Connor Chiropractic, as Your Harrogate's Ricky Durkin donned the Free Fuel Fairy outfit to top up a tank for free.

Clues were given on the radio and on social media to hint at where the Your Harrogate Free Fuel Fairy would be appearing after 10am.

And Sue was the first to find Ricky and utter the phrase that pays:


'Are you Your Harrogate's Free Fuel Fairy?'

Today’s secret location was the Shell petrol station on Ripon Road in Harrogate.

Sue scooped £65 of petrol and plans to drive to the Lake District next week with family.

Could you be next week's Free Fuel Friday winner? Just follow the clues next week and be the first to find our Free Fuel Fairy.
