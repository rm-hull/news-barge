Firefighters were called to a deliberate fire in the early hours of this morning (Friday).

They went to reports of a fire in a field in **Selby** at**3.06am**.

This turned out to be a motorbike well alight.

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “The crew used knapsack sprayers, buckets of water and a thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire.

“The cause is believed to have been deliberate.”
