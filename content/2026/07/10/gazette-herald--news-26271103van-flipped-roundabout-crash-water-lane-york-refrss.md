A VAN has been flipped on its side following a crash in York.

The incident took place on the roundabout leading to Tamworth Road in Water Lane in Clifton shortly after 1.30pm on Friday (July 10).

  


The crash, which residents said involved two vehicles, resulted in a white van being flipped on the road.

A tow van was seen in the area following the crash and one person was taken to hospital.

A spokesperson for Yorkshire Ambulance Service said: "We were made aware of a collision on the junction of Lancaster Way and Water Lane in Rawcliffe earlier today, but ambulance resources were not required."
