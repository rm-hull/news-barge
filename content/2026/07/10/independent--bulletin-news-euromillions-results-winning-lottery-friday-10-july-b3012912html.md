# EuroMillions results: Winning lottery and Thunderball numbers for Friday 10 July

- Tonight’s EuroMillions draw has offered players the chance to win a jackpot of £24million.
- To win the prize, a player had to get five main numbers plus two lucky stars.
- In the UK, every EuroMillions entry automatically includes a unique "UK Millionaire Maker" code, entering you into an additional draw with a guaranteed £1 million winner in every draw.
- The winning EuroMillions numbers were **2, 14, 28, 33** and**48**. The Lucky Stars were** 8**and** 10**.
- The winning Thunderball numbers were **1, 8, 11, 23 and 26**. The Thunderball was** 9**.
