CREWS were called to reports of a fire on the North York Moors yesterday afternoon (Thursday).

Crews from Helmsley, Kirkbymoorside, Tadcaster and over the border appliance Coulby Newham were mobilised to the incident at Chop Gate at 1.08pm.
