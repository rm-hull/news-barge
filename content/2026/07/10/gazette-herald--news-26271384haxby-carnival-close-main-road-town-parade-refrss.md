FINAL preparations are underway for an annual town celebration and carnival.

Residents and road users are being reminded of several road closures to make way for the Haxby Carnival on Saturday (July 11).

The special event will include a town parade starting from Grey Firs at 11am.

  


City of York Council will close The Village, between its junctions with Westfield Road and Station Road from 10:45am to 11:25am.

As reported by The Press, York Road from its junction with Station Road to its junction with Calf Close number 110 and the junction with York Road.

Alternative routes for diverted traffic will be signposted and will include Station Road, Usher Lane, Crossmoor Lane, Moor Lane, and The Village, the council said.

Traffic signs and barriers will be in place to mark the extent of the restrictions.

The parade will finish at Ethel Ward Playing Field in York Road, where a carnival will be waiting.

Families will be able to enjoy fun games, animal encounters, a miniature train, fun fair, penalty shoot-out, a sensory den and more.

Food vendors will be parked at the playing fields, along with market stalls until 5.30pm.
