Uber began as a modern alternative to taxis, allowing the rider to order conveniently from an app on their phone, follow the driver’s route as they ride, share their location with contacts and even split fare through the app—it was the convenience-obsessed answer to the old way of hailing cabs. Then in 2014, Uber leveled up with Uber Eats. Much like Uber was the convenient, modern answer to taxis, Uber Eats was the equivalent to the world of food delivery.

Instead of calling a local restaurant and enlisting a delivery driver from said restaurant, Uber Eats draws from hundreds of participating restaurants, cafes, and stores from your local region, tailoring deals and picks based on your location and preference. So if you want fresh sushi from your favorite local spot for a date-night in, or just need some Advil and Gatorade after a night out, Uber Eats delivers you the goods in minutes flat. Whether you’re new to the service, or an old devotee, we have an Uber Eats promo code and other Uber Eats coupon codes to help you save big—and stay at home.
