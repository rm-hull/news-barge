                                    Police are appealing for information after an attempted burglary in Harrogate.

It happened at 3am this morning (Friday 10th July), on Hookstone Grange Way and involved a garage door being forced open.

A loud bang was heard, waking the victim. Nothing is believed to have been stolen from the garage.

A police spokesperson said:

"We’re particularly appealing for any witnesses or if any suspicious behaviour was seen or heard around the time of the incident, leading up to it or afterwards."


Anyone with information that could assist the investigation is asked to email: Jess Lovelock on [Jess.Lovelock@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:Jess.Lovelock@northyorkshire.police.uk) or call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

*Please quote reference 12260130065 when providing information.*
