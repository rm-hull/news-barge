A MAN carrying a young child as a passenger whilst riding an e-scooter illegally has been stopped by police officers.

It was seized in Sherburn-in-Elmet near Tadcaster on Tuesday, July 7 as the officer was out on patrol.

  


The e-scooter was stopped and confiscated after the officer explained the law to the rider.

Acting Sergeant Ben Pepper, from the Selby Neighbourhood Policing Team, said: "This serves as a reminder that privately owned e-scooters cannot legally be used on public roads, pavements or other public places."
