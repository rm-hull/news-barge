Fire crews are tackling another field blaze in North Yorkshire this afternoon.

A tractor and baler went up in flames, which have now spread to a field near Common Lane, Ulleskelf, south of Tadcaster.

A number of fire crews and a water bowser are at the scene.

#### Common Lane, Ulleskelf

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “Firefighters are working hard to create a fire break and prevent the fire from spreading further.

“There is a significant amount of smoke in the area which may affect visibility on nearby roads.

“Please avoid the area if possible and keep windows and doors closed if you are affected by smoke.”
