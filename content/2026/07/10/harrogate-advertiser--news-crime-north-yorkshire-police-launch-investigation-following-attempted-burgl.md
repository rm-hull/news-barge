# North Yorkshire Police launch investigation following attempted burglary at property in Harrogate

## North Yorkshire Police has launched an appeal for information and witnesses following an attempted burglary at a property in Harrogate.

The incident happened at 3am this morning (Friday 10 July), on Hookstone Grange Way, and involved a garage door being forced open.

A loud bang was heard, waking the victim.

Nothing is believed to have been stolen from the garage.

![The police have launched an appeal for information and witnesses following an attempted burglary at a property on Hookstone Grange Way in Harrogate](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOjBhYmY0YTdlLTliOWUtNDQzMi1iOTdlLTU2NGY5NjM3NjFlYjozYzAyOGExZC0wMzNlLTRlNjctYmRmMC1mMWE0ZmExNWY2YTA=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: “We’re particularly appealing for any witnesses or if any suspicious behaviour was seen or heard around the time of the incident, leading up to it or afterwards.”

You can also call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

You should quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12260130065 when providing any details regarding the incident.
