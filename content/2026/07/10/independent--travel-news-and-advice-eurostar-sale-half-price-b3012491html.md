Eurostar has launched a limited-time sale with one-way tickets to a number of destinations slashed by up to 50 per cent. Right now travellers can get one-way tickets from London starting at £35.

The discount applies to seats on one-way journeys to Paris, Lille, Brussels, Amsterdam direct, or Rotterdam direct, for Eurostar Plus and Eurostar Premier tickets only, so it is not applicable to standard Eurostar tickets.

The sale ends 13 July at 22:59 so travellers will need to be quick to take advantage of the summer savings. Train times and prices can be checked on the Eurostar website.

The offer only applies to adult tickets for two or more travellers, and a maximum of nine. It’s worth noting that those with a Child or Youth & Senior ticket will not be eligible.

Bookings will need to be made from London St Pancras International, for travel between 28 July and 23 September 2026, but there are certain dates when the offer will not be applied.

Eurostar Plus tickets are refundable seven days or more before the date of departure, but there is a £25/€25 fee per person for one way trips and a £50/€50 per person fee for a full refund of a return ticket. Eurostar Premier tickets are refundable with no fee up until 48 hours after your original departure time.

Eurostar runs sales and flash sales throughout the year, offering reduced prices for travellers who’re looking to explore Europe on a tighter budget. Club Eurostar is another way to save on tickets by earning points that can be used to pay for journeys, and with the opportunity to upgrade seats, among other perks.
