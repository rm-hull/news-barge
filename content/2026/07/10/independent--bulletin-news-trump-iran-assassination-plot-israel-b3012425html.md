# Israel reportedly warned Trump of new Iranian assassination threat

![President Donald Trump speaks from the East Room of the White House in Washington, Saturday, June 21, 2025, after the U.S. military struck three Iranian nuclear and military sites](https://static.independent.co.uk/2025/06/23/14/38/Mideast-Wars-Trump-noxyyhvi.jpeg)

- Israel reportedly shared intelligence with the U.S. regarding an alleged Iranian plot to assassinate President Donald Trump.
- This news surfaced amid heightened tensions between the U.S. and Iran, following recent exchanges of strikes.
- President Trump publicly acknowledged being a target, stating, 'They want to take out the U.S. leader — me.'
- U.S. officials have not verified the alleged plot, with some suggesting Israel's warning might be an attempt to influence the Trump administration's stance on the ongoing conflict.
- The U.S.-Israel relationship has faced recent strains due to disagreements over the Iran war and the Trump administration's criticism of Israeli military tactics.
