## Andris Kulbergs became Latvia's Prime Minister in May after the previous coalition government collapsed over its handling of stray drones. He speaks exclusively to Euronews as US Secretary of State, Marco Rubio, meets today Russian Foreign Minister Sergey Lavrov to discuss ending the war in Ukraine.

## On today's show:

Europe Today's anchor **Mared Gwyn** interviews Latvian Prime Minister**Andris Kulbergs** about NATO, European security and the alliance's future.

**Shona Murray** reports on what a meeting between US Secretary of State**Marco Rubio** with Russian Foreign Minister**Sergey Lavrov** could mean for peace in Ukraine, and speaks to**Colonel Martin O'Donnell**, NATO's senior military adviser.

**Luca Bertuzzi** explains why the EU is set to fine Google for breaching the bloc's Digital Markets Act (DMA).

We also speak to Germany's Minister of State for Europe, **Gunther Krichbaum**, about EU enlargement and plans to make the accession process more credible.
