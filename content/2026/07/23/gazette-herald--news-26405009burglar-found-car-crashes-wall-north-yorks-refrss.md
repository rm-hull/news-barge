A suspected burglar was found with a haul of allegedly stolen goods – after their vehicle crashed into a wall in North Yorkshire.

Cleveland Police said it received a report of a burglary in Ashmore Gardens, Stockton, on Tuesday morning (July 21), where watches and a Land Rover Discover were taken.

The force said it alerted North Yorkshire Police after the reportedly stolen car was spotted leaving the Cleveland area via the A19.

North Yorkshire Police located the car, which police said had collided with a wall.

The two occupants of the vehicle were taken to hospital to be checked over and suspected stolen items were recovered from the vehicle, Cleveland Police said.

Detective Inspector Barnes from Stockton CID said: “This was some fantastic partnership work from our Response teams, North Yorkshire colleagues and Stockton CID.

“We will continue to do all we can to tackle the issues that matter most to our communities. Please continue to report incidents and information to us.”

Police said a 51-year-old man was arrested and, following further investigation by Stockton CID, charged with burglary and aggravated vehicle taking. He was remanded in custody to appear at Teesside Magistrates’ Court on Thursday (July 23).
