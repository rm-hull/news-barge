                                    Organisers of Symphonic Ibiza are appealing for information after two of their promotional posters were damaged in Harrogate town centre.

The organisers say one poster was vandalised a few weeks ago and initially believed it was an isolated incident.

A replacement poster was installed, but the incident repeated itself just a few doors away.

The repeated damage has prompted them to appeal to the public for help.

As a thank you, organisers are offering two VIP Garden tickets to Symphonic Ibiza if information leads to those responsible being identified.

Anyone with information, CCTV or dashcam footage from the area is being encouraged to get in touch.

Despite the setback, the team say they remain focussed on delivering a memorable event at the Great Yorkshire Showground on Saturday 15th August.

The concert will feature the 40-piece Symphonic Ibiza Orchestra, alongside Heather Small, Rebecca Ferguson, Alison Limerick, DJ Alex Bowen and special guest Vernon Kay.
