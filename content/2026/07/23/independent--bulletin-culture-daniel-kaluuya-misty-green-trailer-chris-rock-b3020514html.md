Daniel Kaluuya, who won an Oscar in 2021, is making a comeback to live-action films after appearing in 2022's Nope.

The first trailer for his new film, Misty Green, directed by Chris Rock, was recently released online.

Kaluuya has taken on voice roles and behind-the-scenes work, including co-writing and producing The Kitchen and serving as associate artistic director at London's Roundhouse.

His upcoming projects include the thriller Hotel Hotel Hotel Hotel, reprising his role as Spider-Punk in a Spider-Verse sequel, and a rumored Barney the Dinosaur movie.

Widely regarded as one of the best actors of his generation, Kaluuya was ranked No. 6 among the greatest film actors of the 21st century in 2024.
