An equestrian bedding manufacturer has renewed its official partnership to support British Showjumping.

UNIBED, based at South Milford near Selby and offering a range of equestrian bedding options derived from straw sourced from Yorkshire farmers, has extended its official partnership with British Showjumping for another three years.

  The collaboration includes UNIBED’s role as official bedding supplier to the National Training Centre 

*(Image: Supplied)*

The collaboration includes UNIBED’s role as official bedding supplier to the National Training Centre.

A spokesman for British Showjumping said: "We are proud to align with UNIBED, a company renowned for its dedication to optimizing the health and well-being of horses.

  Jessica Vickers, commercial and operations director at UNIBED 

*(Image: Supplied)*

"This partnership not only allows us to showcase UNIBED's high-performance equine bedding to a wider audience but also highlights our shared commitment to sustainability."

Jessica Vickers, Commercial and Operations Director of UNIBED, added: "UNIBED is extremely proud to be continuing our partnership with British Showjumping, a partnership we hold with great regard.

  UNIBED is a family-run business, centred in its agricultural roots 

*(Image: Supplied)*

"This partnership allows us to showcase what we can offer the equestrian community and spread our message that they do not need to compromise on their horse's health nor the efficiency of their bedding when choosing a cost-effective option."

UNIBED is a family-run business, centred in its agricultural roots, offering a range of equestrian bedding options all derived from the straw it sources from Yorkshire farmers.
