# Shania Twain turned down an invitation to Taylor Swift’s wedding – here’s why

- Shania Twain turned down an invitation to Taylor Swift's wedding to Travis Kelce in New York City.
- Twain was opening for Swift’s ex Harry Styles at Wembley Stadium during his London residency, which coincided with the wedding on 3 July, meaning she was unable to attend.
- Speaking on *The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon,* Twain expressed being “very honoured” by the invitation, adding: “Next time I see her, I will of course give her a big congratulations.”
- Twain and Styles have performed together previously, including at Coachella and the Brit Awards.
- The Canadian singer's appearance on the show was to promote her new album, *Little Miss Twain*.
