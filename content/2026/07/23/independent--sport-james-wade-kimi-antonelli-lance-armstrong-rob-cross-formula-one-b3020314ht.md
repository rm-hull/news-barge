Formula One returns for another week as the drivers get set for practice at the Hungarian Grand Prix.

The Betfred World Matchplay is beginning to get into the business end of the competition as we head into the quarter-finals.

We also head into day four of The Hundred as Birmingham Phoenix take on Trent Rockets.

## F1 heads to Hungary

![Kimi Antonelli won in Belgium last week (Omar Havana/AP)](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/23/15/19-398139d2825548bfbecc97c802a6b3a6.jpg)

After **Kimi Antonelli** claimed his sixth Formula One victory of the season, he will be looking to take that form into the**Hungarian Grand Prix** which starts with first practice live on** Sky Sports Main Event** and **Sky Sports F1** from**midday.** Second practice is also live on**Sky Sports F1** at**3.45pm**. Britain’s** George Russell**crashed out of the Belgian Grand Prix following a collision with compatriot** Lewis Hamilton**.

## World Matchplay hots up

![Gerwyn Price is in action on Friday (Bradley Collyer/PA)](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/23/15/89241e2d157b1da80199990da6d5810aY29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzg0ODk1Njg4-2.84773518.jpg)

The second set of **World Matchplay** quarter-finals will get under way live at the Winter Gardens in Blackpool on **Sky Sports Action** at **8pm** and**Sky Sports Main Event** at**9.30pm**.** Gerwyn Price **saw off Rob Cross in the last round and he sets up a meeting with** Ross Smith**for a place in the last four, while** James Wade**continues his search for a second successive final in this competition when he faces** Gian van Veen**.

## First round of The Hundred concludes

![The first round of fixtures concludes (Steven Paston/PA)](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/23/15/3b67b622224bf7852283ce0a07d7004aY29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzg0ODk1NzQw-2.85375476.jpg)

All the teams have played their first set of fixtures in the sixth edition of **The Hundred**, apart from** Birmingham Phoenix**and** Trent Rockets**– the pair will conclude the first round live with coverage of the women’s game starting at** 2.45pm**on** Sky Sports Cricket**and** 3pm**on** Sky Sports Main Event**and the men’s game on the same channels from** 6.00pm**.

## What else is on?

![The Commonwealth Games is up and running in Glasgow (Andrew Milligan/PA)](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/23/15/1dbd61c59fabeec42e9d32fe3c8f0f37Y29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzg0ODk1ODE0-2.85371757.jpg)

**The Commonwealth Games** is under way in**Glasgow** and there is action from swimming, basketball, boxing, powerlifting and gymnastics live on **TNT Sports 2** from**8.30am** and**TNT Sports 4** at**6.30pm**. The** Tour de France **is drawing closer to the finish line and Stage 19 will commence from Gap to Alpe d’Huez on** TNT Sports 1**at** 12:30pm**. In golf, the** ISPS HANDA Women’s Scottish Open**continues on** Sky Sports Main Event**and** Sky Sports Golf**at** 9.30am**, the** Senior Open Championship**is on** Sky Sports Golf**at** 1.30pm**and** 3M Open**coverage is on the same channel at** 7pm**.

## On this day in history

![Ole Gunnar Solskjaer signed a new deal with Manchester United on this day five years ago (John Walton/PA)](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/23/15/bb0a5c334c6343efe91d7fc72cb730b9Y29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzg0ODk2MTU5-2.63864399.jpg)

2005: Lance Armstrong won a record seventh-successive Tour de France – his final Tour before his temporary retirement. He was later stripped of all his titles after evidence of his doping emerged.

2021: Ole Gunnar Solskjaer signed a new three-year deal as Manchester United manager – the Norwegian was sacked less than four months later.

2023: Former Birmingham and England forward Trevor Francis, who became Britain’s first £1million footballer when he joined Nottingham Forest in 1979, died at the age of 69.
