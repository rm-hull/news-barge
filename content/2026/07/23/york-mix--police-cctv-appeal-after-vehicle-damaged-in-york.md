Police have issued this image of a man that they would like to speak to following an incident of criminal damage to a vehicle in York.

He may have vital information that could help with the investigation.

It happened on the 20th July, on Bowling Green Court, and involved a male scratching the victims vehicle with a sharp implement causing thousands of pounds worth of damage.

Please contact Police if you recognise the man.
