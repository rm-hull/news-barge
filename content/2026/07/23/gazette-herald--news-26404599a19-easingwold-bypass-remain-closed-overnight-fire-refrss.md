A stretch of the A19 in North Yorkshire will remain closed overnight after a trailer laden with hay caught fire, the council has said.

North Yorkshire Council said the A19 Easingwold bypass will remain closed overnight from Thursday (July 23) after the fire broke out at about 10.45am on Wednesday.

Firefighters tackled the burning hay bales but the road remains closed for emergency repairs.

It is not known what caused the straw to catch light or the cost of the damage.

The AA said the A19 is blocked both ways from York Road to Thirsk Road but traffic is “coping well”.

North Yorkshire Council urged drivers to “please avoid the area and use an alternative route”.
