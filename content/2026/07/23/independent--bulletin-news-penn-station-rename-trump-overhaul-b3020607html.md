The Trump administration is signaling its intention to rename New York’s Penn Station, potentially after the president.

Transportation Secretary Sean Duffy outlined "administration priorities" for the Manhattan transit hub, which is slated for an $8 billion federal makeover.

Duffy's letter to Congress directs Amtrak to rename New York Penn Station, and he previously suggested "Trump Station" as a possibility.

Reports indicate the president has employed various tactics to rename the station after himself, including linking federal funding for a rail project to the renaming, though he dismissed some reports as "fake news."

Other institutions, such as the U.S. Institute of Peace and Palm Beach International Airport, have already been rebranded after the president during his second term.
