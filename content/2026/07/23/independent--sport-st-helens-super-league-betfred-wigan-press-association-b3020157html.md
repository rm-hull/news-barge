St Helens head coach Paul Rowley has stepped down with immediate effect, the BetfredSuper League club have announced.

Rowley’s departure, less than a year into the job, comes amid a difficult season and just a day before the club face arch-rivals Wigan at the Brick Community Stadium.

Assistant coach Eamon O’Carroll will take charge of first-team affairs on an interim basis.
