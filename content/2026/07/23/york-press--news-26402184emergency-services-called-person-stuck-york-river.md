EMERGENCY services have been called to reports of a person stuck in a York river.

  


Fire crews from the city went to a rescue operation at the scene in Foss Bank at 5am today (Thursday, July 23).

A service spokesperson said: "York and Acomb crews were mobilised.

"On arrival the person was out of the river and then brought into the care of ambulance on scene."

The Press has contacted Yorkshire Ambulance Service NHS Trust and will update the story here when we have more information.
