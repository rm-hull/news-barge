Police investigating an incident at a York shop want to identify these two people.

It happened at the **Holland and Barrett** store at**York Designer Outlet** at**5.25pm** on**Friday, 26 June**.

In a social media post, North Yorkshire Police said: “They are alleged to have taken reduced stickers from items for sale and placed them on non-reduced items before making purchase causing a loss to the store (the word alleged is doing some heavy lifting here since it is all on CCTV).”
