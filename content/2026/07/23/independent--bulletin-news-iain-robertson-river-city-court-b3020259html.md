Actor Iain Robertson has been found guilty of rape following a trial at the High Court in Glasgow.

Robertson, aged 45, was convicted of a total of five offences committed against a number of women.

The offences took place over a 15-year period, specifically between 2004 and 2019, with the rape occurring between 2018 and 2019.

During the trial, a woman testified that Robertson had sex with her despite her making clear she was not consenting, while Robertson denied any wrongdoing.

He is scheduled to be sentenced at the High Court in Edinburgh on 23 July.
