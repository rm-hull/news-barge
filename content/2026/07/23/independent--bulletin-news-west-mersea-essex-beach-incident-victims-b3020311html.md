Emergency services called to ‘tragic incident’ off Essex coast

Mother Shally Rahman, 42, and her 15-year-old daughter Samiha Rahman have been named as the two individuals who died after getting into difficulty in the water off the Essex coast.

A seven-year-old boy, also part of the family, remains in a serious condition in hospital following the “tragic incident”.

Emergency services were called to West Mersea beach near Colchester at 5:35pm on Wednesday evening.

Witnesses reported seeing a group of people on a seabank calling for help as the tide came in.

Police are appealing for witnesses to assist their ongoing investigation, saying that those involved in the incident are believed to have known each other.
