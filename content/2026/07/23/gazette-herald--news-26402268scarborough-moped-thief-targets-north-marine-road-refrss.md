A THIEF has used "brute force" to snap the lock of a moped and wheel it away in a North Yorkshire town.

The county's force said it happened in North Marine Road, Scarborough.

  



North Yorkshire Police have released a CCTV image of a person they wish to speak to, saying he may have information which can support its investigation.

A force spokesperson said: "Anyone who recognises the individual pictured, or who has information that could help the investigation, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police.

"Information can be provided by emailing [mark.burrows@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:mark.burrows@northyorkshire.police.uk), calling 101, or contacting Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

"Please quote reference 12260072438."
