A wizarding-themed afternoon tea is back and available in York.

The Grand, York is running the special afternoon tea event until 30 September, inviting guests of all ages to "step into an enchanting world of fantasy".

From themed decor and miniature cauldrons to bubbling potions, tables are adorned with these touches.

Visitors will be welcomed with an assortment of vibrant brews, potions, and butter beer, all deemed essential refreshments for a day of spellcasting.

Sweet and savoury delights, including the Grand Talisman, a cinnamon doughnut with mascarpone honey chantilly, and Yorkshire brie and celeriac quiche, headline the magical menu.

Alongside the wizarding menu, the classic afternoon tea will still be available.

For little ones wishing to partake in the enchantment, there's a tailored children's wizarding afternoon tea.

To mark the return of the wizarding afternoon tea, The Grand is also running a school competition, offering a child and their classmates the chance to win an afternoon tea school trip.

For the competition, pupils get to showcase their creativity by designing their own magical potion bottle.

The most powerful potion will earn a school trip for the winning pupil's class and teachers to enjoy an afternoon tea at the hotel.
