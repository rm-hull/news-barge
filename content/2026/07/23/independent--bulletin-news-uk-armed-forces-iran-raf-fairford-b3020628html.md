The UK government responded by stating its armed forces are ready to defend the country from any attacks, highlighting its layered air and missile defence capabilities.

Tehran accused the 'British monarchy' of being the 'main cause of the misfortunes of the people of our region' in its warning.

The warning followed the UK's recommitment to an agreement allowing the US to use British bases for defensive operations, including degrading missile sites in the Strait of Hormuz.

The UK has temporarily withdrawn diplomatic staff from Iran due to the escalating security situation, with the Foreign Office advising against all travel to the country.
