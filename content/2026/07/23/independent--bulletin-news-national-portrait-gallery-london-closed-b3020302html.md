# Tickets refunded as power outage closes National Portrait Gallery

- The National Portrait Gallery in central London is closing on Thursday due to an “unexpected” power outage.
- The closure affects the entire gallery, including the Marilyn Monroe: A Portrait exhibition and The Portrait Restaurant.
- Ticketholders for the affected day can exchange their tickets for another day or receive a full refund.
- The gallery's Audrey Green Cafe will remain open despite the power issue.
- The Marilyn Monroe exhibition, which opened in June, features works by artists such as Andy Warhol and Marlene Dumas.
