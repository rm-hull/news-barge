Dyson's supersonic has become one of the most recognisable hair dryers on the market, thanks to its fast drying performance and technology that is designed to combat frizz and boost shine. While Aldi's latest launch isn't trying to replace the premium styling tool, it does share one feature with the cult favourite: ionic technology.

Ionic technology is designed to help minimise static and tame flyaways while drying. Now, the supermarket has launched the Ambiano ionic hair dryer as part of its latest Specialbuys drop, offering shoppers a budget-friendly way to enjoy smoother-looking blow outs, without spending hundreds of pounds on a salon do.

The lightweight hair dryer also offers three heat and two speed settings to suit different hair types and styling routines. You can use the concentrator nozzle to help achieve a sleeker blowout, while the diffuser attachment can be used to dry curly hair. At just £14.99, it’s a seriously tempting option for anyone looking to upgrade their hair styling tools while keeping to a budget. You can find it in stores from Sunday, 26 July.

The Ambiano ionic hair dryer will land in stores from Sunday, 26 July as part of the Specialbuys range. This means it won’t be around for long, so if you’re interested, head down to your local Aldi this Sunday before stock disappears.

## How much is **Aldi’s new hair dryer?**

Aldi’s hair dryer will cost £14.99, making it a very budget-friendly styling option. You’ll get two attachments: a concentrator nozzle for directing airflow during styling and a diffuser attachment for adding volume and enhancing natural curls or waves. You’ll also have three colour options to choose from: purple/black, cream/black and black.
