Angela Rayner refuses to repay £16k severance payout after returning to cabinet

Angela Rayner has defended her decision not to return a nearly £17,000 severance payment received after resigning from government in 2025.

She received the payment following her resignation from roles including housing secretary and deputy Labour Party leader, amidst a dispute over her tax affairs.

HMRC subsequently cleared her of any wrongdoing regarding her tax affairs.

She justified keeping the payment by stating she had worked in government for a period, was out for 'just under a year,' and is now back in government.
