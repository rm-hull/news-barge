POLICE have been called in after a fire in a York suburb was thought to have been started deliberately.

  


North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said a crew from Acomb was called to Northfield around 6.40pm yesterday (Wednesday, July 22).

The fire was one square metre and made up of a disposable barbeque and rubbish.

A service spokesperson said: "Crews used knapsack sprayers to extinguish the fire.

"Police were informed as this fire was believed to have been started deliberately."
