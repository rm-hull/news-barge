England will prepare for the Ashes with a five-match One Day International series against Pakistan and a one-off Test against Bangladesh at Lord's.

The England and Wales Cricket Board also announced that The Oval will host next year's World Test Championship final, following its role in the 2023 showpiece. Ashes tickets are likely go on sale in September.
