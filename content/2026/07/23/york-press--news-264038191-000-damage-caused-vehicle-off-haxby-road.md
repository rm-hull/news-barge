More than £1,000 worth of damage has been caused to a vehicle in York.

North Yorkshire Police has issued a photograph of a man officers want to speak to about the damage in Bowling Green Court, off Haxby Road, on Monday (July 20).

The force said the man pictured may have information about the damage, which it said was caused by a man scratching the vehicle.

  Police want to speak to this man about the damage in Bowling Green Court, off Haxby Road in York 

*(Image: North Yorkshire Police)*

A police spokesperson said more than £1,000 worth of damage was caused after the vehicle was scratched “with a sharp implement”.

“Please contact us if you recognise the male pictured on CCTV, as he may have information that will assist our investigation,” they said.

Anyone with information is asked to report it on North Yorkshire Police website, email [ed.chatland@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:ed.chatland@northyorkshire.police.uk) or phone 101.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via its website.

Quote reference 12260139677 when providing information to police or to Crimestoppers.
