Sam Smith has partnered with BBC Introducing to find 10 emerging musical acts.

These selected artists will open Smith's upcoming UK residency shows in Manchester and London.

Aspiring musicians can upload their music to the BBC Introducing Uploader between 29 July and 5 August, using the “Sam Smith” tag.

A BBC Introducing panel will shortlist entries, with Smith personally selecting the final 10 acts.

The residency shows are scheduled for 3 and 4 September in Manchester, and from 8 to 19 September in London, following the release of Smith's new album, Hazel Eyes.
