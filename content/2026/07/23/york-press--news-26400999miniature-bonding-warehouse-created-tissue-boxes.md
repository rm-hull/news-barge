A Grade II-listed riverside landmark has been immortalised in miniature form as a charming papercraft kit.

The Bonding Warehouse, overlooking the River Ouse in York, now features as a tissue box cover design created by Helen Reynolds, founder of Viscount Folly and a long-time admirer of the building.

Ms Reynolds said: "I’ve always loved this building from being a teenager, and as a member of its co-working space, it made sense to include it in my collection."

Built in 1875, the Bonding Warehouse originally stored untaxed goods before later serving as a pub, steakhouse, and comedy club.

It has since been converted into luxury apartments and a co-working hub managed by Patch.

Lottie Maeda, community and events executive at Patch, said: "At Patch, we strive to create space and opportunity for local businesses to shine."

The kit is currently available exclusively at the Patch pop-up market, which is open during office hours.
