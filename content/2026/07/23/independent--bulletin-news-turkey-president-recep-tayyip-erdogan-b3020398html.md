Turkishgovernor Mehmet Fatih Cicekli was dismissed by presidential decree after photos of him in snug cycling shorts went viral and drew criticism.

The dismissal, signed by President Recep Tayyip Erdogan, offered no official explanation but followed widespread criticism from some Turkish politicians.

Critics argued that the tight cycling attire was inappropriate for a governor on duty and clashed with cultural norms in a conservative region like Ardahan.

Mr Cicekli defended his choice, stating he is an athlete who practices various sports and that the clothing was sportswear.

A campaign for his reinstatement has garnered over 10,000 signatures, with supporters highlighting his efforts to promote sports and tourism in the province.
