Terrifying moment car hurled into the air by tornado crossing highway

Dashcam footage shows a car being tossed through the air by a powerful tornado in China.

The incident occurred in Zhejiang province on Sunday, 19 July, with the vehicle lifted and flung to the roadside.

It is currently unclear if anyone was injured during the tornado.

This event follows Typhoon Bavi, which made landfall in Zhejiang on 11 July, affecting several regions in Asia.

Typhoon Bavi was the strongest July typhoon in the province since 1949, leading to the evacuation of over 2.2 million people, widespread flooding, and travel cancellations.
