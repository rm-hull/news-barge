A GROUP of passengers have been stuck in a lift in a coastal North Yorkshire town.

  



North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said it was called to St Nicholas Cliff in Scarborough around 10.30pm yesterday (Wednesday, July 22).

Five people were rescued from the lift which was in a residential building.

A spokesperson for North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said: "Crews released all persons safely using lift keys.

"Advice was given and an engineer contacted to inspect the lift."
