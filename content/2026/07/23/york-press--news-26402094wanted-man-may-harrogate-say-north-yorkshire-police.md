NORTH Yorkshire Police has renewed an appeal to the public for help to trace a man in connection with an assault that occurred in Knaresborough.

Officers want to locate to 32-year-old Ethan Wells-Smith, who is believed to be in the Harrogate area but also has links to Boroughbridge.

Wells-Smith is described as white with brown hair and blue eyes.

  


Extensive enquiries are ongoing to locate him, and police are now appealing for public assistance.

A spokesman for North Yorkshire Police said: "Anyone who has an immediate sighting of him is asked to call 999.

"If you have information about his current whereabouts, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

"Information can also be passed to independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

"Please quote reference number 12260138208 when passing on any information."
