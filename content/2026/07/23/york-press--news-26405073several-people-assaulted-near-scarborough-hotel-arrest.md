A man has been arrested after several people were allegedly assaulted and a number of vehicles were reportedly damaged in a North Yorkshire town.

North Yorkshire Police said the alleged incident happened near the Esplanade Hotel in Scarborough at around 8.10am on Wednesday (July 22).

The force said it involved a “man assaulting several people and causing damage to a number of vehicles”.

A police spokesperson said officers want to speak to a man “who we understand was assaulted during the incident”.

“We also believe that a woman may have also been assaulted and would also urge her to come forward,” they said.

“We are also appealing to anyone who witnessed the incident, or who has any information that could assist our enquiries, to contact us.”

Anyone with information is asked to email [Leah.Driscoll@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:Leah.Driscoll@northyorkshire.police.uk) or phone 101.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously via its website or on 0800 555 111.

Quote reference 12260139725 when providing information to police or to Crimestoppers.

Police said a 23-year-old man was arrested in connection with the alleged incident and has since been released on bail.
