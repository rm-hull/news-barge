# How to watch the Commonwealth Games – everything you need to know

- Glasgow is hosting a slimmed-down version of the Commonwealth Games after the Australian state of Victoria withdrew due to the cost of hosting.
- Athletes from 74 nations and territories will compete in a 10-sport programme, which includes six Para sports.
- Key sports featured are athletics, gymnastics, cycling, and swimming, taking place across four venues in Glasgow.
- Scotstoun Stadium will stage the athletics, featuring new mile world record holder Josh Kerr.
- The Games will be shown live on **TNT Sports** and**HBO Max**, rather than on the BBC or terrestrial television.
