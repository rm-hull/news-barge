Search the Public Notice PortalA well-known retailer has applied to extend its alcohol sale hours at its Foss Islands Retail Park store.

B&M, located at Unit 4, Foss Islands Retail Park, has requested permission from the City of York Council to change its premises licence.

The proposed changes would allow alcohol to be sold from 7am to 11pm every day, beginning July 16, 2026.

In addition to extended alcohol sales, the store plans to adjust its layout to include self-service checkouts.

B&M also wants to update the current premises licence conditions, with the proposed changes being part of B&M's plan to modernise its service offering.

The application was made on July 16.

The public can view the licensing application register by contacting the City of York Council licensing section at Hazel Court EcoDepot.

The public can submit written representations regarding the application.

More information on how to make a representation can be found on the City of York Council website.

It is a criminal offence to make a false statement in connection with a premises licence application, with offenders liable to an unlimited fine.

The application, handled by Keystone Law Solicitors, is open for public feedback until August 13.

Comments can be submitted via the City of York Council licensing section.
