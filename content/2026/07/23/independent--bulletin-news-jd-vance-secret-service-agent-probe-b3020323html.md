# Secret Service agent ‘on leave’ amid probe into leaked JD Vance info

![The agent is believed to have been a source for an MS NOW story this month about agents on Vance’s team](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/23/14/23/GettyImages-2283606603.jpg)

- A Secret Service agent assigned to Vice President JD Vance has reportedly been placed on administrative leave following allegations of leaking information to the media.
- The agent is believed to have been a source for an *MS NOW* story detailing Secret Service agents' frustration with "inappropriate" travel requests from Vance, including a canceled helicopter trip with his son.
- The publication of operational details in the *MS NOW* report angered the Secret Service and FBI, prompting an internal agency review for the unidentified agent,*CNN* reported.
- This incident occurs amidst the Trump administration's broader efforts to curb leaks to the press and penalize the disclosure of sensitive information.
- Recent actions by the administration include the Department of Justice issuing subpoenas to *New York Times* journalists and a proposal for a government-wide mandate requiring federal workers to sign non-disclosure agreements.
