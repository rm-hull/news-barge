A children’s charity is inviting Yorkshire businesses and supporters to join a special fundraising event.

The Principle Trust Children’s Charity will host A Night at the Seaside on Saturday, September 26, at The Queens Hotel in Leeds.

The event promises an immersive celebration inspired by classic British seaside holidays, featuring circus performances, music, traditional games, and a themed three-course menu.

The evening will raise funds to support the charity’s continued work, with 100 per cent of profits helping children and families in need.

Linda Yates, corporate development manager at The Principle Trust, said: "As we celebrate 15 years of creating life-changing memories for children and families, we would love Yorkshire businesses to be part of this landmark evening.

"A Night at the Seaside offers a fantastic opportunity for organisations to bring colleagues together for such a brilliant cause."

Tickets cost £99 per person or £950 for a table of 10.

The charity, founded in 2011 by Mike Davies MBE, has provided respite breaks to more than 6,000 children across Yorkshire facing adversity.

The mother of one beneficiary, Arlo, said: "As a family, we were pretty low, but watching the boys play on the beach, playing games together, and just laughing the whole time…it was just what we needed."
