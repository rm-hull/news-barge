FLY-tipped waste has been set on fire in a coastal North Yorkshire town.

  



North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said a crew from Whitby was called to Mayfield Road in the town at 5pm last night (Wednesday, July 22).

The service said a bag of textiles had been dumped and ignited.

A service spokesperson said crews put the fire out using a bucket of water.

Mayfield Road forms part of the A171 and is a key route in the town.
