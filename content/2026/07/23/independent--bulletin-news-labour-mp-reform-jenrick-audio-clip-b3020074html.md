Labour MP Jo White has demanded an apology from Reform UK, accusing them of sharing a misleadingly edited audio clip about her stance on migrant boat crossings.

Reform UK posted a clip that appeared to show Ms White, the newly appointed Immigration Minister, saying, “I don’t care if we stop the boats”.

Ms White shared an extended version of the clip, clarifying her actual statement was, “I don’t care who will stop the boats… my number one priority is to stop those boats”.

She accused Reform UK of deliberately twisting her words to mislead the public and called for them to withdraw their false claim and apologise.

The incident coincided with a busy day for Channel crossings, with migrants seen attempting to reach the UK from Equihen-Plage, France.
