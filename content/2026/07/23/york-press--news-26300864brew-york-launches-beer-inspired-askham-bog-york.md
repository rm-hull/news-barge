## Top York brewery releases special ale to mark 80 years of city nature reserve

How the new beer looks *(Image: Pic supplied)*

A new collaboration is raising a glass to 80 years of nature conservation.

Yorkshire Wildlife Trust has teamed up with Brew York to launch Hare of the Bog, a hazy session IPA inspired by the charity’s first nature reserve.

The limited-edition beer celebrates the trust’s 80th anniversary and is named after Askham Bog, the York site that inspired the beer’s name.

Lauren Perthen, interim director of fundraising and engagement at Yorkshire Wildlife Trust, said: "Hare of the Bog lands at an exciting time for us as the trust celebrates its 80th anniversary and shines a spotlight on our first nature reserve, Askham Bog, which is reopening to the public following months of renovation work.

"Brew York has been an exceptional partner, combining their creativity with a shared passion for Yorkshire's natural environment to create a unique beer that celebrates our local wildlife and one of the very special places we work hard to protect.

"It's a fitting way to mark this milestone, and I hope people all across the county will join us in raising a toast to another 80 years of driving nature's recovery in Yorkshire."

The 4.5% hazy IPA has been brewed at Brew York’s Osbaldwick site and features artwork designed by the Yorkshire Wildlife Trust’s in-house team.

  How the new beer looks 

*(Image: Pic supplied)*

Andrew Stark, head of marketing at Brew York, said: "We’re a York-born company ourselves and it’s an absolute pleasure to work with, and support, this iconic organisation.

"They do an outstanding amount of incredible work protecting vital ecological assets such as Askham Bog and other nature reserves which I’ve personally benefitted from and enjoyed over the years.

  How the new beer looks 

*(Image: Pic supplied)*

"Hare of the Bog is a fitting way to celebrate 80 years."

The beer was first revealed to Yorkshire Wildlife Trust staff and volunteers during the organisation’s 80th anniversary celebration last week.

Hare of the Bog will be available through Brew York’s online store and at selected venues.
