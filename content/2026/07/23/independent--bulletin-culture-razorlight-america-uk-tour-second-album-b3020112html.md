Razorlight frontman Johnny Borrell revealed the band considered giving away their chart-topping single “America” before its release.

The admission occurred during an intimate acoustic performance in London, marking the 20th anniversary of their self-titled second album.

Borrell explained they offered the song to music publishers, hoping another artist would record it, drawing a parallel to Prince giving away “Nothing Compares 2 U”.

After no other artist picked it up, the band developed the track themselves, which subsequently became their first and only UK number one single.

The original Razorlight line-up is set to embark on a tour later this year, performing their second album in full across Europe, Australia, the UK and Ireland.
