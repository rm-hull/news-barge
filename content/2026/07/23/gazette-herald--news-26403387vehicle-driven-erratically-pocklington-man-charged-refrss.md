A man has been charged after police reportedly saw a vehicle being driven “erratically” in East Yorkshire.

Humberside Police said a driver was pulled over in Pocklington on Wednesday night (July 22) after officers “spotted the vehicle being driven erratically”.

The force said the driver reportedly failed a roadside breath test and was subsequently arrested.

Police said Adam Bettles, 41, of Westfield Road in Hull, has been charged with drink driving following an appearance at Hull Magistrates Court on Thursday. He was released on bail to appear at a further court date in August.
