Firefighters were called to a fire started deliberately in York last night (Wednesday).

They went to **Northfield** at**6.41pm**.

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “A crew from Acomb responded to a small fire measuring one metre squared, consisting of a disposable barbecue and rubbish.

#### Northfield, York

“Crews used knapsack sprayers to extinguish the fire and police were informed as this fire was believed deliberate.”
