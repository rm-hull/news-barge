A new motoring event is coming to North Yorkshire next month from the team behind a popular TV series.

The inaugural Mathewsons Live will be held at the Great Yorkshire Showground on August 22 and 23.

Organised by the Bangers & Cash producers, the event will bring together over 600 display vehicles, 30 vehicle clubs, a live auction, rally stage demonstrations, live music, family entertainment and a host of exhibitors.

The event marks the first time Mathewsons has staged its own a large-scale live show and promises something for enthusiasts of all ages, whether their passion is classic cars, motorbikes, trucks, rally cars or automotive history.

Derek Mathewson said: "We're really looking forward to welcoming everyone to Mathewsons Live. It's been fantastic to see the support we've had already and we can't wait to bring together so many vehicles, clubs and enthusiasts for what should be a brilliant weekend.”

Taking place at the Great Yorkshire Showground in Harrogate, visitors will be able to explore hundreds of vehicles, enjoy rally stage action throughout the weekend and watch a live vehicle auction, alongside a packed programme of entertainment.

With just one month to go, tickets are on sale now.

For more information and to book tickets, visit www.mathewsons.co.uk/mathewsons-live
