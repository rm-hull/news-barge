### Student, youth and key worker discounts

Students and young people aged 16-26 years can access discounts of up to 30 per cent through Samsung’s student programme. Offers vary depending on the product and are updated regularly. You’ll need to verify your student or age status using your student ID, university email address or via Unidays.

Samsung also offers exclusive benefits to healthcare workers (including NHS staff), teachers, emergency services, military and social care workers. Discounts are often in the 20 to 30 per cent range and require ID verification through a recognised service such as Blue Light Card or Defence Service.

Registered companies can apply for a business account to access exclusive perks, including a 10 per cent or more discount off each order, free delivery, order tracking and expert technical support. To qualify, your business must have a valid Company’s House registration number.

### Trade-in or trade-up discounts

Samsung’s trade-in and trade-up programmes are open to everyone, not just returning customers, and can be combined with other onsite promotions and voucher codes. You can exchange old devices from a variety of brands, including phones, tablets, smartwatches and home appliances.

In most cases, items don’t need to be in working condition to qualify. Savings can reach up to £735 on smartphones, up to £310 on tablets or up to £60 on wearables.

The best Samsung deals usually appear during major sales events, including back-to-school, Black Friday and Cyber Monday. ### What is the Samsung returns policy?

Samsung offers a 14-day cooling-off period if you change your mind. Once you’ve requested a refund or exchange, you’ll have a further 14 days to return your item. If a product has been lightly used — such as being installed, connected to a water supply or having software activated — Samsung may deduct 20 per cent or more from your refund.

### What is the Samsung trade-in process?

Samsung’s trade-in programme lets you exchange your old smartphone, tablet or smartwatch for money off a new purchase. The value offered depends on the condition of your device. You can choose either credit towards your next order or an instant discount on a Galaxy device. To get started, visit the trade-in section of the Samsung website and register your device.
