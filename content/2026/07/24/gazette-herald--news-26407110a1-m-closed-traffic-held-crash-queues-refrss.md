TRAFFIC is being held in a major North Yorkshire road - with queues stretching for four miles.

All lanes of the A1(M) northbound are currently closed after a crash that took place earlier this morning (July 24) at around 11am.

Traffic is being described as 'stationary' by the AA.

A spokesperson for National Highways said: "Traffic is being held on the #A1M northbound between J46 Wetherby and J47 A59 Knaresborough due to a collision.

"There are long delays in excess of 60 minutes and approx. 4 miles of congestion on approach. Please allow extra travel time, we'll keep you updated."
