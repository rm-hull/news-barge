Search the Public Notice PortalTwo roads in North Yorkshire are set to be temporarily closed for essential works next week.

North Yorkshire Council has announced that Middleton Carr Lane in Middleton will be shut from July 27 to August 7 for Northern Gas Networks to replace pipework.

The maximum duration scheduled for this work is August 14.

Meanwhile, Lodge Road in Church Houses will be closed from July 27 to September 11 for essential work.

This work is being conducted by United Infrastructure and has a maximum duration until September 30.

During these periods, access to the premises will be maintained, and the closures will be clearly indicated by traffic signs.

Alternative routes will be signed on-site where appropriate.

For further information, residents can contact Northern Gas Networks on 07714 148353 or visit northyorks.gov.uk/roadworks-map.

United Infrastructure can be contacted on 07493 983062 for any enquiries regarding the Lodge Road closure.
