THIEVES have targeted a university campus hub in York.

North Yorkshire Police said an e-bike was stolen from the bike park area of the Ron Cooke Hub in Deramore Lane, Heslington, which is part of the University of York.

A force spokesperson said a black "Eleglide M2" e-bike with mudguards front and back was stolen between 8.15am and 4.30pm on Thursday, July 16.

  



The bike has the serial number "MEL05050099".

North Yorkshire Police said if anyone has seen the bike, or been offered it for sale, or has any information, to get in touch with the force.

The spokesperson said: "Email PC Ben Robinson-Brockhill on [ben.robinson-brockhill@northyorkshirepolice.uk.](mailto:ben.robinson-brockhill@northyorkshirepolice.uk.)

"Alternatively, visit our website, select ‘Tell us about’, then choose ‘An existing case or report’.

"If you are unable to use these options, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

"Please quote reference 12260135901 when providing information."
