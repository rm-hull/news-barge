## See inside riverside York flat for sale for £325,000 - with links to Judi Dench

The Westgate Apartments building in Leeman Road, York *(Image: Linley and Simpson/Zoopla)*

A flat has hit the market at a well-known York building where Dame Judi Dench was rumoured to have bought a home.

The two-bedroom flat is for sale at the Westgate Apartments in Leeman Road, where Dame Judi denied having bought a home – despite rumours that she had done so.

  Dame Judi Dench who denied buying a property in Westgate Apartments in York in 2002 

*(Image: Ian West/PA Wire)*

Speaking about the rumours in 2002 when the York-born star was made an honorary Freeman of the City, Dame Judi said: "You tell me who started that rumour!

"I live in a village in the south of England and I am very happy. I would love to live in York, but it isn't practical.

"But I love coming back. That feeling of coming up and suddenly seeing the Minster, it's a lovely feeling."


Meanwhile, the flat for sale inside Westgate Apartments for £325,000 is an “exceptional” second-floor home, according to Linley and Simpson, which is marketing the property.

Inside, there is a hallway leading to the longue/dining area and modern kitchen.

There are two double bedrooms, with the main bedroom featuring an en-suite shower room and the second bedroom served by the home’s main bathroom.

Outside, the home has an allocated parking space and bicycle storage to the rear of the building.

The flat is a leasehold property with the lease running until the year 3001, according to the listing.

There is a ground rent of 150 per year and a monthly £250 service charge. The service charge includes water rates, building insurance and the cleaning of communal areas, the listing said.
