A VANDAL has kicked in the door and damaged its frame in a North Yorkshire town centre shop.

The county's force said it happened in a premises in Beluah Street, Harrogate, around 5.25pm on July 4.

  



North Yorkshire Police has released a CCTV image of a man the force said may have information which could support its investigation.

A force spokesperson said: "Anyone who has information regarding this person is asked to email [mark.burrows@northyorkshire.police.uk.](mailto:mark.burrows@northyorkshire.police.uk.)

"Alternatively, call police on 101, or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

"Please quote reference 12260126897 when passing on information."
