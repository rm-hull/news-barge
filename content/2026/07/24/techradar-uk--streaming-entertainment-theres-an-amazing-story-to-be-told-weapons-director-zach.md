Get excited *Weapons* fans, because Aunt Gladys' story will be explored in a new prequel movie that's being written now.

During *Collider’* s Directors on Directing panel at San Diego Comic-Con, director Zach Cregger gave a status update and confirmed that the Gladys-led prequel was in the works.

“First of all, I’m really excited about the *Gladys* movie, and I loved working with Amy,” Cregger said. “I loved that character. For the movie*Weapons*, it really made a lot of sense not to dive into her backstory because the device of* Weapons* was to keep her very mysterious.”

He added: “However, there’s an amazing story to be told about where Gladys comes from and how she became what we know her as. It’s got a lot of twists and turns and mysteries and crazy things, like *Weapons* does. I think it’s going to be great.

"The script is in progress now, but we’re taking that super seriously, and it’s moving in the right direction. So yeah, it’s being written, is the short answer.”
