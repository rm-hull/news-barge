A THIEF has targeted a national supermarket chain store in a North Yorkshire town.

The county's police force said groceries were stolen from Sainsbury's Local in Cold Bath Road, Harrogate on Thursday, June 11.

  


Police have released CCTV images of a man they want to speak to, saying he may have information which could assist its investigation.

Anyone who recognises him or has information is asked to contact PC1488 Bell at [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk.](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk.)

Alternatively, call North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference 12260109599 or contact Crimestoppers anonymously.
