A CAR has crashed on North Yorkshire's main motorway close to the junction for York.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said it happened on the A1(M) at junction 47, close to Allerton Park and the A59 for York and Harrogate, around 4.55am (Friday, July 24).

  


Crews from Knaresborough, Harrogate and Tadcaster were called to the scene.

The car ended up in the central reservation and police were at the scene to move it to the hard shoulder.

A North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service spokesperson said: "The driver was being checked by paramedics for minor injuries."

Traffic is reported to be running normally in the area this morning.
