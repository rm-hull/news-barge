## Why are York Railway Station cycle lanes still closed weeks after works finish?

Cycle lanes blocked on approach to York Railway Station. Photo supplied

  **Why are the cycle lanes by York Railway Station still closed - with barriers?**

MUCH attention has been given to the cost of the station frontage work, at last emerging with new pedestrian paved areas and the main crossing now in use.

But the cycle lanes are still closed with barriers, weeks after most of the work has been completed.

  Cycle lanes blocked around York Railway Station. Photo supplied


  Cycle lanes blocked around York Railway Station. Photo supplied


This means signs saying 'narrow lanes, don't over take cyclists' are in place, alongside closed off cycle lanes!

It also means pedestrians are getting used to standing IN the bike lane, when waiting for the crossing lights to change!

After all this effort, surely most of the new bike lanes can be opened, even if there's a longer term missing section, ironically alongside 'Cycle Heaven' store!
