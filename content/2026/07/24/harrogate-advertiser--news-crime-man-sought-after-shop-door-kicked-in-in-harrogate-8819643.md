# Man sought after shop door kicked in in Harrogate

## Officers from North Yorkshire Police have released still images, taken from CCTV footage, of a person they would like to speak to, following a criminal damage incident on Beulah Street, Harrogate.

It happened at about 5.25pm on Saturday July 4, when a male kicked a shop door, causing damage to both the door, and the frame.

Email: [email protected] if you recognise the person pictured on CCTV, as they may have information that will assist the investigation.

Alternatively, call North Yorkshire Police on 101, or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

Quote reference 12260126897 when passing on information.
