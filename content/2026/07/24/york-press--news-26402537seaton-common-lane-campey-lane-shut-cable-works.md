Search the Public Notice PortalA road in an East Riding village is set to close for three days for telecoms works.

Seaton Common Lane and Campey Lane in Melbourne will be closed to traffic from August 10 to August 12 to allow for cable installation.

The closure, between the junction of the two lanes and Bibbill Lane, will be in place from 8am until 6pm each day.

The work is expected to take three days, and is described as necessary for "ensuring the safety of the public" by East Riding of Yorkshire Council.

An alternative route will be available via Campey Lane, Main Street, Common Road, Melbourne Road, Everingham Road, and Seaton Common Lane.

The roads will be closed to all through traffic but will remain accessible to residents, emergency vehicles, and pedestrians.

The order for the closure, made by East Riding of Yorkshire Council, will be in effect for up to 18 months, or until the work is completed.

Residents can contact Streetscene Services, Highways Maintenance, on 01482 393939.

The closure is expected to cause some disruption, so motorists are advised to plan their journeys accordingly.
