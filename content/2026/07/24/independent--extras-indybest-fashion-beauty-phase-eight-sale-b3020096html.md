The Princess of Wales has long been known for her sophisticated, polished wardrobe choices, and she’s no stranger to championing high street fashion. Over the years, she’s stepped out in pieces from brands that many shoppers can actually buy themselves, including Phase Eight, the occasion wear label loved for its elegant dresses and timeless silhouettes. The brand has become a favourite among shoppers looking for sophisticated styles without the designer price tag.

Now, shoppers can pick up some of the brand’s most popular styles for less, thanks to Phase Eight’s sale that offers up to 60 per cent off. Whether you’re searching for a wedding guest outfit, a summer dress, or something to wear to a special event, there are plenty of discounted pieces worth browsing. From flattering midi dresses to sophisticated jumpsuits, the sale includes a range of styles.

Below, I’ve picked out some of the standout pieces from the Phase Eight sale, with some pieces reduced by more than £100.
