# What is the bag and camera policy for Ariana Grande’s London shows?

- Ariana Grande fans attending her shows at London's O2 Arena will face strict new entry rules, including a “mandatory” clear plastic bag policy.
- Concertgoers are permitted only one transparent bag made from clear plastic, vinyl, or PVC, with no colour, prints, patterns, or logos, and must place essential items individually inside, according to Ticketmaster UK.
- The robust security measures also include a ban on cameras and recording devices, though phones with built-in cameras are allowed.
- Grande is set to release her new album, *Petal*, on 31 July, and is performing at the O2 Arena from 15 August to 1 September.
- The pop star recently launched the Brighter Days Ahead Foundation, a charitable initiative supporting in areas such as LGBT+ rights, mental healthcare, and reproductive justice.
