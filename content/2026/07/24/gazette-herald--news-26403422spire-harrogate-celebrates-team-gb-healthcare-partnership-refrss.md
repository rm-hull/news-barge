A new partnership is set to connect elite sport and healthcare in the run-up to the Olympic Games.

Spire Healthcare, which operates Spire Harrogate, will serve as the exclusive Official Healthcare Services Partner for Team GB through to the Los Angeles 2028 Olympic Games.

The partnership aims to promote healthier, more active lifestyles by highlighting the connections between elite sport and public wellbeing.

Tim Cook, general manager at Spire Harrogate, said: "We’re proud that Spire has partnered with Team GB, combining elite sport and healthcare expertise to inspire healthier lifestyles across the UK.

"Here at our clinic and across Spire, we see the benefits of staying active for improving health and supporting recovery day-in, day-out."

Spire Healthcare will work with Team GB athletes on campaigns and storytelling projects, focusing on patient journeys and recovery.

The collaboration also offers opportunities for Spire staff and clinical partners to take part in exclusive events with athletes.

Spire Healthcare treats more than 1.3 million patients annually and is one of the UK’s leading independent healthcare providers.

The partnership will be supported by a nationwide marketing campaign and is part of Spire’s long-term plan to boost brand awareness.

It also aims to increase community impact and advance public health through accessible, high-quality care.
