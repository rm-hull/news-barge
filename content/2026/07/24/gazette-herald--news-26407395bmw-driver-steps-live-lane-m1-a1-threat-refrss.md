A DRIVER has stepped into a "live lane" of a motorway to make threats to another motorist after dangerous manoeuvres were made on a major North Yorkshire road.

North Yorkshire Police said the driver of a white BMW estate car was on the A1 and M1 "driving at excess speed, undertaking, causing other motorists to take evasive action, or sharp braking and intimidating other road users" - on the afternoon of Tuesday, July 21.

  


A force spokesperson said: "When the flow of traffic slowed down, the male driver of the white BMW exited his vehicle in a live lane between junction 42 and 41 of the M1 southbound, approached another vehicle on the carriageway and made threats of violence to the driver."

North Yorkshire Police said it wants to hear from vehicle owners with dashcams or those with mobile phone footage of the white BMW or the incident which occurred on the carriageway later.

Anyone with information is asked to email PC1864 Marshall at [fiona.marshall@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:fiona.marshall@northyorkshire.police.uk), quoting reference 12260140627.

If unable to use these options, information can be provided by calling 101 or contacting Crimestoppers anonymously.
