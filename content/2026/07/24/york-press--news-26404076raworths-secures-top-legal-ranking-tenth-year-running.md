A law firm’s specialist team has been named the best in the region for private client work.

Harrogate-based Raworths, the only firm ranked in Band 1 for the York, Hull and surrounding area, has achieved the top ranking for its Trusts, Wills and Estates team in the Chambers High Net Worth Guide 2026 for the tenth consecutive year.

The guide highlights the team as one of the largest private client teams in Yorkshire.

Rachel Tunnicliffe, head of private client services at Raworths, said: "This is fantastic recognition for the exceptional expertise of our Trusts, Wills and Estates team at every level.

"To maintain our Band 1 rankings for a decade based on the feedback of our clients and professional contacts is also a ringing endorsement of our workplace culture, which places value on building trusted relationships with our colleagues, clients and industry peers."

The team also holds a Band 1 ranking in the Legal 500.
