# Police appeal following theft of groceries in Harrogate

## North Yorkshire Police has issued a CCTV image of a man officers would like to speak to, following a shop theft in Harrogate.

It happened on Thursday June 11, at Sainsbury’s on Cold Bath Road and involved the theft of grocery items.

![North Yorkshire Police has released an image of a man officers would like to speak to following a theft from Sainsbury's in Harrogate](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOjk2YmZmODk3LTlhNzgtNGEwNC1iZThhLTA0NjIyOGY0ZDVlMDozYWU4YWUyNS1mMGIxLTRlZTMtOGIxNC1jYzM0YjFhMzdhMGI=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



A police spokesperson said: “Please contact us if you recognise the male pictured on CCTV, as he may have information that will assist our investigation. • Email: PC1488 Bell on [email protected] • Online: Visit our website, select ‘Tell us about’, then choose ‘An existing case or report’ • Live chat: Available via our website, Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm “If you are unable to use these options, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101 or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.”

Quote reference 12260109599 when providing information.
