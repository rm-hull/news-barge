## Supermarkets offering even more free food this summer

### Tesco

Tesco is expanding its support for families over the holidays.

Alongside its Kids Eat Free café offer, the supermarket is bringing back Free Fruit for Kids until August 30 , with children able to pick up a free apple at checkouts in more than 800 Tesco stores and 77 larger Tesco Express stores.


### Morrisons

Morrisons is once again running its free Kellogg's Breakfast Clubs throughout the summer holidays.

Adults and children can enjoy a free bowl of Corn Flakes or Rice Krispies, served with milk or a dairy alternative, plus a free apple or banana every day during the school holidays.

The supermarket is also continuing its Kids Eat Free café offer, meaning families can enjoy both breakfast and lunch for less.

## Expert tips for saving even more

Ben Smye from Hotukdeals recommends:

- Book ahead , as restaurants fill up quickly during the holidays.
- Download restaurant apps , as many offers are app-only.
- Join free loyalty schemes to unlock extra discounts.
- Check your local branch , as not every venue takes part.
- Read the small print , including age limits, minimum spends and valid days.
- Try apps like Too Good To Go to pick up discounted food from cafés, bakeries and restaurants while helping reduce food waste.

He adds: "Eating out as a family can feel like a luxury. Planning around kids-eat-free offers can cut a typical £100 family meal bill almost in half."

With some family meals costing well over £80, planning around these offers could save parents hundreds of pounds over the summer holidays.
