Police have been called in following a large fire last night (Thursday).

Huntington firefighters were called out at **9.48pm** to the fire in the open off**Ryecroft, Strensall**.

That’s close to Strensall Camp and the River Foss.

#### Incident location

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “On arrival 100 squared metres of grass was well alight.

“Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish. The cause was believed to have been deliberate and the Police were notified.”
