PART of a key road in North Yorkshire has reopened this morning (Friday, July 24) following emergency repairs.

North Yorkshire Council said the A19 Easingwold bypass was closed following a trailer fire on Wednesday evening.

  


The vehicle was loaded with hay bales when the fire sparked around 10.45am on July 22, leading to the closure.

The A19 Easingwold bypass has reopened. [https://t.co/siTCU8kyBh](https://t.co/siTCU8kyBh)

— North Yorkshire Council (@northyorksc) July 24, 2026


Firefighters tackled the burning hay bales, but repairs were required and the AA had reported the road was closed in both directions, with drivers forced to use an alternative route.

Traffic is running normally on that stretch of the A19 this morning.
