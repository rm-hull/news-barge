# Body found in River Aire as police investigate man's death

## Police are appealing for information after a man's body was recovered from the River Aire in Leeds.

Shortly after 4pm on Thursday (Jul 23), police were called to East Street where a member of the public reported seeing a body in the water.

Officers attended and found the body of a man who was pronounced dead at the scene.

![A man's body has been recovered from the River Aire after a week-long search. Photo: Leeds City Council.](https://www.yorkshirepost.co.uk/jpim-static/image/2024/05/30/17/35/onecms_81fa2961-368d-4202-a8e2-695341e4b403.jpg.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



Police have launched an investigation to establish the man’s identity and the circumstances surrounding his death.

The man’s death is currently being treated as unexplained.

He has been described as black, of a tall muscular build with black short dreadlock hair and a black beard.

The man was wearing a khaki green Lacoste T-shirt and cargo shorts.
