# Tadej Pogacar’s Tour de France hopes potentially under threat as illness strikes team

- Tadej Pogacar's lead in the Tour de France could come under threat due to a sickness bug affecting his UAE Team Emirates XRG.
- Brandon McNulty abandoned Stage 18 due to illness, while Adam Yates and other key *domestiques* have also apparently been battling sickness.
- Pogacar told *The Guardian*: “I think we can manage. It’s the third week, all the teams are suffering. There are bodies everywhere on a stage like today. We will keep fighting and I believe we can do it.”
- There are two potentially defining mountain tests to come as the peloton takes on Alpe d’Huez on back-to-back stages, before the final stage around Paris.
- Riders like Nils Politt and Felix Grossschartner have a greater importance placed upon them to help guide Pogacar and Del Toro through the next three days, with efforts made to minimise further infection.
