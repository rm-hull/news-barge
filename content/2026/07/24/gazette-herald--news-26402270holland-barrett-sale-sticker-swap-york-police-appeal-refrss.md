A MAN and woman are wanted by police after an audacious incident in a York shop.

North Yorkshire Police is asking for the public's assistance following an allegation that "reduced price stickers have been placed on non-reduced items" in a popular health and wellbeing store in York.

The non-reduced items were then bought from the store.

The force said the activity took place in the Holland & Barrett store in McArthurGlen York Designer Outlet in Fulford at 5.25pm on Friday, June 26.

North Yorkshire Police has issued images of two people the force said it would like to speak to saying they may have information which could support its investigation.

A force spokesperson said: "If you can help our appeal, please email [peter.henderson@northyorkshire.police.uk.](mailto:peter.henderson@northyorkshire.police.uk.)

"Or call 101 and ask for PC1668 Henderson.

"If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

"Please quote reference 12260120596 when passing on information."
