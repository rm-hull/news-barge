If your wardrobe is crying out for a refresh but you’re looking to fill it with more sustainable fashion pieces, MyTheresa’s summer sale has come about at the perfect time. The luxury retailer has dropped prices across a selection of designer names, including The Row, Leset, and even more. The online luxury retailer is home to hundreds of designer brands, from established fashion houses to more contemporary labels.

If you’ve been searching for a timeless staple you’ll wear for years or want to add a touch of luxury to your everyday outfits, the sale includes plenty of investment-worthy pieces at a seriously tempting price point, with some deals saving you hundreds of pounds. And there’s everything to shop from casual pieces to handbags, shoes and even gold jewellery.

Luxury shopping doesn’t always have to mean paying full price, and I’ve rounded up some of the best savings to shop now, including standout pieces where you can save hundreds.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/22/15/17/Magda-Butrym-Brigitte-suede-shoulder-bag.png)

We love this modern twist on a baguette bag from Magda Butrym, with an elegant suede rose detail on the handle. The shoulder bag is made in Italy from burgundy suede calf leather and features two top handles, with a two-way zipped top closure. It has a fabric-lined interior with a single compartment and card slot to keep small valuables tucked away.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/22/14/20/The-Row-Round-90s-suede-tote-bag.png)

A nod to the butter yellow trend, this gives the chance to try the colour in a more muted tone. The Row’s round ’90s tote is made in Italy from suede calf leather, with a leather lining and single interior compartment. It features a top handle, adjustable strap and zipped top closure, and comes with a dust bag for storage.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/22/14/25/Jil-Sander-Turtleneck-maxi-dress.png)

Jil Sander is known for its timeless styles and clean cuts, as seen in its turtleneck dress. The maxi dress beautifully pools on the hem and can be paired with simple gold accessories. It’s great for brides looking for something a bit more simple, too.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/24/11/34/savette-suede-bag.png)

Savette’s Florence suede bag immediately adds a pop of colour to neutral outfits. The small size is perfect for weekends and features a discreet self-tie closure. This is real suede, so take care when wearing this out and about, particularly in the rain.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/22/14/19/Jacquemus-Le-Chiquito-leather-tote-bag.png)

The Jacquemus le chiquito mini tote undoubtedly started the micro bag trend and now it’s got 30 per cent off at MyTheresa. It’s made in Italy from black cow leather and features a top handle, plus a detachable adjustable shoulder strap for crossbody wear. It has a fabric-lined interior, gold-tone fastening and comes with a dust bag for storage.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/24/11/04/Magda-Butrym-Baroque-pearl-gold-plated-drop-earrings.png)

Sometimes all you need to freshen up an outfit is some new accessories. Statement jewellery, like these Magda Butrym pearl drop earrings, immediately draws the eye, especially paired with a neutral outfit. These are made from gold-plated brass and baroque-style freshwater pearls.
