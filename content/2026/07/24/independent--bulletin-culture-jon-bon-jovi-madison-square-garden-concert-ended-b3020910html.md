# Why Jon Bon Jovi suddenly ended his show at Madison Square Garden

- Jon Bon Jovi ended his concert at Madison Square Garden after 90 minutes due to a sinus infection.
- The musician was performing the eighth night of a nine-show residency in New York City as part of Bon Jovi’s Forever Tour.
- As seen in videos, he instructed the audience: “Don’t throw away your ticket stubs, I’m gonna figure something out, OK?”
- A spokesperson confirmed in a statement to Rolling Stone that the early ending was due to a sinus infection, with “updated information” to “be available shortly.”
- It follows Bon Jovi's break from touring after having surgery for a strained vocal cord in 2024.
