# Sainsbury's shop theft leads to police appeal in Harrogate

## North Yorkshire Police has issued a CCTV image of a man officers would like to speak to, following a shop theft in Harrogate.

It happened on Saturday June 13, at Sainsburys on Cold Bath Road and involved the theft of grocery items.

![Police have appealed for help to identify a man following a shop theft in Harrogate](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOmM1MDBmMTAzLTI2OTAtNGU1OS1iZGJhLWEyZmM5OTA1NTU3YToyNjQ4ZGE5Mi1iZDhhLTQ3MmYtOGMzZi03MDlhMjVmMWE3NzM=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



A police spokesperson said: “Please contact us if you recognise the male pictured on CCTV, as he may have information that will assist our investigation.

• Live chat: Available via our website, Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm

“If you are unable to use these options, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101 or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.”

Quote reference 12260109331 when providing information.
