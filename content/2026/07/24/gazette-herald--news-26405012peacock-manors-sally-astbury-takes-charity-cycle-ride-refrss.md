A nursing home in York is looking to raise £1,000 for Alzheimer's Society with a charity bike ride.

Peacock Manor Nursing Home's activities coordinator, Sally Astbury, will lead the 30-mile ride on Friday, July 31.

She will start and finish in Whixley and will cycle through several villages including Thorpe Underwood, Alne, Boroughbridge, and Great Ouseburn.

The ride is expected to take roughly three hours.

The home is aiming to raise awareness of dementia's impact on families and communities.

People can support the fundraiser by donating through a JustGiving page.

Peacock Manor supports people living with dementia, providing compassionate, person-centred care.
