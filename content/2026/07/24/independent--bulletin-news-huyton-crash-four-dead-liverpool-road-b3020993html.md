Four people have died in the crash (PA Archive)

Four men, all in their 20s, tragically died after their black Seat Cupra crashed into a lamppost on Liverpool Road in Huyton during the early hours of Friday morning.

The fatal collision occurred just after 2am as the vehicle was travelling from Liverpool towards Knowsley.

The deceased include two men aged 23, one aged 24, and another aged 26, all of whom were pronounced dead at the scene.

A 27-year-old male passenger from the car survived the incident and has been taken to hospital, where he remains in a stable condition.

Merseyside Police are currently investigating the circumstances of the crash, have informed the next of kin, and are appealing for witnesses or anyone with relevant doorbell footage to come forward.
