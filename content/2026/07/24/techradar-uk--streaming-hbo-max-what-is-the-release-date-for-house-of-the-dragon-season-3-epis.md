*House of the Dragon* season 3 continues to engross us as it enters its home stretch. And, with its sixth episode less than 72 hours away, we'll soon find out how Houses Targaryen and Hightower will continue to try and one-up the other as the battle for the Iron Throne intensifies.

In the meantime, scroll on to learn when and where *House of the Dragon*'s next episode will air.

## What time can I watch House of the Dragon season 3 episode 6?

 ![A stern looking Rhaenyra sitting on the Iron Throne in House of the Dragon season 3 episode 6](https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/EeUNkLrXC6crkPtMVU38rX.jpg) 


*House of the Dragon* season 3's next chapter will be released on**Sunday, July 26 at 6pm PT / 9pm ET**.

That's when the *Game of Thrones* spin-off's forthcoming episode will be available in the US and Canada, anyway. If you live elsewhere, you'll have to wait until Monday, July 27 to stream it. Here are the all-important times you need to know about:

- **US**— Sunday, July 26 at 6pm PT / 9pm ET
- **Canada**— Sunday, July 26 at 6pm PT / 9pm ET
- **UK**— Monday, July 27 at 2am BST
- **India**— Monday, July 27 at 6:30am IST
- **Singapore**— Monday, July 27 at 9am SGT
- **Australia**— Monday, July 27 at 11am AEST
- **New Zealand**— Monday, July 27 at 1pm NZST
