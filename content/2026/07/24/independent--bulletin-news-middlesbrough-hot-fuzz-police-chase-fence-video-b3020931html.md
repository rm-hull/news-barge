Suspect falls through fence while trying to escape police in Hot Fuzz-style blunder

A teenager's attempt to escape from police in Middlesbrough was foiled when a fence he tried to climb collapsed.

The incident, which occurred on Tuesday, 21 July, was captured on footage released by Cleveland Police.

Plain-clothes officers observed two males involved in a suspected drug deal with a stolen bike.

When intercepted, the pair fled, with one teenager attempting to scale a fence that buckled and fell on top of both.

A 14-year-old boy was subsequently arrested on suspicion of offences including possession with intent to supply class A and B drugs, and theft of a motor vehicle.
