# Trump warns damage to ships will be paid for by Iranian money

- President Donald Trump warned that any damages to ships or cargo in Middle Eastern waterways would be paid for by Iranian money held and controlled by the United States.
- He stated on Truth Social that these damages, potentially substantial, would be a fair and equitable outcome.
- Trump is seriously considering restarting major combat operations in Iran, suggesting the country has not yet experienced enough consequences.
- This follows his previous threat of "major military punishment" against Iran after Houthi rebels attacked Saudi oil tankers.
- He also accused Iran of "evil intentions" and reiterated his stance that the regime must not acquire nuclear weapons, amid concerns over disrupted oil supply routes.
