Duchess of Sussex has released new photos of her family’s summer (Instagram)

The Duchess of Sussex shared a series of new photographs on Instagram, offering a glimpse into the family's summer holiday activities, which included beach strolls, exploring Europe, and moments of laughter.

Among the images, Prince Harry is seen playfully throwing Princess Lilibet into a swimming pool, while other photos depict Meghan relaxing on a sun-lounger and browsing a homewares shop.

Further snapshots show Prince Archie sitting in a plane cockpit surrounded by pilots, and Princess Lilibet strolling through gardens alongside her brother and father.

Earlier in the month, Meghan and the children flew privately from Europe to London to join Prince Harry, who was in the UK to promote his upcoming Invictus Games.

During their UK visit, Prince Archie and Princess Lilibet were reunited with their grandfather, the King, at Highgrove for the first time in four years, an event that occurred while Prince William was participating in a charity polo match.
