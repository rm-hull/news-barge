# The European tourist destinations most affected by heatwaves

- Extreme heat is gripping parts of Europe, with temperatures of up to 44C forecast in Spain as the country issued an "extraordinary danger" warning.
- Wildfires are raging across several countries, including Spain, where a blaze in Guadalajara has burned roughly 79,000 acres, and France, which has seen thousands evacuated near Bordeaux.
- Italy is also experiencing red weather warnings in Cagliari and Palermo, alongside wildfires that tragically claimed the life of a firefighter in Sicily.
- While some regions like Portugal are seeing slightly above-average temperatures without weather warnings, France is facing high forest fire risks and severe droughts impacting river levels.
- Greece is bracing for heavy rain and thunderstorms after a recent heatwave, while Croatia expects mostly sunny weather with no significant red alerts.
