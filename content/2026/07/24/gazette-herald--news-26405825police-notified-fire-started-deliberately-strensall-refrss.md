POLICE have been brought in after a large fire on the outskirts of a York village was deliberately started.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue service said crews from Huntington were called to Ryecroft in Strensall around 9.50pm yesterday (Thursday, July 23).

A North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service spokesperson said: "On arrival 100 square metres of grass was well alight.

"Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish.

"The cause was believed to have been deliberate.

"Police were notified."
