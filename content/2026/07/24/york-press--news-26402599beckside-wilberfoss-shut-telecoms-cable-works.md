Search the Public Notice PortalA road in Wilberfoss will be closed for three days for telecoms work.

Beckside will be closed from its junction with Willow Park Road for the installation of 14 metres of telecoms cable as part of the fibre-to-home scheme.

The work will begin on August 11 at 8am and is expected to be completed by August 13 at 6pm.

The closure is to ensure public safety during the excavation and installation process.

While the road is closed to through traffic, access will still be maintained for residents, emergency services, and pedestrians.

A diversion route will be in place for affected traffic, directing vehicles via Willow Park Road, north along Woldview Road, onto Field Lane, and on to Birker Lane.

The order for the closure was made by East Riding of Yorkshire Council and will remain in effect for up to 18 months, or until the works are completed, whichever is sooner.

For more information, contact Streetscene Services, Highways Maintenance on 01482 393939, quoting reference CB.
