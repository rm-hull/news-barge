                                    Police have launched an appeal following a criminal damage incident on Beulah Street in Harrogate.

It happened at around 5:25pm on 4th July, where a male kicked a shop door, causing damage to both the door and the frame.

Officers have asked residents to email: [mark.burrows@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:mark.burrows@northyorkshire.police.uk) if you recognise the person pictured on CCTV, as they may have information that will assist with the investigation.

Alternatively, you can call North Yorkshire Police on 101, or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

*Please quote reference 12260126897 when passing on information.*
