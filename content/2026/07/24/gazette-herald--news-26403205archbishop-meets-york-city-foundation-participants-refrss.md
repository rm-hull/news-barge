The Archbishop of York has praised a football foundation for its work.

Stephen Cottrell visited the LNER Community Stadium to learn about York City Football Club Foundation’s work transforming lives across the city through sport, health and community engagement, and met participants taking part in activities across the site.

During the visit, Mr Cottrell visited the Stadium's mini-pitches and spoke with people involved in the Foundation’s Walking Football programme.

The programme is delivered in partnership with Parkinson's UK and provides opportunities for people living with Parkinson's to stay active, improve wellbeing and enjoy the social benefits of regular physical activity.

Archbishop Cottrell said: "It was wonderful to see the Foundation's work first-hand and to meet the people whose lives are being enriched by it.

"The positive difference these programmes are making to individuals and the wider community is clear to see.

"The sense of unity and shared joy we've witnessed during this World Cup is a reminder of football's unique ability to bring people together, and it's inspiring to see that same spirit making a lasting difference here in York."

The Foundation exists to transform lives and strengthen communities through inclusive programmes that encourage active, healthy and happy living.

Programmes are delivered in collaboration with schools, community groups, and partner organisations across York.

Paula Stainton, chief executive of York City Football Club Foundation, said: "It was a pleasure to welcome Archbishop Stephen and share the work we do every day.

"Our focus is on improving wellbeing and creating opportunities where everyone feels included, connected and able to thrive through the power of football.

"Football brings people together, as we’ve seen recently during this World Cup."

The Archbishop visited the Foundation’s Community Hub and met with young people and adults taking part in activities designed to encourage physical activity, teamwork and confidence.

York City Football Club Foundation is the official charity of York City Football Club and is guided by its mission of "Making A Positive Difference".

It delivers programmes in education, health, inclusion, and community engagement for people of all ages and backgrounds.

Through its work, the Foundation aims to transform lives and strengthen communities while advocating for active, healthy, and happy living.
