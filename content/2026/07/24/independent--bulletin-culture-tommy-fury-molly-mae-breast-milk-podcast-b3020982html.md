Fury claimed on the What the Fury? podcast that consuming the breast milk caused his arms to “double in size” and provided “real growth hormone”.

He described the taste as similar to “almond soy milk” and insisted “it’s one of the best things you can drink”, despite his father John Fury's shocked reaction.

Other celebrities, such as Joe Wicks and Kourtney Kardashian, have also claimed health benefits from drinking breast milk.

However, Nutri Peak dietitian Amie Alexander previously told The Independent that the antibodies and protein found in human milk are only beneficial for actual babies.
