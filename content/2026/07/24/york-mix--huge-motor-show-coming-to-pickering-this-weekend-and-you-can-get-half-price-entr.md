Calling all motor enthusiasts – this is the perfect event for you.

The Pickering Motor Show is back for a second year at Galtres Pickering Showground on **Saturday 25 and Sunday 26 July** for a jam-packed weekend of cars, trucks and motorcycles.

With an indoor exhibition hall and acres of outdoor display space, there will be hundreds of motorised exhibits including cars, trucks and motorcycles in a variety of styles including modified, performance, custom, American and vintage.

Whatever your age, this event offers the opportunity to see some of your favourite vehicles up close.

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/pickering-motor-show-2026-2.webp)

And we have an exclusive offer with YorkMix vouchers! Get a half price family ticket for two adults and three children at the link below.

There’ll be fun for all the family across the weekend, with performances from popular covers band Bite the Bullitt on the Saturday evening, and an incredible display from Scott Wilson; high-energy, family-friendly Mountain Bike Stunt Show.

As a former World Champion, European Champion, and 7x British Champion, Scott delivers a thrilling and interactive experience featuring custom-built equipment – including platforms, ladders, and even the roof of his van.

There will also be a magical entertainment from Magic Mike, featuring magic tricks, ventriloquism, balloon modelling, juggling, and more.

Visitors can also look forward to trade stands, autojumble, fun fair, catering and licensed bar.

It’s the event any motor fan wouldn’t want to miss! Find out more at their website here.
