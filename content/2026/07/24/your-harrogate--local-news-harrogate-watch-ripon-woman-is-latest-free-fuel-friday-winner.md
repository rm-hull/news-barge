                                    A Ripon woman has become the thirteenth winner to claim a full tank of free fuel as part of our Free Fuel Friday competition.

Your Harrogate's George Dickson donned the Free Fuel Fairy outfit for the weekly competition in which we top up a tank for free.

Clues were given on the radio and on social media to hint at where the Your Harrogate Free Fuel Fairy would be appearing after 10am.

And today’s winner was Leonie from Ripon!

She found the fairy at the Morrisons petrol station on Harrogate Road.
