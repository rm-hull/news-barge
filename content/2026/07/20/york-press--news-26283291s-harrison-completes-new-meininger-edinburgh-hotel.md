A new 157-bedroom hotel, delivered by a York-based property developer, has been completed.

The building, in Edinburgh’s Haymarket district, was completed by S Harrison and will welcome its first guests from August 5, 2026.

It will become the 38th property operated by MEININGER Hotels, part of a long-term agreement between S Harrison and the European hotel group.

Ann Scott, managing director at S Harrison, said: "After working meticulously to create a vision for this building that suited its size and location, before securing planning permission and selecting the right occupier, it’s fantastic to see it almost complete, before it opens next month.

  The 157-bedroom site is operated by MEININGER Hotels 

*(Image: Supplied)*

"This scheme has completely transformed a tired office building into a modern development that makes a positive contribution to the streetscape and enhances this important part of the city.

"Crucially, it’s also in a highly sought-after location, just west of Edinburgh city centre, within walking distance of Princes Street and only a few minutes from Haymarket railway station and tram stop, which will make it hugely popular with anyone visiting one of Europe’s most popular destinations for business and leisure travellers."

MEININGER, which operates 37 hotels across 27 European cities, specialises in hybrid, flexible accommodation with unique designs.

Ajit Menon, CEO of MEININGER Hotels, said: "Expanding into Edinburgh is a key tactical milestone for our brand.

"The city’s resilient, year-round demand profile and diverse visitor base makes it a naturally high-yield market for our hybrid hospitality model.

"In partnership with S Harrison, whose exceptional development expertise is key to transforming this prominent asset, we are thrilled to enhance the local hospitality offering and support the sustainable growth of Edinburgh’s tourism economy."

Edinburgh continues to be a focus for S Harrison’s investment strategy.

The developer previously completed the Malmaison hotel in New Town and has delivered multiple student accommodation schemes, including a recent project off Newington Road.
