# Tehran threatens Trump: ‘Our missiles will rain down on you for years’

- Iran's Islamic Revolutionary Guards Corps (IRGC) vowed to continue striking American targets for years if necessary, rejecting claims of munitions shortages.
- “[He] should know that, by God's grace, even if this war lasts for several years, until the very last day, our missiles and drones will rain down on American criminals,” the statement said.
- An American official indicated the US is “planning for a wider war” and deploying military aircraft, despite concerns about potential shortages in air defenses and long-range munitions.
- Iran's Revolutionary Guard threatened to block the Strait of Hormuz for oil and gas transit if US “aggression” persists, following unverified claims of targeting US aircraft and tanker explosions.
- The US military completed its ninth consecutive night of strikes on Iran, targeting military command centers and maritime capabilities to protect commercial shipping in the Strait of Hormuz.
