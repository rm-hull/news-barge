                                    North Yorkshire Police is appealing for help to trace a wanted man from Harrogate.

Officers are searching for 24-year-old Guy Elms, who is wanted for breaching a restraining order.

Extensive enquiries have already been carried out to locate him, but police are now asking for the public's help.

Police believe Elms could be in Harrogate, Ripon or Leeds.

He is described as white, of slim to medium build, around 5ft 10in tall, with brown hair and brown eyes.

*Anyone who sees Elms or has information about his whereabouts is asked to call North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference number 12250240376.*

*Information can also be passed anonymously to Crimestoppers. Anyone who has an immediate sighting of Elms should call 999.*
