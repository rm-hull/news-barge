A care home in Thirsk has been named among the UK’s best sites.

Ruby Lodge Care Home was named in the top 100 at the annual Lottie Awards, which celebrate excellence in care across the country.

The home was also placed in Lottie’s Top 10 for Wellbeing, highlighting its commitment to meaningful and engaging experiences for residents.

Holly Hunton, home manager at Ruby Lodge, said: "We are delighted to be named one of the UK’s top 100 care homes, but to also be listed in the Top 10 UK Care Homes Best for Wellbeing is a special honour.

"I am incredibly proud of our dedicated team and grateful for the continued support of our wonderful residents, their families, and the wider community.

"Receiving these two awards is a fantastic achievement for everyone at Ruby Lodge."

The site offers residential, dementia, and respite care for up to 70 residents.

The home’s wellbeing programme is tailored to individual interests and life stories, and includes regular intergenerational activities with children from Lara Nursery, located on the same site.

Will Donnelly, Co-founder and CEO of Lottie, said: "To be named among the Top 100 from more than 14,000 care homes across the UK is a huge achievement."
