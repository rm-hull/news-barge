# North Yorkshire Police release CCTV images of man following bank card theft at Harrogate hotel

## North Yorkshire Police has issued two CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to following a theft at a hotel in Harrogate.

The incident happened on Thursday, July 2 at the DoubleTree by Hilton Harrogate Majestic Hotel & Spa and involved the theft of a bank card, which was then used in the town centre.

A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: “Please contact us if you recognise the male pictured in the CCTV image as he may have information that will assist our investigation.”
