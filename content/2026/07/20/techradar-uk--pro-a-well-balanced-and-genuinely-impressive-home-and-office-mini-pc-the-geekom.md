If you're looking for a mini PC that can comfortably handle work, study, and everyday computing without taking over your desk, I've found a great deal on the Geekom A5 Pro.

Powered by AMD's Ryzen 5 7530U processor, a six-core, 12-thread chip with boost speeds of up to 4.5GHz, the Geekom A5 Pro is now $513 (was $699) at Amazon, a saving of 27%.

The mini PC comes paired with an upgradeable 16GB of DDR4 memory and a 1TB PCIe NVMe SSD, both of which can be upgraded later if your needs change. It will be able to handle multitasking, office applications, video meetings, web browsing, and light creative work. In the UK, the same spec mini PC is now £442 (was £519) at Amazon.

## Today's top Geekom mini PC deal

![Geekom A5 Pro (2026) Mini PC Geekom A5 Pro (2026) Mini PC](https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/KPUZs5CURjHs3rV3QFAsjk-200-80.jpg.webp)

Powered by an AMD Ryzen 5 7530U processor, the Geekom A5 Pro includes 16GB DDR4 memory (upgradeable to 96GB), a 1TB SSD, quad-display support, Windows 11 Pro, Wi-Fi 6, and 2.5Gb Ethernet in a compact mini PC.

**In the UK:** now £442 (was £519)

In his review, our mini PC expert Alastair called the A5 Pro "a well-balanced and genuinely impressive home and office mini PC."

Despite its compact size, the A5 Pro mini PC supports up to four displays via its dual HDMI and dual USB-C ports, making it a practical choice for anyone who works across multiple windows or applications.

It also supports resolutions up to 8K, delivering crisp text and detailed visuals for documents, presentations, spreadsheets, and media.

For connectivity, it offers 2.5 Gigabit Ethernet, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, four USB ports, an SD card reader, and HDMI 2.1.

Windows 11 Pro comes pre-installed, providing business-focused features such as BitLocker encryption, Remote Desktop, and domain support all straight out of the box.

The A5 Pro uses Geekom's IceBlast 3.0 cooling system to keep temperatures under control while maintaining low noise levels. The company backs the system with a three-year warranty, considerably longer than the single year offered by many rival manufacturers.

Priced at just over $500, the Geekom A5 Pro offers an attractive combination of performance, connectivity, upgradeability, and compact design. In the US, you can also pick up the A5 Pro with a Ryzen 5 7430U chip, 16GB RAM and a 512GB SSD for $399, down from $449. Or, a model with a Ryzen 5 7530U CPU, 16GB RAM and a 512GB SSD for $449.10, down from $549.

Whether you're replacing an aging desktop, setting up a home office, or simply need a capable everyday PC that takes up very little space, this is a terrific money-saving deal at Amazon.
