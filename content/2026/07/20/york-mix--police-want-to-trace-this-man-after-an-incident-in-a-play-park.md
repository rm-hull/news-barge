Police in York are appealing for information to help trace this man in connection with an ongoing investigation into outraging public decency in Strensall.

They believe he may have information that could help them.

The incident happened in The Village play park at 6.45pm on Sunday 28 June 2026, near to the Strensall and Towthorpe Hall.
