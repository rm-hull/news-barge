# From hundreds to thousands: How India's 'cockroach' protest movement has grown

Thousands of people are attempting to march to India's parliament after the Cockroach Janta Party (CJP) called a protest demanding reforms to the education system.

The group, which is not a real political party, started out as satire. But on Monday, it held its biggest protest yet.

BBC correspondent Azadeh Moshiri, who attended the group's first protest in early June, reports from the ground in Delhi.
