# Tynan Thompson: Manchester United sign young Tottenham prospect in deal worth up to £8m

- Tottenham's highly-rated winger Tynan Thompson has joined Manchester United.
- The deal is valued at up to £8m, comprising an initial £4m fee and an additional £4m in add-ons.
- The 18-year-old was understood to have started to grow frustrated at a lack of first-team chances at Tottenham.
- Personal family circumstances have also been cited as a factor behind the departure.
- The transfer agreement includes a sell-on clause in the region of 15 to 20 per cent and a matching rights condition, potentially allowing Tottenham to re-sign Thompson in the future.
