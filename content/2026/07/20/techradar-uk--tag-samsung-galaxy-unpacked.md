# Samsung Galaxy Unpacked

![Samsung Galaxy Unpacked July 2026 Teaser Invite](https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/zofBCzzfmn3x25iUoFveYL-1200-80.jpg) 

Samsung Galaxy Unpacked is the tech giant's biannual launch event for its flagship devices — think Galaxy smartphones, watches, earbuds and more.

It takes place roughly at the start of every year and then midway through — typically January and July, but some have been in February/August instead; indeed, its first event in 2026 was in February.

The first event of the year is where we meet the flagship S-series smartphones — this year, it unveiled the S26, S26 Plus and S26 Ultra, while the second one tends to be focused on its foldables: the Galaxy Flip and Fold series. Buds, Watches and other devices such as XR headsets can appear at either. These days, you can also expect a lot of AI chat at both.

The next Unpacked takes place this week: Wednesday, July 22 to be precise. We'll be covering it all live from the event in London, and you can follow it below.
