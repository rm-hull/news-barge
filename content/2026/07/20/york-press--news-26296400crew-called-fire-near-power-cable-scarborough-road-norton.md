A crew was called to a report of a fire in the open near to a power line in a North Yorkshire town earlier this afternoon (Monday).

The crew, from Malton, attended the scene on Scarborough Road, Norton, at 2.42pm.

A spokesperson for North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue service said "This was 5m x 5m of shrubland on fire caused by the cables from the power line. A hose reel jet was used and the incident handed over to National Power Grid for cable repairs."
