POLICE are searching for a missing child who was last seen in York city centre in the early hours of this morning,

North Yorkshire Police say that its officers are currently searching for 12-year-old Alfie, who was last seen in York city centre at 1.30am today (July 20).

He is described being slim, around 4ft 6in tall, with short brown hair and was last seen wearing grey tracksuit bottoms, a black hoody and black trainers.

Alfie also has access to a bike.

“Extensive enquiries are ongoing to locate Alfie and as part of our enquiries, we are asking for anyone who may have seen Alfie, or who has information about his whereabouts, to contact us immediately,” said a spokesperson for the force.

If you can help with the investigation, please call 101 or visit the North Yorkshire Police website and live chat; call 999 for an immediate sighting.

Please quote reference 12260137987 when providing information.
