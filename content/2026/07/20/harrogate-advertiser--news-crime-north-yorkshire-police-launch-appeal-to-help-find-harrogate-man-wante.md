# North Yorkshire Police launch appeal to help find Harrogate man wanted for breaching restraining order

## North Yorkshire Police has released an image and launched an appeal to help find a wanted 24-year-old man from Harrogate.

Guy Elms, 24, is wanted for breaching a restraining order.

Officers believe could be in Harrogate, Ripon, or Leeds.

He is described as white, slim/medium build, 5ft 10inch tall, with brown hair and brown eyes.

![Guy Elms, 24, is wanted by police for breaching a restraining order and could be in Harrogate, Ripon or Leeds](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOmIzMjYxNTY2LTc0ZDMtNDU5Zi1hNDYxLTYzNDJhODdkMzRkZjoxNzZiODA4Zi1jOWQ4LTQ5NTUtYTQ5ZS03MTFlOTM3YzczNDE=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: “Extensive enquiries have been ongoing to locate him and we’re now appealing for your help to help find him.”

If you see Guy Elms, or have any information about his current whereabouts, you should call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

If the sighting is immediate, you should call 999.

Alternatively if you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or by visiting [https://crimestoppers-uk.org/](https://crimestoppers-uk.org/)

You should quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12250240376 when providing any details regarding his whereabouts.
