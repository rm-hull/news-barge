A MAN has been arrested after allegedly threatening people with a hammer in a coastal town.

Humberside Police has confirmed that a 34-year-old man has been arrested after officers were called to Chapel Street, Bridlington, yesterday afternoon (July 19).

“We were called to reports of theft from a shop in Bridlington, and shortly after to reports of a man making threats to the public with a hammer.

Officers swiftly attended and a man matching the caller’s description was located and arrested on suspicion of theft from a shop, possession of a weapon in a public place and affray,” said a spokesperson for the force.

He is currently being held in custody and helping with the investigation.
