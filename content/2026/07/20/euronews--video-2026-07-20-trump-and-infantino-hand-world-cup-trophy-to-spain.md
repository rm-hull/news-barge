US President Donald Trump and FIFA President Gianni Infantino handed the 2026 World Cup trophy to Spain captain Rodri after the team defeated Argentina 1-0 in the final.

The video shows the trophy presentation at the New York/New Jersey Stadium, with Spanish players taking their places on stage, medals being awarded and the famous trophy lifted under a shower of confetti.

Spain secured their second World Cup title after Ferran Torres scored the decisive goal in extra time, ending Argentina's reign as world champions.

The closing ceremony followed a tense final watched by around 80,000 spectators near New York.

As Trump and Infantino entered the pitch, they were met with boos from parts of the crowd.
