# How to sign up for app pushes from Bulletin sport

If you’ve enjoyed our World Cup coverage, there’s an easy way to stay in the loop with the latest football news

- Download the Bulletin app from the iOS or Android app stores for free.
- If you already have an account with The Independent, you can log in with your account credentials. Otherwise you can register for a new account in just a few minutes.
- Click on the head and shoulders profile icon in the top right hand corner, and click ‘Your notifications’ in the Customise section.
- Toggle the notification settings to turn sports app pushes on, along with any other topics you’re interested in.
- That’s it! You’re now signed up to concise, swipeable updates on the biggest stories from our trusted sports journalists.
