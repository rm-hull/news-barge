Controversial influencers Andrew and Tristan Tate are expected to appear in a US court on Monday following their arrest in Miami.

The Crown Prosecution Service (CPS) has confirmed it is seeking their extradition from the US to the UK over alleged sexual offences.

The CPS announced new charges against the brothers, stemming from allegations made by four additional complainants.

Andrew Tate faces 32 new charges, including rape, trafficking for sexual exploitation, assault and offences related to indecent images of a child.

Tristan Tate faces six new charges, including sexual assault, rape, and trafficking for sexual exploitation, with all alleged offences occurring between July 2010 and August 2017.
