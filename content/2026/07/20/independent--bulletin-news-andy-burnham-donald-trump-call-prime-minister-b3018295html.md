# Andy Burnham speaks with Donald Trump in one of his first calls as PM

- Andy Burnham has officially become the new prime minister, receiving congratulatory messages from various world leaders.
- In one of his initial calls, Mr Burnham spoke with US President Donald Trump, who commented on the new prime minister's perceived liberalism and potential backing for North Sea oil and gas drilling.
- European Union leaders, including Ursula von der Leyen and Antonio Costa, expressed their desire for strengthened EU-UK relations under Mr Burnham's leadership.
- Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky, Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese, French President Emmanuel Macron, Italian Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni, and German Chancellor Friedrich Merz also extended their congratulations.
- International leaders highlighted the importance of continued cooperation, shared values, and addressing global challenges alongside the United Kingdom.
