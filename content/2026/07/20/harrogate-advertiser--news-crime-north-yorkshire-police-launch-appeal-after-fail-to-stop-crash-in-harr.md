# North Yorkshire Police launch appeal after fail-to-stop crash in Harrogate leaves car with thousands of pounds of damage

## North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information and witnesses following a fail-to-stop road traffic collision in Harrogate.

The incident happened between 2.25pm and 2.35pm on Monday, July 13 on Railway Road, close to the entrances to the Sainsbury's car park.

Officers say the collision involved a black Honda and an SUV estate, believed to be either grey or blue in colour.

Following the collision, the SUV failed to stop and turned left at the Wetherby Road junction, heading towards Woodlands Corner.

![The police are appealing for information and witnesses after a fail-to-stop collision on Railway Road in Harrogate left a black Honda with several thousand pounds worth of damage](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOmVjOWVjYTk4LWMyNjYtNDZmYy1hYWU3LTY1NTQ1MTI1OTBjYjplNTVjMGZjZi0wZmUzLTQ5YzgtOTk2OC0zMDQ4YWJhMjc4N2M=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



Police believe the vehicle involved is likely to have sustained some level of front-end damage.

The collision caused several thousand pounds worth of damage to the black Honda.

A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: "If anyone witnessed the accident or was driving in the area or has dashcam footage or information that could assist our investigation, please contact us."

You can also call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

You should quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12260135109 when providing any details regarding the incident.
