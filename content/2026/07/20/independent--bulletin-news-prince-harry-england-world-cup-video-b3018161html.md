# Prince Harry says England World Cup defeat was down to ‘mindset’

- The Duke of Sussex has stated that England needs a shift in mindset to win a World Cup.
- His comments followed England's semi-final defeat to Argentina, where they conceded two late goals.
- Speaking on Fifa World Cup on Fox After Hours, Prince Harry suggested the team appeared 'shocked' after scoring their first goal.
- He observed an immediate, unplanned tendency for the team to fall into defence after taking the lead.
- Prince Harry attributed the loss to a flawed mindset, arguing the team should believe they can extend a one-nil lead to two-nil.
