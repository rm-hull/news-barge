# AOC weighs in on potential 2028 presidential run

- New York Democratic Representative Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez stated that she has not ruled out a 2028 presidential run.
- She is working to mend relationships with the left wing of her party, who have criticized her for moving closer to the political center.
- Ocasio-Cortez has consistently polled well, at one point surpassing Vice President JD Vance in a survey.
- She emphasizes the importance of providing "real" solutions to voters' economic problems over "disconnected partisan motivation."
- She has faced criticism from left-wing allies, including the Democratic Socialists of America, over her stances on issues like Israel and her perceived alignment with establishment Democrats.
