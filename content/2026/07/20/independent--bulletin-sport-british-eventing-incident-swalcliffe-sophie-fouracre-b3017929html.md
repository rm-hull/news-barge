British Eventing confirms deaths of two riders in separate incidents

British Eventing announced the deaths of two riders, Sophie Fouracre and Kerry Donoghue, following separate cross-country accidents on Saturday.

Sophie Fouracre, 34, was fatally injured during an event at Swalcliffe Park in Oxfordshire.

Kerry Donoghue, 42, died hours later after a cross-country accident at Penrith, Cumbria.

Both horses involved in the incidents were examined by vets and confirmed to be uninjured.

British Eventing will conduct comprehensive reviews of the accidents and expressed deep heartbreak and condolences to the families and friends of the deceased.
