A VEHICLE has crashed into a building in a coastal North Yorkshire town.

  



North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said crews from Filey joined local crews from Scarborough at the scene in Hoxton Road in the town centre around 12.10 this morning (Monday, July 20).

A service spokesperson said an incident support unit was also present.

The spokesperson added: "All occupants were out on arrival.

"Crews made the scene safe."
