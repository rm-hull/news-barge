North Yorkshire police officers are currently searching for 12-year-old Alfie, who was last seen today at 1.30am (Monday 20 July) in York city centre.

Extensive enquiries are ongoing to locate Alfie and as part of our enquiries, we are asking for anyone who may have seen Alfie, or who has information about his whereabouts, to contact us immediately.

He is described as slim build, around 4ft 6in tall, and short brown hair. He was last seen wearing grey tracksuit bottoms, a black hoody and black trainers. Alfie also has access to a pedal cycle.

If you have an immediate sighting, please call 999.
