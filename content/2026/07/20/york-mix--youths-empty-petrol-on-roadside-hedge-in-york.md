Police and fire crews were called to an incident in York last night (Sunday).

A fire crew from Knaresborough, providing cover in the city, were called by police to **Kingsway West** at**7.56am**.

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said they were asked to “douse a hedge line measuring 26m by 2m, with water, after youths had emptied a petrol can over the hedge line.

**Kingsway West, York**

“Police were on scene and crews dowsed the hedge using a hose reel jet.”
