# Marc Cucurella continued secret tradition before Spain’s World Cup victory

- Marc Cucurella continued a Spanish football tradition by burying a “lucky” coin in the pitch before Spain's recent World Cup final victory.
- The ritual was requested by Joan Capdevila, a 2010 World Cup winner, who performed the same act before Spain's triumph 16 years ago.
- Cucurella was seen discreetly placing the coin into the MetLife Stadium grass just before kick-off against Argentina.
- Capdevila later confirmed on X that he had asked Cucurella to replicate his superstition, celebrating Spain's extra-time win.
- Capdevila himself faced travel issues with his ESTA application due to a past visit to Iran, which was resolved, allowing him to attend the final.
