BBC commentator Guy Mowbray mistakenly identified musician Pharrell Williams as A$AP Rocky during the World Cup final.

The error occurred when cameras showed Williams in the stands during the match between Spain and Argentina, with Mowbray saying: “I think that is AKA Rocky...or A$AP Rocky, sorry.”

Fox Sports announcer John Strong also made a similar blunder, confusing Matt Damon with Brad Pitt.

Spain claimed their second World Cup title by defeating Argentina 1-0 in extra time.

The star-studded event featured numerous celebrities, including Dua Lipa and Timothée Chalamet.
