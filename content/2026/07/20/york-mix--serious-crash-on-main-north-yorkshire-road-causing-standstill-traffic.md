A serious crash is causing problems in North Yorkshire this morning (Monday).

It’s on the A162 between Towton and Tadcaster, close to the junction with the A64.

Emergency services are on the scene.

#### Crash location

Witnesses say a motorbike is one of the vehicles involved.

Drivers report standstill traffic on the A162, and the crash is having a knock-on effect on traffic on the A64.
