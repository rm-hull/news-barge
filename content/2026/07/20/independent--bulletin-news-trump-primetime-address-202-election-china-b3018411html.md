# Trump advisers reportedly tried to dissuade him from primetime address

- President Donald Trump delivered a primetime address last week, accusing China of interfering in the 2020 election to ensure his defeat.
- Advisers attempted to dissuade Trump from giving the speech, but he proceeded despite their efforts, *The Washington Post* reports.
- Many Republican operatives and strategists expressed concerns, stating that voters are not interested in revisiting 2020 election grievances.
- GOP strategists urged Trump to focus on issues like the economy and affordability, which they believe are more relevant to voters.
- The address was intended to pressure Republicans into passing the SAVE Act, a bill requiring specific documentation for voting, which currently lacks sufficient Senate support.
