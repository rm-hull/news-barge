Tom Stoltman, a three-time World's Strongest Man winner and the first Scottish athlete to achieve this title, has officially joined the cast of the BBC's Gladiators reboot.

Stoltman, who has also won Britain's Strongest Man multiple times, expressed that joining the show is a 'dream opportunity' and a new challenge, aiming to inspire viewers with his strength and determination.

The popular programme, hosted by Bradley and Barney Walsh, is set to return for its fourth series on BBC One and iPlayer in 2027, with filming commencing this month in Sheffield.

Gladiators, a reboot of the 1992 ITV show, has garnered significant public interest since its relaunch, with its first episode in 2024 attracting over six million viewers.

The show has expanded its reach with celebrity specials and live arena tours across major UK cities, with a second tour planned to include new locations from October.
