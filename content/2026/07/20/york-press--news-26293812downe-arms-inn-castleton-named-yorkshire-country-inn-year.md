## North Yorkshire Moors pub named Country Inn of the Year at prestigious awards

A fabulous beer garden offers spectacular views. *(Image: Pic supplied)*

A traditional North Yorkshire pub has been named Country Inn of the Year 2026/2027 at the Yorkshire Prestige Awards.

The Downe Arms Inn, located in Castleton, near Whitby claimed the title for its outstanding hospitality, customer service, and guest experience.

Judges praised the pub’s excellence across hospitality, customer service, quality and overall guest experience.

Publicans Johnathon and Ashley Cairns took over the 18th Century inn on the North Yorkshire Moors earlier this year.

Mr Cairns said: “When we took on the Downe Arms Inn, our vision was simple: to create a traditional country inn where guests feel genuinely welcomed, enjoy great food and a proper pint and leave wanting to return.

"Drawing on our years of hospitality experience, we have worked tirelessly to breathe new life into this wonderful village inn while preserving everything that makes it so special.”

Mrs Cairns described their approach to running the business.

She said: “We believe a good pub isn’t just about what’s on tap, it’s about the people, the atmosphere and a sense of belonging.”

  The exterior of the Downe Arms at Castleton, near Whitby. 

*(Image: Pic supplied)*

The inn, owned by Punch Pubs & Co, is a traditional country pub offering freshly prepared food, comfortable accommodation, and a warm welcome in the heart of the North Yorkshire countryside.

Paul Hutchinson, operations manager at Punch Pubs & Co, praised the couple’s dedication.

He said: “This award is a reflection of the passion, commitment and hard work shown every single day by our incredible team at The Downe Arms.

"Johnathon and Ashley have been amazing since they arrived and I’m proud to support them and the team on their journey.”

The Downe Arms Inn is located at 3 High Street, Castleton.

The popular venue receives 4.4 stars out of five on TripAdvisor, based on 223 reviews.

Google awards 4.6 stars out of five, based on 443 reviews.
