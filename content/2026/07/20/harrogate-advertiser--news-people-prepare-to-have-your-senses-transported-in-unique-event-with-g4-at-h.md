# Prepare to have your senses transported in unique event with G4 at Harrogate's biggest venue

## An unusual event in Harrogate has been upgraded to the town’s biggest event after “exceptional demand” for tickets.

Starring four-piece singing superstars G4, it had been intended that RSPB Night of Nature would take place at the Royal Hall in Harrogate next month.

But not only did that venue sell out, demand for tickets for this special night blending live music with a celebration of the natural world just kept going.

The event marks the first time the RSPB's acclaimed Night of Nature has been staged outside London where its premiere took place last year, bringing the celebration of wildlife to Yorkshire for the very first time.

![The forthcoming Harrogate event starring vocal superstars G4 marks the first time the RSPB's acclaimed Night of Nature has been staged outside London where its premiere took place last year. (Picture contributed)](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOmY1ZjRmYjI5LTA0ZmQtNDYxMS1iNTRmLTRhZjgxMmRkOWI2MToyYmI4NzkxMC0xOTQ2LTQ4ZTgtYjk3Ni0wOTBlMmMzY2FiYjc=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



Hosted by The Royal Society for the Protection of Birds’s Vice President Kate Humble, the immersive event will be headlined by vocal group G4, with support from the Blackheath Quartet.

With a stirring blend of storytelling and visuals, Night of Nature aims to narrow the divide between humans and the nature surrounding us, striving towards a future where people, wildlife and wild places can all thrive alongside each other.

Taking place on Saturday, August 15, phenomenal interest means the show has been upgraded to the larger Harrogate Convention Centre, with additional tickets on sale now.

Speaking about the event’s blend of film, concert and conservation, RSPB CEO Beccy Speight said: “Following the overwhelming success of Night of Nature 2025 in London, and the incredible feedback we received from our guests, we are delighted to bring this special event back.

"This year, we’re taking Night of Nature north from London to Harrogate, sharing the magic of this unforgettable evening with even more of our supporters.

“Night of Nature will once again be a celebration of the beauty and wonder of the natural world, brought to life through live music, immersive visuals, and shared inspiration”

RSPB teams from nearby reserves including Bempton Cliffs, Leighton Moss and Aire Valley will also attend.

G4 originally shot to fame on TV in The X Factor, bringing ‘pop opera’ both to mainstream audiences and selling two million albums in the UK.
