  **Update - The road has now been reopened following the earlier crash.**

Motorists are being warned of delays after an accident closed part of a major road near York.

The A64 is currently blocked in the eastbound direction between Barton Hill and High Hutton following the crash.

  **Recommended reads:**

Emergency services are at the scene and it is understood traffic is being be diverted towards Castle Howard.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said the outside lane of the westbound carriageway may have temporary restrictions as a result.

"Please be prepared for delays if you are travelling in the area."
