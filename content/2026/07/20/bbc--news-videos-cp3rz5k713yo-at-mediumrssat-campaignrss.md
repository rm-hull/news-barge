# How is the new PM's cabinet shaping up?

Prime Minister Andy Burnham has started building his new cabinet after taking office following the resignation of Sir Keir Starmer.

Among his appointments are Ed Miliband, joining as Foreign Secretary, Shabana Mahmood, who remains Home Secretary, and former Labour Deputy Leader Angela Rayner as Housing Secretary.

The BBC's Annita McVeigh talks us through some of these key appointments.
