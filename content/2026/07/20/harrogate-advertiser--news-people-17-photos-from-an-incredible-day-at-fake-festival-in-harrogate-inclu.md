Fake Festival is a tribute-based music event which tours the United Kingdom, with a licensed bar, food traders and entertainment on offer.

The line-up saw some brilliant acts take to the stage including Flash as Queen, Coldplace as Coldplay, The Fillers as The Killers, Bon Jovi Experience as Bon Jovi, Young Elton as Elton John and Kings of Lyon as Kings of Leon.

These are some of the best photos from a fantastic day at Fake Festival in Harrogate thanks to our photographer Gerard Binks…
