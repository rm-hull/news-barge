- **Sleepy Hollow**-** is getting a reboot at Sony, according to reports from Deadline**
- **Euphoria**-** star Sydney Sweeney is believed to be leading the cast**
- **Lindsey Anderson Beer will direct the new movie, which has been dubbed as "an erotic retelling of the Sleepy Hollow story"**

A *Sleepy Hollow* reboot wasn't something I was expecting, but it's happening at Sony according to reports from Deadline.

At the moment, the new movie has just been announced but we do know that it will be an "erotic retelling" of *Sleepy Hollow*, directed by* Pet Semetary: Bloodlines* director Lindsey Anderson Beer.

In addition to this, *Euphoria* star Sydney Sweeney will reportedly be leading the cast of the new *Sleepy Hollow* movie, but further stars have not been announced.

Anderson Beer's new story follows Katrina Van Tassel, who was played by Christina Ricci in the 90s movie. Instead of being the love interest for the protagonist, his time, Tassel will be the hero in a mystery that will feature a "seductive supernatural love triangle."

The story is also said to blend "gothic atmosphere, psychological intrigue, and erotic thriller elements into a contemporary, high-concept reinvention of the classic tale."

 ![Cassie holding a melting ice cream in Euphoria season 3](https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/yoSdKncB5ndYH8yyNodsTf.jpg)

*Sleepy Hollow* is not the first horror movie Sydney Sweeney has starred in, as she had the leading role in the religious horror movie *Immaculate* in 2024. So it will be interesting to see how she leads an adaptation of an iconic story.

Of course, it isn't a straight adaptation of the *Sleepy Hollow* novel by Washington Irving. Anderson Beer is publishing a companion novel to go alongside her *Sleepy Hollow* movie, which is expected to be released in fall 2026.
