A WALL in a North Yorkshire town has been badly damaged.

The wall, at the junction of Saville Street and Market Place in Malton, appears to have been hit by a vehicle.

A spokesperson for Malton Town Council said the incident had been reported to the Fitzwilliam (Malton) Estate.
