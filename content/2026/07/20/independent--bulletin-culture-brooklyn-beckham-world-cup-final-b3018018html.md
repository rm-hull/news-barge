# Brooklyn Beckham skips VIP box at World Cup final amid family drama

- Brooklyn Beckham was seen watching the World Cup final at home in a picture shared to his wife’s Instagram, while his parents, David and Victoria Beckham, and siblings Romeo, Cruz, and Harper, attended the match at MetLife Stadium.
- Cruz Beckham, who was born in Spain, shared photos from the final, celebrating with his girlfriend and former Spain defender Sergio Ramos.
- It comes after Brooklyn confirmed he was no longer on speaking terms with his parents in an Instagram post in January.
- He previously featured in a DoorDash advertising campaign, saying: “You're probably wondering why I'm watching the Fifa World Cup 2026 from home… It's a long story.”
- When asked about the feud in April, Victoria Beckham said: “All we’ve ever tried to do is protect our children and love our children.”
