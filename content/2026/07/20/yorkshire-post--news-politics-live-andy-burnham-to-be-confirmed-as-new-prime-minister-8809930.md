## Andy Burnham will enter 10 Downing Street promising to give people “breathing room” against the rising cost of living.

After his speech, Mr *Burnham* is expected to name his*cabinet*.

He has been tight-lipped about his selections, but is believed to have been weighing up either Energy Secretary Ed Miliband or Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood to be chancellor.

Monday caps a rapid transition for Mr *Burnham*, coming barely a month after he returned to Westminster following his victory in the Makerfield by-election.
