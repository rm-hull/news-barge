A top North Yorkshire hotel, spa and report has won yet another accolade, this one chosen by its customers.

Grantley Hall, near Ripon, has been declared a Tripadvisor ‘Best of the Best’ for 2026.

The accolade is based on online reviews given by customers throughout the whole of 2025.

Tripadvisor has awarded Grantley Hall 4.7 stars out of five, ranking it 19th in the whole of the United Kingdom.

Yorkshire had only one other entrant in the UK top 20, The Chambers – Hotel and Serviced apartments in Leeds.

Top in the UK hotel category was The Royal Lancaster in London, which was also ranked fourth in the world.

Tripadvisor said of Grantley Hall: “Rolling up to Grantley Hall, you get those landscaped gardens and Yorkshire views that really set the scene.

“Inside, you’ll find rooms with Italian marble bathrooms, and the vibe is classic meets contemporary.

“There’s a Michelin-starred restaurant, lively bars, an award-winning spa, indoor and outdoor pools, plus a full-on wellness center—so you’re pretty much sorted for any mood.

A Grantley Hall spokesperson told the Press: “It’s great to be recognised in Tripadvisor's Best of the Best awards for the first time, and to be included among the UK's top 20 hotels.

“Every guest review represents someone who has chosen to spend time with us, and we're incredibly grateful to everyone who has taken the time to share their experience.

“Guest feedback is at the heart of everything we do, helping us celebrate what's working and continuing to strive for more.”
