A site manager in North Yorkshire has won a national award for excellence in housebuilding.

Ricky Coulter-Bell, 32, who oversees the Bramble Wood development, received the Pride in the Job Quality Award from the National House Building Council (NHBC) for his work with David Wilson Homes.

The awards, recognised as a benchmark of industry excellence, highlight the UK’s top-performing site managers.

Ricky Coulter-Bell, who has worked with David Wilson Homes for 15 years, said: "Winning this award is a real honour.

"It feels very rewarding to be recognised for the quality homes we deliver and the effort put in by the team to meet outstanding quality standards."

It is his first time receiving the award.

Daniel Smith, managing director at David Wilson Homes Yorkshire East, said: "We’re so proud of Ricky for achieving this prestigious Pride in the Job Quality Award.

"It’s amazing news for both the team at Bramble Wood and for our customers."
