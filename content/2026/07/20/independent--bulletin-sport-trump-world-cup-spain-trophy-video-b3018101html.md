# Moment Trump is asked to step aside by Spain after handing over World Cup trophy

- Spain secured a 1-0 victory against reigning champions Argentina in the World Cup final, with Ferran Torres scoring the decisive goal in extra time.
- Donald Trump, accompanied by Fifa's Gianni Infantino, presented the World Cup trophy to the victorious Spanish team.
- Spanish captain Rodri, who was also awarded the Golden Ball for player of the tournament, was observed gesturing for Mr Trump to move aside during the trophy presentation.
- Despite Rodri's gesture and apparent urging from Infantino, Mr Trump remained at the side of the stage, close to the players.
- This incident follows a similar event last year where Mr Trump remained prominently positioned during the Club World Cup trophy lift with Chelsea.
