*X-Men 97* season 2 took us on something of a narrative detour with last week's episode, so some viewers will be pleased to know that its next chapter gets things back on track. And by that, I mean this season's sixth entry will address the fallout from*X-Men 97* season 2 episode 4's emotionally devastating ending.

Want to know when the Marvel TV show's next installment will make its bow on Disney+? Of course you do, otherwise you wouldn't be here. I'll stop beating around the bush, then, and tell you to scroll on for more details…

## What is the launch time for X-Men 97 season 2 episode 6?

 ![Sabretooth, Lady Deathstrike, Wolverine, and Maverick standing in the show at night in X-Men 97 season 2 episode 5](https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/WpftVoZy9uQatdqQnXQqvh.jpg) 


Titled 'Danger.exe', the next episode of *X-Men 97*'s sophomore season will premiere at**12am PT / 3am ET on Wednesday, July 22** in the US and Canada.

The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU)-adjacent TV series' forthcoming chapter will also come out in other nations on July 22, albeit at a different time depending on where you live. Check out the list below to see when it'll be released in your country:

- **US**— 12am PT / 3am ET
- **Canada**— 12am PT / 3am ET
- **UK**— 8am BST
- **India**— 12:30pm IST
- **Singapore**— 3pm SGT
- **Australia**— 5pm AEST
- **New Zealand**— 7pm NZST
