THREE people have been taken to hospital after the air ambulance attended a crash in a major North Yorkshire.

The A19 at the Balne Cross Roads – which is located just to the south of Selby – is currently closed and has been since just after 10am today (July 20) following a crash.

North Yorkshire Police has asked drivers to avoid the A19 at the Balne Cross Roads whilst emergency services attend the scene.

It was also confirmed that Yorkshire Ambulance Service attended the scene at 10.14am.

“A number of resources, including three ambulances, the Hazardous Area Response Team and an air ambulance, were dispatched to the scene and three patients have been conveyed to hospital,” said a spokesperson for the force.
