A ‘DELIBERATE’ fire set on a North Yorkshire allotment has been put out after spreading to a nearby structure.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service says its crew from Selby were called out to Ouse Bank at 3.32pm today (July 20) after reports of fires being set in allotments.
