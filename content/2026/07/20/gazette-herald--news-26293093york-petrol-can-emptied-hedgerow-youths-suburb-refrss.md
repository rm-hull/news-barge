YOUTHS have emptied a can of petrol over a hedgerow in a York suburb.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said a crew from Knaresborough were contacted by the county's police force around 8pm last night (Sunday, July 19).

  


The hedge line in Kingsway West in Acomb measured 26 metres long by two metres wide, a service spokesperson said.

A spokesperson for North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said: "The hedge line was dowsed with water after youth emptied a petrol can over it.

"Police were also on the scene.

"Crews dowsed the hedge using a hose reel jet."
