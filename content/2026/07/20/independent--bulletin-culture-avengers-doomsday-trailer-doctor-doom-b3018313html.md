# Avengers: Doomsday trailer released with Robert Downey Jr as new supervillain

- Marvel has released the first full trailer for *Avengers: Doomsday*, showcasing Chris Hemsworth’s Thor uniting heroes from across multiple universes.
- The film introduces Robert Downey Jr. as the new supervillain, Doctor Doom, a character entirely separate from his previous role as Iron Man.
- The trailer confirms the return of Chris Evans’ Steve Rogers and features a vast array of characters from the Marvel Cinematic Universe, X-Men, and Fantastic Four.
- Thor's dialogue in the trailer warns of an unprecedented threat, stressing the need for unity and a “miracle” to overcome the challenge.
- *Avengers: Doomsday*, which marks the beginning of the Multiverse Saga's final chapter, is set to be released in cinemas on 18 December.
