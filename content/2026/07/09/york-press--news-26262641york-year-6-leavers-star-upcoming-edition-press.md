Year 6 pupils are set to take centre stage in a special collection of memories.

The Press will publish its Last Class 2026 photo special on Tuesday, July 14, featuring photographs from primary schools across York and surrounding areas in a commemorative supplement.

The edition celebrates school leavers with a keepsake snapshot of their final year.

You can order your copy online at [https://store.newsquest.co.uk/last-class-2026](https://store.newsquest.co.uk/last-class-2026) if you are unable to get the newspaper in store.

Visit your local newsagent or supermarket on Tuesday to secure your copy while stocks last.

A spokesperson said: "Filled with leavers' photographs from local primary schools, this edition is a wonderful way to mark the end of an important chapter.

"From proud smiles to school uniforms for the very last time — it's all here, captured in print for you to keep forever."
