# France given Kylian Mbappe injury scare as forward limps off against Morocco

- Kylian Mbappe was substituted as a precaution during France's 2-0 World Cup quarter-final victory over Morocco.
- The French captain sustained an ankle knock after a foul by Morocco defender Issa Diop, who received a yellow card.
- Mbappe had ice strapped to his ankle on the substitutes' bench but did not appear overly concerned by the injury.
- He was later seen celebrating unimpeded with his teammates after the match.
- France is scheduled to play in the semi-final in five days, with Mbappe's condition not expected to be a major issue.
