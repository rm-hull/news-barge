A family-friendly cycle guide is helping parents across North Yorkshire plan summer adventures with a twist.

Children's bike brand Woom has created the Family Micro-Adventure Guide, which highlights cycling routes across the region and encourages families to turn bike rides into outdoor experiences packed with fun and exploration.

One recommended route included in the guide is the Sutton Bank Family Cycle Trail.

The route offers easy cycling and plenty of opportunities for wildlife spotting, den building, playground stops and scenic picnic spots.

Simon Timlett, Country Manager UK of woom, said: "Summer doesn't have to mean expensive days out or long journeys.

"Some of the best family memories are made close to home, and cycling is a fantastic way to explore local areas while making the most of quality time together outdoors.

"A route with plenty of opportunities to stop, play and discover new things helps keep children engaged and turns a bike ride into a genuine adventure."

With children aged eight to 14 spending an estimated two to four hours online daily, cycling offers a healthy alternative that combines exercise, exploration and family bonding.

The scheme aims to inspire families to make the most of the summer holidays by getting outside and exploring together.
