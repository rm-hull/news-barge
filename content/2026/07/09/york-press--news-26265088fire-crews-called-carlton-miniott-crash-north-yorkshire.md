FIRE crews were called in after a crash involving three vehicles in a North Yorkshire village.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service says its crews from Northallerton and Ripon were called to Carlton Miniott at 6.15pm yesterday (July 8) after a crash.

"All occupants of the vehicles were out on arrival of the Fire Service. Crews assisted with traffic management and making the scene safe," said a spokesperson for the force.

The scene was left with police.
