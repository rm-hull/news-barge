## Are there any Alton Towers discount codes right now?

Alton Towers does not frequently release traditional promo codes for checkout use. Instead, most savings come from advance ticket pricing, seasonal offers, Merlin passes and partner deals.

This means you’ll usually find the biggest discounts by booking online early rather than searching for a code at checkout.

## When is Alton Towers cheapest?

Tickets are usually cheapest during off-peak periods, particularly midweek dates outside of school holidays. Prices increase significantly during peak summer weeks, weekends and special events.

Booking as early as possible typically guarantees the lowest available price for your chosen date.

## Ways to save on Alton Towers tickets in 2026

### Advanced half-price tickets

For a day trip, you can save more than 50 per cent on tickets, with prices starting from £29.75 when booked in advance. This means you can plan ahead to avoid peak dates if you prefer – typically, the higher the price, the busier the day will be.

The Merlin group often runs two-for-one ticket offers on its theme parks, including Alton Towers, Thorpe Park, Legoland and Chessington. This automatically unlocks a second day out at an attraction of your choice, free of charge.

Also keep an eye out for two-for-one Alton Towers tickets, which are often available through partner promotions, including Kellogg’s cereal boxes, Cadbury packs and Carex products.
