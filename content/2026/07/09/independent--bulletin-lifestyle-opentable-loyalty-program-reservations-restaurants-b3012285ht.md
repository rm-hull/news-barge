# OpenTable launches new loyalty program for hard-to-get reservations

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/09/22/01KWTQ98BGY0GVGV92WH47T8VA.jpg)

- OpenTable launched its new Gold Tables program on Wednesday, expanding its existing Regulars loyalty program.
- Diners can achieve Gold status by completing six reservations through OpenTable within a 12-month period.
- Gold members gain exclusive access to reservations at over 500 in-demand restaurants across more than 50 cities, including major dining hubs.
- Additional perks for Gold members include early alerts for last-minute openings via OpenTable's Notify Me feature.
- Members also receive six months of free Uber One membership, offering benefits like credits on Uber rides and $0 delivery fees on Uber Eats orders.
