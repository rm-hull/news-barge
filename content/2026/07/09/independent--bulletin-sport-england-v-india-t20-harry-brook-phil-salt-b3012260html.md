# England wrap up T20 series win over India with explosive batting display

![Brook (right) and Salt guided England to victory](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/09/21/01KX45Z3MTRHK01P3QZS4AX3SW.jpg)

- England secured a dominant nine-wicket victory over India in Bristol on Thursday, sealing their T20 series with a match to spare and establishing an unassailable 3-0 lead.
- Captain Harry Brook led England's chase with an unbeaten 79 from just 35 balls, featuring eight fours and four sixes, while Phil Salt contributed an undefeated 59.
- The pair formed an extraordinary 146-run partnership, effortlessly chasing down India’s modest target of 158 for seven in only 13.5 overs.
- England's bowlers, including Jofra Archer and Josh Tongue who each claimed two wickets, restricted India despite captain Shreyas Iyer's isolated resistance of an unbeaten 80 from 49 deliveries.
- This comprehensive win marks England's first bilateral series victory against India in two or more games and represents India's record fifth consecutive defeat.
