Tournament favourites France face dark horses Morocco in the first quarter-final of the FIFA World Cup 2026 in Boston — and you can live stream the game around the world for free.

This fixture evokes memories of Qatar 2022, when the Atlas Lions became the first African side to reach a World Cup semi-final, only to see their run ended in a 2-0 defeat by Les Bleus. The scary part for Morocco - and the other teams remaining in the tournament - is that France look even better now than when they reached the final four years ago. Didier Deschamps' men showed they possess brawn as well as beauty in a battling 1-0 win over Paraguay in the last 16, courtesy of Kylian Mbappe's 70th-minute penalty. France's captain and talisman is now on seven goals for the tournament and looks determined to lift the World Cup for a second time.

Morocco's 3-0 win over Canada in the last 16 appears comfortable enough on the surface, but Mohamed Ouahbi's men had to survive heavy first-half pressure and registered only five shots in total. Tournament football is all about getting the job done, though, and the Atlas Lions have built up plenty of experience of winning knockout matches. The victory over Canada was particularly impressive considering they lost top goalscorer Ismael Saibari to a hamstring injury after just 22 minutes and he will be absent against France, potentially striking a fatal blow to Morocco's hopes of progression.

The winners will meet Spain or Belgium in the first semi-final in Dallas next Tuesday.
