The Independent Travel Awards have arrived, and we want to hear about your most unforgettable travel experiences for an opportunity to win one of 12 incredible prizes.

As part of the awards, Tui is giving away a gift card worth £10,000 for travellers to take the trip of a lifetime.

The travel and tourism company operates airlines, hotels, tours and cruises, and is one of the biggest package holiday brands in the UK.

Its holidays include everything from globe-trotting tours and adventures at sea to beach-based getaways in Europe and beyond.

From two weeks at the Tui Blue Sarigerme Parkresort in Dalaman, Turkey, to a Greek holiday at Lindos Blu Luxury Hotel & Suite or a family getaway to Crystal Cove in Barbados, this prize promises the trip of a lifetime at one of Tui’s top resorts.

Tui Group airlines fly to more than 180 destinations worldwide and operate from 21 regional UK airports.

With an all-in-one package approach and Atol-protected end-to-end travel, there are plenty of flight, hotel and transfer bundles to choose from.

Lucky winners will be able to redeem the Tui travel gift card as a full or part payment for travel products at all Tui Stores, excluding the Republic of Ireland. T&Cs apply.
