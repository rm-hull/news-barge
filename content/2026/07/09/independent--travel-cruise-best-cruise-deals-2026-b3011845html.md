Lured by iconic cities and beautiful beaches, an increasing numbers of travellers are choosing a cruise for their holiday – and you can still bag a bargain for this year and beyond.

Passengers are spoilt for choice, with several new shipsnow on the water. Royal Caribbean’s latest* Star of the Seas*and Virgin Voyages’* Brilliant Lady*hit the water in August 2025, while Princess Cruises* Star Princess* launched last October.

Guests can choose from convenient UK departures with brands such as Celebrity Cruises, Princess, P&O Cruises and Disney Cruise Line. Alternatively, there are plenty of fly-cruise options to travel the Mediterranean, the Caribbean and beyond.

The cruise industry’s traditional sales period – known as wave season – runs from January until the end of March but there are still decent deals available now.

It is also worth keeping an eye on your favourite cruise line’s website as deals can change regularly.

Here are some of the best cruise deals for wave season and beyond to catch a top cabin in 2026 or to plan ahead as far as 2028.

## Best cruise deals 2026

**Ambassador Cruise Line**

Ambassador Cruise Line is offering up to 20 per cent off sailings when booked by 29 July.

The offer is available on departures in 2026, 2027 and 2028 for short breaks and for cruises of up to 14 nights.

Guests can sail on Ambassador’s 13-night Solar Eclipse Cruise around the Arctic region, departing from Dundee, from £1,754 per person.

## Celebrity Cruises

Celebrity Cruises is offering up to 60 per cent off a second guest in the same cabin, plus European flights from £199 per person.

The cruise line is also offering savings worth up to £600 on European sailings depending on the length of the cruise.

Guests can save £80 on an Inside or Ocean view cabin on a three to five-night sailing, rising to £150 for the Balcony and Aqua category and £300 on Retreat suites.

Passengers sailing for six nights or longer can get £120 off per Inside or Ocean view cabin, £250 for a Balcony or Aqua room or £600 in the Retreat.

The offer applies to cruises departing until 30 April 2028, including on the cruise line’s newest ship, *Celebrity Xcel.*

## Cunard

Guests can get £200 off a balcony cabin and $250 (£186) of onboard credit with Cunard on select sailings across its fleet including in the Norwegian fjords and transatlantic voyages.

Bookings need to be made by 31 August for sailings in 2027 and 2028.

## Explora Journeys

Luxury cruise brand Explora Journeys is offering savings of up to £3,000 when booking its Ocean Terrace Suites.

Bookings need to be made by 28 July for sailings until 19 November 2026.

For example, you could sail on the cruise line’s newest ship *Explora III f* or eight nights from Miami to San Juan on 20 December for £3,800 per person instead of £5,650 each.

**Holland America Line**

Holland America Line has launched a “Fly and Cruise Caribbean” offer for its 2026/27 season.

Guests booking until 31 August 2026 can get air credit worth £600 per person, a free one-night pre cruise hotel stay and free return transfers from the airport, hotel and ship.

The deal applies to more than 120 Caribbean cruises sailing from October to April covering the eastern, western or southern Caribbean, as well as the Panama Canal, with select calls taking in Belize and Mexico.

Holland America has six ships in the Caribbean for its Caribbean season,*Nieuw Statendam, Rotterdam, Nieuw Amsterdam, Eurodam, Volendam* and*Zuiderdam*, with iteniraries ranging from seven to 21 days. Prices start from £899 per person.

## HX Expeditions

HX Expeditions is offering 25 per cent off or a complimentary suite upgrade on its 2026 Antarctica departures to mark its 130th anniversary this summer.

The expedition cruise line’s Highlights of Antarctica sailing now starts from £7,576 per person, compared with £10,999 per person previously, on select departures between October and December 2026.

The price includes all meals and select drinks, guided activities, tips, wifi, expedition gear and a professional photo log of your trip.

## Marella Cruises

Marella is offering passengers savings of up to £200 per booking on selected sailings departing until 30 April 2027.

Deals include a repositioning cruise from Gran Canaria to the Caribbean, departing on 26 April 2027 from £1,978 per person.

## Norwegian Cruise Line

Fares are on offer from £699 per person on Norwegian Cruise Line (NCL) sailings, including on the new *Norwegian Luna**.*

Guests booking a balcony can also get up to £250 of airfare credit per person towards European flights and can upgrade to NCL’s “More at Sea” package for wifi, drinks and speciality dining from £249 per person.

**Oceania**

Culinary-focused luxury cruise brand Oceania, which recently launched a new ship *Allura*, is currently offering up to 50 per cent off on more than 150 sailings across 2026 and 2027.

Guests can also choose from complimentary wine and beer or shore excursions.

Offers include a seven-day sailing from Barcelona to Rome aboard the newest ship in the fleet, *Oceania Allura,* from £2,272 per person.

Guests need to book by 8 September 2026.

## P&O Cruises

P&O Cruises is offering customers who have previously sailed with them 10 per cent off their next booking.

The offer is available until 3 August and can be used on sailings between 1 October 2026 and 30 March 2028.

## Royal Caribbean

Royal Caribbean regularly launches flash sales for short periods so it is worth signing up to its newsletters so you don’t miss out.

The cruise brand is currently offering up to $186 (£140) to spend on board all 2027 sailings.

## Virgin Voyages

Fans of Virgin Voyages can currently benefit from 70 per cent off a second “sailor” as well as an upgrade to a balcony and a $500 (£375) bar tab.

The deal is available on sailings until 10 April 2028. Itineraries include the cruise line’s seven-night Comedy Fest aboard *Valiant Lady*. It departs from Portsmouth on 20 August for a northern Europe round trip and prices currently start at £946 per person. Acts include Milton Jones and Lucy Beaumont.

## Viva Cruises

River cruise operator Viva Cruises is offering free cabin upgrades on selected three to four-night sailings.

The Cabin Upgrade Special is valid for bookings made between now and 15 August 2026 for depatures between January and March 2027.

Cabin upgrades will be made up to the next cabin category.
