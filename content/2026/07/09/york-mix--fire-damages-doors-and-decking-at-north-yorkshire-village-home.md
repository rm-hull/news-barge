Residents tackled a fire in their garden in a North Yorkshire village last night (Wednesday).

Fire crews from Harrogate and Thirsk were called to the incident on **Easingwold Road, Huby** at**6.06pm**.

The fire spread from decking to conservatory doors and was put out with a hosepipe before the fire service arrived.

#### Easingwold Road, Huby

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “Crews used small tools to remove sections of the decking, and a thermal imaging camera to check for fire spread and hot spots.

“They also used a positive pressure ventilation fan to ventilate the ground floor which suffered some smoke logging.”
