# Premier League champions Arsenal confirm new goalkeeper signing

- Arsenal have officially confirmed the signing of 26-year-old French goalkeeper Illan Meslier on a free transfer, following his departure from Leeds United.
- Meslier spent seven seasons at Elland Road, making 215 appearances and keeping 70 clean sheets, and played a key role in Leeds' promotion to the Premier League.
- The goalkeeper expressed his excitement to join Arsenal, stating his ambition to win trophies with the club and to develop his skills and gain more game time.
- His arrival is expected to intensify competition for the first-choice goalkeeper position, currently held by David Raya, who secured the Premier League Golden Glove award last season.
- Meslier will wear the number 30 shirt and is set to join his new teammates for pre-season training ahead of Arsenal's opening league fixture against Coventry City on 21 August.
