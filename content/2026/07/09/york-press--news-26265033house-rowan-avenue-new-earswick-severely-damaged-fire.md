## Home in York village severely damaged after fire - smoke seen for miles

A house was damaged by fire in New Earswick *(Image: North Yorkshire Weather Updates)*

A HOUSE has been severely damaged after a fire in a York village.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service says three of its crews from Huntington, Ripon and Selby were called to a property in Rowan Avenue in New Earswick last night (July 8) at 9.58pm.

They said the fire originally started in a garden, but spread to the house and hedgerow.

“Significant damage to roof and kitchen of property. Crews used numerous hose reels and jets to extinguish the fire,” said a spokesperson for the service.
