RAZORS have been stolen from a York city centre supermarket.

North Yorkshire Police have issued this CCTV image of a man that its officers would like to speak to after a theft from Sainsbury's in Bootham earlier this year.

It happened at 11.45pm on Wednesday, May 13, and saw razors being taken.
