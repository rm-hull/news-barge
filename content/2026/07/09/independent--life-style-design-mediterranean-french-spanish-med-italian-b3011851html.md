There’s nothing like the prospect of an endless summer, soaring temperatures and azure-blue skies to put you in a sunny frame of mind.

And with a Mediterranean climate on our doorstep, all the more reason to soak up indoor-outdoor living.

Think Moroccan-inspired tiles, Italian ceramics, Spanish tableware, French style linens, Sicilian lemon motifs and a gallery wall to showcase your favourite strip of coastal scenery.

Here’s how to evoke a taste of the Med and bring the riviera vibe into your living space…

**1. Lemons Linen Bread Baskets (Set of 2), £40, Sophie Allport**

![(Sophie Allport/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/09/12/08152433-ff52c5f5-3eec-474c-891d-e54668631c1a.jpg)

Naturally breathable, linen bread baskets wick away moisture, allowing your baguette or artisan loaf stay fresh and crusty. And the hand-painted lemons add a touch of summer charm to a kitchen table.

**2. Ceramic Tapas Bowls Set of Three, £24.50, Oliver Bonas**

![(Oliver Bonas/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/09/12/08152608-88c0d8e9-a425-460c-bcae-2532299ff796.jpg)

Illustrated with vine tomatoes, green peppers and sardines, these tapas bowls are *perfecto* for small bites.

**3. Florrial Blue Tiles (20cm x 20cm), £30.75 (£1.23 per tile), Topps Tiles**

![(Topps Tiles/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/09/12/08152712-92633777-dfec-4030-857d-6de2fc0ba11e.jpg)

Artfully effortless, you can’t beat the timeless beauty of mosaic tiles, especially soft blues and greys teamed with reclaimed timber, natural stone and marble. Suitable for floors and walls for a cohesive scheme.

**4. Blue Lemon (Not the White Lotus Variety) Bust Statue, £360, CASA By Josephine Jenno**

![(CASA By Josephine Jenno/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/09/12/08153104-1fdaf8cd-8a63-4736-a9e1-928da82af3f3.jpg)

A thing of beauty and a real statement piece, this hand-painted Sicilian ceramic vase with its vivid colours is ideal for bunches of wild flowers; setting the tone for relaxed, island living.

**5. Studio Collection Canvas Art Prints: Road To The Sea Canvas Print, from £34.30 (was £49), 30 x 40cm; Seaside Parasols Canvas Print, £34.30 (was £49), 30 x 40cm; unframed, rest of items from a selection, Desenio**

![(Desenio/\PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/09/12/08153259-144228dd-486f-4ec2-92df-e1eebe77dee7.jpg)

Get the look…. channel the coastal connection with a gallery wall capturing sun-soaked days on the beach, the golden hour glow in a convertible; and you’re in the driving seat for the coastal road trip of your dreams.

**6. Lemon Ceramic Striped Desk & Table Lamp, £56 (was £85), Oliver Bonas**

![(Oliver Bonas/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/09/12/08153446-d91dc1f7-3c6a-45d6-a2b4-024d0c0daa43.jpg)

With its pretty sky-blue striped shade and whimsy lemon-shaped base, this table lamp evokes thoughts of relaxed escapism with warm, accent lighting.

**7. Joelle Rattan Chair, £295, Graham And Green**

![(Graham And Green/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/09/12/08153716-36c93562-5c29-4d9c-8e80-1db5e1c19a53.jpg)

With its gentle curves, scalloped back and natural finish, this perfectly sized rattan chair is easy on the eye and captures the spirit of Mediterranean living. Style with a blue summery cotton throw, or faux fur with the switch in seasons.

**8. Palm Mosaic Small Side Plate, £12, Rockett St George**

![(Rockett St George/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/09/12/08153833-b2bdfd3b-386a-46f0-8597-e63238e0c81e.jpg)

Up your tablescape, or create a Med-style decorative plate wall… this palm mosaic can be hung individually or in a cluster.

**9. Mediterranean Stairs Poster, £10.47 (was £17.45), 30 x 40cm; oak frame, £18.70 (was £22), 30 x 40cm, Poster Store**

![(Poster Store/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/09/12/08153948-b4fc4fec-a3f5-4fa6-81e3-5ea083817ea6.jpg)

The iconic stone steps and whitewashed homes of Santorini keeps the mood relaxed, carefree and infuses some Greek charm.

**10. OHS 2-Seater Indoor/Outdoor Lemon Print Bench Pad – White/Blue, £7 (was £17), Online Home Shop**

![(Online Home Shop/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/09/12/08154322-fca94627-8589-4e49-9070-a0fe661e9649.jpg)

Introducing a colourful lemon printed bench pad brings the outdoors in – and a joyous way to give tired garden seating a new boost and refresh with its Moroccan-inspired print… long live the holiday spirit!
