                                    Harrogate Brewing Co is celebrating after one of its beers was recognised among the best in the country.

The pub on Royal Parade's Nidd Mild has been awarded Silver in the Milds category at the 2026 Champion Beer of Britain Awards, organised by the Campaign for Real Ale (CAMRA).

The prestigious competition is one of the UK's most respected beer awards, with breweries from across the country competing for national honours.

Royal Parade owners Julie and Joe Joyce marked the achievement by raising a pint outside the Harrogate venue, proudly displaying the award certificate.

One customer said:

"That Nidd Mild is a cracking pint... well deserved award."


The award-winning Nidd Mild is now available on tap at the pub on Royal Parade.
