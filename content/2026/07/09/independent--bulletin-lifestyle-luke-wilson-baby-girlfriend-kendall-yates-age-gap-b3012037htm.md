# Legally Blonde actor quietly welcomes first child at 54 with 24-year-old girlfriend

- Actor Luke Wilson and his girlfriend, Kendall Yates, have welcomed their first child together.
- The couple was seen holding their baby girl in a carrier at an event for Wilson's upcoming Netflix series, 'The Hawk', in California.
- People magazine confirmed that Wilson and Yates introduced their newborn to attendees at the industry event.
- Wilson, 54, and Yates, 24, have largely kept their relationship private since they began dating in 2023.
- Wilson had previously expressed a desire to have children, and his brothers, Owen and Andrew Wilson, also have children.
