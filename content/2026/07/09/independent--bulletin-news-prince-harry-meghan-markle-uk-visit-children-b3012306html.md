# Meghan and children could join Prince Harry on UK trip ‘after security concerns mitigated’

- Speculation is mounting that Meghan, the Duchess of Sussex, and her children, Archie and Lilibet, could join Prince Harry during his week-long visit to the UK.
- Prince Harry initially arrived in London alone, with his family reportedly pulling out due to security concerns, though these may now have been mitigated.
- Archie, seven, and Lilibet, five, last saw King Charles in person during Queen Elizabeth II’s Platinum Jubilee celebrations in 2022.
- During his trip, Harry has promoted WellChild, visiting Birmingham Children’s Hospital to mark 20 years of its specialist nursing programme.
- Meghan was set to join Harry at the Invictus Games one-year-to-go event in Birmingham, where he will participate in exhibition events.
