## Popular Crystal Ski holiday destinations

When it comes to planning a ski escape that combines effortless package holidays with standout experiences, Crystal Ski remains a top choice for Brits. Here are a few top resorts to provide some inspiration for your next adventure.

- **Obergurgl, Austria:** One of the Alps’ most snow-reliable resorts, Obergurgl is loved for its uncrowded pistes, long ski season and spa hotels. Prices typically hover between £1,600 and £2,300 per person.
- **Val Thorens, France:** Europe’s highest ski resort, Val Thorens, offers guaranteed snow and direct access to the Three Valleys’ extensive lift-linked terrain. You can expect to pay anywhere between £1,200 and £2,000 per person.
- **Selva Val Gardena, Italy:** Set in the heart of the Dolomites, Selva Val Gardena offers immaculate slopes, access to Sella Ronda and lively apres ski. Prices sit between £1,300 and £2,100 per person for a week.
- **Zermatt, Switzerland:** Sitting beneath the Matterhorn, Zermatt offers high-altitude glacier skiing and extensive international terrain that’s linked with Cervinia. Expect to pay around £1,800 to £2,800 per person for a week.
- **Bankso, Bulgaria:** One of the country’s best-known ski resorts, Bankso combines reliable winter conditions with excellent value for money, making it one of Europe's most cost-effective ski destinations. Week-long trips often come in at around £700 to £1,200 per person.

### What is the Crystal Ski Holidays snow promise?

The Crystal Ski “snow promise” applies to all package holidays (at no extra cost) that were booked on or before 30 November for travel in the same ski season. For example, you would need to have booked by 30 November 2026 for holidays between 1 December 2026 and 30 April 2027. If fewer than 10 per cent of runs are open, or fewer than two blue runs or higher-level ski runs are open, you can change your travel dates fee-free up to four days before departure.

For added flexibility, you can upgrade to “snow promise extra” for £25 per person. This allows you to change your dates if fewer than 50 per cent of runs are open, making it ideal for keen skiers or flexible travellers. Snow Promise benefits can be combined with a Crystal Ski voucher code, where available.

## About Crystal Ski

Founded in 1981, Crystal Ski Holidays is one of the UK’s largest ski and snowboard holiday providers. Now part of the Tui Group, it offers a wide range of package holidays to more than 140 ski resorts in 14 different countries, including France, Austria, Italy, Switzerland, Canada and more.

Packages typically include flights, transfers and accommodation, catering to a wide range of travellers, including beginners, families, groups and advanced skiers. ### ABTA and ATOL protection

Every Crystal Ski package that includes flights is ATOL (Air Travel Organiser's Licence) protected, while land-only packages are covered by ABTA (Association of British Travel Agents). Both give you financial protection and peace of mind when booking.
