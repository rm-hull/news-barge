A MUCH-LOVED York bakery is set to open the doors on a new location.

Bluebird Bakery, a York based family business, is set to welcome customers to its seventh site in Chapel Allerton in Leeds next Thursday (July 16).

Located at 6 Stainbeck Lane, the bakery is the latest addition to the growing brand that began 14 years ago at the York home of founders Al and Nicky Kippax and still continues to make its bread in Acomb.

Nicky said: "We are thrilled to be opening a new shop and cafe in such a vibrant, buzzy suburb. Leeds is where I grew up – and we have both lived here. It’s a special place for us.

"Our Kirkgate Market shop is lively, so we are hoping that an appetite for our products will continue into Chapel Allerton, which is full of independent businesses like ours."

  


The bakery uses local, organic flour and avoids flour improvers, saturated fats and other additives, relying instead on traditional recipes of flour, water, salt, and time.

A member of the Real Bread Campaign, Bluebird Bakery is part of a movement that promotes bread made with all-natural ingredients and without the use of any processing aids, chemical leavening or other additives.
