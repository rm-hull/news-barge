## PICTURES - Extensive damage to Malton Hospital unit following fire

THESE striking photos show the full extent of the damage caused by yesterday's (Wednesday) fire at Malton Hospital. *(Image: Mark Wilson)*

THESE striking photos show the full extent of the damage caused by yesterday's (Wednesday) fire at Malton Hospital.

Following the incident the hospital remains temporarily closed.

A spokesperson for The Tees, Esk and Wear Valleys NHS Foundation Trust (TEWV), which runs the Springwood unit, said the fire was extinguished safely.

"We would like to reassure patients and the local community that there were no injuries.

If you have an appointment at Malton Hospital today, please do not attend. Our teams are reviewing all affected appointments and will contact patients directly to rearrange appointments or discuss the next steps.

  "Malton Urgent Treatment Centre also remains temporarily closed. If you require medical advice, please visit **111.nhs.uk** or call 111. In a life-threatening emergency, always call 999.

"The GP Out of Hours service has not been affected by the incident. If you need urgent medical advice when your GP practice is closed, please continue to contact NHS 111 as usual for Out of Hours support.

  "We are continuing to work closely with North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service and specialist teams to assess the site and ensure it is safe before reopening the hospital and restoring services. Our priority is to reopen as soon as it is safe to do so.**"**
