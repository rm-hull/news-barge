## Popular pub in North Yorkshire town under new management

 *(Image: Staff)*

A COMMUNITY pub in a North Yorkshire town is under new ownership.

The Broken Bridge in Tadcaster has been acquired by Punch Pubs & Co and has reopened its doors under new management.

Long-standing publicans Mick and Julie have stepped down after more than a decade, with experienced operator Doug Smith now taking the reins.

Andrew Cannons, head of acquisitions at Punch Pubs & Co, said: "We’re delighted to have acquired this fantastic community pub, which already has a great reputation locally.

"I do not doubt that with Punch’s industry-leading training and support, and under the helm of Doug, the pub will continue to be run as the heart of the community, in Mick and Julie’s legacy, for many years to come."

Known for its home-cooked food, welcoming atmosphere, and family-friendly environment, The Broken Bridge has long been a staple of Tadcaster.

  The Broken Bridge in Tadcaster 

*(Image: Punch Pubs & Co)*

Jason Moran, operations manager at Punch Pubs, said: "Doug is a fantastic operator and has some exciting plans for the pub.

"His top priority is continuing the wonderful legacy that Mick and Julie have left behind, while ensuring the pub remains a community hub for everyone to enjoy."
