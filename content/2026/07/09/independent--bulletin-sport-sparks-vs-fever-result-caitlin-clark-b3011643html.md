# Los Angeles Sparks end losing streak with win over Indiana Fever

- The Los Angeles Sparks secured a 106-92 victory over the Indiana Fever on Wednesday, breaking a three-game losing streak.
- Nneka Ogwumike led the Sparks with 24 points and eight rebounds, supported by Rae Burrell's 22 points.
- All five Los Angeles starters reached double figures, with Dearica Hamby contributing 21 points and nine rebounds.
- Indiana's Caitlin Clark returned to the lineup after missing two games but was limited to 15:39 of play, scoring nine points.
- Kelsey Mitchell topped the scoring for the Fever with 29 points, though Indiana was without key player Aliyah Boston due to a leg injury.
