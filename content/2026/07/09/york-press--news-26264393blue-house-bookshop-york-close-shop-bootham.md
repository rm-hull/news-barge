## Much-loved bookshop to close its shop in York after seven years

The Blue House Bookshop in Bootham is closing its doors after seven years *(Image: Pexels)*

A RETAILER in York is set to begin a new chapter by closing the doors to its much-loved bookshop and launching a social enterprise.

The Blue House Bookshop opened in the historic building of 10 Bootham seven years ago.

In September it will set aside its brick-and-mortar shop to tour schools and communities with creative writing courses and storytelling performances.

  


The company website states that it seeks to become a Community Interest Company (CIC), generating income from online sales and book subscriptions.

  In September the company will set aside its brick-and-mortar shop to tour schools and communities with creative writing courses and storytelling performances 

*(Image: Newsquest)*

A spokesperson said: “Sincere thanks and love to each and everyone who has encountered The Blue House Bookshop as a browser, customer, subscriber, illustrator, author, creative course participant, each and every one of you have been and will be very important to us.

“Not everything has run smoothly. A covid shutdown within six months of our opening, then another, a flood destroying a thousand pounds worth of books.

“But then, wonderful author visits, school visits, courses, mindfulness events, Santa’s workshop, Blind Date with a Book, our St George’s Day evening, Books and Roses, one day 24-hour opening and kids running the bookshop for Independent Bookshop Day.

“It’s very sad to have to let the shop go, but as Dr Seuss would say “don’t cry because it’s over, smile because it happened.”

  'It’s very sad to have to let the shop go, but as Dr Seuss would say “don’t cry because it’s over, smile because it happened' 

*(Image: Newsquest)*

The shop will be holding Buy One Get One Free offers on a range of its books through July and August.

Blue House Books is also providing a 25 per cent discount to schools and nurseries for their first book orders, and a 20 per cent discount for follow-up orders.

A company representative said: “We are also taking bookings for our popup shop at school fairs, parent’s evenings or just as an in-school event this autumn.”
