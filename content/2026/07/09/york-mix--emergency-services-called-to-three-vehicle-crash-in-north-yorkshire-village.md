Emergency services went to a crash in a village last night (Wednesday).

It was on **Carlton Road, Carlton Miniott**, near Thirsk at** 6.14pm**.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Crews from Northallerton and Ripon responded to a three vehicle road traffic collision.

**Carlton Road, Carlton Miniott**

“All occupants of the vehicles were out on arrival of the fire service.

“Crews assisted with traffic management and making the scene safe. The incident was then left with police.”
