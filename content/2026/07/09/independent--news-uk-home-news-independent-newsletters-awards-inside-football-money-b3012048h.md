*The Independent* has won two honours at the 2026 Publisher Newsletter Awards, with*Inside Football* and*Independent Money* recognised among the UK’s best newsletters.

*Inside Football*, written by chief football writer Miguel Delaney, was named best sport newsletter, while* Independent Money*, written by business and money editor Karl Matchett, won best financial newsletter.

The judges praised *Inside Football* as “an enjoyable and authoritative newsletter which is playing an impressive role in the publisher's reader revenue strategy.”

Commenting on *Independent Money*, they added: “This newsletter is clearly connected to and cares about its readership, and has an impressive commercial and growth plan with real thought behind it.”

*Inside Football* delivers exclusive insight beyond the daily football headlines, with behind-the-scenes reporting from Miguel’s travels across the football world, agenda-setting analysis, weekly reader Q&As and insider knowledge from his extensive network of sources.

*Independent Money* helps readers make informed financial decisions through expert analysis of savings, mortgages, pensions, investing and the biggest economic stories affecting household finances.

Reacting to the win, Karl said: “It is a real honour for *Independent Money* to receive this award, especially given the strength of the competition from some genuinely fantastic finance newsletters.

“The most important people to thank are our subscribers – and, by extension, my mum and dad, who were the first people to sign up when the newsletter launched in 2024.”

![Miguel Delaney's Inside Football newsletter lands in your inbox every Friday](https://static.independent.co.uk/2025/08/04/11/34/Miguel-Delaneys-Inside-Football-3_2.png)

Organised by Media Voices, the Publisher Newsletter Awards celebrate excellence in newsletter publishing across the media industry. This year’s awards attracted hundreds of entries, with winners selected by a panel of industry judges assessing editorial quality, audience engagement, brand identity and audience growth strategies.

*The Independent* was also shortlisted in several other categories, with*Independent Women*, written by regular columnist Victoria Richards and Voices, led by deputy comment editor Paul Clements, both nominated for best individual-led newsletter – news and current affairs.

Meanwhile, *Travel Insider* was shortlisted for best individual-led newsletter – specialist, and*Well Enough*, written by fitness coach Harry Bullmore, was recognised in the best health and lifestyle newsletter category.

The recognition reflects the continued growth of *The Independent’* s newsletter portfolio, which reaches readers across news, sport, business, travel, lifestyle and culture with expert reporting, analysis and exclusive subscriber content.
