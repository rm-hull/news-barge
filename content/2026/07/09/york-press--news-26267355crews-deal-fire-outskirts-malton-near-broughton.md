## Crews deal with field fire on the outskirts of Malton - warning of smoke over A64

Crews deal with fire on outskirts of Malton near Broughton *(Image: North Yorkshire Weather Updates)*

CREWS are dealing with a field fire on the outskirts of Malton.

A number of crews are attending a fire in a field on Broughton Road.

A spokesperson for North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue said: "Please be aware of smoke in the area especially if you are travelling on the A64.

"If you can avoid the area please do so. "
