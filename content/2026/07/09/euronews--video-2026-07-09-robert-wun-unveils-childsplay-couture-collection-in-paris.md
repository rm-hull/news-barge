Known for dramatic silhouettes and theatrical designs, Robert Wun presented a collection titled 'Childsplay', inspired by the simplicity and imagination of childhood.

The designer said he wanted to explore the beauty of uncomplicated ideas through couture.

The show featured sculptural gowns, detailed tailoring and handcrafted pieces, reflecting Wun's distinctive approach to fashion.

After the presentation, the designer joined guests and celebrities including Cardi B to pose for photos in the French capital.
