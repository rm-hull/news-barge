A landmark bridge in a North Yorkshire coastal town will be closed until next week.

Whitby Swing Bridge will remain closed to vehicles until next Friday (July 17), North Yorkshire Council said.

The bridge will also close to pedestrians from 6pm on Monday until 2am on Tuesday.

A council spokesperson said the closures are needed to carry out work which requires the bridge to be opened for river traffic.

They said a shuttle bus will operate on Monday from 6pm until midnight.

The announcement follows Whitby Swing Bridge being closed over the past weeks after the bridge malfunctioned.

Work has seen the bridge checked for the effects of extreme heat and corrosive build ups, the council said.
