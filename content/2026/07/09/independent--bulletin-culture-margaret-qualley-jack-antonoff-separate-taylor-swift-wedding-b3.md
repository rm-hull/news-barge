# Margaret Qualley and Jack Antonoff reportedly calling it quits after five years together

- Actor Margaret Qualley and singer-producer Jack Antonoff are reportedly separating after five years together.
- *People* confirmed the split, with a source describing their relationship as “rocky”, though another suggested they were “figuring things out”.
- Speculation about their relationship status grew after Qualley removed photos from Instagram from their 2023 wedding and was absent from Taylor Swift's recent wedding, with Antonoff seen bringing his fashion designer sister, Rachel Antonoff, as his plus-one instead.
- Qualley has also been pictured with actor Drew Starkey, her co-star in an upcoming horror film.
- Qualley and Antonoff met in 2021, got engaged in 2022, and their star-studded wedding in August 2023 was attended by numerous celebrities.
