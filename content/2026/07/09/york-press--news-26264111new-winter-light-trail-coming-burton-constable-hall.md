## Burton Constable Hall to launch first-ever illuminated winter trail

Winterlight: The Tudor Glow is Burton Constable Hall’s first-ever illuminated winter trail *(Image: Burton Constable Hall)*

A new illuminated winter trail is set to light up Hull and East Yorkshire this festive season.

Winterlight: The Tudor Glow is Burton Constable Hall’s first-ever illuminated winter trail.

It aims to transform its historic parkland into a luminous Tudor-inspired wonderland from December 3 to 23, promising an "unforgettable winter evening" for visitors.

Alasdair Hutson, chief executive of the Burton Constable Foundation, said: "Across the UK, illuminated winter trails have become hugely popular, and we are delighted to bring our own unique version to Hull & East Yorkshire.

  The trail will transform its historic parkland into a luminous Tudor-inspired wonderland 

*(Image: Burton Constable Hall)*

"Drawing inspiration from Burton Constable's Tudor heritage, Winterlight: The Tudor Glow will transform our historic parkland with light, colour and storytelling, inviting visitors to experience Burton Constable in an entirely new way."

The immersive, after-dark trail has been designed exclusively for the Hall and pays tribute to the craftsmanship, colour, and grandeur of the Tudor and Elizabethan eras.

Visitors will follow a beautifully illuminated route through the parkland, where light, colour and projection combine to create an unforgettable winter evening.

Mr Hutson added: "For generations, Burton Constable has inspired wonder through its history, beauty and landscape.

"This winter, we hope visitors will rediscover this remarkable place in a different light, creating magical memories with family and friends and making Winterlight a new highlight of the region's festive season for many years to come."

  Alongside the trail, guests can enjoy festive food and drinks such as mince pies, mulled wine, and hot chocolate 

*(Image: Burton Constable Hall)*

Alongside the trail, guests can enjoy festive food and drinks such as mince pies, mulled wine, hot chocolate, and other seasonal treats.

The event offers timed entry from 4pm each evening.

Organisers are encouraging visitors to book early to secure their preferred date.

Winterlight: The Tudor Glow promises to become a new favourite on the region’s festive calendar, offering a magical evening out for families, couples, and friends alike.
