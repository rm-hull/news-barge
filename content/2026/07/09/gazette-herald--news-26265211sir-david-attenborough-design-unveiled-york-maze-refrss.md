York Maze has unveiled its Sir David Attenborough theme to celebrate the broadcaster’s 100th birthday.

The design has been carved into the maze at the attraction, near Elvington.

  York Maze unveils its 2026 maze, honouring David Attenborough on his 100th birthday year 

*(Image: Charley Atkins/SWNS)*

Staff who created the corn design said it was made to celebrate Sir David’s 100th birthday, which was on May 8, this year.

Alongside Sir David’s face, an elephant, tiger, and a gorilla were also etched into the 15-acre cornfield.

  York Maze unveils its 2026 maze, honouring David Attenborough on his 100th birthday year 

*(Image: Charley Atkins/SWNS)*

York Maze covers an area the size of eight Wembley football pitches and is believed to be the biggest maze in Europe and one of the largest in the world.

Creator and farmer Tom Pearcy has cut out more than 5km of pathways in his field of over a million plants.

  Sir David Attenborough 

*(Image: Charley Atkins/SWNS)*

Tom said: "I grew up watching David Attenborough, he is an icon of British broadcasting and I’m honoured to make my contribution to his 100th birthday celebrations.

“Over a million maize seeds have been planted in the 15-acre field.”

The Sir David theme follows York Maze’s Toy Story theme last year which celebrated 30 years of the much-loved animation film.
