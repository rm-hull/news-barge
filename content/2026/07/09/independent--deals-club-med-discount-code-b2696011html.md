## Helpful booking information

### Club Med guarantees

All Club Med holidays are ABTA and ATOL-protected and covered by a price guarantee, so the price you've paid won’t change.

Club Med ski holidays come with a snow guarantee. This means that if more than 50 per cent of your booked ski domain is closed for two or more consecutive days, within five days of departure, you’ll have the option to cancel or rearrange your holiday.

### Cancellation policy

Club Med’s cancellation policy has a flexible approach on selected rates and a best-rate guarantee when booking. Club Med assistance insurance provides all guests with coverage for emergency medical expenses during their stay. You can choose to upgrade to Serenity Insurance, which has trip cancellation in case of unforeseen circumstances.

Club Med doesn’t often release traditional voucher codes. Instead, most savings come from early booking discounts, last-minute deals and limited-time promotions such as flash sales or referral credits.

### Where can I apply my Club Med discount code?

Copy your code by clicking the "see code" button on your chosen deal above. **Is there a Club Med discount code for first bookings?**

You can save on your first Club Med holiday when you are referred by a friend. Through Club Med’s refer-a-friend programme, you’ll receive a £200 voucher towards your first holiday and your friend will receive 2,500 loyalty points as well as a £200 voucher.

### Can you stack Club Med discount codes?

No, Club Med only allows one offer per booking, but deals such as referral credits and seasonal promotions can be combined in most cases.

### When is the best time to book Club Med?

Booking early typically offers the best prices, with discounts of up to 15 per cent. Similarly, if you’re looking for a last-minute summer holiday, you can find deals with up to 15 per cent off on selected dates.

### Does Club Med do Black Friday deals?

Yes, Club Med runs Black Friday promotions. In November 2025, there were savings of up to £600 per person on all sun and ski holidays.
