Fire crews tackled a house blaze in a York village last night (Wednesday).

Huntington, Ripon and Selby crews went to the incident on **Rowan Avenue, New Earswick** at**9.58pm**. 

A fire which had started in a garden shed then spread to property and a hedgerow.

**Rowan Avenue, New Earswick**

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “There was significant damage to roof and kitchen of property.

“Crews used numerous hose reels and jets to extinguish the fire.”
