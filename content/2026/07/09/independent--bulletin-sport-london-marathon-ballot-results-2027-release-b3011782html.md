# Double London Marathon ballot results to be released today – everything you need to know

- Applicants for the Double TCS London Marathon are set to receive their ballot results today, London Marathon Events has confirmed.
- It comes after 1,338,544 runners entered the ballot to compete in the race, which is being hosted across the weekend of 24-25 April 2027.
- According to London Marathon Events, those who entered the ballot will be sent their results via email “over the course of today”.
- The organisers added: “If you have not received your result by 09:00 (BST) on Friday 10 July, please get in touch with us.”
- Those who have been offered a place will “need to confirm your [their] details by 16:00 BST on Friday 31 July to secure your [their] entry”
