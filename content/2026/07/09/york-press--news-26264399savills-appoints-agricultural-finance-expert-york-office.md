A real estate firm has strengthened its northern team with the appointment of an agricultural finance specialist.

Savills has announced that Rob Sanderson will join the Food and Farming team as an agricultural financial consultant.

Mr Sanderson will primarily be based at the company’s York office, bringing more than 15 years of experience in agricultural finance and business planning.

Andrew Wraith, head of food and farming at Savills, said: "Rob specialises in agricultural finance and brings a wealth of experience gained over the last 15 years working closely with UK banks on financial monitoring and business restructures.

"His strong background in agriculture and allied industries, combined with his practical farming experience, will be a real asset to our clients.

"We are very pleased to have him join our thriving York office and look forward to seeing the impact he will make across the region."

Prior to joining Savills, Mr Sanderson held a role with Barclays Corporate Banking, managing a portfolio of agricultural clients.

Mr Sanderson said: "I look forward to working with the Food & Farming team to support farm businesses across the North, drawing on my financial and farm management experience to help clients navigate both challenges and opportunities."
