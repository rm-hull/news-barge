# France vs Morocco: What time is kick-off – and how can I watch the game?

- France and Morocco are scheduled to meet in a World Cup 2026 quarter-final, a repeat of their 2022 semi-final encounter.
- The match is set to kick off at **9pm BST**(4pm local time) on Thursday 9 July in Boston.
- UK viewers can watch the game live on ITV, with coverage beginning at 8pm BST, or stream it via ITVX. Those in the US can watch the match on Fox Sports.
- Kylian Mbappe, who is still vying for the Golden Boot, will feature for France, while Morocco will be seeking revenge for their 2-0 defeat in Qatar.
- Aurelien Tchouameni remains a doubt for France, and Ismael Saibari and Chadi Riad are uncertain for Morocco. The victor will progress to face either Spain or Belgium in the semi-finals.
