A PERSON has fallen 20 metres down a cliff close to a North Yorkshire coastal town.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue said a multi-service operation began as it was called to Primrose Valley, Filey around 11pm yesterday (Friday, July 3) after the person lost their footing.


A service spokesperson said: "Fire crews assisted coastguard with setting up line rescue equipment prior to its helicopters arrival to see if they could also assist."

The helicopter winch operation was called off due to downwash from the rotary blades.

The spokesperson added: "Crews then used the line rescue equipment which had already been set up to access the casualty and winch them to safety on a stretcher.

"The casualty was taken to hospital for injuries not believed life threatening."
