# Paul McCartney and Tom Hanks spotted leaving MSG after Taylor Swift’s wedding

![](https://cdn.jwplayer.com/v2/media/rCJX4zW6/poster.jpg?width=720)

- Taylor Swift and her wedding guests may have been treated to a performance from Beatles legend Paul McCartney, who was seen leaving MSG with Hollywood pal Tom Hanks after the ceremony.
- Swift is a big McCartney fan, having interviewed him for a *Rolling Stone*cover story in 2020, and she championed his latest album, The Boys of Dungeon Lane, on Instagram.
- *The Hollywood Reporter*reported that Swift was due to perform with McCartney and her other hero and friend, Stevie Nicks.
- Swift and Hanks recently teamed up on the new Toy Story film, in which Hanks voices main character Woody, with Swift contributing the original song *I Knew It, I Knew You.*
- Other stars who were seen entering MSG included Jennifer Lopez, Ed Sheeran, Jimmy Fallon and Abby Wambach. Hugh Grant, Jason Sudeikis and Ethan Hawke also walked into the venue.
