# Watch: Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce wed in NYC - here's how the day unfolded

The pop superstar and American football player made their love story official at a star-studded celebration in New York City's Madison Square Garden.

The pair both dressed in Christian Dior, according to Swift's longtime publicist, and elected to skip bridesmaids and groomsmen. Swift's brother Austin Swift served as her man of honour while her new husband's brother, retired American footballer Jason Kelce, was the groom's best man.

Groups of fans huddled outside throughout the day, with some singing Swift tunes and donning concert merchandise. BBC reporter Floyd Cush was there for all the festivities.
