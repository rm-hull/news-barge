## Met Office forecasts warm, dry weekend for York with sunny intervals

People have enjoyed warm temperatures in recent weeks. *(Image: PA)*

The Met Office has forecast a warm and dry weekend for York, with temperatures reaching 21°C.

Saturday will start off mild at 16°C with partly cloudy skies.

Sunshine will become more frequent through the morning, with sunny intervals developing by around 11am.

Temperatures will gradually rise to 17°C by midday.

Rain is very unlikely, with a 10 per cent chance or less throughout the morning.

Winds will increase steadily from 7mph at dawn to around 11mph by lunchtime.

In the afternoon, temperatures will climb steadily from 17°C to around 20°C, and will remain completely dry with only a very low chance of rain.

Winds will become noticeably breezier, reaching 13–16mph.

The warmest part of the day will be in the evening, with temperatures peaking at 21°C.

Sunny intervals will continue into the evening, with winds remaining brisk at 16–17mph.

As daylight fades, skies will become partly cloudy.

Temperatures will slowly ease from 21°C to around 18°C by midnight.

Dry conditions will continue with almost no risk of rain.

Winds will gradually ease to around 11mph overnight.

Sunday will be another warm and dry day, although it will start off much cloudier before brightening during the afternoon.

The morning will begin mostly cloudy with temperatures around 16°C.

Cloud cover will persist through much of the morning while temperatures slowly rise to 19–20°C.

There is a slight chance of a light shower early on, but most places are expected to stay dry.

Winds will remain fairly light to moderate at 10–13mph.

Cloud will gradually break up during the early afternoon, allowing sunny intervals to develop.

Temperatures will reach a comfortable 21°C, matching Saturday's maximum, and rain chances will remain very low.

Winds will hold steady at 13–14mph.

The evening will be fine with sunny intervals gradually giving way to longer spells of cloud.

Temperatures will slowly fall from 20°C to 19°C, and winds will ease slightly to 11–13mph.
