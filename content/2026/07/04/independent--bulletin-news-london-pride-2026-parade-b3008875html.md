# Up to 1.8 million expected to attend London Pride celebrations

![](https://cdn.jwplayer.com/v2/media/w9jrb88Z/poster.jpg?width=720)

- Thousands, including the Mayor of London, marched in the Pride in London parade from midday, with up to 1.8 million people anticipated to attend the event, marking a day of celebration and activism for LGBT+ communities.
- The parade follows an iconic route from Hyde Park to Whitehall, passing landmarks such as Trafalgar Square.
- This year's event is centred on the campaign 'An Alliance of Defiance', emphasising queer people supporting each other.
- Singer MNEK is headlining the main stage in Trafalgar Square, with additional performances across a Cabaret stage and a Trans and Non-Binary Community stage.
- Attendees are advised to stay hydrated and use sunscreen due to temperatures reaching 28C, while a 'Quiet Pride' initiative offers an accessible viewing experience.
