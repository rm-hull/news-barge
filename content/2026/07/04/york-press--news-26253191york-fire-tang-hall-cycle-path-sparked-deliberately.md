A fire on a well-used cycle path linking one side of York to another is thought to have been deliberately sparked.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said a crew from the city was called to Tang Hall around 5.40pm yesterday (Friday, July 3).

  



A service spokesperson said the blaze measured nine square metres before the crew extinguished it.

The spokesperson added that police have been informed that the fire was thought to have been started deliberately.
