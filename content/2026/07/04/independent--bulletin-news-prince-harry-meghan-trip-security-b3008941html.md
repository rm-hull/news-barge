# Prince Harry to not bring Meghan and children on UK trip

![](https://cdn.jwplayer.com/v2/media/KYXF0GRv/poster.jpg?width=720)

- The Duchess of Sussex, Prince Archie, and Princess Lilibet will not accompany Prince Harry to London next week.
- This decision follows a security dispute, with a request for police protection for the family being denied.
- The outcome has reportedly dashed hopes for a reunion between King Charles and his grandchildren, whom he has not seen in four years.
- Prince Harry was said to be "desperate" for his children to see their grandfather and has been in direct contact with King Charles regarding potential meetings.
- The Duke of Sussex is currently engaged in a legal challenge against the Home Office concerning security arrangements for his family in the UK, following a downgrade in his protection in 2020.
