# Cristiano Ronaldo celebrates with fans outside Toronto hotel after World Cup win

Cristiano Ronaldo cheered along with fans from a hotel balcony in Toronto after Portugal beat Croatia in their World Cup knockout game on 2 July.

Crowds lined the streets as the squad, led by Ronaldo, waved to and celebrated with jubilant Portugal fans.

Footage shared by the Portuguese Football Federation shows supporters chanting outside the hotel before the Portugal captain steps onto his balcony, applauding and waving back to the crowd.

Ronaldo scored the first World Cup knockout-stage goal of his career, while Goncalo Ramos headed home a stoppage-time winner as Portugal beat Croatia 2-1 on Thursday to set up a last-16 clash with Spain.
