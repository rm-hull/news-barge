A THIEF has struck at a local pet shop in a North Yorkshire town, stealing £10 of stock.

North Yorkshire Police have released CCTV of a woman they said they want to speak to in connection with the incident, which took place at Linton Pet Shop in Market Place, Malton, on June 12.

  


Police said she may have information which will assist the force's investigation.

A force spokesperson said anyone with information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, ask for PC Bell and quote reference 12260107285, or email [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk)

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or visit their website.
