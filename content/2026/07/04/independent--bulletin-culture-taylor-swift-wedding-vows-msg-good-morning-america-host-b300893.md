# Taylor Swift’s MSG wedding was ‘so personal and intimate,’ says Good Morning America host

![](https://cdn.jwplayer.com/v2/media/1o9Lrq5F/poster.jpg?width=720)

- Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce held their wedding at Madison Square Garden Friday and while no photos were have been released yet despite an estimated 1,000 guests, some details about her big day are trickling out.
- Good Morning America hosts who were invited to the wedding confirmed key details including a performance by Stevie Nicks and that the couple wrote their own vows.
- “As intimate as it could possibly be given it was Madison Square Garden. Really this garden inside the garden, just so beautiful,” said George Stephanopoulos. “It’s hard to imagine a place that big and a wedding with such stars could feel so personal and so intimate.”
- The wedding attire for Swift and Kelce was designed by Jonathan Anderson for Christian Dior Haute Couture, complemented by custom-made shoes from Christian Louboutin.
- A spokesperson for Christian Dior Haute Couture confirmed that the outfits were created in Paris in close collaboration with the couple, with Swift also accessorising with Cartier jewellery.
