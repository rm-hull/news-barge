## Last chance to buy ‘luxury’ £1.45 million home in York – after 3 other mansions sell

Outside the four-bedroom home, called the Dragoon, for sale in Fulford Road, York, 1.45 million *(Image: Naish)*

Buyers have been given their last chance to buy a £1.45 million "luxury" mansion in York – after the three other properties in the new high-end estate all sold for more than £1 million.

Just one property remains for sale in the Urban Renaissance development by Mulgrave Properties, opposite the Imphal Barracks in Fulford Road.

  Inside the four-bedroom home, called the Dragoon, for sale in Fulford Road, York, 1.45 million 

*(Image: Naish)*

The four and five-bedroom detached properties have been built in the grounds of the Grade II-listed Ousefield House and form part of the development.

One four-bedroom home, called the Dragoon, is still on the market and has been built to “exceptional specification and craftsmanship”, according to Naish estate agents, which is marketing the property.

Naish’s managing director, Matthew Hendry, said there has been a “fantastic” response to the Urban Renaissance properties – despite their high price tags.

"The sales demonstrate that exceptional homes in outstanding locations continue to attract discerning buyers,” he said. “York remains one of the country's most desirable places to live, and there is clearly strong demand for beautifully designed, high-specification homes that are built to last."

He urged buyers interested in the remaining home to “arrange a viewing promptly to avoid missing the opportunity to secure a place in this exclusive development”.
