# Kimi Antonelli secures first sprint race victory at Silverstone

![](https://cdn.jwplayer.com/v2/media/fCR7t3er/poster.jpg?width=720)

- Kimi Antonelli secured his first-ever sprint race victory at the British Grand Prix, extending his lead in the drivers' championship.
- Antonelli, driving for Mercedes, overtook Ferrari's Lewis Hamilton on the Hanger straight after eight laps of racing.
- Lewis Hamilton, who started the race on pole position, was unable to launch a counter-attack due to the need to manage his battery power.
- This win gives Antonelli a 43-point advantage heading into Sunday's Grand Prix.
- The current top three in the drivers' championship are Kimi Antonelli (179 points), George Russell (136 points), and Lewis Hamilton (132 points).
