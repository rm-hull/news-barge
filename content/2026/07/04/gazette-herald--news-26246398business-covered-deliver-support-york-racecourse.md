An IT company has secured a contract to support York Racecourse.

York-based IT Covered will deliver a 12-month IT strategy and support services for York Racecourse, covering infrastructure management, cloud solutions, cyber security, technical support, and staff training.

Ross Marshall, director at IT Covered, said: "We are very happy to be working with York Racecourse.

“We have made a great start on building and implementing a 12-month IT strategy that will support the team, enable them to replace ageing equipment cost effectively, and stay on top of their Cyber Essentials with ongoing guidance and regular staff training.

"They are dedicated to providing visitors to the site an exceptional experience, which is something that we appreciate as we aim to give our customers the same high levels of service."

The partnership also includes AI strategy consultation and ongoing staff training.

Chris Ward, head of finance at York Racecourse, said: "From the first time I sat down with Ross, I have been impressed by his knowledge, skills and his drive to deliver exceptional service.

"His team is proactive, responsive and straightforward."

"We now have a comprehensive plan that is helping the business manage costs and keep our systems running smoothly.”

Mr Marshall added: “Our primary aims are to make IT one less thing for companies to worry about and make sure their IT strategy supports their wider business strategy.

"IT support isn’t about having the latest shiny gadget.

"It’s about reliability, clarity and giving people the tools and support to do their jobs without technology getting in the way.

"I’m looking forward to seeing our partnership with York Racecourse develop."

IT Covered provides strategic IT services to SMEs across Yorkshire.
