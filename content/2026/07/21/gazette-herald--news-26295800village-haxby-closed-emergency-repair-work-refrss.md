MOTORISTS are being reminded of a road closure in York.

The Village in Haxby, between its junctions with Westfield Lane and Westfield Road, for essential works until 11.59pm on Thursday (July 23).

City of York Council said signs and barriers are in place to divert road users.

  


An alternative route via York Road, the A1237, Wigginton Road, Mill Lane, Ascot Road and Winsor Drive is available.

Access for residents, businesses and emergency services will be maintained throughout the works period.

For further information please contact: [highway.regulation@york.gov.uk.](mailto:highway.regulation@york.gov.uk.)
