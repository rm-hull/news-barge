This marks their first reunion in over a decade, with the three-part programme exploring their early career rivalries and “fights, fall-outs and feuds”.

The series will delve into how these chefs revolutionised British fine dining, featuring insights from food critics, maîtres d’ and former commis chefs.

Gordon Ramsay previously worked for Marco Pierre White, and the two have had a well-documented feud, though Pierre White recently claimed they had reconciled.

An air date for The Rise Of The Rockstar Chef, which will air on BBC One and iPlayer, is yet to be announced.
