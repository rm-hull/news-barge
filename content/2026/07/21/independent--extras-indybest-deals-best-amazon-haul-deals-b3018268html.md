Amazon Haul is the budget-friendly part of Amazon that you may not have even heard of. Whether it’s beauty products, sportswear, everyday essentials or something else, this is where you’ll find affordable options – with all items priced at £20 or less.

While Amazon Haul sells mostly white-label products, a limited number of well-known brands, such as Salter, Lego and Nip & Fab, can be found there too. Before shopping, it’s also worth noting that the prices are low because Amazon sources nearly everything directly from manufacturers, which means a longer delivery time of one to two weeks.

Much like the main Amazon homepage, Amazon Haul runs flash deals and discounts. I’ve rounded up some of the best deals that are available right now.
