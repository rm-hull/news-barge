Harry Styles has cancelled his concert scheduled for Tuesday evening in São Paolo, Brazil, due to an unspecified "tour illness".

Live Nation Brazil announced the cancellation on Tuesday, just hours before Styles was due to take the stage, saying that tickets for the affected show will be fully refunded.

Fans with tickets to the cancelled Tuesday performance will be offered discounted tickets for Styles' Friday show in São Paolo, which is still planned to go ahead.

“We have worked with the event venue to release as many tickets as possible for the Friday show, but please keep in mind that availability is extremely limited,” Live Nation Brazil told fans.

This cancellation follows a previous incident during a London concert where Styles briefly collapsed on stage, though he later assured fans he was fine.
