A new mural now brightens the entrance to Malton Town Council’s office.

Created by councillor Sue Hawes and artist Lynne Cole, the artwork celebrates the town's heritage and took two years to complete, with both artists working on the piece every Sunday.

Mayor Jason Aldrich praised their dedication and said: "Sue and Lynne have given their time freely to enhance the new council office entrance hall.

"Their artwork will certainly bring cheer to those who visit the office."

Cllr Aldrich said both artists also volunteer at the Ryedale YMCA Sidings project working with young people aged 18 - 25 enabling them to enhance their self worth and confidence.

"Artwork from this project is being exhibited and sold in a national auction this September where the proceeds will be fed back into the charity at our local level.”
