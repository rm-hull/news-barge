# Mitch McConnell health questions grow as aides remain silent

- Senator Mitch McConnell was hospitalized on June 14 after a fall and also suffered from pneumonia.
- His staff has remained tight-lipped about his recovery, leading to concerns and calls for transparency from constituents and politicians.
- McConnell addressed health rumors in a letter to constituents on July 12, stating he is alive and recovering, but provided few specific details.
- Kentucky Governor Andy Beshear expressed concern over the lack of updates, even receiving calls suggesting McConnell had passed away before the senator's recent statement.
- Fellow Republican Senator Josh Hawley criticized the lack of communication, emphasizing the obligation to inform constituents and the impact on legislative voting.
