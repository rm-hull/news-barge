What do you mean *Heartstopper Forever* hasn't actually ended? Alongside the fact that the new movie is now streaming on Netflix, the streaming service has slyly announced a "companion piece" —*Heartstopper: Ending on a Hi.*

Obviously, we learned exactly what would happen to the gang once the cameras stopped rolling, and were left happy in the belief that Nick (Kit Connor) and Charlie (Joe Locke) definitely weren't breaking up, despite how much their terrible communication threatened to do just that.

So... what is actually coming out way? And when does *Heartstopper: Ending on a Hi* arrive on Netflix?

## What time can I watch Heartstopper: Ending on a Hi on Netflix?

 ![Elle, Charlie, Isaac and Tao sit on the bedroom floor eating pasta](https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/uWavTLtdEidFtZudyriYiA.jpg) 


*Heartstopper Forever* drops on Netflix on July 24, 2026.

As for the exact time, it should be the standard 12am PT release that we saw for the movie.

For global regions, here's when you need to be prepared:

- **US**– 12am PT / 3am ET
- **Canada**– 12am PT / 3am ET
- **UK**– 8am BST
- **India**– 12:30pm IST
- **Singapore**– 3pm SGT
- **Australia**– 6pm AEST
- **New Zealand**– 8pm NZDT

## What exactly is Heartstopper: Ending on a Hi?

 ![Nick and Charlie look at each other](https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/NGBD9gwqcxYf8k4CFiY8Zh.jpg) 


*Heartstopper: Ending on a Hi* is an official 35-minute behind-the-scenes documentary special.
