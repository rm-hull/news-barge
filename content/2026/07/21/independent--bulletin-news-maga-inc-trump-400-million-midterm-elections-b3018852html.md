MAGA Inc., a super PAC aligned with President Donald Trump, has amassed over $400 million for the 2026 midterm elections.

The group began the year with approximately $300 million and reported holding $382 million in cash by the end of June.

The group also received a $1 million donation from NASA Administrator Jared Isaacman and $10 million from billionaire twins Tyler and Cameron Winklevoss.

The substantial funds are expected to be used in key Senate races, such as those in Texas and North Carolina, to help Republicans defend their narrow congressional majorities.

As a super PAC, MAGA Inc. can raise unlimited sums from individuals and corporations but is legally prohibited from coordinating its spending with candidates or party committees.
