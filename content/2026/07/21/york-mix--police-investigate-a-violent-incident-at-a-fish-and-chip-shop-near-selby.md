Police are looking for witnesses after an incident at a fish and chip shop on Weeland Road in Eggborough on the morning of Saturday 11 July.

There was a violent disturbance that happened in the car park of Eggborough Fisheries.

It happened at around 9:30am and involved a number of people.

Two people have been arrested in connection with the incident and released while under investigation.

Extensive enquiries are ongoing and officers are now appealing to anyone who witnessed the incident or has any video footage, including any passing motorists who may have captured the incident on dashcam, to get in touch.
