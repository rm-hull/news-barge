2026 has been a bumper year for horror, and it's about to get even bigger when one of my anticipated new movies *Other Mommy* arrives in theaters on October 9.

*Other Mommy* follows Bela, a young girl, who gets mixed up with a mysterious force in her house that resembles her mother. Here, Jessica Chastain takes on a dual role as Bela’s mother and as “Other Mommy,” the supernatural entity that's haunting the place.

I'm already excited for this movie because it's got a stellar team behind it. Chastain stars alongside *Severance*'s Dichen Lachman and it's directed by Rob Savage, who you may know for directing the Zoom horror movie* Host*.

If you're not convinced yet, check out the first look trailer for *Other Mommy* below.

![Other Mommy | Official Trailer - YouTube](https://img.youtube.com/vi/bEpTgowZ1dI/maxresdefault.jpg) 

I'm very excited for *Other Mommy* because recent box office figures have shown that audiences love original horror which spins a unique tale of terror, instead of more reboots or remakes.

In the cover image for *Other Mommy*'s trailer**we can see recent horror hits* Obsession***and* Backrooms***referenced. It's clear just how influential they have been in championing this new wave of horror stories.

This is also a huge return to the genre for actor Jessica Chastain, after she appeared in *IT: Chapter Two* in 2019. It's great to see her back taking on a creepy new role, requiring her to play two versions of the same character.
