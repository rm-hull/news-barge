Senator Darline Graham is sworn in following her brother Lindsey Graham’s death

Darline Graham Nordone, interim senator for South Carolina, has announced her candidacy for a full six-year term to fill the seat of her late brother, Senator Lindsey Graham.

Graham Nordone was appointed to the interim position by South Carolina Governor Henry McMaster and was sworn into office last Tuesday.

She confirmed her decision to run in the upcoming special primary election during an interview with Fox News, stating, "I'm in."

Her candidacy has received the "Complete and Total Endorsement" from President Donald Trump, while Representative Nancy Mace has decided not to run for the Senate seat.
