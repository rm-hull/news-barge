A MAN has been charged with robbery, theft and possession of an offensive weapon in East Yorkshire.

Michael Meenaghan, 34, of no fixed abode, was remanded into custody to appear at Hull Magistrates Court on Tuesday (July 21).

  


It came following a theft from a shop in Chapel Street, Bridlington, on Sunday (July 19).

A Humberside Police spokesperson said: “Officers were called to reports of theft from a shop in Bridlington, and shortly after to reports of a man making threats to the public with a hammer.”
