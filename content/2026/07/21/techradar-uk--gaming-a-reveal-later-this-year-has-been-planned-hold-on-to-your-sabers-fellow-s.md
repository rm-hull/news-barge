- **Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic remake**-** will reportedly be re-revealed later this year**
- **A content creator reports that development at Saber Interactive has been "ramping up" and going "smoothly"**
- **Recent reports suggest the game could launch in 2028**

Five years after being announced, *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic remake* will reportedly be re-revealed later this year.

That's according to reliable Star Wars insider and YouTuber 100% Star Wars (via Bespin Bulletin), who addressed reports that the *KOTOR* remake could launch in 2028.

In a new video, 100% Star Wars claims that Saber Interactive sources said the game's "best-case scenario" was a mid-to-late 2027 launch to coincide with the 50th anniversary of Star Wars, but a 2028 release window would align with the original 2003 game's 25th anniversary.

"A reveal later this year has been planned," 100% Star Wars said.

The *KOTOR* remake was announced in 2021 and was originally being developed by Aspyr Media. However, development was paused for some time before the project was eventually handed over to Saber Interactive in August 2022.

When asked about the game's progress earlier this year, Saber's chief creative officer, Tim Willits, said, "Yes, it is still in development. That's all I can say."

Aside from the teaser trailer revealed five years ago, we haven't heard or seen anything else about the game since, but according to 100% Star Wars, development has been smooth sailing since Saber took up the reins.

"I know about the extremely positive development cycle since the game has been at Saber since 2023," the YouTuber said. "Development on the game has not only been ramping up, but it’s been going extremely smoothly over the past 3 years since Saber started from scratch.

"They’ve been hiring some extremely high-end talent from across the industry and Saber is very much a unified front when it comes to the development of this game with resources and talent being pulled from all over the company."

While we wait for the *KOTOR remake*, Star Wars fans can look forward to* Star Wars: Fate of the Old Republic**,***a role-playing game (RPG) from Arcnaut Studios being led by 

*KOTOR* and

*Mass Effect* trilogy director Casey Hudson.

A new turn-based single-player game, *Star Wars: Zero Company*, from Bit Reactor will also launch this year on August 27, as well as Fuse Games'* Star Wars: Galactic Racer*, which arrives on October 6.
