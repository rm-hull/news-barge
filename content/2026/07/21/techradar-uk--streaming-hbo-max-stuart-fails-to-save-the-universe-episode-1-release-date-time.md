Who'd have thought that the first big spinoff to come out of *The Big Bang Theory***(not including* Young Sheldon*and* Georgie & Mandy's First Marriage,* as they're Sheldon-adjacent) would focus on awkward comic store owner Stuart (Kevin Sussman)?

Our new heroic lead, on-off girlfriend Denise (Lauren Lapkus), as well as friends Kripke (John Ross Bowie) and Bert (Brian Posehn), are brought back together when Stuart triggers a multiverse Armageddon by accidentally breaking a device built by Sheldon (Jim Parsons) and Leonard (Johnny Galecki).

Theoretically, the new comedy show could run until the end of time. But first things first: when does *Stuart Fails to Save the Universe* episode 1 arrive on HBO Max?

## What time can I watch Stuart Fails to Save the Universe episode 1 on HBO Max?

![Stuart Fails to Save the Universe | Official Trailer | HBO Max - YouTube](https://img.youtube.com/vi/_blrWb1yZiY/maxresdefault.jpg) 

For US viewers, *Stuart Fails to Save the Universe* episode 1 will drop on**Thursday, July 23** at 6pm PT/ 9pm ET.

Internationally, you're looking out for these timings:

- **US**– 6pm PT / 9pm ET
- **Canada**– 6pm PT / 9pm ET
- **UK**– Friday, July 24 2am BST
- **India**– Friday, July 24 at 6:30am IST
- **Singapore**– Friday, July 24 at 9am SGT
- **Australia**– Friday, July 24 at 11am AEDT
- **New Zealand**– Friday, July 24 at 12pm NZDT

## When do new episodes of Stuart Fails to Save the Universe come out?

 ![The gang look at something offscreen](https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/R9u7heFGzUwBdvyMkUtYsS.jpg) 


New episodes of *Stuart Fails to Save the Universe* will make landfall every Thursday in the US and on Fridays everywhere else. Here are the all-important dates you need to know about:
