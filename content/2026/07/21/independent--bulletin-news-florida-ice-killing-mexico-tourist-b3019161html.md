According to his wife and a farmworker non-profit, Coronilla Durán was in the U.S. on a valid tourist visa and had a flight booked to return to Mexico.

The Department of Homeland Security confirmed an incident involving a Mexican national's death and stated that the Florida Highway Patrol and Homeland Security Investigations have launched a probe.

Coronilla Durán's death follows two other recent incidents in July where ICE agents fatally shot Mexican and Colombian nationals during encounters.
