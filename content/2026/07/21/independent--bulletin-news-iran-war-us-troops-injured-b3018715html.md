# US admits dozens of troops injured in Iran after claims it withheld information

- Nearly 100 U.S. service members have sustained injuries since 7 July, with the Pentagon saying that most were minor concussions and 96 per cent have returned to duty.
- The Pentagon disputed claims by the *New York Times* that it withheld the full extent of injury numbers, labelling such reports as “baseless.”
- Iran's Revolutionary Guards have claimed responsibility for targeting Amazon's data infrastructure in Bahrain, though there has been no official confirmation from Bahrain.
- Donald Trump pledged to make Iran pay “many times over” for the deaths of three American soldiers, as U.S. troops continued their tenth consecutive night of attacks.
- Iran, meanwhile, issued a statement threatening to continue missile and drone attacks on “American criminals” for several years if the conflict persists.
