# Iran claims to have struck Amazon facility in Bahrain

- Iran's Revolutionary Guards claim to have targeted infrastructure belonging to the US company Amazon in Bahrain, according to state media reports.
- There has been no official confirmation from Bahrain regarding the alleged incident.
- US President Donald Trump has vowed to make Iran pay “many times over” for the recent deaths of three American soldiers in Jordan and Iraq.
- The Iranian army, meanwhile, said it had targeted three major US military installations in Kuwait using attack drones, and vowed to continue striking American targets “for years” if required.
- Iran's Revolutionary Guard dismissed Trump's claims that their munitions were running out, saying that their missiles and drones would continue to “rain down” on American forces.
