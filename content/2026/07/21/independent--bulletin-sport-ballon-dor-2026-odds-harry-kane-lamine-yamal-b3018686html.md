# Harry Kane favourite for Ballon d’Or as odds revealed

- The sport's top individual prize, the Ballon d’Or, will be decided in London on 26 October.
- Harry Kane is currently the favourite to win, building on his strong form for Bayern Munich and becoming England's all-time top World Cup scorer.
- Lamine Yamal and Rodri, who won the award in 2024, are also strong contenders.
- Lionel Messi remains in contention after leading Argentina to a second consecutive final, while reigning winner Ousmane Dembele is a candidate after securing back-to-back Champions League titles.
- The odds are as follows: Harry Kane at 13/8, Lamine Yamal at 9/4, Lionel Messi at 11/4, Kylian Mbappe at 7/2, Rodri at 9/2 and Ousmane Dembele at 14/1.
