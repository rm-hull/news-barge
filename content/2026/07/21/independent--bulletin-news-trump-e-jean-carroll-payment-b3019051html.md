Trump Pays $5.6M In Abuse and Defamation Case To E. Jean Carroll

President Donald Trump is attempting to force E. Jean Carroll to return $5.6 million he was ordered to pay her in a sexual abuse and defamation case.

The payment was disbursed to Carroll from a court-controlled escrow account on July 9, following a federal jury's verdict.

Trump's legal team is seeking an emergency injunction, hoping the Supreme Court will reverse the judgment and return the funds to him.

Carroll's lawyers argue the judgment is final, the money has been disbursed and Trump's repeated defamatory attacks continue to subject her to harassment and threats.

This legal maneuver follows a seven-year battle, with Trump also facing an additional $83 million defamation judgment and seeking government intervention for immunity claims.
