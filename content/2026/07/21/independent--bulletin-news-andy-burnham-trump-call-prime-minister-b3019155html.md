Andy Burnham is Prime Minister. Now what?

Andy Burnham's initial 20-minute phone call with Donald Trump was described as "extremely warm" by Downing Street.

The US leader offered an early endorsement of Mr Burnham, stating he has a "big job ahead" and pledging US support for the UK.

The call signals a fresh start for the transatlantic partnership, with Mr Burnham confirming the continuation of UK bases for US defensive strikes against Iran.

Newly appointed Foreign Secretary Ed Miliband is expected to hold his first bilateral talks with US counterpart Marco Rubio during the upcoming ASEAN summit.

Downing Street sidestepped questions regarding Mr Burnham's stance on North Sea drilling, a policy championed by the US leader, despite Labour's manifesto opposing new licences.
