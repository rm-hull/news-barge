Morgan Rogers has completed his transfer to Chelsea from Aston Villa for an estimated £117 million, signing a seven-year contract to become the club's new record signing.

The 23-year-old's move to Stamford Bridge was finalised on Saturday, with Chelsea securing his signature despite strong interest from Arsenal.

A key factor in Rogers' decision was the opportunity to work with new Chelsea manager Xabi Alonso, following his recent contribution to England's third-place finish at the World Cup.

Rogers, a versatile forward, enjoyed a rapid rise at Villa Park, helping the club achieve a top-four finish and qualify for the Europa League last season.

This deal signifies a shift in Chelsea's transfer policy, moving beyond exclusively signing young, untested players, and is the fourth summer signing for the Blues.
