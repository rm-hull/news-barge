AN extensive police hunt has begun to find a man who has failed to comply with his sex offender notification requirements.

Lee Haslam, 41, has links to Scarborough, Huddersfield, Salford, Waterfoot and North London.

  


He is 5ft 6 inches, with shoulder-length ginger hair, a moustache and a beard.

He is known to use the aliases 'Manny Lee' and 'Tight'.

For sightings of Haslam, please call 101 or email [mosovoeast@lancashire.police.uk](mailto:mosovoeast@lancashire.police.uk), quoting log 290 of July 13.
