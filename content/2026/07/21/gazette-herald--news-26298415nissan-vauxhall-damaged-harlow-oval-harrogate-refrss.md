VANDALS have struck in a North Yorkshire town, damaging two cars.

The county's police force say the incident involved a grey Nissan and a white Vauxhall parked at Kilburn Court on Harlow Oval in Harrogate, with damage believed to have occurred overnight between July 16 and 17, 2026.

Police are urging anyone who witnessed the incident or has relevant information to come forward.

  


A spokesman for North Yorkshire Police said: "If anyone witnessed the damage being caused, has CCTV or was driving and has Dashcam footage or information that could assist our investigation, please contact us online in the first instance.

"This can be done by emailing [ben.robinson-brockhill@northyorkshirepolice.uk](mailto:ben.robinson-brockhill@northyorkshirepolice.uk), or reporting it online via our website.

"Live chat is also available via our website, Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.

"If you are unable to use these options, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

"Please quote reference 12260136226 when providing information."
