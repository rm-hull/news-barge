Malton, Norton and District Lions Club has awarded its annual grants to twelve lucky local charities in its annual Community Awards scheme.

The grants of up to £600 have been distributed to a wide variety of organisations covering all age groups, including the Ryedale Warriors Basketball Club, the Ryedale Leisure Club and Community Counselling (North Yorkshire), to contribute to and fund small locally based projects.

Other successful groups included Kirkham Henry Performing Arts, to update their music editing computer system, Amotherby and Swinton Football Club to carry out repairs to their pitch and Norton County Primary School to buy books for the school library, alongside Ryedale Community Foodbank to provide additional ‘summer packs’ to help feed local children during the school summer holidays.

A grant also went to Swinton Parish Council towards the cost of replacing equipment in the village play park following recent vandalism.

Attending one of his last duties before handing over the baton to a new pair of hands, Lion President 2025 – 26, Nick Simpson, said: “Our awards are very popular and as always we had some difficult decisions to make, but we are absolutely delighted to award grants to some excellent and hard working organisations from Malton, Norton and the surrounding villages.

“This year we were particularly pleased to receive such a variety of applications from so many different groups and organisations - it was great to learn more about the diverse activities that are happening right here on our doorstop in Ryedale.

“We aim to support local charities and groups who may be finding it difficult to source grants in these challenging economic times. We want to provide grants to help projects that these smaller charities and organisations really want to deliver locally and just need a modest grant to support their work.”

Grants were awarded to: Ryedale YMCA, Kirkham Henry Performing Arts, Community Counselling (Norton Yorkshire),The Ryedale Community Foodbank, Ryedale Warriors Basketball Club, Amotherby & Swinton Football Club, Malton Museum, Norton Primary School, Malton Police Cadets, Ryedale Leisure Club, The Milton Rooms and Swinton Parish Council.
