It’s beginning to look a lot like Christmas—just with sand between your toes instead of snow.

The countdown is on, with Bridmas set to return to the East Yorkshire coast on Wednesday, (August 5).

It will bring early festive cheer to Bridlington South Beach for its third year with a full day of Christmas-themed fun for all the family.

  


The summer event combines the best of Christmas and seaside holidays, with carols, crafts, competitions, and even a grotto by the sea.

Bridmas also marks the launch of seasonal offers for December events in the town, with exclusive discounts available only on the day.

A spokesperson for East Riding of Yorkshire Council said: "Jingle bells and sea shells.

"Put on your shorts and don your ugly sweaters, it's that time of year for carolling and ice-creams at Bridlington South Beach."

Activities will take place along the South Beach and promenade, as well as at other locations across Bridlington.

Entertainment and activities will run throughout the day, with something for all ages.

At Bridlington Spa, families can enjoy a free screening of The Grinch at 1.30pm.

On the beach, the Coastal Voices community choir will perform Christmas songs at 10.30am, 11.30am, and 12.30pm.

A festive face painter will be available from 10am to 11.30am and again from 1.30pm to 2pm, with charges applying for designs.

Other highlights include a Christmas tree and snow angel stilt walker from 10am to 1pm, an Elf and Bubble Show starting at 10am, and Santa’s Grotto, which will be open during set times throughout the day.

There will also be drop-in train sessions, Christmas crafts, and games.

The promenade will be lively from the start of the day, with organ music at 9.30am and 11am, and a performance by the Driffield Brass Band from 12pm to 2pm.

Learn2Ride bicycle sessions for children will take place in three slots throughout the day, but booking is essential.

In the town centre, the King Street Christmas Market will run from 8am to 2pm, and Salt on the Harbour will be decorated for the occasion.

There’s also a Christmas Cracker Trail for families, with a prize for those who successfully find all the hidden crackers.

Sewerby Hall and Gardens will also join in the fun (standard admission applies), with the MR Bubbles Christmas Show at 11am and 12.30pm, and a giant bubble display at 2pm, weather permitting.

Full details on all events and booking information are available at www.bridspa.com.
