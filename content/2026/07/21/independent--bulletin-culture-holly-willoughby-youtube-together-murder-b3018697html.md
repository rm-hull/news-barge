Holly Willoughby has spoken about the plot to kidnap, rape, and murder her, saying that the experience put “everything in perspective”.

Gavin Plumb was sentenced to life with a minimum term of 16 years last July for soliciting murder and encouraging others to rape and kidnap the presenter.

Willoughby, 45, is launching her new YouTube channel, Together, on 28 July, after leaving This Morning in October 2023.

She said that the ordeal taught her “what real fear feels like” and has motivated her to pursue new ventures without fear of public opinion.

She said she had learned she is “very resilient” as a result of the ordeal, which she said “doesn’t define you”.
