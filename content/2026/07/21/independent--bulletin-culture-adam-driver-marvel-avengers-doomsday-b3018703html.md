Marvel studio boss Kevin Feige revealed that actor Adam Driver has turned down offers to star in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), making it a "tradition" for him to pass on roles.

Driver, known for his roles in Girls and Star Wars, has been a long-standing target for Feige.

Speaking on the Happy Sad Confused podcast, Feige said: “It's no secret that we'd like to [work with Adam], but it's a tradition to Zoom with him and then he passes.”

The upcoming Marvel film, Avengers: Doomsday, will feature a vast ensemble cast including Chris Hemsworth, Chris Evans, and Robert Downey Jr. as the new villain Doctor Doom.

Avengers: Doomsday is scheduled for release on 18 December.
