The BBC has begun announcing the celebrity line-up for Strictly Come Dancing 2026, with seven contestants officially confirmed to date.

EastEndersstar Lacey Turner was the first to be revealed, followed by Dani Dyer, who is returning after withdrawing from last year's series due to injury.

Australian singer Delta Goodrem, celebrity hairstylist Chris Appleton, and TV presenter Will Best are also confirmed to be taking part in the dance competition.

Love Island's Cach Mercer has also been announced, alongside Paralympian Dame Sarah Storey.

Rumoured contestants include actors Jaime Winstone and Jake Quickenden, former footballer Shaun Wright-Phillips, and ex-referee Mark Clattenburg, with Emma Willis, Josh Widdicombe, and Johannes Radebe joining as new presenters.
