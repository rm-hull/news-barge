# Voyeur caught on video filming women through windows at night

![Anthony Durkan, who has admitted filming women through their windows in the early hours of the morning](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/21/17/f7a7803b0b23ff216ef7a7fc027d42b5Y29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzg0NzM2MTAz-2.85374783.jpg)

- Anthony Durkan, a 36-year-old father-of-two from Islington, north London, pleaded guilty to a total of 26 charges of voyeurism at Highbury Corner Magistrates’ Court.
- The charges include 19 guilty pleas on Tuesday, following an earlier seven pleas on March 26, stemming from incidents where he filmed women through their windows while dressed in black.
- Metropolitan Police released video footage showing Durkan approaching houses on tiptoes and using his phone to film inside, alongside body-worn camera footage of his arrest on March 25.
- During a police interview, officers discovered a hidden folder on Durkan's phone containing 162 videos and 99 photos, almost all depicting unaware women being filmed.
- The Metropolitan Police are appealing for women in Islington and north London who may have noticed unusual activity around their homes, particularly late at night, between January 2020 and March 2026, to come forward as they work to identify additional victims.
