Maryland has launched a cookie sandwich featuring a popular York chocolate brand.

The new Maryland S’wich combines the brand’s signature chocolate chip cookie with the flavour of Terry’s Chocolate Orange, creating what fans on social media have called “these feel like game changers”.

Scott Perry, chief marketing officer at Fox’s Burtons Companies, said: "This is undoubtedly one of the most exciting biscuit collaborations of the year.

"Following the hugely successful launch of Maryland S'wich last year, we're excited to bring fans an entirely new flavour experience with Terry's Chocolate Orange.

"It's a collaboration that brings together two iconic British brands and delivers a delicious combination of chocolate, orange and cookie in every bite."

This marks the first time Terry’s Chocolate Orange flavour has appeared in a biscuit.

The product is already flying off shelves at Tesco stores nationwide.

One food reviewer on Instagram commented: "A pack of these with a cuppa tea = unreal."

The Terry’s Chocolate Orange S’wich is the first new flavour to join Maryland’s S’wich range since its launch last year.

Maryland S’wich Terry’s Chocolate Orange will be available at other major retailers from 12th August with an MRSP of £1.50 for a limited time only.
