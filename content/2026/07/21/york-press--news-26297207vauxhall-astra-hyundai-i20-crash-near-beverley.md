AN 85-year-old woman has been taken to hospital after a two-vehicle crash in East Yorkshire.

The incident involved a black Vauxhall Astra and a black Hyundai i20, and happened just after the crossroads in Killingwoldgraves Lane with Newbald Road and Walkington Heads, near Beverley at around 2.35pm on Friday (July 10).

  


The woman was a passenger in the Hyundai and was taken to hospital with injuries which are not thought to be life-threatening at this time.

A force spokesperson said: “Since the collision, enquiries have been conducted to understand the full circumstances of the collision, including speaking to witnesses.

“Officers are asking for anyone who may have been in the area around the time of the collision, who has dashcam footage or witnessed the collision to contact us on our non-emergency number 101 quoting log 332 of 10 July.”
