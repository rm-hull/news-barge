# Country concert from hell as family claims fan urinated on them

![Morgan Wallen addressed the Coldplay 'kiss cam' scandal at his latest concert](https://static.independent.co.uk/2025/07/21/13/29/MixCollage-21-Jul-2025-09-10-AM-1847.jpg)

- A fan named Troy Grevelding alleges he and his family, including an 8-year-old niece, were urinated on by another concertgoer at a Morgan Wallen concert in Baltimore.
- The incident reportedly took place on Saturday, July 18, at M&T Bank Stadium during the country superstar's performance.
- Grevelding claims the man was ejected by concert security, but Maryland State Police initially told him they could not make an arrest as they had not witnessed the act.
- A video circulating on social media shows Grevelding confronting officers about their inability to take the alleged assailant into custody.
- Maryland Transportation Authority Police have confirmed they are aware of the incident and the video, stating that an active criminal investigation is underway.
