# Chinese nationals among injured in Ukraine’s drone attack on Moscow

- At least three Chinese nationals were wounded in a major Ukrainian drone attack on Moscow, which involved around 400 drones over Sunday night and Monday morning.
- Four Indian nationals were killed and another critically injured after Russia hit the Guinea-Bissau-flagged ship Golden Leo with three cruise missiles near Odesa.
- New British Prime Minister Andy Burnham spoke with Ukraine's Volodymyr Zelensky and Donald Trump, pledging Britain's unwavering support for Ukraine.
- The International Monetary Fund (IMF) approved access to about $690m for Ukraine, bringing total disbursements under the programme to about $2.2bn.
- Thousands of Ukrainians have protested against the removal of defence minister Mykhailo Fedorov and demanded the dismissal of Commander-in-Chief Oleksandr Syrskyi, exposing deep divisions in the defence leadership.
