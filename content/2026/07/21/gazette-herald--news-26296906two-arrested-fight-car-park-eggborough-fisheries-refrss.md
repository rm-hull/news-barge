TWO people have been arrested after a fight broke out in the car park of a fish and chip shop in North Yorkshire.

The incident took place in the car park of Eggborough Fisheries, in Weeland Road, on Saturday (July 11) at around 9.30am and involved multiple individuals.

North Yorkshire Police are continuing their investigation and are appealing for information and video footage from the public, including dashcam recordings from passing motorists.

  


A police spokesperson said: "Extensive enquiries are ongoing and officers are now appealing to anyone who witnessed the incident or has any video footage, including any passing motorists who may have captured the incident on dashcam, to get in touch if they have not already spoken to the police."

The two individuals have been released under investigation.

Members of the public with information can contact police online or call 101, quoting reference 12260131194.
