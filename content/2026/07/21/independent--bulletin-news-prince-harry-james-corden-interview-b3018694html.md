# Duke of Sussex names UK’s most famous Harry – and it’s not himself

- The Duke of Sussex appeared on *Fifa World Cup on Fox After Hours with**James Corden*, where he ranked famous Harrys.
- He placed pop superstar Harry Styles first, England captain Harry Kane second, and himself joint last with fictional character Harry Potter.
- The Duke also reflected on England's semi-final defeat to Argentina, adding: “We’re one-nil up against Argentina, and then there was this immediate temptation to fall into defence. I don’t think it was a tactic; I don’t think that was the plan. It just happened.”
- Separately, after his appearance on the show, he welcomed the announcement that Daejeon, South Korea, will host the 2029 Invictus Games.
- He said that South Korea is the first Asian nation to host the event, marking “an exciting new chapter”.
