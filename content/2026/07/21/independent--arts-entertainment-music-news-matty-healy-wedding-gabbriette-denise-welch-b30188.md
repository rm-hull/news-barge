Television personality Denise Welch has shared her joy following her son Matty Healy's wedding, describing the celebrations as the "most magical 3 days of my life".

The 1975 frontman, 37, exchanged vows with American model Gabbriette, 28, in Los Angeles on Saturday.

Welch, a familiar face from soaps like *Coronation Street*,* Waterloo Road*, and* Hollyoaks*, and a panellist on* Loose Women*, offered glimpses into the festivities via Instagram. 

Alongside photos and videos featuring friends, including her former *Coronation Street* co-star Sally Dynevor, she captioned a post: "This weekend was the most magical 3 days of my life!!! I can’t wait to share it with you!" 

She added: "All the aunties together in LA."

At the weekend, a statement from the couple’s representatives said: “Matty Healy and Gabbriette Bechtel were married yesterday at a private ceremony in Los Angeles, attended by their family and close friends.”

![Welch, best known for her roles in soaps including Coronation Street, Waterloo Road and Hollyoaks, and as a Loose Women panellist, gave some details of her son's wedding](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/21/15/01KWKZNAGH1GVBMB4C80VW0X73.jpg)

The couple were first linked months after he split from Taylor Swift, whom he dated for a few months in 2023.

Gabbriette sparked rumours of their engagement after posting a photo of her wearing a large black ring on her ring finger on Instagram in June 2024.

She wrote: “Marrying The 1975 is very brat.” Healy, who was tagged in the post, also re-posted the photo on his own story.

The next day Welch confirmed the news on*Loose Women* and said: “I obviously have known for a few weeks that Matty got engaged.

“They went over to see their friends Charli (XCX) and George (Daniel) in New York…and I woke up this morning to the fact they’ve put it on Instagram.”

She added: “I couldn’t be more thrilled. We couldn’t be happier – she is everything I would want in a daughter-in-law. I’m thrilled.”

![Healy, whose band headlined Glastonbury in 2025, is also said to have performed a set with his fellow musicians at the wedding reception](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/20/11/01KXXERW3MC2ANSPB82C9PMQB1.jpg)

Last month Welch said on the Tom’s Hirschsprung’s Podcast: “Matty gets married in July, which we’re very excited about.”

Healy celebrated his impending nuptials with a stag do that involved performing with The 1975 tribute band, with videos circulating online of the frontman singing on stage with the band he hired for the party.

Gabbriette also posted a string of snaps from her Las Vegas hen do including her being handcuffed and carried on to a plane by a man dressed as a police officer. She wore a vintage white Chanel tweed skirt suit with matching bag and heels.

Gabbriette is lead vocalist and songwriter of the punk rock band Nasty Cherry, which was formed by Charli XCX, whose husband George Daniel is The 1975’s drummer.
