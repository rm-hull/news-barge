Three people have appeared at court facing a series of charges following incidents in Scarborough on Sunday night.

Police were called to three reports of criminal damage in the town.

The first, at **11.18pm**, was at a property on** Grange Avenue, Filey**.

Another property was then damaged at **West Street** in**Scarborough**.

The third took place on **Hoxton Road** in**Scarborough** where a car rammed into the house. 

No one was hurt in the incidents, which police described as “linked and targeted”.

After a police investigation, three people from Hartlepool appeared in court today (Tuesday).

- **Damian Hadfield**, 29 – charged with conspiracy to blackmail, conspiracy to damage or destroy property, possessing a knife in a public place

- **Suzanne Allen**, 44 – charged with conspiracy to blackmail, conspiracy to damage or destroy property, possessing a knife in a public place, and dangerous driving

- A **17-year-old boy**– charged with conspiracy to blackmail, conspiracy to damage or destroy property, possessing a knife in a public place.

The three defendants are due to appear at Teesside Crown Court on 18 August.

A 51-year-old woman who was also arrested remains in police custody for questioning.
