A marmoset, named "Oyster", was discovered abandoned in a laundry bag on a London bus.

The RSPCA rescued Oyster, who has since been seen by a specialist vet and moved to a primate sanctuary.

The RSPCA warns that this abandonment could be the first of many following new Animal Welfare Regulations in England.

Since April, new legislation requires all primate pet owners in England to obtain a license.

Concerns have been raised by the RSPCA and Born Free about the welfare of an estimated 5,000 pet primates, due to low uptake of the new licensing system.
