# Jhon Duran: Ex-Aston Villa striker signs for seventh club of his career aged 22

![Jhon Duran playing for Aston Villa](https://static.independent.co.uk/2024/10/22/23/0a8ce92af047dfdc45a0c691e32cb046Y29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzI5NzIyMjYy-2.77918958.jpg)

- Former Aston Villa striker Jhon Duran has joined Benfica on a season-long loan deal from Al-Nassr.
- The 22-year-old Colombia international's loan for the 2026-27 season cost Benfica €6.1m, approximately £5.2m.
- Benfica has negotiated a €30m option to buy, which would make the move permanent for around £25.5m.
- Duran previously moved to Al-Nassr from Aston Villa in January 2025 for £64m – with Benfica becoming the seventh club of his young career.
- He expressed high expectations for his time at Benfica, aiming to play well and become a champion.
