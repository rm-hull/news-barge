Holly Willoughby is launching a new YouTube show, 'Holly Willoughby Together', marking her return to screens almost three years after her departure from 'This Morning'.

The programme, produced in partnership with her husband Dan Baldwin's company, features five distinct strands including 'Talk Together', 'Cook Together', and 'Hang Out Together' with celebrity guests.

Willoughby aims for a more 'normal' and relatable approach on her new digital platform, moving away from traditional broadcasters and seeking to 'unlearn all the telly stuff'.

Her exit from 'This Morning' followed a tumultuous period, including the Phillip Schofield scandal and a kidnap plot against her, for which a man was jailed for life in July 2024.

Willoughby recently told Vogue UK that these traumatic events have given her perspective, making her less concerned about public opinion and more focused on connecting with an interested audience on her own terms.
