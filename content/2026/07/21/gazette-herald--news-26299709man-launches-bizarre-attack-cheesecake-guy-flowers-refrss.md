A MAN has launched a bizarre attack on a flower display outside a shop in Shambles in York.

The moment, caught on CCTV, shows the vandal checking for onlookers before ripping out neat rows of flowers from a planter box outside The Cheesecake Guy at around 8pm on Monday (July 20).

The video shows him throwing them on the floor.

  


A representative from the company said that the flowers had only been planted outside the shop on Saturday evening.

  The attack happened outside The Cheesecake Guy in Shambles, York 

*(Image: Newsquest)*

She said: “Everything looked great on Saturday evening.

“The planter detaches and we made a point of remembering to take it down at night and bringing it in for this reason.”

The owner of the shop said in a post on social media that he wanted to speak with the culprit after the flowerpot had been destroyed for “no reason whatsoever”.

The planter has since been replenished.

The CCTV was sent to The Cheesecake Guy by The Kings Inn – a pub opposite the shop in Shambles.

The pub’s clear footage has been used to help authorities capture perpetrators in more than 15 crimes along Shambles in the last 18 months.
