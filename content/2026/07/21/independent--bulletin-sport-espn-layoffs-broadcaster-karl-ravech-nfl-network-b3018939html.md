# In ESPN layoffs related to NFL Network deal, 30-year veteran broadcaster gets booted

- Longtime ESPN broadcaster Karl Ravech is expected to be among the latest layoffs at the network, sources told *The Athletic* Tuesday.
- Ravech, a 31-year ESPN veteran, has been a prominent figure, serving as the lead baseball play-by-play announcer, covering college basketball, and anchoring SportsCenter.
- ESPN Chairman Jimmy Pitaro's memo to employees indicated that most job cuts are related to integrating acquired NFL assets, but other parts of the company are also impacted.
- The company stated its commitment to treating affected employees with compassion and respect as they navigate this transition.
- These layoffs follow ESPN's recent decision to part ways with NFL analyst Ryan Clark, and additional cuts are anticipated, particularly within NFL Network.
