A five-bedroom semi-detached house with an attic space has gone on the market for £535,000.

The property in Almsford Drive, Acomb, is spread over three floors and has been described by the estate agent Bishops Personal Agents as a substantial family home.

The ground floor has an entrance porch and hallway, a living room with a bay window and fireplace, a sunroom, a utility room, and a cloakroom.

  Living room with bay window and fireplace 

*(Image: Bishops Personal Agents)*

There is also an open-plan kitchen and dining area which has skylights, French doors leading out into the garden, and an island with a breakfast bar.

The kitchen is fitted with wall and base units, work preparation surfaces, and an inset ceramic drainer sink.

It also has an integral range cooker, extractor hood, dishwasher, and fridge, as well as space for an American-style fridge.

  West-facing rear garden with lawn and patio areas 

*(Image: Bishops Personal Agents)*

The first floor has four bedrooms, with the principal bedroom having a dressing area with built-in wardrobes and an en-suite shower room.

The single bedroom has been described as being perfect for an at-home office space.

There is also a family bathroom with a bath and shower head, a wash hand basin, and a low-level toilet.

The fifth bedroom is on the second floor and is within a converted attic space with storage cupboards and skylights.

The rear of the property has a west-facing garden with a lawn and patio areas.

  Open-plan kitchen and dining area with island, skylights and French doors 

*(Image: Bishops Personal Agents)*

There are also outbuildings which are currently used as storage and a covered garden room.

The front of the house has a block-paved driveway with ample parking space.

The house is in a sought-after location, according to the estate agent, with access to nearby shops and schools, including Manor C of E school.

There is also easy access into York city centre and the outer ring road, making it perfect for commuters.

  Outbuilding currently used for storage 

*(Image: Bishops Personal Agents)*

Bishops Personal Agents said an early internal inspection is highly recommended to fully appreciate this fabulous home.

The semi-detached house is freehold and has an energy performance certificate (EPC) rating of D.
