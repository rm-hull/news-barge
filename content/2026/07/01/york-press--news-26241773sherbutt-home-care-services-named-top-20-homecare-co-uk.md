A care provider in Pocklington has been named among the best in the region.

Sherbutt Home Care Services was awarded a spot in the top 20 home care providers in Yorkshire and the Humber by homecare.co.uk as part of its annual Top 20 Home Care Awards.

The awards celebrate the highest-rated providers across the UK based on independent reviews.

Darren Woodhead, finance director of Sherbutt Home Care Services, said: "Receiving the Homecare.co.uk Top 20 Home Care Provider Award is a tremendous honour for everyone at Sherbutt Home Care Services Ltd.

"This recognition reflects the dedication, compassion, and professionalism that our team demonstrates every single day in supporting people to live independently and with dignity in their own homes.

"Knowing that those we support have taken the time to share their positive experiences is incredibly rewarding and reinforces our commitment to delivering the highest standards of care."

The reviews are submitted by individuals who use home care services and their families.

Sherbutt Home Care Services was recognised for its commitment to delivering person-centred care that enables people to stay in their own homes for as long as possible, supporting their independence and dignity.

Amanda Hopkins, reviews manager at homecare.co.uk, said: "High-quality home care plays a vital role in enabling people to remain independent and continue living in their own homes for as long as possible.

"We would like to congratulate Sherbutt Home Care Services on being recognised as one of Yorkshire & The Humber's Top 20 Home Care Providers for 2026."
