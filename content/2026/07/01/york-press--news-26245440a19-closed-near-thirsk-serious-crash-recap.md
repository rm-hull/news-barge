## Our live feed has now finished.

- One person has been taken to hospital after a serious crash on the A19 near Thirsk.
- North Yorkshire Police is on the scene close to Great Thirkleby on Wednesday afternoon (July 1).
- Yorkshire Air Ambulance says its crews - including two air ambulances - were called to the scene shortly after 2.15pm today and one person has been taken to hospital.
- Police say the road is closed in both directions and is expected to remain closed for "some time while emergency services respond and enquiries are carried out".
- Drivers are being urged to avoid the area.
