Four women have taken up new leadership roles with regional bus operator Transdev.

The new leaders include Kat Ayre as operations manager at Yorkshire Coastliner, Victoria Giannini as tourism manager at CitySightseeing York, Dawn Thompson-Cullen as operations supervisor at Harrogate Bus Company, and Lucy Harris as central stores supervisor.

Ms Ayre brings almost 22 years of industry experience, starting her career as a driver.

She said: "People are the main part of our business; we need colleagues to work together in a collaborative and positive environment so that we can provide the best service possible to customers."

Ms Giannini, based in York, leads the CitySightseeing operation, added: "We have a fantastic team here at CitySightseeing who serve as ambassadors for everything that York offers to visitors and for me personally, it’s been really interesting already."

Ms Harris now oversees Transdev’s central stores network across Lancashire and Yorkshire.

Claire Swann, head of people at Transdev, said: "We're delighted to welcome Kat and Victoria to our management team, while also seeing Dawn and Lucy step into more senior roles within the business.

"These appointments reflect both our commitment to bringing in new talent and our focus on developing people from within."
