A THIEF has targeted a shop in one of York's most visited and photographed streets.

North Yorkshire Police said £14.99 of stock was stolen from a shop in Shambles on Monday, June 8.

The force has released a CCTV image of a woman they wish to speak to, saying she may have information which will assist its investigation.

  


Members of the public who recognise her are asked to contact North Yorkshire Police.

Information can be emailed to [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk) or by calling 101 and asking for PC 1488 Bell, the force said.
