TWO people have been trapped in an outbuilding in a coastal North Yorkshire town.

The county's fire and rescue service said a crew from Scarborough was called to Ash Grove in the town around 12.30 this morning.

  



A service spokesperson said the outbuilding's locking mechanisms failed and crews were able to release both people unharmed.
