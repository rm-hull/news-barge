Yorkshire Water has announced a new round of online events for customers to share their views and questions directly with company leaders.

The water supplier will host two 'Your Yorkshire Water, Your Say' online customer engagement events on Thursday, July 9, and Tuesday, July 14, both from 6pm to 7.30pm.

The sessions will offer the public a chance to speak directly with CEO Nicola Shaw and members of the senior leadership team about the company’s performance, investment plans, and to share questions and feedback.

Matt Pinder, Director of Customer Distribution & Collection at Yorkshire Water, said: "We’re pleased to announce our third set of 'Your Yorkshire Water, Your Voice' online sessions as part of our ongoing commitment to have an open conversation with our customers.

"The sessions we held last year were invaluable in providing a space for customers to ask questions directly to our senior team.

"This dialogue helps us to get closer to the people we serve, to listen to their concerns and understand their priorities for improvement."

The events will be hosted by an independent third party, DJS Research Ltd.

Almost 100 people took part in similar sessions held in November last year.

Questions can be submitted in advance during registration at: [https://www.yorkshirewater.com/about-us/get-involved/your-yorkshire-water-your-say/](https://www.yorkshirewater.com/about-us/get-involved/your-yorkshire-water-your-say/)
