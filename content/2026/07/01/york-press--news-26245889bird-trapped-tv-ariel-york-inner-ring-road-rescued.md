A bird trapped in a television aerial on top of a property in York has been rescued.

Firefighters were called to the scene on the York Inner Ring Road shortly after 10am on Wednesday (July 1).

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said its crew used a nine-metre long ladder and a roof ladder to remove the bird so it could be taken to a vet.

In a statement, the fire service said: “York rescued a bird trapped in the TV aerial on top of a property.

“Crews used a nine-metre ladder and roof ladder to remove the bird safely and left them in the hands of a responsible person who will take it to a local vet.”
