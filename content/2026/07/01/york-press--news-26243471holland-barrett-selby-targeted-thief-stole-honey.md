A THIEF has targeted a national health food chain in a North Yorkshire town.

North Yorkshire Police said manuka honey was stolen from Holland & Barrett in Market Lane, Selby, on Saturday, April 25.

  


The force released a CCTV image of a man the force said may have information which will assist its investigation.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: "Please contact us if you recognise the person pictured.

"Email [scott.nixon@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:scott.nixon@northyorkshire.police.uk) if you can help.

"Alternatively, you can call us on 101 or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

"Please quote reference 12260074421 when passing on information."
