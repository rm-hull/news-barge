## Thirsk café celebrates second birthday with 'crazy' one-day drinks offer

Brew & Bite Café will serve up any drink for just £2 on Wednesday, July 1 to celebrate its second birthday *(Image: Brew & Bite)*

A café in Thirsk is marking its second birthday with a “crazy” drinks offer.

Brew & Bite Café in North Yorkshire will serve up any drink for just £2 on Wednesday, July 1.

The deal is available to customers dining in or taking away.

Eleanor Fletcher, founder and owner of Brew & Bite Café, said: "I know this is a crazy price for a drink, but we wanted to say thank you to all our customers and friends, whether they are local regulars or visitors to Thirsk.

  The deal is available to customers dining in or taking away 

*(Image: Brew & Bite)*

"Like all independent cafes, we rely on our customers to make our business thrive, so we are delighted to mark this 2nd birthday as a key milestone."

The café opened in July 2024 as part of a project to bring a disused high street building back into use.

It recently launched a sister site, Little + Loud, North Yorkshire’s newest play café, which opened just 10 weeks ago next door.

  The café opened in July 2024 as part of a project to bring a disused high street building back into use 

*(Image: Brew & Bite)*

Both businesses share the same team and menus.

Brew & Bite is known for its all-day Yorkshire breakfast, as well as sandwiches, salads, soups and jacket potatoes.

The team said it wanted to show appreciation for the community’s support over the past two years.
