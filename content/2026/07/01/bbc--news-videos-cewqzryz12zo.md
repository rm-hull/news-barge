# Watch: Man comes face-to-face with bear inside truck

A man was startled to find a black bear in the front seat of a pickup truck in Steamboat Springs, Colorado. Security camera footage shows the man using several methods to coax the bear out of the vehicle. The man eventually used a wooden plank to guide the bear out of the truck, allowing both to walk away unharmed.
