## Look inside ‘characterful’ period home as it hits the market in York – for £675,000

A “characterful” period home has hit the market in York – for £675,000.

The three-bedroom home is for sale in St Martins Lane, off Micklegate.

Hudson Moody, which is marketing the property, said it is a “superb four-storey townhouse” that offers “generous living accommodation, a wealth of original features, and excellent access to local amenities”.

  Outside at the three-bedroom home for sale in St Martins Lane, off Micklegate, for £675,000 

*(Image: Hudson Moody/Zoopla)*

Inside is an open-plan kitchen, alongside a dining area with a fireplace and large window.

On the first floor is a living room, as well as the main bathroom.

Three double bedrooms are on the upper floors, including an attic bedroom “flooded with natural light from three rooflights”, according to Hudson Moody.

A spokesperson for the estate agent added that the home is “ideally positioned within easy reach of a wide range of shops, cafés and everyday amenities, the property also benefits from convenient access to the railway station and city centre”.
