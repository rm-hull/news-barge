Leading kitchen retailer, Wren Kitchens & Bedrooms, is set to open a brand-new showroom in Bridlington this July,

Opening on Friday July 3, the new showroom at Bessingby Industrial Estate, Bessingby Way will become Wren's 125th UK showroom.

The showroom, which is open to consumers as well as the trade, marks m the company's first showroom opening of the year.

The 1,400 sq. ft showroom will feature six kitchen and three-bedroom displays, showcasing a range of styles to suit different homes, tastes, and budgets.

As part of Wren's commitment to innovation, the retailer says the showroom will include two virtual reality planning suites, allowing customers to step inside their designs and experience their future kitchen or bedroom in immersive 3D before placing an order.

Connor Blackburn, showroom manager at Wren Bridlington, said: "We're excited to be bringing Wren to Bridlington and opening our doors as the company's 125th UK showroom.

"We know customers are looking for great design, quality products and value for money, and that's exactly what our team will deliver. Whether someone is planning a new kitchen, updating a bedroom, or looking to make better use of the space in their home, we're here to help turn those ideas into reality.

"We're also looking forward to welcoming local tradespeople into the showroom and showing them what Wren Trade has to offer. With quality products, competitive pricing and a wide choice of styles, we're well placed to support both trade professionals and the customers they work with."

Alongside kitchen and bedroom displays, customers will also be able to explore a wide selection of frontals, handles, worktops, flooring, tiling, sinks, and taps, helping them personalise every detail of their project. When they’re ready to begin planning, Wren's expert designers will create a free 3D design and provide a no-obligation quote with clear, transparent pricing.

Wren says the showroom's expertise extends beyond kitchens and bedrooms, with the Bridlington team also able to support the design of home offices, walk-in wardrobes, boot rooms, and utility rooms, helping customers make the most of every area of their home.

For more information or to book an appointment at Wren Bridlington, visit wren.com.
