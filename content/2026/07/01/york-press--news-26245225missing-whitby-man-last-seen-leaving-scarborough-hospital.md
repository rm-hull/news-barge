Police have issued an urgent appeal to find a missing man last seen leaving a North Yorkshire hospital.

Ben Moor has been missing since he was seen leaving Scarborough Hospital at 2.30am on Wednesday (July 1).

North Yorkshire Police said “extensive enquires” are ongoing to locate Mr Moor, 46, from Whitby.

The force has urged anyone with information about his whereabouts to contact the force.

Mr Moor is described as white, slim build with brown hair and a full beard. He may be wearing black jeans, a dark-coloured jacket, grey top and black Skecher shoes, police said.

A police spokesperson said: “If you see Ben, please call 999.

“If you have any other information that could help us find them, please call us on 101 and select option 1 to speak to our control room.

“Please quote 12260122972 when sharing information.”
