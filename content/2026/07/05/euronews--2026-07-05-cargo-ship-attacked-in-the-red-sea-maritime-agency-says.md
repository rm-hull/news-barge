## "Vessels are advised to transit with caution and report any suspicious activity to UKMTO," the agency said.

A cargo ship reported coming under attack in the Red Sea on Sunday, a British maritime agency announced.

The UK Maritime Trade Operations centre (UKMTO) said the vessel sent a distress signal saying they had been attacked by "unknown armed assailants" off the coast of al-Hudaydah, a major port city in Yemen.

UKMTO said authorities were investigating the incident.

"Vessels are advised to transit with caution and report any suspicious activity to UKMTO," it added.

The attack has so far not been claimed by any group.
