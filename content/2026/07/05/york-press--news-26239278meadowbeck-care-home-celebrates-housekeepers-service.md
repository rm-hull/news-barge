A York-based housekeeper has been honoured for 20 years of dedicated service.

Lisa, who works at Meadowbeck Care Home in York, was presented with a 20 Year Service Award to recognise her long-standing employment with Barchester Healthcare, one of the UK’s largest care providers.

Adele Keenan, Employee Services Director at Barchester, said: "I’m always pleased to hear stories about the long service of Barchester staff and am delighted Lisa has achieved this milestone.

"It is dedication like this that ensures our residents are provided with a happy place to live."

Lisa joined Barchester Healthcare in June 2006 and has spent the past two decades supporting residents and their relatives through her work.

Annaliza Kemp, General Manager of Meadowbeck Care Home, said: "We’re delighted to be celebrating 20 years of loyal service with Lisa.

"She has demonstrated her dedication and loyalty to this home and its residents year after year.

"I speak for all of us here at Meadowbeck when I say that I’m looking forward to many more years of working with Lisa."

Meadowbeck Care Home provides residential care, nursing care and dementia care for 60 residents, from respite care to long-term stays.
