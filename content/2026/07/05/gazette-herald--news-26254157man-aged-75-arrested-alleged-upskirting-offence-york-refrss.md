A 75-year-old man has been arrested for allegedly "taking pictures up a woman’s skirt" on a train in York.

British Transport Police said the alleged upskirting offence is said to have happened at around 5.20pm on Friday, June 19, as the woman was gathering her belongings to leave a service arriving from Harrogate.

  


She confronted the man, "continued to challenge him on the platform, and then reported it to station staff" who called the police.

The man left the station on a bike but has since been arrested and released on bail while officers conduct further inquiries.
