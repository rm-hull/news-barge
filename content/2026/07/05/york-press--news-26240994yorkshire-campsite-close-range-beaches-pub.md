An adults-only campsite close to the Yorkshire coast has been named as one of the best in the UK.

Campsites.co.uk’s annual list of the top 150 Outstanding Sites has been released for 2026, including a few sites that are easy to reach from York.

Wold Meadow can be found on the edge of the village of Wold Newton, which is only a short drive away from several beaches, including Bridlington.

The campsite won the award for Best Glamping Site in the North East of England at the 2025 Camping and Glamping Awards.


## What is the Wold Meadow campsite like?

The site has a Stargazer bell tent and five “beautifully furnished yurts” that have been individually decorated to choose from.

Each yurt and tent has an outdoor hot tub that you can relax in while watching the sunset.

You will also find equipment to cook with, including a BBQ, kettle, cutlery, and a toaster, alongside a communal camp kitchen with a range of food hampers you can pre-book.

There is a pub called The Anvil Arms, that serves food and is only a 10-minute walk away, plus a Co-op, only a 10-minute drive away.

During the day, you can take a walk around the surrounding countryside or spend a day exploring a seaside spot.


Campsites said: “Escape to Wold Meadow, an adult only glamping retreat just 15 minutes from the Yorkshire coast.

“Stay in the Stargazer bell tent or one of the five yurts, each sleeping two and featuring wood-fired hot tubs.

“Unwind with a glass of bubbly, explore the peaceful countryside and enjoy easy access to the Wolds Way, with the nearest section just 20 minutes from your yurt!

“Filey beaches are just 15 minutes away, and dogs are welcome to join your stay.”
