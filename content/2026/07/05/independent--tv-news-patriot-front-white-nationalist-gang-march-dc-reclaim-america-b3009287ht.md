# Hundreds of ‘Patriot Front’ white nationalists march through DC chanting ‘reclaim America’

Hundreds of members of the white nationalist group Patriot Front marched through the nation’s capitol to commemorate the Fourth of July.

The neo-fascist group could be heard chanting “reclaim America” and “life, liberty, victory” as they paraded around DC carrying American and Confederate flags in footage taken on Saturday (4 July).

The group, founded in the wake of the lethal Unite the Right rally in 2017, promotes the idea of a homogenous, white ethnostate while viewing immigration and a multi-racial democracy as an existential threat to their vision of the US, according to the George Washington University Program on Extremism.
