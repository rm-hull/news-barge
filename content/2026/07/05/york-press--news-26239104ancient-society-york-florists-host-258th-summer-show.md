The world's oldest horticultural society is set to hold its summer show.

The Ancient Society of York Florists will host their event on Saturday, July 11, at Wigginton Recreation Hall.

Founded 258 years ago, the event will feature various classes including flowers, fruit, vegetables, handicrafts, and photography.

New categories for art and children have also been introduced.

The show will run from 11am to 3pm, with a £1 entry fee, with visitors able to enjoy homemade refreshments and free parking.

For more details, visit the society's website: ancientsocietyofyorkflorists.weebly.com.
