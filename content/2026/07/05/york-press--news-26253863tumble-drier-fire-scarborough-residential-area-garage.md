A GARAGE fire involving a tumble dryer has broken out in a residential area of a North Yorkshire town.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said two of its crews from Scarborough were called to West Street in the town yesterday around 9.17pm (Saturday, July 4).

  



A service spokesperson said its crew put the fire out which had caused smoke and heat damage to other items in the garage, as well as the tumble drier.
