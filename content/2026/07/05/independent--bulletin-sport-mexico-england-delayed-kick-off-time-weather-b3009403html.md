# Mexico v England World Cup game delayed after thunderstorm warning

- England's World Cup 2026 last 16 match against Mexico has been delayed due to an electrical storm at the Azteca Stadium.
- The tie, originally scheduled for 6pm local time (1am BST), has been rescheduled for 7pm local time (2am BST), with potential for further delays.
- Severe weather, including heavy rain, thunder, and lightning, prevented fans from entering the stadium and delayed team arrivals.
- The stadium's electrical storm protocol was activated, advising fans and officials to seek cover.
- The winner of this match will proceed to face Norway in the quarter-finals on Saturday, following Norway's 2-1 victory over Brazil.
