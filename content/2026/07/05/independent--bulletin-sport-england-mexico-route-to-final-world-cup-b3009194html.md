# Who will England play if they beat Mexico? Everything we know

- England's pursuit of their first World Cup in 60 years continues after successfully navigating the group stage.
- The Three Lions secured a 2-1 victory over DR Congo in their opening knockout-stage fixture, with captain Harry Kane netting both goals.
- Their next challenge is a last-16 clash against co-hosts Mexico, set to take place at the high-altitude Estadio Azteca.
- Should they progress, England could potentially face Brazil in the quarter-finals, going off Fifa ranking, and Argentina in the semi-finals if all goes as predicted.
- The projected tournament path indicates a challenging route to the final, with a potential showdown against top-ranked Spain.
