## Have a look inside this ‘superb’ period townhouse for sale in York – for £795,000

The four-bedroom townhouse for sale in Holgate Road, York, for £795,000 *(Image: Hudson Moody/Zoopla)*

A “superb” period home has hit the market in York – for £795,000.

The four-bedroom townhouse is for sale in Holgate Road.

Hudson Moody, which is marketing the property, said it has been “finished to a very high contemporary standard”, but still “retains some charming original features”.

  Inside the four-bedroom townhouse for sale in Holgate Road, York, for £795,000 

*(Image: Hudson Moody/Zoopla)*

Inside, a sitting room with a large bay window is at the front of the property with a modern dining kitchen at the rear.

The kitchen has bi-folding doors opening onto the rear courtyard.

Upstairs, the main bedroom, complete with a gas fireplace, is on the first floor, along with another double bedroom. Both rooms are served by a shower room.

Two more double bedrooms are on the second floor, as well as a “beautifully appointed house bathroom”, according to Hudson Moody.

Outside, a patio extends directly from the dining kitchen with steps leading to an enclosed courtyard garden.

A Hudson Moody spokesperson said the property is a “superbly updated mid-terraced period house set in an ideal location close to York city centre”.
