A mother-and-daughter Pilates studio in York is marking its first birthday with a charity fundraiser.

Hayley and Autumn Robinson, who run Peak4 Studios at Clifton Moor Business Village, will be raising money for York Against Cancer on Tuesday, July 14.

All proceeds from their Reformer Pilates classes that day will be donated to the charity.

Hayley Robinson said: "Since opening our doors a year ago, Peak4 Studios has built a wonderful community and we wanted to mark this milestone by giving back to a local charity that provides invaluable support to people and families affected by cancer across York and the surrounding area.

"The team hopes to raise as much money as possible for this worthy cause and encourages the local community to get involved, whether by attending a class, purchasing raffle tickets, or simply dropping in for a cuppa and a chat."

Classes run from 7.15am to 7.30pm and cost £10 each.
