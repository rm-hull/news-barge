A 19-year-old man has suffered life-threatening injuries after a quad bike he was riding crashed into stationary vehicles in an East Yorkshire village.

Humberside Police said it happened in Main Street in Tickton, near Beverley at around 2.50pm on Saturday, July 4, 2026.

  



A spokesperson for the force said the man was riding the white quad bike and was travelling along the street before colliding with a black Vauxhall, a white Volkswagen, a silver Volkswagen and a black Hyundai.

The rider was taken to hospital for treatment, and the road was closed by police until later that day.

Humberside Police is appealing for anyone who witnessed the crash or has dashcam footage to call 101, quoting log 332 of July 4.
