# Photo of Black woman surrounded by white supremacists sparks widespread reaction

- A photo depicting a Black woman surrounded by members of the white nationalist group Patriot Front on a Washington, D.C., Metro train has gone viral.
- Taken by *Reuters* photographer Cheney Orr on July 4, the image is being widely shared and described as a "defining picture of this moment in America."
- The Patriot Front members were in Washington, D.C. for a march, chanting slogans such as "reclaim America" and carrying American and Confederate flags.
- Actor Wendell Pierce, known for his roles in *The Wire* and*Jack Ryan*, re-shared the photo on X, calling it an "instant Pulitzer Prize winning photograph."
- Interior Secretary Doug Burgum condemned Patriot Front's ideology but defended their right to free speech, and Washington's Metropolitan Police Department reported no arrests during the march.
