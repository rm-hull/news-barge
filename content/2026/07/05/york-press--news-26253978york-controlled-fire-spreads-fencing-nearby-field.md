A CONTROLLED fire has spread to fencing and field close to a major road in York.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said crews from Acomb and York were called to The Burn, a cul-de-sac on the outskirts of the city close to the A1237 Ring Road, around 3.20pm today (Sunday, July 5).

  


A service spokesperson said: "Crews used two hose reel jets to extinguish the fire.

"Advice was given and left with the responsible person on scene."
