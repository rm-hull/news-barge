Police are investigating after a woman was knocked unconscious in York city centre.

The assault happened at around **5am** on**Thursday 21 May** on the junction of**High Ousegate** and**Nessgate**.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “An investigation has already been launched and we are now trying to identify this woman as part of our enquiries.”

#### Crime location

They are asking anyone who recognises her, or who has any other information, to contact them.
