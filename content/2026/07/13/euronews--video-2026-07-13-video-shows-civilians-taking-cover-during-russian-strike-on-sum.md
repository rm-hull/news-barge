CCTV footage from the northeastern Ukrainian city of Sumy shows customers inside a coffee shop rushing to safety after explosions shattered the windows during a Russian air strike.

A second camera captured people outside dropping to the ground before fleeing as blasts echoed through the area. Ukrainian officials said two Russian guided glide bombs struck a crowded part of the city, killing five people, including a child, and injuring 30 others.

One bomb landed near a public transport stop, damaging buses, cars and nearby residential buildings.
