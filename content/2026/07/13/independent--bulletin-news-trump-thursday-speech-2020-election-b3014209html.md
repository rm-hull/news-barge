# Trump’s Thursday address to reveal alleged foreign interference in 2020 election

- President Donald Trump is scheduled to deliver a "Speech to the Nation" on Thursday at 9 p.m. ET, where he is expected to reveal alleged foreign interference in the 2020 election.
- These claims will reportedly be based on declassified intelligence information, according to MS Now.
- Trump has repeatedly asserted, without providing evidence, that he won the 2020 election.
- A March 2021 report from the Office of the Director of National Intelligence concluded that no foreign adversary altered or hacked any technical aspect of the 2020 voting process.
- The ODNI report did note that Russia spread misinformation against Joe Biden and Iran conducted an influence campaign aimed at undermining Trump's re-election.
