A VEHICLE and tools have been stolen during an early morning raid at a farm outbuilding in a North Yorkshire village.

The theft took place between 1am and 4am in Carlton on Wednesday, July 8.

A black Mitsubishi and equipment including a welder, chainsaw, petrol knocker, tipping machines, and other handheld tools were taken.

  


Entry was gained by breaking padlocks and removing chains.

North Yorkshire Police are appealing for information and CCTV footage from the area around the time of the incident.

Anyone who can help is asked to call 101, quoting reference 12260128509.
