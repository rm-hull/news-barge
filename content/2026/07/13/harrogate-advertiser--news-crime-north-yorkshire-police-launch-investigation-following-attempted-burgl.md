# North Yorkshire Police launch investigation following attempted burglary at property in Harrogate

## North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information and witnesses following an attempted burglary at a property in Harrogate.

The incident happened at approximately 1am on Friday, July 10, at a property on Brooke Close, and involved two suspects forcing open a garage door and attempting to gain entry.

A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: “We’re particularly appealing for information about two individuals wearing sportswear and face coverings.

“If you have any information that could assist our investigation, please contact us.”
