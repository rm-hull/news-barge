# Trump announces 20% toll on Strait of Hormuz – what we know

- Donald Trump announced the US would impose a 20 per cent toll on all cargo shipped through the Strait of Hormuz.
- He declared the US as 'THE GUARDIAN OF THE HORUMZ STRAIT' and stated the toll would serve as reimbursement.
- Trump also announced the 'reinstating THE IRANIAN BLOCKADE', preventing Iranian ships or customers from entering or leaving the strait.
- These statements followed overnight attacks by Iran's Revolutionary Guard on facilities in Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman and Jordan.
- The Iranian attacks were in response to earlier US military strikes on Iranian targets, casting doubt on a recent interim US-Iranian agreement.
