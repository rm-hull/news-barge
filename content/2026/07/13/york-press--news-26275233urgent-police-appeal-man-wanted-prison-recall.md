POLICE are searching for a wanted man who may be in North Yorkshire.

Nicky Watts, 37, is wanted for prison recall for breaching his licence conditions.

He may be in the Scarborough or Hull, North Yorkshire Police have said.

  


Watts also has links to Middlesborough.

  He is described as a white male, 6ft 2in tall, of heavy build, with short brown hair.

  

  If you have seen Watts or have any information about his current whereabouts, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

  

  Alternatively, you can provide information anonymously via the independent charity Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or through their website.

  

  If you see Watts and his location is known at the time, please call 999 immediately.

  

  Please quote reference 12260119224 when providing any information.
