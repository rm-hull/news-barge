# Hunter Biden says Trump changed the Lindsey Graham he once knew

- Hunter Biden stated he prefers to remember the late Senator Lindsey Graham for his friendship with his father, Joe Biden, rather than his subsequent support for Donald Trump and MAGA.
- Senator Graham, a four-term South Carolina Republican, passed away suddenly over the weekend from a ruptured aorta after returning from a diplomatic mission to Ukraine.
- Biden recalled a time before Trump when senators from opposing parties could maintain friendships and cited Graham's past praise for Joe Biden's character.
- Joe Biden expressed shock at Graham's death, while former Republican congressman Adam Kinzinger echoed Hunter Biden's sentiment, noting his own friendship with Graham ended due to his shift to Trumpism.
- Donald Trump led tributes, forgiving Graham's past criticisms and remembering him as a 'tough cookie' who became an unquestioning supporter of his foreign policy.
