COUNTERFEIT £20 notes have been used in a £640 mobile phone sale in a coastal North Yorkshire town.

The incident took place at 4.04pm on May 27, 2026, at 161 North Marine Road, Scarborough where the victim exchanged the phone for the cash, according to the county's police force.

After the sale, the buyer left the scene.

  


North Yorkshire Police released CCTV images of a person they wish to identify, saying he may have information which could assist its investigation.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Constable 1222 David Fulford at North Yorkshire Police by email at [david.fulford@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:david.fulford@northyorkshire.police.uk) quoting reference 12260101184.

Alternatively, call 101 or contact police via their website.
