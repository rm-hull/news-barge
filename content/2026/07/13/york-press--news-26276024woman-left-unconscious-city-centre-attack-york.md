A WOMAN was left unconscious following an attack in the centre of York, close to a main shopping area.

North Yorkshire Police said the assault happened around 5am on Thursday, May 21 at the junction of High Ousegate and Nessgate.

  



Officers are appealing for the public's help and have released an image of a woman they wish to speak to, saying she may have information which could support its investigation.

A police spokesperson said: "An investigation has already been launched, and we are now trying to identify this woman as part of our enquiries."

"If you can help our investigation, please email [dean.stilgoe@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:dean.stilgoe@northyorkshire.police.uk) or go online via the North Yorkshire Police website, or through the live chat service available Monday to Friday 9am and 5pm."

Please quote reference 12260091921 when speaking to police.
