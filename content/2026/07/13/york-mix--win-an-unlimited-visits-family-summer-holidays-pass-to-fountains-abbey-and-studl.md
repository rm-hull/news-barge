Just in time for the school Summer Holidays we’ve got together with Fountains Abbey & Studley Royal in Ripon to give you the chance to win a summer pass for a family to visit as many times as you’d like across the school holidays!

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/NTV_E_D_525_S_C-2.png)

People of York, your next summer adventure is easier than ever! With increased direct bus services between York and Fountains Abbey & Studley Royal during the school holidays, you can leave the car at home and enjoy a stress-free day out. Better still, children and young people under 18 can visit for just £1 when arriving by bus and showing a valid ticket.

Plus, once you’ve arrived, there’s a whole Summer of Play to dive into, with space hopper races, balance bikes, soft archery, Pokémon-themed activities, an adventure playground and acres of beautiful World Heritage landscape to explore.

Hop on the bus and make this summer one to remember at Fountains Abbey & Studley Royal in Ripon!

To be in with a chance of winning, simply answer the question on the entry form below.

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/©-National-Trust.-Registered-Charity-no.-205846.-National-Trust-Images-Chris-Lacey.webp)
