## Top rated York brunch cafe and bar to open sister outlet in city centre

Owners Naiya and Arpit Arora *(Image: Pic supplied)*

A highly-rated café and bar is to open a sister outlet in the city centre.

Bruberrie Café And Bar opened at The Mount, near Micklegate, in March 2024 and has gained a top reputation.

TripAdvisor awards the venue 4.8 stars out of five, based on 160 reviews, ranking it 33rd out of 799 York restaurants.

Google also awards 4.8 stars out of five, based on 477 reviews.

The business is owned by Naiya Arora and her partner Arpit.

  The Bruberrie in Bootham, which is set to open in the coming weeks. 

*(Image: Pic supplied)*

Naiya says there remains some work to be done at the second site in Bootham, but she expects it will be open in two or three weeks’ time.

She told The Press: “We wanted to be close to the city centre. Here at the Mount, we miss the traffic at weekends.”

  The exterior of the existing Bruberrie face at The Mount 

*(Image: Pic supplied)*

“When this Bootham site became available, we grabbed it. It’s next to the historic buildings. It has a car park opposite. We thought it would be a great addition. We will continue with The Mount.”

The couple have had The Mount for over two years, with Naiya previously working as a project manager and Arpit having a catering business for nearly 20 years.

Naiya said: “Our passion was always to have a business of our own. Since we opened there has been no turning back. We love working with the customers. When we open at Bootham, we want people to come and try our food.”

Bootham will have a few items unique to itself and Naiya is still finalising the menu.

  A most beautiful brunch 

*(Image: Pic supplied)*

But expect pancakes, breakfasts, sourdough, coffee, cakes and croissants.

Already, the couple appear to have found the recipe for success, as shown by the 4.8 star ratings and it being a finalist in last year’s Best Coffee Shop contest from The Press.

Bruberrie Café And Bar’s latest review on TripAdvisor gave five stars and said it was “our brunch spot when in York".

They added: “Excellent service. Delicious food, beautifully presented. Something for every taste and appetite.”
