                                    Harrogate Town defender Chanse Headman has completed a permanent move to League One side Bromley for an undisclosed fee.

The 21-year-old joined Town from Brentford in January and went on to make 16 appearances for the Sulphurites.

Headman also scored twice during Harrogate's victory away at Tranmere Rovers, with his performances earning him a move to the third tier.

A spokesperson for Harrogate Town said:

"We would like to thank Chanse for his efforts while at the club and wish him good luck for the future."
