The best women’s razors are a year-round bathroom staple, but they really earn their keep through summer, when spontaneous beach days, holidays and sundresses call for quick, fuss-free hair removal.

While IPL machines promise longer-lasting results, they require patience and a bigger upfront investment, and a session with an at-home waxing kit isn't everyone's idea of relaxing pre-holiday prep. Hair removal creams offer another alternative, though they don't suit every skin type.

For speed, convenience and affordability, however, it's hard to beat a razor. They’ve come a long way from the days when you’d leave the shower with numerous nicks around the ankles and knees and, these days, the best designs glide comfortably over legs, underarms and bikini lines, with features such as moisturising strips, flexible heads and blades designed for sensitive skin. Many also use replaceable cartridges, cutting down on plastic waste compared with disposable options.

To find the top performers, from Venus to Estrid, I tested a range of women's razors for every budget and hair removal need.
