A man has denied attempted murder after an incident in York.

Dean Barker pleaded not guilty to attempted murder at Leeds Crown Court on Monday (July 13).

He was arrested after an incident in Tudor Road, Acomb, on May 29.

Barker, 42, of Tudor Road, is to stand trial at Leeds Crown Court from November 30.

He was remanded in custody ahead of his next court appearance on November 3.

Barker’s case will be heard in Leeds because he is charged with attempted murder and York Crown Court no longer tries cases involving murder or attempted murder.
