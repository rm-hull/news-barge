## Woman found dead at home with next of kin sought

Forensic investigators (File image from PA) *(Image: PA)*

A WOMAN has been found dead at a house in Bridlington.

Humberside Police is appealing to find the family of Jacqueline Cooper, 75, who died in her home in Northorpe Rise, in the old town, on Monday, July 8.

  


  Humberside Police said 75-year-old Jacqueline Cooper died at her home in Northorpe Rise in Bridlington on Monday, July 8 

*(Image: Newsquest)*

Her death is not being treated as suspicious, but efforts to trace her next of kin have so far been unsuccessful.

Police launched an appeal for help in identifying the next of kin on Monday afternoon.

Anyone who is the next of kin or has information is asked to contact the coroner’s officer on 01482 613 009.
