## Take a look inside the £800,000 four bedroom Huntington home

Newly built detached home with private driveway and garage *(Image: Hudson Moody)*

A newly built detached home in Huntington is for sale at a guide price of £800,000.

The property, located in Lea Gardens, offers four bedrooms, three bathrooms, and two reception rooms.

The estate agent, Hudson Moody, says it is the last available plot on the new development.

The house is also said to have energy-saving features and fibre broadband.

  Open-plan living and dining area with bi-fold doors to the garden 

*(Image: Hudson Moody)*

It is said to have a fully fitted kitchen, with integrated appliances, that forms part of an open-plan living and dining area.

The dining space has bi-fold doors that open onto the garden.

The master bedroom is said to have an en-suite bathroom and a dressing room, while one of the other three double bedrooms also has an en-suite.

The property also features a utility room, an additional living area, and a ground-floor WC.

  The separate living room space at the home 

*(Image: Hudson Moody)*

There is also a private driveway and garage.

The estate agent says the house is energy-efficient, fitted with a smart meter and fibre broadband.

The property comes with a 10-year building warranty.

It is in a development of five detached homes, all designed to combine timeless design with modern efficiency.

  Garden accessed through bi-fold doors from the dining area 

*(Image: Hudson Moody)*

They have air source heat pumps and ultrafast full fibre broadband.

Kyme Homes, the house builder responsible for the development, has been praised for creating homes with character, quality, and a deep understanding of modern living.

There is an open day for the property on Saturday, July 11 from 9am to 11am.

For more information, or to book a viewing, contact estate agent Hudson Moody on 01904 595149.
