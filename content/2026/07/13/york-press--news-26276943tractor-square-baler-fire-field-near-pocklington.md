NORTH Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service has been called to a fire in a field in East Yorkshire.

A tractor and square baler are reported to be on fire in a field near Pocklington.

  


The incident was reported at 4.50pm on Monday (July 13).

Farmers are said to be making fire-breaks around the incident to slow the spread of the blaze until firefighters arrive.
