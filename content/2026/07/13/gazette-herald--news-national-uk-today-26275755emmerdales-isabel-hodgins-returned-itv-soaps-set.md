An Emmerdale star returned to the set for a brief heartwarming moment.

Isabel Hodgins, who plays Victoria Sugden, has shared the moment she took her baby daughter to see her co-stars and team on the soap’s set.

She made it a family affair, taking her husband Adam too.

Isabel is still on maternity leave, having given birth in February this year, but made a special visit to show her family where she works.


Sharing a video on her Instagram account, Isabel said: “Yesterday was special ❤️

“For the first time, I got to show Georgie (and Adam) around the village that's been such a huge part of my life.

“I've been lucky enough to call this place work since I was 12 years old, so sharing it with my little family meant so much.

“GG's verdict? She'd quite happily move in🥹”.

Adam commented on the post: “Our little team 🫶🏼”.

The video showed a variety of short clips of Isabel walking around the Emmerdale village while pushing their daughter Georgie in a pushchair.

They visited the iconic Emmerdale village sign and walked through the streets, stopping at key locations such as the pub, the playground, the church and the graveyard where Jack Sugden’s grave is.


They ended the trip with an ice cream and fans have shared their excitement for Georgie as she got her first taste of what Emmerdale is all about.

One commented: “What a wonderful memory for you showing baby girl where her Mummy works 😍😍😍, absolutely gorgeous family ❤️❤️❤️”.

Someone said: “Lovely looking forward to seeing you back on screen x”.
