Whether you’re in a compact flat or a bustling family home, the best cordless vacuum cleaners deliver impressive suction without the hassle of trailing cables, so you can reach every inch of your rooms.

After testing dozens of models on everything from muddy hallways to crushed cereal and everyday dust, it’s clear that cordless technology has come a long way. In fact, the best cordless vacuum cleaners in 2026 now comfortably rival traditional uprights.

Today’s cordless models are powerful enough to lift embedded pet hair from deep-pile carpets, come equipped with HEPA filtration to capture allergens, and can quickly transform into handheld vacuums to tackle chores such as cleaning the car.

However, with LCD displays, laser dust detection, particle sensors and specialist attachments among the features on offer, cordless vacuum cleaner prices can stretch close to £1,000. Knowing which models are genuinely worth the investment isn’t always straightforward but that’s where I can help. Keep scrolling for my pick of the best cordless vacuums, which all performed when tested in my own home.
