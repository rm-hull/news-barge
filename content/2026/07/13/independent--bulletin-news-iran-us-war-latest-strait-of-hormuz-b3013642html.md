# Iran hits US military bases as Middle East conflict escalates

- Iran’s Revolutionary Guard (IRGC) launched missile and drone attacks on US military bases in Bahrain and Jordan overnight, claiming retaliation for earlier US strikes on Iranian coastal bases.
- Missile alert sirens sounded in Bahrain, home to the US Navy’s 5th Fleet, and the IRGC reported setting ablaze fuel tanks and ammunition depots at Jordan's Prince Hassan Air Base.
- These Iranian actions followed earlier US military strikes on Iran, which Washington stated were to hold Iranian forces accountable amidst escalating tensions.
- The core of the conflict is the contested control of the Strait of Hormuz, with Iran asserting its territorial rights and Washington denying Tehran's control.
- Trump claimed on NBC's Meet the Press that a “perfect” deal had been agreed with Tehran before further attacks were launched.
