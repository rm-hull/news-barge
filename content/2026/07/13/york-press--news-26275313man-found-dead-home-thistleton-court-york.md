## Man found dead at home in York with public urged to help

Police and forensic investigators. (File image) *(Image: PA/Jacob King)*

A MAN has been found dead at a home in York.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing to find the family of Christopher Gannon, who passed away at Thistleton Court on Friday (July 10).

  


  Christopher Gannon passed away at Thistleton Court on Friday (July 10) 

*(Image: North Yorkshire Police)*

Mr Gannon was 64-years-old and his death is not thought to be suspicious.

Police are urging anyone who knew Mr Gannon or who can help find his family to get in touch.

“If you are related to him or know someone who is, please email [coroner@northyorkshirepolice.uk.](mailto:coroner@northyorkshirepolice.uk.)"
