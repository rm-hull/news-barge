# North Yorkshire Police release CCTV images of man and woman following theft from shop in Ripon

## North Yorkshire Police has issued two CCTV images of a male and a female they would like to speak to following a theft at a shop in Ripon.

The incident happened on the Thursday 25 June at Sainsbury’s, on Market Place East, and involved the theft of bottles of alcohol.

A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: “Please contact us if you recognise the male or female pictured in the CCTV images, as they may have information that will assist our investigation.”
