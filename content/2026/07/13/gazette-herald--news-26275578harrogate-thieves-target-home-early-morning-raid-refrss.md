THIEVES have targeted a home on the edge of a North Yorkshire town in an overnight raid.

The county's police force said it happened at 1am on Friday, July 10 in Brooke Close, Harrogate.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said a garage door was forced in an attempt to gain entry.

  


A force spokesperson said: "We're appealing for information, particularly about two individuals wearing sportswear and face coverings.

"If you have information that could assist our investigation, please email PC Heap - [izzy.heap@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:izzy.heap@northyorkshire.police.uk), or call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

"Please quote reference 12260130322 when providing information."
