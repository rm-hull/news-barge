# Strictly star issues warning after audience member removed from Dublin theatre show

- West End star Amber Davies had an audience member removed from a performance of 'Legally Blonde' in Dublin for filming the show.
- Davies stated that the audience member's actions distracted the cast and ruined their morale during Act One.
- She issued a warning to fans against filming at the theatre, asserting that she would ensure rule-breakers are asked to leave.
- Davies noted that this was not an isolated incident, as she had observed other theatregoers filming in Ireland.
- This incident follows similar calls for better theatre etiquette from other actors, including Rosamund Pike, Daniel Craig, and Hugh Jackman.
