THREE people have been taken to hospital following a crash involving three vehicles in an East Yorkshire village.

Humberside Fire and Rescue Service said it happened in Leafield Road, Bainton, around 4.30pm yesterday (Sunday, July 12).

  



The road is close to a junction between the A614 Goole to Bridlington road and the B1248 Malton to Beverley road.

Another person was checked over by firefighters at the scene for minor injuries.

A service spokesperson said paramedic also treated the injured at the scene.
