# England kit colour revealed for World Cup semi-final showdown with Argentina

- Thomas Tuchel’s England are set to face Argentina in a huge World Cup semi-final clash in Atlanta on Wednesday.
- It has been confirmed that England will play in their all-white home kit as they aim to reach their first World Cup final for 60 years.
- Meanwhile, Argentina will play in their navy blue away shirt, while wearing black shorts and black socks.
- Argentina are the defending champions and remain on course for a fourth World Cup triumph, but England do have a 3-2 lead in the head-to-head record at World Cups.
- Wednesday’s match is due to kick-off at 8pm BST, and will be available to watch on BBC One and BBC iPlayer.
