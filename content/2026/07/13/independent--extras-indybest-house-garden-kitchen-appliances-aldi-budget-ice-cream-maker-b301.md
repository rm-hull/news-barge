One of the tastiest ways to stay cool during the heat wave is by eating a cool, silky ice cream, and thanks to the best ice cream makers, it’s never been easier to make a tub at home. While the freezing, churning machines don’t come cheap, in usual fashion Aldi is launching a budget alternative – this time one that rivals Ninja’s premium creami.

Arriving in Aldi’s Specialbuys middle aisle this week, the Ambiano ice cream machine uses “smoothify” technology to transform frozen ingredients into a creamy texture by shaving them with a rotating blade. Whether you fancy homemade ice cream, refreshing sorbet, milkshakes or even frozen cocktails, it opens up plenty of possibilities for creating frozen favourites at home.

Priced at just £69.99, Aldi’s machine undercuts the Ninja creami by around £130. It also features a similar design and comes with comparable accessories, making it an appealing alternative for anyone tempted by Ninja’s cult-favourite appliance. While the Ninja creami deluxe is our top-rated model, its premium price makes Aldi’s more affordable option all the more enticing.

Upkeep is seemingly simple too, with a self-cleaning function, adjustable blade and dishwasher-safe accessories. Plus it comes with three 500ml preparation cups and ten preset programmes.
