# Jay-Z explains three-hour delay at Yankee Stadium concert was due to safety issue

- Jay-Z's final Yankee Stadium residency show started over three hours late, concluding at nearly 3 am, much to the frustration of waiting fans.
- The significant delay was attributed to crowd safety concerns, as thousands of people without tickets attempted to storm the venue's gates.
- Jay-Z addressed the audience, explaining that the show was held back to prevent trampling and ensure the safety of all attendees.
- Roc Nation confirmed that individuals without tickets breached security at various entrances, leading to gates being closed under the guidance of the NYPD.
- Despite the late start, the concert featured surprise appearances from high-profile artists including Rihanna, Beyoncé, Usher, and Pharrell Williams, as part of a celebration of Jay-Z's album anniversaries.
