                                    A teacher at Ripley Endowed CE Primary School has received a special end of term treat thanks to Harrogate BID.

We asked listeners to nominate a teacher that deserves a treat as thanks for their hard work over the course of the academic year.

It's part of our 'End of Term Treat' campaign with Harrogate BID, with five teachers set to receive a £50 Harrogate gift card.

And the first winner is Mrs Bassitt, who was nominated for going “above and beyond everyday” to support children at the school.

The nomination read:

“You can tell she loves her job and we as parents can see how amazing she is with all the children.

“Both my girls love her and are very happy in her classroom. She 100% deserves this treat and more!”
