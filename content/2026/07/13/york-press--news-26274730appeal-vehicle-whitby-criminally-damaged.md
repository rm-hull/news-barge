TWO suspects are being investigated after a vehicle in Whitby was criminally damaged.

The incident happened in Ammonite Grove at 11.13pm on Saturday (July 11).

North Yorkshire Police have launched an appeal for footage of the area at the time.

  


A police spokesperson said: “If you have any information that could assist our investigation, please contact us online in the first instance.

“You can do this by emailing [ryan.buckton@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:ryan.buckton@northyorkshire.police.uk) or visiting our website, selecting ‘Tell us about’, then choosing ‘An existing case or report’.

“Live chat is available via our website, Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.

“If you are unable to use these options, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

“Please quote reference 12260132158 when providing information.”
