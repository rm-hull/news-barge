# Trump reveals what Lindsey Graham told him behind closed doors after Jan 6

- Donald Trump claimed Senator Lindsey Graham apologized for publicly criticizing him after the January 6th Capitol riot.
- Trump stated in a Fox & Friends interview that Graham, who recently died, called him 40 minutes after his Senate speech to retract his remarks.
- During his January 6th speech, Graham declared, "Count me out. Enough is enough," and acknowledged Joe Biden and Kamala Harris were "lawfully elected."
- There is no corroboration for Trump's assertion, and White House phone logs from January 6th do not show any contact between Trump and Graham.
- Despite his initial public break, Graham quickly returned to supporting Trump within days of the Capitol attack.
