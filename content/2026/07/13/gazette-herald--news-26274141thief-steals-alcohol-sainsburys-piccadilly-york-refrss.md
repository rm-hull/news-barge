A THIEF has targeted a well-known supermarket in York city centre.

Alcohol was stolen from Sainsbury’s in Piccadilly on June 24.

North Yorkshire Police have launched a CCTV appeal of a man officers would like to speak to, as he may have information that could assist the investigation.
