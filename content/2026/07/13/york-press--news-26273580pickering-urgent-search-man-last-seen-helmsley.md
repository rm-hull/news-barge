A 49-year-old man from an area close to a North Yorkshire town has been reported missing, and police are appealing for help to find him.

Christopher Clark, from the Pickering area, was last seen in Helmsley at 12.30pm on Thursday, July 9, 2026.

The force described Mr Clark as white, around 5ft 6in tall, with a slim build and brown, wavy, shoulder-length hair.

  


He may be wearing navy blue or khaki shorts, a brown T-shirt, and black and grey Merrell walking shoes.

He is believed to be carrying a black Osprey rucksack, which may contain a black North Face shell jacket and a dark blue Rab puffer jacket.

A spokesman for North Yorkshire Police said Christopher is a keen walker and has a knowledge of the local area.

Anyone who sees Christopher is urged to call 999 immediately.

Other information about his whereabouts' can be shared by calling North Yorkshire Police on 101.
