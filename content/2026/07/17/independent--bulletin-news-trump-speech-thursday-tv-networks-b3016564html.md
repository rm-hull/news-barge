# White House blasts major networks for not airing Trump’s speech live

- The White House is angered that major TV news networks will not broadcast President Donald Trump’s upcoming speech live on their primary channels.
- ABC News, NBC News and CNN plan to stream the speech on their digital platforms, while Fox News intends to air it in its entirety.
- White House communications director Steven Cheung criticized the networks on X, accusing them of being “cowards” and attempting to conceal facts from the public.
- The president is scheduled to deliver his speech at 9 p.m. Eastern time, with specific details of his remarks not yet released.
- Reports indicate the president may reiterate unsubstantiated claims regarding the 2020 election, a prospect that has reportedly caused concern among some Republicans.
