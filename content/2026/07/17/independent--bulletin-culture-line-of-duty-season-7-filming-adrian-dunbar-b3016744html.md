Line of Duty is currently filming its seventh series, with star Adrian Dunbar expressing his annoyance at the heat while shooting in Belfast.

Dunbar, who portrays Superintendent Ted Hastings, expressed happiness about reuniting with castmates and praised series creator Jed Mercurio's "fabulous script" in an interview with The Times.

The show's return follows widespread viewer disappointment with the series six finale.

Dunbar previously said that the finale "robbed us of a huge denouement", adding: “When you find out it is this idiot, Buckells, it is so frustrating.”

The upcoming series will feature Robert Carlyle joining the cast and is expected to reintroduce the plotline surrounding "H".
