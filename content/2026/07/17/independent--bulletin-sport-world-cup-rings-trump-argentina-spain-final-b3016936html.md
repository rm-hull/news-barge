FIFA is set to award Super Bowl-style rings to the winners of the World Cup final contested between Argentina and Spain.

Thirty limited-edition rings will be presented to the victorious team, with an additional 1,996 rings available for fans to purchase.

Each ring will incorporate a mini World Cup trophy in its design and be custom-engraved to reflect the winning nation's culture.

Donald Trump will attend the final, his sole match of the tournament, and is confirmed to present the World Cup trophy to the champions.

Trump previously caused controversy by lobbying FIFA president Gianni Infantino to overturn a red card for player Folarin Balogun, which resulted in the card's suspension.
