Families and music lovers are in for a treat with a packed summer programme of entertainment.

Bridlington Spa has unveiled a full line-up of shows for the school holidays, ranging from family favourites and tribute acts to a major touring musical, promising to draw thousands to the Yorkshire coast throughout July and August.

Mark Lonsdale, general manager of Bridlington Spa, said: "Summer is always one of our busiest and most exciting times of the year, and we’ve put together a programme that truly has something for everyone.

"Whether you’re looking for a fun family day out, an evening singing along to your favourite artists, or a fantastic night at the theatre, there’s plenty to enjoy at Bridlington Spa this summer."

Returning to the stage is Bridlington favourite Troy Harris with a brand-new adventure in Muddles Live!

This interactive family show features a pirate-themed quest alongside characters Molly and Spartie the Seagull and promises "songs, laughter, and audience participation".

Other highlights include Taylormania, a tribute to Taylor Swift, and Flowers & Friendship Bracelets, a concert inspired by chart-topping stars such as Swift, Sabrina Carpenter, Miley Cyrus, and Chappell Roan.

Families can also enjoy The Dinosaur That Pooped: A Rock Show!, which brings the popular children’s books to life with music and comedy.

The summer schedule also includes the hit musical 9 to 5, running from Tuesday, August 11 to Saturday, August 15.

Tickets are available now from the Bridlington Spa Box Office by calling 01262 678258, or online at bridspa.com.
