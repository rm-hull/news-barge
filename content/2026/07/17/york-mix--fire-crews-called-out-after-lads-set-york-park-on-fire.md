Firefighters were called to a fire in a York park last night (Thursday).

The Acomb crew went to the incident in **West Bank Park** at**7.17pm**.

One resident posted on Facebook that “two lads set it on fire, witnessed by a group of kids playing in the area”.

#### West Bank Park, York

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “Acomb’s crew responded to a fire in a wooded area that had been lit deliberately.

“Crews used one hose reel jet to extinguish and the incident was left with police.”

Shortly afterwards, at **7.31pm**, York firefighters were called to an attempt to set gorse bushes on fire on** Hob Moor**.

“Crews checked for hotspots using one thermal imaging camera. However no fire fighting action taken,” the spokesperson said.
