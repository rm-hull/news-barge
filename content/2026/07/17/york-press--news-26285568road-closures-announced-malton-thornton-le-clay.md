The Public Notice Portal carries statutory public notices published in local newspapers and is the fastest and most effective way of finding out what is happening in YOUR neighbourhood.

Search the Public Notice PortalNorth Yorkshire Council has announced multiple temporary road closures in the latest public notice.

Newgate, Malton, will be shut for two weeks for footway, kerbing, and road marking works, with closure signs in place and an alternative route marked.

The closure starts on July 20 and ends on August 3.

Moor Lane, Thornton-le-Clay, will also be closed from July 20 until July 31, as Quickline Communications installs new ducts, with a maximum closure duration until August 31.

An alternative route will be signposted.

Paul’s Row, Spital Street, and Yorkersgate, Malton, will also face a week-long closure for kerbing works.

The closure starts on August 3 and ends on August 10.

All closures are subject to access to premises.

For more information, you can contact North Yorkshire Council’s customer service centre on 0300 131 2 131 or check their roadworks map at northyorks.gov.uk/roadworks-map.

For Moor Lane, you can also contact Spectrum Comms Solutions Ltd on behalf of Quickline Communications on 07856 910564.
