The actor and comedian is reportedly in negotiations to join an upcoming Netflix adaptation of Harlan Coben’s Myron Bolitar.

In an Instagram statement, Fineman described her time at SNL as the “greatest privilege” of her life and said it felt like the “right time” for her next chapter.

She joined the show in 2019 and was particularly known for her impersonations of celebrities including Melania Trump, Britney Spears, and Nicole Kidman.

Fineman reflected on the intense and emotional experience of working on SNL, calling the people there her “family” and the place her “home”.
