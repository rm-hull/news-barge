## New York gift shop wins plaudits as it sells worldwide

Filiz Oz in front of her shop *(Image: Darren Greenwood)*

A recently opened gift store is receiving plaudits and enjoying success with its locally-designed and unique items.

Filiz Oz opened York Curios at 24 Coney Street a few weeks ago, where the Peggy bridal outlet used to be.

Filiz, who came to York from Turkey 12 years ago, has a background in wholesale, supplying businesses across Jersey, Ireland, London and elsewhere.

But with her children getting older, she felt it was the right time to open her own business.

The mum-of-two told The Press: “We design and manufacturer it ourselves. It is mostly made in Turkey but designed in York.

“We have materials like cotton, we use wood, stone, we have cotton bags. Our guy Fawkes socks are 80 per cent good quality. Everything is good quality.”

  Filiz with some of the jewelry 

*(Image: Darren Greenwood)*

“We don’t get anything from suppliers. Everything we have in the shop is designed exclusively. It’s all unique to us.”

The signage for the store was done by Tom Banks of York and Filiz says “people love the sign.”

  An extensive range of bags are available 

*(Image: Darren Greenwood)*

Among the best sellers is a bag featuring ghosts, with customers buying them from places including Ireland and Alaska.

“I have sold a hundred so far," Filiz said.

She continued: “The other souvenir shops are more Oxford Street and London. My customers say our store is so unique. I am creating products for people that love York.”

  Plenty of fridge magnets to choose from 

*(Image: Darren Greenwood)*

This includes a jewellery section at the back, with designs all created by Filiz.

Filiz said: “This is a 400-year-old building owned by the National Trust. We are so proud to be in this building. We are so happy to be in Coney Street.

“I wanted to be in the city centre. I wanted to be within the city walls, to show my products to local shoppers and tourists.”

  So many colours to enjoy 

*(Image: Darren Greenwood)*

“A customer who visited last week sent a letter to say they loved the shop. I feel lucky I am surrounded by such lovely people in York.”

Such praise is already borne online, with Google awarding the shop 4.5 stars out of five, based on eight reviews.

The latest gave five stars out of five and said: “Today, while strolling around York, we stopped at this souvenir shop.

  The ghost bag has been extremely popular 

*(Image: Darren Greenwood)*

“They had some really nice souvenirs; we were just planning to buy magnets, but our cards didn't work, so the shop owner gave us one for free. We'd like to thank them again!”

Another said: “Visited today. Lovely shop with lots of really nice gifts. Lady was really nice, friendly and helpful. Will be visiting again tomorrow, before we go home. Lovely gifts and great prices. Well worth a visit.”
