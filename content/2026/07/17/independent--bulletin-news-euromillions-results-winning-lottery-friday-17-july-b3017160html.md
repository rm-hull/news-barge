# EuroMillions results: Winning lottery and Thunderball numbers for Friday 17 July

- Tonight’s EuroMillions draw has offered players the chance to win a jackpot of £42million.
- To win the prize, a player had to get five main numbers plus two lucky stars.
- In the UK, every EuroMillions entry automatically includes a unique "UK Millionaire Maker" code, entering you into an additional draw with a guaranteed £1 million winner in every draw.
- The winning EuroMillions numbers were **12, 21, 23, 34** and**40**. The Lucky Stars were** 9**and** 10**.
- The winning Thunderball numbers were **5, 8, 20, 24** and**25**. The Thunderball was** 1**.
