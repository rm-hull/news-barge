# Watch moment water sprouts from Yorkshire road as water main bursts - where road closure is in place

## A burst water main has led to a road closure, with a signed diversion in place, in Yorkshire.

Footage shows water sprouting from a burst water main in Yorkshire, which has led to a road closure.

The footage, taken by Joseph Saadeddin on Thursday 16 July, shows water sprouting from Huddersfield Road, near the Spen Valley Greenway.

The road closure is in place from the junction of Ravensfield Road to the traffic lights on Huddersfield Road.

![Burst water main on Huddersfield Road on Thursday 16 July.](https://www.yorkshirepost.co.uk/jpim-static/image/2026/07/17/10/34/Red-And-Black-Modern-Tips-Trick-YouTube-Thumbnail-(1600-x-1080-px)-(1600-x-1080-px)-(1600-x-1080-px)-2c337ee9.jpg?trim=7,0,7,0&width=640&quality=65)



A Yorkshire Water spokesperson said: "We experienced a burst water main on Huddersfield Road yesterday (16 July) afternoon.

“Our team were on site quickly to isolate the main and begin a repair. Due to its location and the consequential damage caused to the road surface, the road is currently closed, with a signed diversion in place, to ensure the repair and road reinstatement can be carried out safely.

“We thank customers in the area for their patience while we complete this work and apologise for any inconvenience caused."
