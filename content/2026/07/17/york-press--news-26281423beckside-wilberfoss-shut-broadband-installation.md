Search the Public Notice PortalWork to install fibre broadband will see part of an East Yorkshire village road shut for three days.

From August 11, a section of Beckside, in Wilberfoss, will be closed while a 14-metre stretch of telecoms cable is put in place.

The closure will last until August 13 and covers the section from the junction with Willow Park Road north to Woldview Road, Field Lane and Birker Lane.

East Riding of Yorkshire Council said the work was needed to link new lines to existing telecoms infrastructure.

A diversion will be in place, directing traffic along Willow Park Road, Woldview Road, Field Lane and Birker Lane.

While the road will be closed to through traffic, access will remain for residents, emergency services and pedestrians.

The council said the order is valid for 18 months, but the work is expected to take three days.

Members of the public seeking further information regarding the road closure can contact Highways Customer Care via East Riding of Yorkshire Council by calling 01482 393939.
