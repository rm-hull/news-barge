A care home is hosting a summer fair to raise funds for a dementia charity.

The event will take place on Friday, July 24, from 2pm to 4pm at Care UK’s Chocolate Works in Bishopthorpe Road.

The fair will include live music, fairground games, a raffle and tombola, and a variety of stalls.

Ella Bond, deputy manager at Chocolate Works, said: "We’re excited to welcome the local community to our summer fair.

"It’s the perfect way to enjoy a sunny afternoon together, whilst raising money for such an important cause.

"Our fairs are always enjoyed by the local community, so the team has been hard at work to organise another event to remember."

Entertainment will be provided by singer Claire Louise, and visitors can browse stalls run by businesses including a bakery and a physiotherapist.

All proceeds will go to Alzheimer’s Society, which supports people living with dementia and their families.

Chocolate Works is a care home that offers residential, nursing, and dementia care, as well as short-term respite care.

The home features on-site amenities such as a cinema, hair salon, and pub, and is designed to enable residents to lead active and fulfilling lives, while also promoting independence.

For more information, visit: careuk.com/chocolate-works
