Amanda Knox discusses new documentary "Mouth of the Wolf: Amanda Knox Returns to Italy"

Amanda Knox’s story is explored in The Twisted Tale of Amanda Knox, with Knox serving as an executive producer on the series.

Knox was initially convicted of murdering British student Meredith Kercher while they were studying in Italy in 2007, before later being acquitted.

After her release from prison, Knox completed an undergraduate degree in creative writing at the University of Washington and published her memoir Waiting to Be Heard in 2013, followed by her second book Free: My Search for Meaning in 2025.

Knox now lives in the Seattle area with her husband, Christopher Robinson, and their two children, while hosting the podcast Hard Knox with Amanda Knox.

She appeared in Netflix’s 2016 documentary Amanda Knox, which was nominated for two Primetime Emmy Awards. In 2022, she returned to Italy for a face-to-face meeting with the prosecutor who helped send her to prison, a reunion that was later documented in the 2026 Hulu documentary Mouth of the Wolf: Amanda Knox Returns to Italy.
