# Jarrod Bowen commits future to West Ham despite Premier League relegation

![Club captain and England international Jarrod Bowen is among West Ham’s most prized players](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/05/24/18/ba5c8e068b434aa7d5fb1edf304deee2Y29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzc5Mjg5Mzk5-2.83804554.jpg)

- West Ham captain Jarrod Bowen has confirmed his commitment to the club, pledging to stay despite their recent relegation from the Premier League to the Championship.
- Bowen's primary motivation is to help the Hammers return to the top-flight, stating that the club means a lot to him after six and a half years of service.
- His decision was solidified after a meeting in Prague with new majority shareholder Daniel Kretinsky and Jiri Svarc, who presented a compelling vision for the club's future direction.
- The 29-year-old forward has a strong track record at West Ham, having scored 85 goals and provided 63 assists in 280 appearances, including the winning goal in the 2023 Conference League final.
- Bowen maintained strong form last season with 11 goals and 12 assists, and West Ham is scheduled to commence their Championship season against Burnley on 16 August.
