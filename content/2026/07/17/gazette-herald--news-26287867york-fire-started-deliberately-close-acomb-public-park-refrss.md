POLICE have been called after a fire was started deliberately in a wooded area close to a park in a York suburb.

  


North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said crews from Acomb were called to James Backhouse Place in Acomb around 7.15pm yesterday (Thursday, July 16).

The popular West Bank Park backs on to the cul-de-sac and a play area is close to the entrance.

A service spokesperson said the fire had been lit deliberately.

Fire crews used a hose reel jet to put it out and referred the incident to North Yorkshire Police.
