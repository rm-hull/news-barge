# Trump weighs in on England’s World Cup semi-final loss

- England lost 2-1 to Argentina in the World Cup 2026 semi-final, despite holding a 1-0 lead late in the game.
- Coach Thomas Tuchel has been widely criticised for making defensive substitutions after England scored, which many believe invited pressure from Argentina.
- Despite the criticism, the Football Association has publicly backed Tuchel, who previously extended his contract through 2028.
- President Donald Trump has weighed in on the debate, questioning Tuchel's tactical decisions during the semi-final match.
- Trump specifically commented on the perceived mistake of having England captain Harry Kane play defensively after the team took the lead.
