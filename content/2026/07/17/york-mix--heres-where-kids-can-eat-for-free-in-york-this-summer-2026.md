The summer school holidays are here and we’ll all looking to save some pennies while making precious memories with family.

### Ask Italian

**Kids eat free this summer**

Get two kids meal’s for free when ordering from the Kids Menu and at least one adult main meal is ordered. For ASK Perks rewards app members only – download the app to get your code.

Valid every day from 17 July to 6 September 2026.

- The Grand Assembly Rooms, Blake St, York YO1 8QG
- Website

### Beefeater The Millfield

**Kids eat free breakfast and kids eat for less** 

Two kids under 16 eat for free with every adult breakfast purchased.

From now until** 1 September**, we’re taking part in the government’s Great British Summer Savings scheme by reducing VAT on kids’ food and drink from ** 20% to 5%***. 

- White Rose Close, York Business Park, Nether Poppleton, York YO26 6RL
- Website

### Bella Italia

**Kids eat free**

Every day from 20 July to 1 September 2026, one child can receive one free Kids’ Meal when accompanied by an adult purchasing a main meal.

Outside of our summer special, kids eat free Sunday – Thursday all year round.

- 89 Low Petergate, York, YO1 2HY & Unit 6, Clifton Moor Gate Leisure Park, Stirling Road, York, YO30 4XY
- Website

- Website

This offer is available from 25 June to 1 September 2026 in our restaurants and includes a free child’s drink.
