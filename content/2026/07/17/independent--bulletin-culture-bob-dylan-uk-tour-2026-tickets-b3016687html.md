# When is Bob Dylan’s UK tour – and how can I get tickets?

- Bob Dylan is scheduled to embark on a UK tour in November and December, featuring five dates in London.
- The tour will also include performances in Blackpool, Sheffield, Birmingham and Bournemouth.
- All concerts will be phone and camera-free, with attendees required to place their mobile devices in Yondr pouches.
- Tickets for the tour will be available for general sale from **10am on 22 July** via the singer's website.
- The 85-year-old acclaimed singer-songwriter last performed in the UK on his Rough And Rowdy Ways Tour.
