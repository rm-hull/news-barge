The set of EastEnders has been broken into by a YouTube streamer for the third time in six years.

Mr Airborne uploaded a video in which he could be seen scaling a fence and accessing the set at Elstree Studios.

An “urban explorer” with over 34,000 subscribers, Mr Airborne claimed this was not his first time trespassing onto the set, having previously done so four years ago.

In a disclaimer, the YouTuber said he made the video for “entertainment and documentary purposes”, urging viewers to “respect private property and never trespass or attempt to recreate what you see in this video”.

Previous break-ins include a YouTube prank duo in 2021 and a trio of streamers during lockdown in 2020.
