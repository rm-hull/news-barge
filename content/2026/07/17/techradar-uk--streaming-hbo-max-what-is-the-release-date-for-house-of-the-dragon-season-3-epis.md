*House of the Dragon* season 3 will officially enter its back half later this week. Indeed, with four episodes down and just as many left to be released before this season's final end credits sequence rolls, the high fantasy show's latest multi-part installment will be over before we know it.

Regardless, plenty of melodrama, fiery action, and shock moments await us over the next few episodes, so when will *House of the Dragon*'s next entry present itself to the world? You know the drill by now: read on to find out more!

## What is the launch time for House of the Dragon season 3 episode 5?

![House of the Dragon | Episode 5 Preview | HBO Max - YouTube](https://img.youtube.com/vi/wZxbzcci56E/maxresdefault.jpg) 

*House of the Dragon* season 3 episode 5 will premiere at**6pm PT / 9pm ET on Sunday, July 19** in the US and Canada. Readers who live elsewhere will have to wait until Monday, July 20 to check it out.

As for the time that the *Game of Thrones***prequel's next installment will internationally, the below list will tell you more:

- **US**— Sunday, July 12 at 6pm PT / 9pm ET
- **Canada**— Sunday, July 12 at 6pm PT / 9pm ET
- **UK**— Monday, July13 at 2am BST
- **India**— Monday, July 13 at 6:30am IST
- **Singapore**— Monday, July 13 at 9am SGT
- **Australia**— Monday, July 13 at 11am AEST
- **New Zealand**— Monday, July 13 at 1pm NZST

## How do I watch House of the Dragon season 3 episode 5?

By loading up one of the best streaming services in your region of the world, of course.

For the vast majority of you, HBO Max is the platform you'll need to start up — as long as you have an active account, anyway — to catch the HBO TV Original's next entry. However, if you haven't signed up to the Warner Bros-owned streamer, or if it hasn't launched where you live, don't fret. Scroll on to see what other TV networks and streamers it'll be available on in your neck of the woods:
