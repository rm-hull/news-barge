The Great Yorkshire Show continued to draw thousands of visitors on its third day as exhibitors, farmers and families enjoyed another packed programme in Harrogate.

The show rings were once again filled with prize-winning livestock, with cattle and horse competitions taking centre stage as handlers of all ages showcased months of hard work.

Elsewhere, visitors gathered around the Main Ring for demonstrations and entertainment, while music added to the festival atmosphere.

The warm weather brought large crowds across the showground, where people explored hundreds of trade stands, food and drink stalls and agricultural exhibits celebrating the very best of Yorkshire's farming community.

Our photographer captured some of the standout moments from Thursday (Jul 16), from young competitors leading cattle into the ring to colourful characters, bustling crowds and the enduring traditions that make the Great Yorkshire Show one of the county's biggest annual events.
