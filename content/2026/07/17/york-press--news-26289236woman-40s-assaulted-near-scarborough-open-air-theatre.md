A WOMAN in her 40s was assaulted while waiting in a queue outside a toilet block near a popular North Yorkshire music venue.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following an assault that took place in Scarborough earlier this month.

The attack happened on Burniston Road, opposite the Open Air Theatre, at 10.35pm on Friday, July 3.
