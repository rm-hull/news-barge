## Homes in York suburbs left without water due to burst mains

An emergency supplier of bulk water is also on site in Fifth Avenue this morning *(Image: Kevin Glenton)*

RESIDENTS of homes in York suburbs may be starting their day with no water this morning (Friday, July 17).

Yorkshire Water is advising its customers in YO10 and YO31 postcodes, Osbaldwick and Tang Hall they may be experiencing no water or low pressure.

  


The utility company currently has engineers on site close to the Fifth Avenue junction with Derwent Way and Ennerdale Avenue, where a water main has burst.

A standpipe has been erected and a lorry which appears to be from an emergency supplier is parked up.
