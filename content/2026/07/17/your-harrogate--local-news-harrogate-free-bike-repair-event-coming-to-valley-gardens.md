                                    Cyclists can get their bikes checked and repaired for free in Harrogate tomorrow (Saturday).

Changing the Landscape CIC will be offering free bike safety checks, tune-ups and basic repairs at Valley Gardens from 11am until 2:30pm as part of Cycling UK's Big Bike Revival initiative.

The community event will take place near the skatepark, with no booking required.

Visitors can also get advice on bike maintenance, servicing and cycling, with volunteers on hand throughout the session.

The event is free to attend and aims to help more people keep their bikes safe and roadworthy while encouraging more cycling in the community.
