Tens of thousands turned out this week for the Great Yorkshire Show 2026.

The 167th edition four-day celebration of farming, food and countryside life ran from Tuesday to Friday (July 14-17) at the Great Yorkshire Showground in Harrogate.

The sold out show included 8,500 animals across livestock classes, sheep-shearing, heavy horses, cattle parades and agricultural machinery on display.
