## Bid to sell booze later at York store lodged

B&M at York's Foss Islands Retail Park could be allowed to sell alcohol later under new plans *(Image: Newsquest)*

Search the Public Notice PortalA York store could sell alcohol later under plans lodged with the council.

A licensing application lodged by B&M, in Foss Islands Retail Park, would see it allowed to open and sell alcohol from 7am to 11pm daily.

The store’s application stated changes to the layout of its licensed area to allow the installation of self-service checkouts were also included in its plans.

B&M’s changes would see it permitted to open an hour earlier than its existing opening time of 8am and one hour later than 10pm when it currently closes.
