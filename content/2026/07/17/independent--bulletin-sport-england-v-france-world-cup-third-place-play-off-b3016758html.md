# What is England’s record in World Cup third place play-off matches?

- England will face France in the World Cup third place play-off in Miami on Saturday, with the game due to kick-off at 10pm BST (5pm ET).
- England failed to reach Sunday’s final after losing 2-1 to Argentina on Wednesday, while France were beaten 2-0 by Spain 24 hours earlier.
- This will be the third time that England have played in the Bronze final, having previously competed in the match in 1990 and 2018.
- England lost 2-1 to hosts Italy in the 1990 play-off, and were then beaten 2-0 by Belgium when the two sides met in the play-off eight years ago.
- Having lost their previous two play-off matches, England will hope its third time lucky as Thomas Tuchel’s men aim to finish the tournament on a high following Wednesday’s semi-final disappointment.
