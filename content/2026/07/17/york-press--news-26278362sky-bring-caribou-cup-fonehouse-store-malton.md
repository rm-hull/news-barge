Football fans are in for a treat this weekend as a legendary trophy goes on display in Malton.

To mark the Start of the 2026/27 Football season live on Sky Sports, Sky in partnership with Fonehouse are bringing customers a unique opportunity to get up close in person with the Caribou Cup.

On Saturday, July 18, the Cup will be in-store at Fonehouse in Wheelgate, from 9.30am giving fans a unique opportunity to see the iconic prize up close in person.

A spokesperson for Sky said: "The event is also the perfect opportunity for customers to explore Sky Stream and Broadband, including incredible savings on Sky products. Customers also have the opportunity to take up great deals on Sky Sports in store to gain access to every over 1400 live games this season,

"Whether you're a dedicated supporter or simply love the atmosphere of the game, this is your chance to experience the real Caribou Cup in person and explore Sky’s latest offers inside your local Fonehouse Store. Come down to store and have your photo taken with the Cup."
