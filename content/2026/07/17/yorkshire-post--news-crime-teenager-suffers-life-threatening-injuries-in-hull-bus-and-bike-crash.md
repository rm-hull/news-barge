# Teenager suffers life-threatening injuries in Hull bus and bike crash

## A 15-year-old boy has been left with life-threatening injuries after a crash involving a bus and a bicycle in Hull.

At around 9:50pm on Thursday (Jul 16), police responded to reports of a crash in Hull.

The incident occurred on Bellfield Avenue and involved a bus and a bicycle.

The bus driver and a cyclist were travelling southbound towards Saltshouse Road before they were involved in the crash.

![](https://www.yorkshirepost.co.uk/jpim-static/image/2026/07/17/12/01/Bellfield-Avenue-Hull.png?trim=0,34,0,34&crop=&width=640&quality=65)



The cyclist, a 15-year-old boy, was taken to hospital to be treated for injuries that are believed to be life threatening at this time.

Police have said no one else is believed to have sustained any serious injuries as a result of the incident.

An appeal has been launched for any witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

A spokesperson for Humberside Police said: “If anyone witnessed the collision, has any dashcam or CCTV that may assist officers with ongoing enquiries, please contact 101 quoting log 639 of 16 July 2026.”
