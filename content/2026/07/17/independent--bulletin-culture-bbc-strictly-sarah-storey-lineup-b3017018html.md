Dame Sarah Storey, Britain's most successful Paralympian with 19 gold medals, has been announced as the seventh celebrity contestant for Strictly Come Dancing 2026.

The 48-year-old, who recently retired from her illustrious career in swimming and cycling, will take on the dancefloor as her next challenge.

Storey expressed her excitement for the new venture, highlighting her long-standing admiration for the show and her desire to learn, inspired by her daughter.

Born with a non-functioning left hand, Dame Sarah began her Paralympic journey in swimming at the age of 14, later transitioning to cycling.

She joins a line-up that includes Lacey Turner, Dani Dyer, Delta Goodrem, Chris Appleton, Cach Mercer, and Will Best, with Emma Willis, Johannes Radebe, and Josh Widdicombe confirmed as new hosts.
