A group of Brownies has helped give a warm welcome to new arrivals in their village through a creative community project.

Barratt Homes teamed up with the 1st Copmanthorpe Brownies to design welcome cards for residents moving into the new Russet Park development in Copmanthorpe.

Daisy Butcher was named the winner of the competition, with her card professionally printed, ready to be distributed around the development.

The cards highlight the Brownies’ favourite places in Copmanthorpe, including parks, cafes, and children’s clubs.

A spokesperson for the 1st Copmanthorpe Brownies said: "This was a great opportunity for the Brownies to be involved in the community, and their artistic talents really shone through."

Paul Hogan, sales director at Barratt Homes Yorkshire East, added: "We’re grateful to the 1st Copmanthorpe Brownie unit for taking part and designing some fantastic cards."

Russet Park offers one- to five-bedroom homes and is located less than five miles from York.
