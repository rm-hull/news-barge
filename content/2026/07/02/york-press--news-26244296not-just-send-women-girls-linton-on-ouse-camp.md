**Why not just send women and girls to Linton-on-Ouse camp?**

INSTEAD of sending over a thousand men to the RAF camp at Linton-on-Ouse, therefore outnumbering the population of 700, why not lower the number to say 300 women and children.

People would feel a lot safer.

  *Joanne Ellis,*

  *Charlton Street,*

  *York*

  **Why York is a City of Sanctuary for all**

COUNCILLOR Mark Warters, of the City of York Council, comments on suggested plans for Linton-On-Ouse to house what he calls "1,200…… illegal immigrants supposedly ‘seeking asylum’ ".

Some facts are in order. Those facing being housed at Linton and other former MOD properties are not here illegally. They have entered the UK, legally sought asylum, and their cases are of sufficient merit for the Home Office to allow them to remain while their application is investigated. It is no fault of asylum seekers that the system is so slow.

Cllr Warters suggests that the 1200 are "supposedly seeking asylum". There is no supposedly about it. These are registered asylum seekers.

He boasts that he was the only councillor to vote against the council seeking to become a City of Sanctuary. What is a City of Sanctuary? It is a place where welcome is offered to those fleeing for their lives and in need of refuge; where hospitality is given to those who have lost their homes in war or disaster elsewhere and need to make a new start in their lives.

It is a city where a pathway to integration in the local community is offered to the sanctuary seeker. Why is Cllr Warters so proud to vote against such a proposal?

He suggests that all those seeking entry to the UK are "invaders". Such a threatening word is written to stoke up fear, distrust and hostility towards the stranger. Is that the country you want to live in? Is Cllr Warters' ward of Osbaldwick and Derwent the part of York where such values are widely held? Really?

  *Paul Wordsworth*

  *Co-ordinator: York City of Sanctuary*
