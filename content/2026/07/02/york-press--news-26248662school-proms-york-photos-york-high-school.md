## Fab photos from York school proms as pupils dress up for end-of-term fun

Pupils from York High School at their prom at Mercure Fairfield Manor

TEENAGERS from a York school stepped out in style for their end of year prom.

Our photos show teens from York High School all dressed up for the event which took place at Mercure York Fairfield Manor Hotel at Skelton.

Press reader Shaunna Stead sent in the photos which show her son Tyler Savory with friends from York High, including Riley Goldsmith and Toby Partridge.
