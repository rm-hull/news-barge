# Popular Harrogate owners look back on 26 years of 'wonderful moments' at their much-loved shop

## This week marks 26 years since a popular Harrogate couple first opened the doors to their highly respected independent shop.

It was on July 1, 2000 that co-owners Sue and Stephen Kramer first launched the independently owned Crown Jewellers.

The cheery couple's little shop at 23 Commercial Street may be small, but its expertise and reputation with customers is huge.

Packed with unique pieces, including beautiful pre-owned, vintage and antique jewellery and new contemporary silver jewellery, it was voted one of the UK’s favourite local businesses in a nationwide competition in 2023 sponsored by local gift card giants Town & City Gift Cards.

This likable couple had been hoping to celebrate Crown Jewellers’s 25th anniversary last year but, busy as ever, they ran out of time!

So, unusually, this year has become a rather special one for both of them.

Sue and Stephen said: “There have been so many wonderful moments, a few unexpected twists and turns, plenty of laughter and, above all, the privilege of getting to know so many lovely people along the way.

"We weren’t able to celebrate our 25th anniversary, and somehow the year has completely run away with us, so we’ve decided to quietly celebrate our 26th by simply saying thank you.

"Over the years you’ve recommended us to family and friends, left lovely Google reviews, sent thoughtful cards and messages, and even surprised us with flowers, chocolates, the occasional bottle of champagne.

"This year, two knitted peas, which now have pride of place in the shop and still make us smile every day.”

In 2024, the couple rebalanced their life a little by moving to opening three days a week.

Some may have looked on it a slight gamble but Sue and Stephen say it showed them that sometimes slowing the pace a little is the best way to keep doing what you love.

"We have adapted our shop to the many challenges, both personal and business, that have been thrown at us and we always come out smiling and sparkling on the other side,” the couple said.

“Our extra ‘day off’ has rather quickly become our website day, and we’re thoroughly enjoying creating something special."

After 26 years of successful trading in the heart of Harrogate, the owners of Crown Jewellers remain grateful to their loyal customers for “quietly cheering us on” and putting their trust in them for all these years.

"Looking back, we feel incredibly fortunate to have helped thousands of customers celebrate life’s special moments, " said Sue and Stephen.

"We look forward to welcoming you into the shop for many years to come.”
