Despite a challenging matchup, York Croquet Club recently secured a 4–2 win over Huddersfield.

The York team, including Cathie Dance, Hazel Qureshi, Helen Griffiths, and Richard Lambourne, started with a win and a loss in the morning's doubles matches.

The afternoon singles saw Lambourne achieve a dominant win, restricting his opponent to just one hoop.

Griffiths also secured a comfortable victory, while Qureshi won a tense match by three hoops in the final innings.

Dance, however, lost her match after falling foul of a rare croquet rule, with Huddersfield known for their detailed knowledge of the game.

Despite this, York's overall performance was enough to secure a convincing victory against one of the circuit's toughest teams, leaving the home side in high spirits.

The match was played at York's home ground, and the win against Huddersfield, known for their strong play, was particularly satisfying for the York team.
