A serious crash shut a stretch of the A19 for several hours this morning (Thursday).

The southbound carriageway was closed from the A684 Northallerton junction to the A168 Thirsk junction.

It has now reopened.

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/a19-closure-2-july-2026.webp)

Police first issued an alert at 4.27am today, although the AA said it was first reported shortly after 2am .

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Please avoid the area if you can, and use alternative routes or use the diversion via Northallerton and Thirsk, where possible while emergency services work at the scene.”

At 9.45am, National Highways Yorkshire posted: “The A19 is now open southbound between A684 Osmotherley and the A168 South Kilvington following a serious collision.

“On scene investigation works have been completed.

“No significant residual delays in the area.”
