A serious crash has closed part of the A19 in North Yorkshire.

Emergency services are on the scene near Thirsk on Thursday morning (July 2).

North Yorkshire Police said the A19 is closed southbound from A684 Northallerton to A168 Thirsk.

A force spokesperson urged drivers to “please avoid the area if you can and use alternative routes or use the diversion via Northallerton and Thirsk, where possible while emergency services work at the scene”.

The AA said traffic is “coping well” with the detour in operation.

National Highways first reported the crash at 2.50am and said the A19 is expected to be closed "until at least midday".

Drivers are facing delays of 10 minutes on the approach, National Highways said at 7.15am.

A National Highways spokesperson said crash investigation work is being undertaken by police and "recovery is likely to be protracted".
