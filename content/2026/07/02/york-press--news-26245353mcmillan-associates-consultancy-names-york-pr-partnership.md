A York-based HR consultancy has appointed a new creative communications partner to support its growth plans.

McMillan & Associates has selected fellow York firm Partners PR to lead its public relations activity with the aim of raising its profile in the SME market.

Founded in 2018 by Natalie McMillan, the consultancy now has a 15-strong team and offers a wide range of HR and organisational development services.

Ms McMillan said: "Our business is poised for growth and there are opportunities to educate the SME sector on key HR topics.

"It was important we found a communications partner that shared our straight-talking, pragmatic style and fully understood our values to get that messaging out there.

"We have found that with Partners PR.

"The team has already added value through the early opportunities they have generated."

Partners PR will deliver media relations, thought leadership, and wider communications support targeting business leaders and HR decision-makers.
