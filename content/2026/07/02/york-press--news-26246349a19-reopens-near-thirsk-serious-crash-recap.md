## Our live feed has now finished.

- A serious crash has closed part of the A19 in North Yorkshire.
- Emergency services are on the scene near Thirsk this morning (Thursday, July 2).
- North Yorkshire Police says the A19 is closed southbound from A684 Northallerton to A168 Thirsk.
- A force spokesperson has urged drivers to “please avoid the area if you can and use alternative routes or use the diversion via Northallerton and Thirsk, where possible while emergency services work at the scene”.
- The AA says traffic is “coping well” with a detour in operation.
- National Highways first reported the crash at 2.50am and says the A19 is expected to be closed "until at least midday".
