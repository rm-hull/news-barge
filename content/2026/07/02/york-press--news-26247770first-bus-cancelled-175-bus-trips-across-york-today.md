MORE than 175 bus journeys are set to be cancelled across York today

First Bus has warned that 179 trips across nine of its services will not run today (July 2) due to issues with the mix of buses in the company’s fleet - with 102 of the cancellations affecting Park and Ride journeys.

These changes will affect the following routes: 1A (10 cancelled trips); 5 (five cancelled trips); 5A (four cancelled trips); 6 (nine cancelled trips); 9 (52 cancelled trip); 10 (ten cancelled trips); 59 (50 cancelled trips); U1 (20 cancelled trips); U2 (19 cancelled trips).

The company added that these changes may continue into the coming days.

A spokesperson for First Bus said: “Unfortunately, due to a technical review of some vehicles, we have had to make some temporary changes to how we allocate buses on our routes in and around York.

"A temporary change to the mix of buses in our York fleet means there will be some minor disruption to services. We have taken steps to minimise this from Thursday and in the coming days, and apologise for any inconvenience.

“You may see a few buses on the park and ride in another livery but destination screens will display the service number and route so you know you are using the correct bus.

“We are very sorry for the disruption and inconvenience caused for our customers in York today.

“We are doing all we can to minimise the impact to journeys as much as possible but we recognise this will mean changes to many journeys."

They added that customers are advised to check before travelling and visit the company’s website for regular updates
