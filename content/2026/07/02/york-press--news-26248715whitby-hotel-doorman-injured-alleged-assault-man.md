THE DOORMAN of a popular hotel in a North Yorkshire coastal town has been injured after an alleged assault.

The incident took place at The George Hotel in Whitby in the early hours of May 31.

North Yorkshire Police said the alleged assault was "completely unprovoked" and said the venue had provided the CCTV image of the suspect, urging anyone who recognises him to come forward.

  


A force spokesperson said: "Please contact us if you recognise the man pictured, we are keen to speak to him.

"Please email [ed.chatland@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:ed.chatland@northyorkshire.police.uk) if you recognise him or have information that could assist.

"Alternatively, you can call North Yorkshire Police on 101, and ask for PC 1824 Ed Chatland or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Website.

"Please quote reference 12260100684 when passing on information."
