# More than 1,000 stunning cars will head for North Yorkshire's beautiful Newby Hall soon

## More than a thousand vintage and classic cars are set to roll into lovely Newby Hall near Ripon in a fortnight’s time.

Taking place on Sunday, July 19 from 10am to 3pm, the Historic Vehicle Rally, organised by North of England Classics and Pre‑War Automobiles, will bring together an impressive line‑up of stunning vintage vehicles from across the country.

From Aston Martins to Wolseleys, the rally showcases a full spectrum of motoring heritage in this historic estate’s award‑winning grounds.

The 1,000-plus vehicles on display hail from all eras and must have first been registered at least 41 years ago.

Alongside the extensive car displays, visitors will be able to browse a large autojumble, ideal for enthusiasts searching for parts, tools and memorabilia.

Real Markets will also be at the show, offering high‑quality Yorkshire produce, handmade crafts and speciality goods from local makers.

Newby Hall is one of Britain's finest Adam Houses.

Built in the 1690s by Sir Christopher Wren, the 17th century award-winning house and famous gardens, was enlarged and adapted by John Carr and Robert Adam.

Still in private ownership, Newby Hall is the home of the Compton family.

The Hall and Gardens won a prestigious gold medal at the RHS Chelsea Flower Show 2025, its first ever at the world’s most famous horticultural event.

A woodland-themed display highlighted some of the most eye-catching and unusual varieties of Cornus, a nod to Newby Hall’s renowned National Plant Collection of Cornus.

Its 25 acre gardens include a newly restored Edwardian rock garden, one of the largest and most significant in the UK, one of Europe’s largest double herbaceous borders, an enchanted woodland walk, a miniature railway and a children’s adventure playground as well as dolls house and teddy bear exhibitions.

Newby Hall and Gardens was Yorkshire Attraction of the Year 2022 and National Garden of the Year 2019.

It is also internationally celebrated for having one of the largest and most complete surviving Chippendale interiors.

Tickets include entry to Newby Hall’s gardens and adventure playground.

Visitors wishing to tour the house can upgrade their ticket once inside.
