## Police swoop on driver in busy York street

 *(Image: Newsquest)*

Police have swooped on a driver in a busy York street.

North Yorkshire Police stopped a vehicle using blue lights in Walmgate at 10.42am on Thursday (July 2).

  


Officers subsequently called on the help of a recovery service to remove the vehicle after checks showed it was being driven without valid vehicle tax.
