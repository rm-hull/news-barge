A person has been taken to hospital after being cut from a vehicle following a crash in a North Yorkshire village.

Firefighters and paramedics were called to the two-vehicle collision in Harrogate Road, North Rigton, near Harrogate, at 8.40pm on Wednesday (July 1).

One person was trapped due to the crash and was cut out of their vehicle then taken to hospital, North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said.

In a statement, the fire service said: “Harrogate crews were mobilised to a two-vehicle road traffic collision with one person trapped.

“Crews used cutting gear to extricate the casualty.

“They were transported to hospital by road ambulance.”
