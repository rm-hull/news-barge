## Inside ‘stylish’ hidden flat off busy York city centre street for sale - for £425,000

Outside at the two-bedroom flat for sale in Stonegate Court, off Blake Street in York, for £425,000 *(Image: Ashtons/Zoopla)*

A “stylish” flat tucked away off a busy York city centre street has hit the market – for £425,000.

The two-bedroom flat is for sale in Stonegate Court, off Blake Street, and has views towards York Minster.

Ashtons, which is marketing the first floor property, said it is offers “stylish and spacious living within the historic city walls”.

  Inside the two-bedroom flat for sale in Stonegate Court, off Blake Street in York, for £425,000 

*(Image: Ashtons/Zoopla)*

Inside, a hallway leads into the open plan space which Ashtons said has a “distinctive New York loft style feel” with exposed brick walls and high ceilings.

An open archway leads to the kitchen with a range of fitted units.

There are two double bedrooms, with the main bedroom benefitting from an en-suite shower room.

The second bedroom is served by the home’s main bathroom and features tiled flooring, an oval bath and rainfall shower.

According to the listing, the flat is a leasehold property with 100 years remaining on the lease.

It has a ground rent of £600 per year and a yearly £2,383.35 service charge.
