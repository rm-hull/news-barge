The fire and rescue service have been called to a crash involving two vehicles in North Yorkshire.

The incident took place at 7.20pm on the B6161, Oaker Bank in Harrogate on Saturday (July 11).

It involved an electric vehicle and a diesel vehicle, North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said.

  


Fire crews from Harrogate and Knaresborough were on hand to provide traffic management and monitor the electric vehicle’s temperature with a thermal imaging camera.

All passengers were out on the firefighters’ arrival and no injuries were recorded.
