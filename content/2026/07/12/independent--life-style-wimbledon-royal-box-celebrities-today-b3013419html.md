The Wimbledon men’s title will be decided today, with Kate Middleton and Nicole Kidman among the celebrities watching the action from the exclusive Royal Box this afternoon (Sunday, 12 July).

Located in a prime viewing spot, the super luxe Centre Court enclosure has been used to welcome friends and guests of Wimbledon since 1922.

So far this tournament, the likes of Benedict Cumberbatch, Celia Imrie and tennis legend Roger Federer have all been bagged invites, with Jason Isaacs, Elle Fanning, and Armando Iannucci also stopping by.

Day 14 of the competition will see World No 1 Jannik Sinner attempt to win his second Wimbledon, with French Open champion Alexander Zverev his final hurdle before victory.

Zverev’s first Wimbledon final is clouded by domestic abuse allegations that have marred his career, and which he has always strenuously denied.

The men’s final follows on from Linda Noskova winning her first grand slam title on Saturday afternoon to became the latest Czech player to lift the Venus Rosewater Dish as she overcame compatriot Karolina Muchova in a thrilling three-set battle.

Here are all the celebrities in the Royal Box today (12 July)...

**Kate Middleton, Prince George and Princess Charlotte**

![Catherine, Princess of Wales, Princess Charlotte of Wales and Prince George of Wales are seen in the Royal Box](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/12/12/40/GettyImages-1548406588.jpg)

After steeping out on centre court in a striking red peplum dress for the women’s final yesterday, Kate Middleton is back at the Championships for the final day, alongside her children George and Charlotte.

The patron of the All England Lawn Tennis Club (AELTC) is joined also by Prince Michael of Kent, Lord and Lady Frederick Windsor, and Lady Gabriella Windsor.

**Nicole Kidman**

![Kidman cut a sharp figure in a white suit](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/12/14/01KXB7SC0VHFS6VTYDGWMKWAHF.jpg)

Hollywood star Nicole Kidman (*Babygirl*,* Eyes Wide Shut*) was spotted taking a rare afternoon off from her numerous projects to enjoy the tennis in the VIP section. Her appearance comes ahead of the release of her forthcoming sequel to her classic 1998 hit* Practical Magic*. 

**Anna Wintour**

![Kidman was joined by former 'Vogue' editor Anna Wintour](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/12/14/01KXB7MHAAA8Z9MYQCRKT0BB6H.jpg)

The former *Vogue* editor Anna Wintour, who stepped down from her position at the fashion magazine in June last year, cut a stylish display as she arrived at the All England club to see the men’s final show down. 

**Sienna Miller and Oli Green**

![Actor couple Sienna Miller and Oli Green will also attend following their recent engagement](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/12/12/25/GettyImages-2224102756.jpg)

*Alfie* star Sienna Miller and her actor husband Oli Green, who got engaged last month shortly after welcoming their second child together in May, enjoyed a day out on sunny centre court. 

**Raye**

![Singer Raye channeled old school glamour as she stepped out at this year's Championships](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/12/12/39/GettyImages-2285743036.jpeg)

“Where is My Husband” singer Raye (born Rachel Keen) was spotted on centre court alongside her manager, British singer-songwriter and producer Paul Keen. Alongside Sarah Keen, he guides the career of Raye and other rising UK acts through their company, Sibling.

**Dustin Hoffman**

![Dustin Hoffman and his wife Lisa are also in attendance for the men's final](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/12/14/01KXAX58KD0EB1XN5MNM7EM7RT.jpg)

Actor Dustin Hoffman (*Midnight Cowboy*,* A Walk on the Moon*,* Tootsie*) looked sharp as he attended the Championships with his wife of almost five decades, Lisa. 

**Ben Stiller**

![Ben Stiller was also in the Royal Box](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/12/14/01KXB5TG98Y9HAQVTQ58066HZS.jpg)

*Zoolander* star Ben Stiller, who made a surprise visit to an airfield in Norfolk earlier this week as part of his research for his new film based on a second world war pilot, attended the Championships alongside his wife and actor Christine Taylor (*The Brady Bunch Movie*,* The Wedding Singer*). 

**Rami Malek**

![Former Bond villain Rami Malek is no stranger to the Royal Box](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/12/12/36/GettyImages-2285505199.jpg)

Actor Rami Malek (*No Time to Die*,* Mr Robot*,* Bohemian Rhapsody*,* Nuremberg*) stepped out at centre court alongside his agent Ben Curtis to watch the men’s singles final. 

**Amol Rajan**

!['The Independent' former editor Amol Rajan was among guests on the tournament's final day](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/12/12/45/GettyImages-2234346244.jpeg)

Journalist, broadcaster and former editor of *The Independent* Amol Rajan also went down to Wimbledon to watch the men’s final. Rajan presented the*Today* programme on BBC Radio 4 from 2021 to 2026 and has presented*University Challenge* on BBC Two since 2023.
