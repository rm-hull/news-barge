# Trump pays tribute to ‘true American Patriot’ Lindsey Graham following his death

- Donald Trump has paid tribute to his close ally, Senator Lindsey Graham, following his death on Saturday evening at 71 after a brief and sudden illness.
- In a post on Truth Social, Trump shared: “Senator Lindsey Graham, one of the greatest people and Senators I have ever known, is dead! He was always working, and was a true American Patriot. Lindsey will be greatly missed!!! DETAILS AND ARRANGEMENTS TO FOLLOW. So sad! President DONALD J. TRUMP”
- International leaders from NATO allies, Ukraine, and Israel have also paid tribute to Senator Graham, remembering him as a steadfast friend, partner, and advocate for trans-Atlantic relations.
- Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu hailed Graham as a "great friend of Israel," acknowledging his dedication to strengthening the US-Israel alliance and advocating for the free world.
- Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky highlighted Graham's ten visits to Ukraine during Russia's full-scale invasion, praising him as a "true defender of freedom"; leaders from Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, and Finland commended Graham for his strong support of NATO's eastern flank and unwavering advocacy for Ukraine against Russian aggression.
