A WOMAN has been arrested after a knife and drugs were found in North Yorkshire.

Officers from Scarborough’s Neighbourhood Policing Team detained a 46-year-old woman on Saturday (July 11).

  


North Yorkshire Police said: “The officers came across the woman in the St Nicholas Gardens whilst on dedicated proactive patrols of the town centre.

“The woman has been charged for the possession of the knife and a Class B drug.

“She will be attending Scarborough Magistrates on Thursday (July 30).
