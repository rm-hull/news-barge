A five-bedroom detached house in East Ayton, Scarborough, has hit the market.

Listed with a guide price of £565,000, the freehold property, in the Meadow Dene development, has been described as an "executive family home".

According to the listing from estate agents Colin Ellis Property Services, the house is set in a cul-de-sac location within the village and benefits from driveway parking, a double garage, gardens, and a patio area.

  Living room with oak flooring and contemporary interior 

*(Image: Colin Ellis Property Services)*

Inside, an entrance hall leads through to the main living areas.

The living room is described as light-filled, with oak flooring and contemporary interiors.

The centre of the house is an open-plan kitchen, dining, and living area, with bi-folding doors opening onto the rear garden. Kitchen features include bespoke cabinetry and quartz work surfaces.

  Dining and living area with bi-fold doors to the garden 

*(Image: Colin Ellis Property Services)*

Also found on the ground floor are a separate snug, which is intended as a home office, a utility room, and a guest WC.

Upstairs, the landing gives access to five bedrooms.

The principal bedroom includes an en-suite shower room, while a second double bedroom also has its own en-suite.

  Open-plan kitchen joining the dining room 

*(Image: Colin Ellis Property Services)*

The family bathroom across the hall provides a freestanding bath, a walk-in shower, chrome towel rail and contemporary tiling.

An air source heat pump system is in place to provide heating and hot water, with the listing stating this reduces running costs and supports a comfortable atmosphere.

Double glazing is also fitted throughout.

  Rear lawn garden with patio area for outdoor dining 

*(Image: Colin Ellis Property Services)*

Outside, the block-paved drive leads to the detached double garage, which has twin doors. The rear garden is laid to lawn and includes a patio area suitable for outdoor dining.

Meadow Dene is within reach of a post office, convenience store, two pubs, a primary school, and countryside walks.
