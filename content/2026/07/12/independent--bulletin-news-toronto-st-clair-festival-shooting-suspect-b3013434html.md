# Suspect still at large after Toronto mass shooting leaves two dead, four injured

- A mass shooting took place Saturday night at the Salsa on St Clair festival in Toronto.
- The incident resulted in two people killed and at least four others wounded, with a total of six victims hit.
- Toronto police secured the scene, but the suspect or suspects involved in the shooting remain at large.
- Authorities have advised the public to avoid the area as a large police presence continues the investigation.
- Ontario Premier Doug Ford expressed devastation over the "senseless violence" and called for the person responsible to be brought to justice.
