# Julian Alvarez stunner sets up World Cup semi-final with England

![](https://cdn.jwplayer.com/v2/media/r7AdeUvd/poster.jpg?width=720)

- Argentina defeated Switzerland 3-1 in extra time to secure their place in the World Cup semi-finals against England.
- Julian Alvarez scored a spectacular goal in extra time, breaking the deadlock after Switzerland had equalised and were subsequently reduced to 10 men.
- Switzerland's Breel Embolo received a red card for diving following a VAR review, a decision that significantly altered the course of the match.
- Despite the victory, Argentina's performance was described as uninspired, highlighting their reliance on individual moments of brilliance and their tendency to make progress challenging.
- Lautaro Martinez sealed the win with a third goal in the 121st minute, ensuring Argentina's progression to their second consecutive World Cup semi-final.
