YOUTHS have started a grass fire in York.

A North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service crew from Acomb were called to Wains Road at 7.47pm on Saturday (July 11).

  


The fire measured two metres by two metres, and North Yorkshire Police have been informed, a service spokesperson said.
