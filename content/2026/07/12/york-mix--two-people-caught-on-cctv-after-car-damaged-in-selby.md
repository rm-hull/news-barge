Police are investigating a criminal damage incident in Selby.

A black Vauxhall Insignia was damaged on D’Arcy Road at about 11.45pm on Friday, 3 July.

Two figures in dark clothing were captured on CCTV running from the location.

#### D’Arcy Road, Selby

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “If anyone lives in the area and has further CCTV, witnessed the incident or was driving in the area or has information that could assist our investigation, please contact us online in the first instance.”
