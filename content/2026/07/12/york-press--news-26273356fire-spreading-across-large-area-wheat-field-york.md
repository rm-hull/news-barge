## Firefighters battling huge fire spreading in fields behind homes in York

The fire has resulted in huge plumes of smoke, covering the Woodthorpe area of York *(Image: Billy Whiteside)*

FIREFIGHTERS are battling a huge fire that has spread through fields in York.

Local resident Robert Loft said he noticed the fire, which has begun to burn across a large area behind his home in Alness Drive, at around 3.40pm today (July 12).

  


  Firefighters are currently attempting to control a fire that has spread across wheat fields behind homes in Alness Drive, York 

*(Image: Robert Loft)*

He said: "It was quite traumatic and was spreading across the wheat field before the fire crews came."

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service are currently at the scene attempting to control the blaze.
