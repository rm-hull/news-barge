## Our live feed has now finished.

- A huge fire spread across fields in the Woodthorpe area of York.
- The blaze was reported at around 3.40pm on Sunday (July 12).
- North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service worked with local farmers to control the spread of the blaze, which caused a large plume of smoke to cover the region, causing extremely visibility for drivers.
- Moor Lane and Askham Lane were closed by North Yorkshire Police.
