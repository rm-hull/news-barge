# Tommy Robinson’s Russia visit ‘funded by Elon Musk’s family foundation’

- Tommy Robinson's recent trip to Russia was covered by the Musk Foundation, a philanthropic organisation founded by Elon Musk and his brother Kimbal Musk.
- Elon Musk's father, Errol Musk, claimed he “brought him out to Russia” and described Robinson as a “fine young man”.
- Robinson and Errol Musk attended an annual Kremlin-backed economic forum during the visit, where topics such as Russia's decline in births were discussed.
- Robinson posted a video with Errol Musk from Moscow, stating they would “cause some trouble” and had previously shared a list of protest locations following a knife attack in Belfast.
- Upon his return to the UK, Robinson claimed he was detained by police at Heathrow Airport and had his phones seized, subsequently asking supporters for donations for his legal defence.
