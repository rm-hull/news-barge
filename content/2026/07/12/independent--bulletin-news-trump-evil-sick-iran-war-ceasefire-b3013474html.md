# Trump says Iran ‘gave up everything’ after backing out of an agreed deal

- Donald Trump called Iran "evil and sick" after claiming it broke an agreed peace deal by firing a drone at a ship.
- Trump stated that Iran launched a drone at a ship within an hour of agreeing to a comprehensive deal.
- He confirmed that the US had "bombed the hell out of them last night" in response to these actions.
- The US launched additional strikes on Iran less than 24 hours after Trump threatened to "decimate and destroy" the country if he were assassinated.
- Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth confirmed the strikes, stating, "Iran made a poor choice. Now they pay," following an alleged Iranian attack on a container ship in the Strait of Hormuz.
