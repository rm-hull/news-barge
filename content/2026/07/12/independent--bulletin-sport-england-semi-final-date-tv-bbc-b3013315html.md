# When do England play next? Three Lions now two games away from World Cup glory

- England have advanced to their second semi-final in three World Cups thanks to a Jude Bellingham brace against Norway.
- Norway initially went ahead in the 36th minute with a bizarre goal from Andreas Schjelderup, with Bellingham drawing the game level before half-time.
- Jude Bellingham scored England's winner just three minutes into extra time, while England had a penalty decision overturned by VAR.
- England will face either Argentina in the semi-final in Atlanta at 8pm BST (3pm ET) on 15 July, with the World Cup final set for 19 July at 8pm BST (3pm ET).
- Viewers in the UK will be able to watch the game on BBC One and stream online via BBC iPlayer.
