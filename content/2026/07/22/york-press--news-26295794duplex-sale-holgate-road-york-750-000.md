## Look inside duplex apartment for sale in ‘magnificent development’ – for £750,000

From above: the three-bedroom duplex for sale in Railway House, off Holgate Road in York, for £750,000 *(Image: Churchills/Zoopla)*

A duplex apartment has hit the market in a “magnificent development” in York – for £750,000.

The three-bedroom home is for sale in Railway House, off Holgate Road.

It is one of eight apartments which were converted from offices into holiday flats as part of a £1 million redevelopment in 2023.

  Inside the three-bedroom duplex for sale in Railway House, off Holgate Road in York, for £750,000 

*(Image: Churchills/Zoopla)*

Churchills, which is marketing the property, said it offers an “ideal city centre residence”.

Arranged over two floors, the home features an entrance hallway leading to an open sitting/dining room area on the ground floor.

The kitchen has a large original window to the front with fitted appliances and a freestanding island.

Outside, the property has a private garden to the front and rear communal gardens with gated access and designated parking.
