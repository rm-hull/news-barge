A LONG-RUNNING Proms celebration is reaching a major milestone.

The Friends of Burton Constable in East Yorkshire will welcome back the Driffield Silver Band for the 15th anniversary of their Last Night of the Proms concert, taking place in the Indoor Riding School at Burton Constable Hall on Friday, August 21, 2026.

The event, which began as a small fundraiser, has grown into a staple of the summer calendar and supports the preservation of Burton Constable Hall.

Jan Ashdown, secretary of the Friends of Burton Constable, said: "The Proms has grown steadily over the years and remains one of our most enjoyable fundraising events.

"We're grateful to everyone who has supported it and helped it reach this milestone."

The Driffield Silver Band, reigning North of England Area Champions, will return with a programme of patriotic favourites and traditional Proms classics.

Peter Kench, musical director of the Driffield Silver Band, said: "The audience always creates a wonderful atmosphere at Burton Constable.

"We're looking forward to returning for what promises to be a memorable evening."

Doors open at 6.30pm, with the concert starting at 7pm and finishing around 9.30pm.

Tickets are priced at £15 and can be purchased online HERE, or in person at the Burton Constable Gift Shop.
