A CAR has ended up in a ditch in a North Yorkshire village, with firefighters rescuing one injured occupant.

  



The county's fire and rescue service was called to the A168 in Hunsingore, close to the A1(M) around 7.30pm yesterday (Tuesday, July 21).

Crews from Knaresborough and Harrogate were at the scene.

A service spokesperson said crew members released an injured person on a spinal board.

The occupant of the vehicle was left in the care of paramedics.
