# North Yorkshire Police launch investigation after two cars damaged overnight in Harrogate

## North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information and witnesses after two vehicles were damaged overnight in Harrogate.

The incident happened at Kilburn Court on Harlow Oval, where a grey Nissan and a white Vauxhall were parked.

The criminal damage is believed to have occurred between the evening of Wednesday, July 16, and the morning of Thursday, July 17.

A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: “If anyone witnessed the damage being caused, has CCTV or was driving and has Dash Cam footage or information that could assist our investigation, please contact us.”
