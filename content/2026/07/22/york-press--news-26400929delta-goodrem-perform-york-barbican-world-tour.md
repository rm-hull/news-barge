## Australian popstar set to visit York on world tour

 *(Image: Newsquest)*

A GLOBAL pop star is set to perform in York as part of a tour that’s set to take her across the world.

Neighbours and Eurovision star Delta Goodrem is set to take to the stage at York Barbican on February 23 as part of her 2027 Pure World Tour – which will see her travel across the UK and Europe.

This follows the release of new singles Hologram and Eclipse from her upcoming album, Pure, with the latter being Australia’s entry for the Eurovision song contest, which finished fourth place in the grand final with 287 points.

Delta said: "Every tour has its own heartbeat, and I can already feel how special this one is going to be. Eclipse was the beginning of this new chapter, and it's only the start of what's to come.

"It's going to be epic – bringing together the songs people have grown up with and the brand-new music from PURE."

  Delta Goodrem performing at Eurovision (Ian West/PA)


The tour follows a series of sold-out shows across the UK and EU and marks a new chapter for Goodrem, who has recently signed with Universal Music and is best known for her 23-times platinum debut album Innocent Eyes.

Tickets for the York concert go on sale at 10am on Friday, July 24 via the York Barbican website.
