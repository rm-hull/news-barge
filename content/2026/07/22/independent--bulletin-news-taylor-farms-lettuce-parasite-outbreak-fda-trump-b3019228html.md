# The explosive diarrhea outbreak just took a very political turn

- Taylor Farms, a produce company, voluntarily recalled iceberg lettuce amid an ongoing diarrhea parasite outbreak.
- The company has donated millions of dollars to groups aligned with President Trump and the Republican Party's agenda.
- Taylor Farms is currently engaged in public disputes and private lobbying with the White House regarding the outbreak response.
- The FDA reported a positive test for the parasite in Taylor Farms lettuce, then retracted it as a 'false positive,' leading to a dispute over whether an apology was issued.
- Critics argue that the administration's cost-cutting programs have weakened federal food safety inspections, potentially contributing to the outbreak.
