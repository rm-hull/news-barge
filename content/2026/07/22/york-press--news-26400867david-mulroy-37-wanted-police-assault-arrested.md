A man who was wanted by police for offences including assault has now been arrested.

North Yorkshire Police made a public appeal for sightings of David Mulroy, 37, in June as he had failed to comply with bail conditions and was wanted for an attack.

  **Recommended reads:**

The force said at the time that he had links to Catterick and urged people who saw him not to approach him, but to call 999 immediately.

A spokesman said the force had now withdrawn its appeal following Mulroy’s arrest, and thanked people for helping to find him.
