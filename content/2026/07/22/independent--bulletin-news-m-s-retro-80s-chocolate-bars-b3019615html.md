# M&S delights shoppers as it brings back four retro 1980s chocolate bars

- Marks & Spencers is reintroducing a range of retro chocolate bars that were popular in stores in the 1980’s, complete with classic St Michael’s packaging.
- In an Instagram post, M&S revealed the revived bars include: **Sparks**(with nougat and soft caramel),** Jet**(with chewy nougat, peanut and soft caramel),** Now!**(with biscuit and caramel) and** Coconut Grove**(with a soft coconut centre).
- Shoppers can find the bars on sale in M&S Foodhalls across the UK now, and many commenters on social media were keen to express their excitement – while urging the store to bring back other favourites from the past.
- Candice Brown, winner of the Great British Bake Off 2016, commented: “Coconut grove allllll dayyyyyyy long.”
- While @halalandfabulous said: “Please bring back the Pecan Pie and the coconut tarts from the 90's! Also, the Delta's crisps were awesome!”
