# Ozzy Osbourne’s daughter shares heartbreaking tribute one year after his death

- Kelly Osbourne marked the first anniversary of her father Ozzy Osbourne's death, saying she "aches every single day" and misses him "with every part of my soul".
- The Black Sabbath frontman, known as the Prince of Darkness, died aged 76 last July from a reported heart attack.
- In her emotional Instagram tribute, Kelly detailed how living without her father “has been the hardest thing I have ever had to endure”.
- She also highlighted seeing glimpses of her father's “spirit” and “mannerisms” in her son, Sidney, calling it “one of the greatest gifts I have ever been given.”
- Ozzy Osbourne was also remembered by his widow Sharon Osbourne in an interview with BBC Radio West Midlands, and by guitarist Billy Morrison.
