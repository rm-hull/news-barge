Luke Littler continues his defence of the Betfred World Matchplay when he faces Josh Rock in Blackpool.

The sixth edition of The Hundred continues as London Spirit take on Manchester Super Giants.

And the Commonwealth Games in Glasgow gets under way with the opening ceremony.

## Littler looks to continue dominance

![Littler will face Rock in the last 16 (Richard Sellers/PA)](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/22/15/7dbf7f05818073f56a9374b23ff9f5c8Y29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzg0ODA0ODQ5-2.84703813.jpg)

World champion **Littler** produced a stunning performance on Tuesday as he hit a nine-darter and averaged almost 114 in his victory over Nathan Aspinall in Blackpool. The defending Matchplay champion is looking in good form to make it back-to-back titles, but in his way stands**Josh Rock** in the last 16.**Dirk van Duijvenbode** knocked out Michael van Gerwen last time out and he faces**Gary Anderson** in the other match, with coverage on**Sky Sports Action** at**8.00pm** and**Sky Sports Main Event** at**9.30pm**.

## The Hundred takes centre stage

![The Hundred tournament is just getting warmed up (Steven Paston/PA)](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/22/15/c8e7a09fcf60f79464f6d47890eea894Y29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzg0ODA0ODkz-2.85375475.jpg)

**The Hundred** enters its third day of action as**London Spirit** take on**Manchester Super Giants**, with the women’s game live on** Sky Sports Main Event**and** Sky Sports Cricket**at** 2.30pm**, while coverage of the men’s match starts at** 6.00pm**. The men’s game will feature the likes of Jos Buttler, Heinrich Klaasen, Liam Livingstone and Gus Atkinson, while Sophie Ecclestone and Smriti Mandhana will be in action earlier on at Lord’s.

## Commonwealth Games begins

![The Commonwealth Games begins (Andrew Milligan/PA)](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/22/15/3f8823139d2c21d63c1577e8f4afc603Y29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzg0ODA0OTQx-2.85371757.jpg)

The **opening ceremony** of the**Commonwealth Games** signals the start of the competition where around 3,000 athletes from 74 nations will battle it out across a number of sports. The ceremony will get under way at the OVO Hydro Arena in Glasgow live on**TNT Sports 2** at**6.30pm** and**Channel 5** from**8.00pm**.

## What else is on?

![Tadej Pogacar will look to tighten his grip of Tour de France (Thibault Camus/AP)](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/22/15/21-5b149568eed4468aaff43b48f4768e3d.jpg)

The **Tour de France** heads towards the finish line with stage 18 from Voiron to the summit finish at Orcieres-Merlette. Race leader Tadej Pogacar will look to tighten his grip on the yellow jersey as he closes in on a fifth Tour title, with action live on**TNT Sports 1** at**11.15am**. In golf, the** ISPS HANDA Women’s Scottish Open**begins on** Sky Sports Main Event**and** Sky Sports Golf**at** 9.30am**, the** Senior Open Championship** on** Sky Sports Golf**at** 1.30pm**and** 3M Open**on the same channel at** 7.00pm**.
