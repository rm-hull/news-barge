# EasyJet chaos after mass brawl on flight from Tenerife to Liverpool

- An EasyJet flight from Tenerife South to Liverpool was forced to return to the Spanish island after a large fight involving approximately 10 passengers erupted on board.
- The captain of flight EZY3352 made the decision to turn back about 30 minutes into the journey, citing the danger the brawl posed to the safety of the flight.
- Upon its return to Tenerife South airport on Tuesday evening, police met the aircraft at the crew's request to address the disruptive behaviour.
- EasyJet confirmed the incident, stating that the plane returned due to a group of passengers behaving disruptively, but subsequently continued its journey to Liverpool.
- The incident highlights ongoing concerns about unruly passengers, with a new UK government proposal potentially allowing airlines to share information and ban disruptive individuals from all carriers.
