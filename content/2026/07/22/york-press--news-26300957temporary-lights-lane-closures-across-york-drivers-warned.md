DRIVERS in York are being reminded of roadworks in and around York as essential utility works are taking place.

Temporary traffic signals are in place on Wigginton Road due to water main works from Rowntree Way to Clifton Moor Gate. The works are expected to finish on Friday, July 24, at 5pm.

Wetherby Road is also affected by electricity work between Middlewood Close and Yew Tree Close with the work expected to finish by 5pm on August 14.

Additionally, gas main work is taking place on Park Grove between Emerald Street and Huntington Road, which started on July 20 and is expected to end on August 27, at 11pm.

A lane is closed on Foss Islands Road due to electricity work both ways from Navigation Road to Mansfield Street, and is causing delays. The work is expected to finish by 11pm on Thursday, July 23.
