What has been often described as simply the best day out in East Yorkshire took place for the 150th time on Wednesday (Jul 22).

It has been championed as one of the biggest agricultural shows in the UK – with more than 23,000 people attending every year.

It has been held at Driffield Showground every year since 1954 when the Driffield Agricultural Society bought the site.

We sent a photographer along to catch all of the action. Here’s a selection of his best shots.
