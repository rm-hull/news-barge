A road closure is expected to be in place until tomorrow afternoon on a main road north of York after a trailer laden with hay caught fire.

The A19 at Easingwold was closed at about 10.45am, as emergency services tackled the burning hay bales with vehicles redirected through Easingwold.

It is not known what caused the straw to catch light or the cost of the damage.

An update issued by North Yorkshire Council at 5.35pm said the road will stay close overnight "and likely for most of tomorrow".

An earlier statement issued by the council said: "The A19 Easingwold bypass has been closed between the Thirsk Road Roundabout and Cross Lanes Roundabout to the south of Easingwold.

  "This is due to a hay trailer fire. The road is expected to remain closed for several hours.

  

  "Please avoid the area and use an alternative route."
