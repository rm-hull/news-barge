Yorkshire Water has apologise to residents in the YO30 area who are experiencing issues with the water supply.

It’s due to a burst main in Burton Stone Lane.

They put out this message at 4pm (Wed 22 July) “We’re on site dealing with the problem. We expect supplies to be restored within 4 to 6 hours.

“You may have no water or low water pressure. It may be cloudy or discoloured for a while when it comes back on, but you can clear this by running your tap for a few minutes.”
