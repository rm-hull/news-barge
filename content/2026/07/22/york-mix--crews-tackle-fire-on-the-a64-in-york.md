Two fire crews were called to a fire on a road in York last night (Tuesday).

It happened on the **A64** at**Hopgrove**.

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “Crews from Acomb and Tadcaster attended a fire to the central reservation of the road.

“Crews used one hose reel jet to extinguish and dampen down surrounding area.”
