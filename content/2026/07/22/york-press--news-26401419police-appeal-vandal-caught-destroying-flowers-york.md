## Police probe after vandal caught destroying York city centre flowerpot

The planter located outside of The Cheesecake Guy in Shambles, York, has since been replenished *(Image: Newsquest)*

POLICE have launched an investigation after a vandal was seen attacking a flower display outside a York city centre shop.

It comes after a man was captured on CCTV “destroying” flowers from a planter placed outside of The Cheesecake Guy, in Shambles, at around 8pm on Monday (July 20).

The footage shows the man checking for onlookers before ripping out neat rows of flowers from the box, which had only been placed outside the shop on Saturday (July 18), and throwing them on the floor.

  The vandalism happened outside The Cheesecake Guy in Shambles, York 

*(Image: Newsquest)*

A spokesperson for North Yorkshire Police has urged anyone with information that can assist the force's investigation to come forward by visiting its website or by calling 101, quoting 12260139231.

  


It comes after the video, posted to social media, more than 300 comments, with many baffled by the man’s actions.


Some have asked for the man to be “named and shamed” and have urged North Yorkshire Police to respond by placing him on a community order.

Another commenter, _yorkie, said: “Now what on earth promoted this? Very sad man.”

YPPMan added: “Someone knows this fellow, but what can you/we do?”

The owner of The Cheesecake Guy said in a separate post that he wanted to speak with the culprit after the flowerpot had been destroyed for “no reason whatsoever”.
