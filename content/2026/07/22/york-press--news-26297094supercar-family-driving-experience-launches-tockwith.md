Families in search of the "ultimate summer adventure on wheels" can now make tracks to Tockwith Motorsports Centre, where a supercar driving experience has arrived just in time for the school holidays.

The new Family of Four Driving Experience Blast, available to book through TrackDays.co.uk, invites groups to drive world-class supercars on the track.

Imran Malek, corporate sales manager at TrackDays.co.uk, said: "Summertime family fun has never been so exciting, and we're thrilled that the Family of Four Driving Experience Blast is now available to enjoy.

"What better way to bring together family harmony than with some family 'car-mony'.

"The summer holiday is the perfect excuse to spend quality time with family and friends while trying something new and exciting."

Each of the four participants, aged 10 and above, can get behind the wheel of models such as the Lamborghini Gallardo, Ferrari California, and Aston Martin DB11, driving for three miles around the circuit.

Additional cars available in the TrackDays.co.uk fleet include the McLaren 720s, Porsche 911, Lotus Evora 400, and Mercedes AMG GT.

Available dates for the experience include July 31, August 1, 3, and 4.

Visitors are given a full safety briefing before heading onto the circuit.
