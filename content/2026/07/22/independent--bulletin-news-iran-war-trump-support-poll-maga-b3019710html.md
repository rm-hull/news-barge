- Support for the war against Iran among President Trump's MAGA base has significantly decreased, with only 37% now backing its continuation if it increases inflation, down from 50% in May, according to a poll released by *Politico*.
- A person close to the White House told *Politico* that it's "at a maximum level of frustration right now," with Trump "fully aware that the only way to the victory that he wants is completely politically impossible."
