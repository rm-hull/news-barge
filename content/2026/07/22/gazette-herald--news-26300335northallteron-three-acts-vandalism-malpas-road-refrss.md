THREE vehicles have been vandalised in a single North Yorkshire town road over a short period of time.

North Yorkshire Police said rear windscreen wipers were broken off or snapped in Malpas Road, Northallerton, between 8pm on Thursday, July 16, and 9am on Friday, July 17.

  



A spokesperson for the force said it is asking the public for information in support of its investigation.

The spokesperson said: "Did you or anyone who you live with, hear or see anything around the above time and date?

"Anyone with information is asked to call police on 101 quoting reference numbers 12260136131, 12260137017, or 12260136721.

"You can also contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 where information can be given anonymously."
