# Boy, 16, charged with murdering his mother in Australia

- A 16-year-old boy from Perth, Australia, has been charged with one count of murder and one count of attempting to unlawfully kill another.
- His 35-year-old mother was discovered deceased at their Bentley residence on Tuesday morning.
- The boy's 12-year-old sister sustained stab wounds but managed to flee to a nearby highway, where bystanders provided life-saving first aid.
- She was subsequently taken to the Children's Hospital and remains in a critical but stable condition.
- Police are treating the incident as a suspected family violence homicide, confirming that there is no ongoing threat to the wider community.
