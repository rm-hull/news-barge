New video of Prince George on Cornwall beach released to celebrate young royal becoming teenager

Prince George celebrated his 13th birthday on Wednesday, 22 July.

The Prince and Princess of Wales paid tribute to their son on his birthday, sharing a new video featuring George enjoying various activities in Cornwall.

The footage shows him climbing rocks, petting his dog, and playing cricket on the beach.

The Kensington Palace account posted the video, expressing gratitude for all the birthday wishes.

This tribute followed an earlier release of a previously unseen photo of George from last month's Trooping the Colour.
