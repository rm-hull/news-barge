A burst water main in part of York caused low pressure in numerous homes on Wednesday morning.

People living in Burton Stone Lane complained of issues with their water, with one resident taking to social media to say it had ‘taken an age’ to fill the bath.

Yorkshire Water advised any customers in the YO30 area who were without water to get in touch.

A spokesman for the utility company said: “We have a burst water main on Burton Stone Lane that was reported shortly after 8am, which is causing low pressure to some properties in the area.

“Our teams are currently on site excavating the main so they can begin a repair. We will complete the repair as soon as possible and this will return the network to usual operation."

Most of the low pressure was resolved later that day after the main was isolated for the repair.
