Kensington Palace released a new photograph of Prince George to commemorate his 13th birthday, which he celebrated on Wednesday 22 July.

The image, shared on the Prince and Princess of Wales’ social media, shows George in a dark suit with an open-necked white shirt, smiling with his hands in his pockets, and was taken by Matt Porteous after Trooping the Colour in June.

George, whose full name is George Alexander Louis, is now second in line to the throne, following his father Prince William.

He is set to begin his secondary schooling at the prestigious Eton College in Windsor this September, following in the footsteps of his father and uncle, Prince Harry.

Eton College, known for its self-reliance and leadership development, has a distinguished list of past students including actors, prime ministers and writers.
