Nolan has last laugh in The Odyssey's ridiculous culture war

Christopher Nolan's film, The Odyssey, has become his best-reviewed movie to date and a significant box office success, making $264m in its opening weekend.

The film's initial performance marks Nolan's best since The Dark Knight Rises in 2012.

The Odyssey appears to overcome online backlash, spearheaded by figures like Elon Musk, who criticised casting choices such as Elliot Page and Lupita Nyong'o.

Separately, following the release of first-look images and trailers, some Odyssey scholars were left frustrated by details such as costume design and language.

However, the film has been widely praised by critics, achieving 95 per cent on Rotten Tomatoes and a 4.4 out of five rating on Letterboxd.
