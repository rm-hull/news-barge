# The Beach Boys cancel tons of shows to ‘recharge’ amid summer concert series

- The Beach Boys have canceled a series of summer concerts, including multiple state fair appearances, scheduled between July 23 and September 3.
- The band, currently on their Endless Summer Gold tour, stated they are taking a “short break to recharge” before resuming performances in September.
- Affected events include state fairs in Wisconsin, Indiana, Oregon, Alaska, and Colorado, among others.
- While some canceled dates are being rescheduled, others, such as the performance at Bethel Woods Center for the Arts, will not be rebooked.
- Ticket holders for canceled shows will automatically receive refunds depending on their point of purchase.
