## The EU is racing to approve a new package of sanctions against Russia, but Greece is unexpectedly blocking it. Meanwhile, one year after the EU struck a controversial trade deal with the US, we examine its impact on Europe and analyse Trump's recent move to impose a 50% tariff on Canadian goods.

## On today's show:

**Jorge Liboreiro** breaks down today's EU ambassadors meeting to discuss the EU's 21st sanctions package against Russia as Greece holds it up over a ban on liquefied natural gas (LNG) that is due to come fully into force in 2027.

**Aadel Haleem** brings the latest from the Middle East as tensions between the US and Iran continue to rise.

**Maria Tadeo** reports on the EU–US trade deal one year after European Commission President Ursula von der Leyen and US President Donald Trump struck the agreement in Turnberry, Scotland, as well as Trump's recent move to impose 50% tariffs on Canadian goods.
