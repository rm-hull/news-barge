AN artisan market is returning to the marketplace, promising a lively celebration of summer.

The Little Bird Monthly Artisan Market returns to Malton on Saturday, July 25, bringing a day of shopping and entertainment in the heart of the town.

Jackie Crozier, managing director and founder of Little Bird Made, said: "We are thrilled to be back in Malton this Saturday, with a spectacular line-up of traders.

"Our markets are all about celebrating high quality, hand crafted goods, and the ‘Made in Malton’ ethos aligns perfectly with what we do.

"It is a fantastic opportunity to shop small, support local, and enjoy a brilliant day out in the sunshine."

Stalls will feature handmade jewellery, homeware, artwork, and food including pies, cheeses, jams, and chutneys.

Visitors are encouraged to explore beyond the market and enjoy Malton’s Georgian streets, independent shops, boutiques, cafes, and pubs.

Dan Sykes, owner of Bar Walls Spirits, said: "There is no better place to be on a summer Saturday than Malton.

"We’re incredibly proud to bring our craft spirits to the Artisan Market this weekend. It’s all about shopping small, discovering the local area, and celebrating the fantastic independent talent we have right here in Yorkshire."

The event runs from 10am.
