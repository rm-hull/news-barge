# Casemiro Inter Miami move to face MLS probe – here’s what we know

![Casemiro signed for Inter Miami as a free agent after leaving Manchester United](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/06/10/11/dd3db5d334d8a89a9188c7d6e72e0790Y29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzgxMTcyMTgx-2.84650102.jpg)

- Former Manchester United and Real Madrid midfielder Casemiro has completed his transfer to Inter Miami as a free agent.
- The deal, which runs until the end of the 2027 MLS Sprint Season with an option to extend, is currently being investigated by Major League Soccer (MLS) for alleged tampering.
- LA Galaxy initially held Casemiro's discovery rights, which by league rule would have prevented Inter Miami from negotiating with him.
- A settlement was reached between Inter Miami and LA Galaxy to facilitate the signing, but the MLS is continuing its probe into the tampering allegations.
- Casemiro, who boasts an impressive career including five Champions League titles, is awaiting visa approval to join Lionel Messi at the club.
