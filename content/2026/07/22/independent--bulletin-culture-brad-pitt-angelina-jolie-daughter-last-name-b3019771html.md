Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt's daughter Vivienne files petition to drop 'Pitt' from last name

Vivienne Jolie-Pitt, the youngest daughter of Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt, has filed to legally drop her father's surname, becoming the fourth of their children to do so.

Her siblings Shiloh, Maddox, and Zahara have also taken steps to remove Pitt from their names, with Shiloh legally changing hers to Shiloh Jolie on her 18th birthday in 2024.

Zahara has publicly used Zahara Marley Jolie at her graduation, and Maddox has been credited as Maddox Jolie in film projects.

Maddox and Zahara fulfilled California's legal requirement for name changes by placing newspaper advertisements earlier this month.

The family's estrangement is reportedly linked to a 2016 airplane incident involving alleged abuse by Pitt, which led to the couple's divorce.
