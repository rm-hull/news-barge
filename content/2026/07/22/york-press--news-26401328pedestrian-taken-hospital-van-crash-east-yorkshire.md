A pedestrian was taken to hospital after being struck by a van in East Yorkshire this morning (Wednesday, July 22).

Emergency services were called to the scene on Trinity Road, near the coast in Bridlington just before 9am.

A spokesperson for the Yorkshire Air Ambulance said: “We received an emergency call just before 9am on Wednesday morning to report a collision between a van and a female pedestrian on Trinity Road, Bridlington.

“An ambulance, a team leader and an air ambulance were dispatched to the scene and one patient was conveyed by road to Hull Royal Infirmary.”
