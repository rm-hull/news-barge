A THIEF has targeted a national supermarket chain shop in York city centre.

North Yorkshire Police said £80 of stock was stolen from Sainsbury's in Piccadilly on Friday, June 12.

The force released a CCTV image of a man they wish to speak to, saying he may have information which will assist its investigation.

  


North Yorkshire Police are appealing for help identifying the man shown in the CCTV image.

Anyone with information or who recognises the man is asked to email [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk) or call 101, quoting reference 12260108739.
