## York Businesses Invited to Grow Connections at York Press Summer Networking Event

Local IQ networking event at York St John University. Picture are Maddie Bricklebank and Lottie Joy. Picture by David Harrison. (Image: Local IQ networking event at York St John University. Picture are Maddie Bricklebank and Lottie Joy.Picture by David *(Image: David Harrison)*

Business leaders from across York and North Yorkshire are being encouraged to book their place at a free business networking event hosted by The York Press and York St John University, in partnership with York College, later this month.

Taking place on Thursday 30 July from 5pm to 7pm at York St John University, the event will bring together organisations from across the city to network and pick up the summer edition of Yorkshire BUSINESSiQ magazine.

The free event is designed to help local businesses make valuable new connections, exchange ideas and discover opportunities for growth in York's thriving business community. Whether you're an ambitious start-up, an established company, a charity, or a sole trader looking to raise your profile, the event offers a relaxed and welcoming environment to expand your professional network.

Guests will enjoy informal networking alongside refreshments, while also hearing from guest speakers and representatives from York College about how they can support local employers and the development of future talent.

Steve Lowe, Sales Director at The York Press and LOCALiQ Yorkshire, said: "York has an incredibly strong and supportive business community, and these networking events continue to create opportunities for organisations of all sizes to connect, collaborate and grow.

"Whether you're looking to generate new business, learn from other local leaders, or simply meet like-minded professionals, this event provides the perfect platform. We're delighted to be working alongside York St John University and York College to bring the business community together once again."

The evening will begin with registration and networking, followed by a welcome and digital marketing update, guest speaker presentations and further networking opportunities.

Previous networking events organised by The York Press and York St John University have attracted businesses from a wide range of sectors, providing attendees with practical advice, new contacts and opportunities to develop partnerships across the region.
