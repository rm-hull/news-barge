POLICE have launched an update following a theft from a York suburb.

North Yorkshire Police said a green and orange CUBE Acid mountain bike was stolen from outside a property in Lime Tree Avenue overnight from Friday, June 26 into June 27.

  



Members of the public are being urged to check any relevant footage that might assist the investigation.

A force spokesperson said: "Please email [mark.burrows@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:mark.burrows@northyorkshire.police.uk) with information.

"Alternatively, call 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

"Quote reference 12260121644."
