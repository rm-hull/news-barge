A MAN has fled into York after being caught taking photos up a woman’s skirt on a train.

British Transport Police has released a CCTV picture of a man they'd like to speak to after a woman was upskirted on a train that arrived into York station.

It took place at around 5.20pm on Friday, June 19 after a woman was preparing to leave the Harrogate to York train, before someone told her that a man had taken photos up her skirt.

A spokesperson for the force said: “The woman challenged the man and the confrontation continued on the platform when she reported the incident to station staff who called BTP.

“The man left the station on a bike and cycled down York’s Queens Street in the direction of Blossom Street.”

Officers believe the man pictured could have information that may assist their enquiries into the incident.

Anyone who recognises him is asked to text BTP on 61016, call 0800 40 50 40, or report any information anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please use the reference 597 of 19 June when reporting any information.
