# Giant visitor’s attraction installed in Harrogate town centre for public

## A giant visitor attraction offering views to die for has returned to dominate the Harrogate skyline.

The popular observation wheel is back in the town centre near the war memorial at Prospect Place just across from Bettys tearoom.

Standing a massive 32 metres tall, which is more than 100ft, the wheel is operated by WM Attractions Limited in co-ordination with North Yorkshire Council.

The iconic feature will open to the public at 11am on Saturday, July 4 and will then run daily from 11am to 7pm until Sunday, July 26.

![Dominating the skyline - The popular observation wheel is back in Harrogate town centre at Prospect Place. (Picture Graham Chalmers)](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOjQ1MzM5MDI1LWUxMmQtNDE5Ni1hODJlLTdiMGU4NDcyMDlmZTpjOTJlYzBhNi1mY2NkLTRmODAtODY2MS02YWRjZjhlZWI3YTg=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



No advance booking is required, with tickets priced at £5 for those under 1.4m and £7 for those over 1.4m.

Its running will be weather dependent and the attraction may close at short notice in bad conditions.

The wheel is accessible to some wheelchairs and mobility scooters, depending on the model, and well-behaved dogs may join their owners on board.
