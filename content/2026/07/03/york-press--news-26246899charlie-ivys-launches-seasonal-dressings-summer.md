A Yorkshire business has unveiled a fresh range of summer dressings.

Thixendale-based Charlie & Ivy’s has created three new rapeseed oil dressings in response to customer requests for seasonal flavours.

The dressings are designed with alfresco dining in mind.

Sara Roustoby, brand account manager at Charlie & Ivy’s, said: "All three dressings are versatile and can be drizzled over crisp salad leaves, or used to marinade and dress chicken and fish, and flavour roasted vegetables."

The new varieties include fennel seed, mint and balsamic, and lemon and caper.

Each dressing blends savoury and sweet elements, with ingredients such as mustard, honey, and Dijon mustard adding depth of flavour.

The dressings are made with cold-pressed rapeseed oil produced by parent company Breckenholme on its Yorkshire Wolds farm.

They are now available online and through selected independent retailers nationwide, priced at £4 for a 250ml jar.

More details can be found at charlieandivys.co.uk.
