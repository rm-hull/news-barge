# Moment huge waterspout twirls over lake in Germany

A large waterspout formed over a lake in Germany near the southern city of Friedrichshafen.

The phenomenon was said to have lasted five to 10 minutes on Wednesday.

Waterspouts occur about two to three times a year, according to the German weather service DWD, as cited by Associated Press.
