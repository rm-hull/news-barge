## Double Olympic champion backs new sports supplements by Yorkshire company

Yorkshire-based HempWell Ltd has announced its partnership with JJ Double Gold to launch a new range of sports supplements *(Image: HempWell Ltd)*

A Yorkshire wellness brand has teamed up with a double Olympic champion to launch a new range of sports supplements.

Yorkshire-based HempWell Ltd has announced its partnership with JJ Double Gold, a sports recovery and performance brand co-founded by British taekwondo icon Jade Jones MBE and Richard Groves.

The range, which is manufactured exclusively by HempWell, includes CBD oils, supplements, topicals, and wellness products designed to aid recovery, boost performance, and support general fitness.

Philip James, Director of HempWell Ltd, said: "We're incredibly proud to be the team behind JJ Double Gold.

  The range includes CBD oils, supplements, topicals, and wellness products 

*(Image: HempWell Ltd)*

"Jade and Richard have a clear vision for what they want to build, and Jade's story – the dedication, the resilience, the relentless pursuit of excellence – mirrors everything we believe in at HempWell."

Ms Jones added: "Recovery has always been at the heart of what I do – it's what keeps you in the game.

"I wanted to create a range I genuinely believe in, made by people I trust.

"Working with HempWell felt like the perfect fit, and I can't wait for people to try it."

The JJ Double Gold products are now available in HempWell’s retail stores in Harrogate, York, and Brighton, as well as online.

Plans for the future include a bespoke range of nutraceutical gummies designed to support individual health needs, with a targeted launch in Autumn 2026.

HempWell said the partnership marks an important step for the business as it expands its presence in the sports nutrition sector.

The company said it sees the collaboration as a natural extension of its commitment to providing high-quality natural remedies to those seeking an alternative to the pharmaceutical standard.

Ms Jones, who recently visited HempWell’s Harrogate store, currently remains focused on training ahead of her upcoming fight at the Misfits event on September 12 in Manchester.
