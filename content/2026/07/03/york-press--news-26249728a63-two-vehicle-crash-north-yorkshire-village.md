TWO vehicles have crashed in a North Yorkshire village.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue service said a crew from Selby was called to the village of Monk Fryston around 5.10pm yesterday. (Thursday, July 2).

  



A service spokesperson said: "All occupants were out on a arrival and the crew made the scene safe.

"The incident was left with the Police to arrange recovery of the vehicles."
