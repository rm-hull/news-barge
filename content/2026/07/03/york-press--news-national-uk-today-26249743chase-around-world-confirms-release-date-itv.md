Fans of The Chase have shared they are "so excited" and "can't wait" to watch a new spin-off of the show, which has confirmed its release date.

The Chase Around the World is a six-part series that will be launching on Thursday, July 16.

It will see six pairs of contestants attempt to track down the Chasers in undisclosed locations around the globe.

Speaking about the show, host Bradley Walsh said: “This bold new take on The Chase combines quizzing with travelling and will test contestants’ knowledge like never before.


"The Chasers are already famous around the world, so fans can look forward to seeing them ply their trade in some superb secret locations.”

Lily Wilson, Managing Director, Bright Entertainment said: “What better way to launch Bright Entertainment - not only with the news of our inaugural commission, but a reimagining of one of the most successful television quiz formats.

"The Chase Around the World is a fittingly global evolution for an iconic format, developed and being produced by a great team.

"We look forward to launching the series later this year and it being the first of many more exciting formats to come.”
