A care provider in York has been named among the best in its region.

Ebor Home Care has been recognised as one of the top 20 home care providers in Yorkshire & The Humber by homecare.co.uk, marking its fourth consecutive year receiving the honour.

Liam Brown, HR manager at Ebor Home Care, said: "We are absolutely delighted to be recognised as one of homecare.co.uk's Top 20 Home Care Companies for the fourth consecutive year.

"This achievement is especially meaningful because it is based on feedback from the people who matter most, our clients and their families.

"Winning this award year after year reflects the dedication, compassion and professionalism of our entire team, who work tirelessly to deliver outstanding care and support every day."

The award is based on reviews from service users and their families, reflecting overall satisfaction with the care provided.

Amanda Hopkins, reviews manager at homecare.co.uk, said: "High-quality home care plays a vital role in enabling people to remain independent and continue living in their own homes for as long as possible.

"We would like to congratulate Ebor Home Care on being recognised as one of Yorkshire & The Humber's Top 20 Home Care Providers for 2026."
