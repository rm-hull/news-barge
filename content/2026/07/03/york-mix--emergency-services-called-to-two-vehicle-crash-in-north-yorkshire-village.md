Emergency services were called to a crash in a North Yorkshire village last night.

It was in **Monk Fryston** near Selby at **5.12pm**.

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “A crew from Selby attended a two vehicle road traffic collision.

#### Monk Fryston

“All persons were out on arrival and crews made the scene safe.

“The incident was left with the police to arrange recovery of the vehicles.”
