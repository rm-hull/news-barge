                                    One driver has been taken to hospital after a two-vehicle crash near Harrogate.

Emergency services were called to Harrogate Road in North Rigton at around 8:40pm on Wednesday (1st July) following the collision.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said one person was trapped inside a vehicle.

Firefighters used cutting equipment to free the casualty before they were taken to hospital by ambulance.

A spokesperson for the fire service said:

"Harrogate crews were mobilised to a two-vehicle road traffic collision with one person trapped.

"Crews used cutting gear to extricate the casualty. They were transported to hospital by road ambulance."
