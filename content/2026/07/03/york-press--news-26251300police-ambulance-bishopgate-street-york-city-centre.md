## Police incident in busy York street - cordon in place

A police cordon is up in Bishopgate Street close to Skeldergate Bridge in York city centre and traffic is being controlled this afternoon (Friday, July 3), although the road is still open *(Image: Alice Kavanagh)*

EMERGENCY services are dealing with an incident just outside York city centre.

North Yorkshire Police and Yorkshire Ambulance Service are at the scene of an emergency in Bishopgate Street, close to Bishopthorpe Road, with a police cordon in place and officers at the scene.

  The cordon runs close to the Bar Walls in Bishopgate Street

The road is open, but there are long delays in nearby streets.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a woman was found injured at the junction of Price's Lane and Bishopgate Street, just outside York city centre this afternoon.

A spokesperson for the force said: “Shortly after noon today, we received a report from the ambulance service that a woman in her 30s had been found with a head injury at the junction of Price's Lane and Bishopgate Street, next to the city walls.

“She has been taken to hospital for treatment. Enquiries are ongoing."

Police added that they are appealing for anyone with information - particularly any members of the public who assisted at the scene – to contact them on 101.
