                                    A Knaresborough woman became the eleventh to bag a free tank of fuel as part of our Free Fuel Friday competition.

The now-famous weekly competition continued today (Friday), giving residents the chance to have their tank filled for free.

Clues were given on the radio and on social media to hint at where the Your Harrogate Free Fuel Fairy would be appearing after 10am.

And Kayla was the first to find George Dickson in his tutu and wig and utter the phrase that pays:


'Are you Your Harrogate's Free Fuel Fairy?'

Today’s secret location was the Asda petrol station on the corner of Manse Lane and Wetherby Road in Knaresborough.

Kayla scooped just over £50 of petrol and plans to drive to Leeds.
