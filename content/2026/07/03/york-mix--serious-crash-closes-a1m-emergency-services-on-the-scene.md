A serious crash has closed a section of the A1(M) today (Friday).

It’s shut the northbound carriageway between junction 43 at Castleford and junction 42, the Selby Fork interchange.

National Highways Yorkshire posted: “Emergency services are on scene. Delays of three miles on approach, 30 minute delay.”

#### Crash location

We have no information on casualties at this time. More information as we get it.
