# Top Harrogate hotel's 'fierce pride in Yorkshire' inspires brand new immersive experience

## A top Harrogate hotel is to pay tribute “to the county we love” with the launch of a new adventure on its 300 acre estate.

Rudding Park, the luxury hotel, spa and golf resort, is excited to announce the addition of The Great Yorkshire Golf Adventure – a new playful, story-led experience that celebrates the county's most famous legends, landmarks and icons through an immersive 18-hole golf adventure.

Launching on Yorkshire Day, Saturday, August 1, this brand new experience will blend adventure, discovery and friendly competition celebrating the people, places and moments that have shaped Yorkshire.

Matthew Mackaness, Joint Managing Director said: "At Rudding Park we always want to ensure our members, guests and visitors experience brilliant times.

"Yorkshire has always inspired fierce pride and The Great Yorkshire Golf Adventure is our tribute to the county we love.

"We've taken Yorkshire's stories, landmarks, myths and achievements and transformed them into a fun, immersive experience to be enjoyed by everyone."

Created as the flagship experience within the new Gimme Golf destination at Rudding Park, The Great Yorkshire Golf Adventure is a first for the county.

From Vikings and Guy Fawkes to York Minster, Whitby, Theakston Brewery to the Yorkshire Dales and beyond, every hole tells a story.

The Great Yorkshire Golf Adventure has been designed to be fun and to appeal to families, friends, visitors and golfers.

For the last five years Rudding Park has hosted Golfest, a sell-out event celebrating everything about golf.

This year, Gimme Golfest will take place on the same day – Yorkshire Day.

Talking about the Gimme Golf concept, Marc Rice, Head of Golf, said: "We have an extensive golf offering including the 18 hole Hawtree Course with a very active membership.

“But golf has evolved and we are on a mission to break down traditional barriers to the game.

"Gimme Golf is about bringing people together through great social experiences, whether that's adventure golf with the family, competing with friends on the TrackMan driving range, grabbing a drink to bite to eat on the terrace, playing shuffleboard or spending quality time together.”
