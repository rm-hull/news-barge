An Italian restaurant chain is giving away spritz cocktails for £1 for only one day.

Bella Italia has announced it will be offering £1 spritzes in celebration of its new Bella Spritz Day.

The £1 offer means visitors can enjoy any of Bella Italia’s five signature Summer Spritzes when buying a main meal or substantial food.

The exclusive offer will run for one day only on Friday, July 10.


## What spritz can I get from Bella Italia?

The spritz offer allows customers to choose from its full seasonal menu including Aperol Spritz, Hugo Spritz, Sarti Spritz, Paradise Spritz, and Crodino Spritz.

The deal means guests will save up to £8.79 on a drink.

More than 70 Bella Italia restaurants across the UK will be participating in the day.

A Bella Italia spokesperson said: “Bella Spritz Day is all about bringing people together to celebrate summer and enjoy those moments that matter most.

“By offering our full range of Summer Spritzes for just £1, we’re giving guests the chance to experience a true taste of Italy at incredible value.”


The restaurant is also running a weekday deal until Friday, July 17, where customers can get £15 off their bill when spending £30 or more.

### 
  **What’s on the Bella Italia menu?**

The Italian restaurant chain offers a range of traditional dishes including bruschetta and caprese salad to start and carbonara and goats cheese ravioli as options for mains.

There are also a huge range of pizzas on offer from simple margherita and pepperoni options to a meat feast or you could create your own by adding extra toppings.

If you do not want a full meal, the restaurant offers half sized pizzas that can be enjoyed with a side salad.
