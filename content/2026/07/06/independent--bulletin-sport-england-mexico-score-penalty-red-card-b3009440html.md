# Mexico v England: Three Lions go through in thrilling game worth staying up for

- England secured a dramatic 3-2 victory over co-hosts Mexico at the Estadio Azteca, advancing to the quarter-finals of the 2026 World Cup.
- Jude Bellingham scored two goals in the first half, giving England an early lead in the match.
- England's task became more challenging when Jarell Quansah was shown a red card, reducing the team to ten men.
- Harry Kane scored a penalty to extend England's lead but later conceded a penalty, allowing Mexico to score.
- Despite being a man down, England defended fiercely to hold onto their lead and will now face Norway in Miami in the next round.
