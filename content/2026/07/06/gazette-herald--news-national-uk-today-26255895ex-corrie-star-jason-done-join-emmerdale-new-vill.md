Emmerdale has confirmed a new character is coming to the ITV soap soon.

Jason Done, known for his roles in Coronation Street and Waterloo Road, will join the Emmerdale cast as a new villain.

Viewers might also recognise the 53-year-old from his roles in Brassic and The Bill.

A source told The Sun that the new villain has “a surprising connection to the village.”


## Jason Done to join Emmerdale as new character

They said: “This is a huge coup for Emmerdale.

“They’ve had massive success with other soap’s stars joining the cast and the hope is that Jason will be another hit.

“Jason’s character is a new villain with a surprising connection to the village.”

Emmerdale has since confirmed to Newsquest that Jason Done will join the soap's cast as a new character named Steve.

However, he will only be on the show for a short stint, and his start date was not confirmed.

## Emmerdale’s TV schedule this week

Emmerdale will see some more TV schedule changes this week as the World Cup continues to be shown on the same channel as the ITV soap.

Usually, Emmerdale airs 30-minute episodes at 8pm on ITV1 Monday to Friday.

Here is the current schedule for Emmerdale as per the Freeview TV guide.

On Monday, July 6, Emmerdale will air earlier than usual with a new episode lasting an hour from 7.30pm.

There is no episode of the soap scheduled for Tuesday, July 7, but it will be back on Wednesday, July 8 with a 30-minute episode starting at 8pm.


ITV will not show a new episode of Emmerdale on Thursday, July 9, but it will return on Friday, July 10, when viewers can tune in at 8pm for one hour.

Although the TV schedule for Emmerdale looks different this week, fans will still get their usual five 30-minute episodes, just in a different format.
