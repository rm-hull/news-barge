HOMES and businesses across North Yorkshire were hit by a power cut.

Properties in and around Knaresborough, Ripon, Boroughbridge, Easingwold, and the surrounding villages have been left without power this afternoon (July 6).

The power cut is thought to have happened around 3pm today.
