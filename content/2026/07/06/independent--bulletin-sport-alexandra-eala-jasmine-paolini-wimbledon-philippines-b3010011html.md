# How Alexandra Eala made history at Wimbledon despite defeat to Jasmine Paolini

- Alexandra Eala's historic Wimbledon run concluded in the fourth round with a three-set defeat to former finalist Jasmine Paolini on Centre Court.
- The 21-year-old Filipino player lost 4-6 6-4 3-6, two days after her notable victory over defending champion Iga Swiatek.
- Eala made history by becoming the first player from the Philippines to reach the fourth round of a Grand Slam and win a main draw match at Wimbledon.
- Her performances have garnered significant attention, with her match highlights and press conference videos being the most-watched of this year's Championships.
- Jasmine Paolini, who has faced a foot injury this season, advanced to her first Grand Slam quarter-final since her breakthrough year in 2024.
