Police are investigating the theft of collectable toys from a York store.

Jelly Cat toys were stolen from a shop in the city centre.

It happened at 4.45pm on Friday, 15 May.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Please contact us if you recognise the person pictured on CCTV, as they may have information that will assist our investigation.”
