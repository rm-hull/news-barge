## How to save at Virgin Media

### Bill credit offers and free gifts

One of the most common Virgin Media promotions is bill credit deals for new customers. Depending on the package you choose, these offers can sometimes reach £100 to £200 back in bill credit, effectively reducing the cost of your broadband or TV package over several months.

Virgin Media also occasionally includes free gifts alongside selected contracts. Previous offers have included gaming consoles, gift cards and tech accessories when you sign up for eligible broadband or bundle packages.

These promotions usually appear during big retail sale periods such as Black Friday, the January sales and bank holiday weekends.

### Look for free setup deals

Virgin Media regularly waives its setup fees during promotional periods, which can be an easy way to save £30 or more up front. Self-installation is often available for eligible properties, but customers choosing engineer installation may usually need to pay an activation or setup charge unless a promotion is running.

If you are not in a rush to switch providers, it can be worth waiting for a free setup event before signing up.

### Refer friends for cashback rewards

Existing customers can also unlock savings through Virgin Media’s referral programme. Customers who refer a friend or family member can currently receive cashback rewards once the new customer’s service is installed and active for a qualifying period.

Referral values change throughout the year, but previous offers have included around £50 for both the referrer and the new customer. If someone you know already uses Virgin Media, checking for a referral link before buying could be worthwhile.

### Students can access exclusive broadband deals

Virgin Media regularly offers student-friendly broadband packages, including shorter contract lengths and discounted pricing compared with standard broadband deals.

Benefits include:

- Discounted student broadband plans
- Flexible 30-day and 12-month contract options
- Access to O2 Priority perks on eligible packages
- Bundled mobile and broadband discounts

These shorter contracts can be particularly useful for students renting temporary accommodation or moving frequently during term time.

### Check if you qualify for Essential broadband

Virgin Media’s Essential broadband packages are designed to provide lower-cost internet access for eligible households receiving certain government benefits.

Prices currently start from around £12.50 per month on a rolling 30-day contract, with no setup fee included. Customers wanting faster speeds may also be eligible for Essential Broadband Plus packages with higher download speeds.

Eligibility is limited to customers receiving qualifying benefits such as Universal Credit, Pension Credit and Income Support, but the scheme can offer significantly lower pricing than standard broadband contracts.

### Supercharge your package with Volt benefits

Customers with both Virgin Media broadband and an eligible O2 contract can unlock additional Volt benefits at no extra cost.

Depending on the package, you can get:

- Broadband speed boosts up to the next available tier
- Double mobile data on eligible O2 plans
- Up to three wifi pods included
- Additional roaming and connectivity perks

For households already using both brands, Volt can add genuine value without increasing the monthly cost.

### Save with Virgin Media bundles

Virgin Media’s biggest savings often appear through bundled services rather than standalone broadband packages. You can combine broadband, TV, home phone and mobile contracts into one monthly deal, with some bundles including Sky Sports, Sky Cinema and Netflix access.

Premium bundles can look expensive up front, but they are often cheaper than paying separately for equivalent TV subscriptions, broadband and mobile data elsewhere.

### Take advantage of the wifi guarantee

Virgin Media includes a wifi guarantee on selected Gigabit Fibre and Volt packages. The provider promises minimum download speeds of 30Mbps in every room, or eligible customers may receive a £100 bill credit if the issue cannot be resolved.

Some packages also include wifi pods, which help improve signal strength throughout larger homes. For households with multiple devices or heavy streaming usage, this can be a useful extra rather than paying separately for mesh wifi equipment.

## Virgin Media FAQs

## Where do I enter my Virgin Media promo code?

If your offer includes a Virgin Media promo code, you can usually enter this during the online checkout process before confirming your order. Some promotions apply automatically instead, particularly bill credit deals and bundle discounts.

### What is the best Virgin Media offer for new customers?

Bill credit promotions are usually among the strongest Virgin Media deals for new customers, with selected broadband and TV packages previously offering up to £200 back. Free gifts and free setup deals can also add significant extra value.

### Does Virgin Media run sales?

Yes, but Virgin Media promotions look slightly different to traditional retail sales. Rather than percentage discounts, offers usually focus on bill credits, discounted monthly pricing, gifts and reduced setup fees.

### Is there an NHS discount for Virgin Media?

Virgin Media does not currently advertise a dedicated NHS or Blue Light discount scheme. However, healthcare workers can still access the same promotional bundle offers, bill credits and broadband discounts available to other customers.
