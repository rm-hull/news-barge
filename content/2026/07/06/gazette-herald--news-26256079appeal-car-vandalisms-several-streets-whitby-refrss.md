MULTIPLE cars have been targeted in the streets of a coastal North Yorkshire town.

North Yorkshire Police have released a CCTV image after a series of car vandalisms that took place in Whitby in May.

They say that vehicles were damaged and car door handles tampered with in the early hours of May 30 in the areas of Waterstead Lane, Fishburn Road, and Oswy Street.

A spokesman for the force said: "We’ve issued this CCTV image of a man we'd like to speak after several cars were damaged, and car door handles tampered with, in Whitby."

Officers are urging anyone who recognises the man or has information that could help the investigation to get in touch with them by contacting Joshua Barnes at North Yorkshire Police via email at [Joshua.Barnes@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:Joshua.Barnes@northyorkshire.police.uk) or by calling 101, quoting reference 12260098973.
