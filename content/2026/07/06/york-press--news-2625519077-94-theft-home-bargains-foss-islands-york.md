## Thieves target Home Bargains in York

One of the men that police hope to speak to *(Image: North Yorkshire Police)i>*

A BARGAIN shop in a York retail park has been the target of thieves.

North Yorkshire Police has released two CCTV pictures of men that its officers would like to speak to after a theft at Home Bargains in Foss Islands.
