A close up of the person that police would like to speak to *(Image: North Yorkshire Police)*

HIGH-VALUE soft toys have been stolen from a York city centre shop.

North Yorkshire Police has released a CCTV picture of a person that its officers would like to speak to after Jellycat soft toys were stolen from a York city centre shop.
