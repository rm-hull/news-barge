Police are appealing for the public’s help following the death of a man in York.

Phillip Roy Knatt has died aged 72. Now police are working on behalf of the coroner to locate his next of kin.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “He was confirmed deceased at his home in West Thorpe, Dringhouses, last week.

“There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

“We would ask any family members to get in touch please.”
