# Sara Cox to host biggest radio show in country as she steps into new role with special guest

![Sarah Cox](https://static.independent.co.uk/2025/05/15/12/cb9a627867a97148b71c3a13dbe07becY29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzQ3MzkyODUw-2.67761238.jpg)

- Sara Cox is set to begin her new role as host of the BBC Radio 2 weekday breakfast show from Monday.
- The Bolton-born DJ will present the slot from 6:30 am to 9:30 am, with Hollywood actor Tom Hanks appearing as her first guest.
- Cox takes over from Scott Mills, who was sacked by the BBC following a police investigation into allegations of serious sexual offences.
- She previously hosted the teatime show on the station since 2018 and described her new breakfast role as a 'dream'.
- Her new programme will feature a fresh format, incorporating popular elements from her previous teatime show.
