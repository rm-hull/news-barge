TWO people are in serious condition and a person has been arrested after a crash in North Yorkshire last week.

The county’s police force says it is appealing for information after a crash on the A19 southbound that took place last Wednesday night (July 1) involving a grey Ford Fiesta.

It happened at around 11pm on the southbound carriage way, near the junction for Knayton and just north of Thirsk.

“Both passengers remain in a serious condition in hospital. The driver was arrested and has been released under investigation at this stage,” said a spokesperson for the force.

As reported by The Press at the time, the road was closed for around eight hours while police investigated the scene and recovered the vehicle.

North Yorkshire Police added that they are appealing for any witnesses, anyone who may have seen the vehicle prior to the collision, or anyone with relevant dashcam footage to contact them.

If you have any information that could assist the investigation, please contact them at [James.Lake@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:James.Lake@northyorkshire.police.uk), through their website, or by calling 101.

Please quote reference 12260123739 when providing information.
