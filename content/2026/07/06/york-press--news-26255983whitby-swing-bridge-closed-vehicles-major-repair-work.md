A BRIDGE in a North Yorkshire town is set to be closed to traffic again whilst major works take place.

Whitby Swing Bridge is set to be closed to all vehicles for the rest of this week after works continue to repair recent issues to the site.

Pedestrian access will be maintained whilst this is taking place.

This follows closures over the past few weeks after the bridge malfunctioned, with a member of the council adding that they will be checking for effects of extreme heat and corrosive build ups.

“We will be excavating behind the steel plate to create additional space for the bridge to swing,” said a spokesperson for North Yorkshire Council.

They added that, at this point, the work is expected to be completed by Friday 10 July, but they won't know the full extent of the work required until excavation begins
