POLICE officers from across the country have launched a hunt for a man wanted across England.

North Yorkshire Police are searching for 20-year-old Noel Abdullah who is wanted for immigration offences and breaching court bail conditions.

“Extensive police enquiries are ongoing to locate him, including in York and Leeds, and also Dartford, London, where he has connections,” said a spokesperson for the force.

He is described as black, about 5ft 4ins tall, slim build, with dark hair possibly in dreadlocks.

If you see him, please call 999 and for any other information that could assist the investigation, please contact police online or call 101.

Please quote reference 12260122542 when providing information.
