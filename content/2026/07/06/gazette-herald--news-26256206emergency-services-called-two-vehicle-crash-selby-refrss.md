**UPDATE: A person has been taken to York Hospital as a result of the collision, reported at 11.45am on Monday (July 6).**

A MAIN road in a North Yorkshire town has been closed following a crash.

Emergency services have arrived in Gowthorpe, Selby after reports of a crash, involving two cars.

  


North Yorkshire Police, along with North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service are in attendance.

The Press has contacted Yorkshire Ambulance Service for comment.
