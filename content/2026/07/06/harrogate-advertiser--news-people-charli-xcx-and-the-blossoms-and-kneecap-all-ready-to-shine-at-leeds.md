# Charli xcx and The Blossoms and Kneecap all ready to shine at Leeds Festival 2026

## With the clock counting down to this year’s Reading & Leeds Festival demand for tickets has proven to be high.

So much so, in fact, that fans are in danger of missing out altogether on a bumper line-up on the August bank holiday weekend running from August 27-30.

The headline acts set to light up Bramham Park include Charli xcx, Fontaines D.C., RAYE, Dave, Florence & The Machine and Chase & Status.

Stockport indie rock giants Blossoms have been announced as a Leeds Festival-exclusive on the Friday night.

Also on the line-up are Sombr, Skepta, Role Model, Jade, Josh Baker, Geese, Kneecap, Kettama, Skye Newman, Declan McKenna, Holly Humberstone, Maisie Peters, Keo and, for the first time ever, a Thursday night headline slot in Leeds from Kasabian.

The standout news for electronic fans is the launch of The Warehouse: a brand-new, purpose-built home for dance music, pairing high-end lighting with a sound system specifically tuned for a fully immersive club experience.

Also announced is the return of Leeds Festival’s famed late-night home LS23, which has become a fixture of the festival over the years.

Soundtracking late into the night and only found at Leeds Festival, this year’s line-up includes the likes of Lens, Soul Mass Transit System, ZERO, CROSSY, Osmosis Jones and more.

Weekend camping tickets will grant access to the campsites from Wednesday or Thursday through to Monday, as well as arena entry across all show days.

Arena-only tickets for specific days are also available.
