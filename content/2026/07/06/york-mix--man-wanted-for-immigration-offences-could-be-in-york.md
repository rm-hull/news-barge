Police say a man wanted for immigration offences could be in York.

Noel Abdullah, 20, is also wanted for breaching court bail conditions.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Extensive police enquiries are ongoing to locate him, including in York and Leeds, and also Dartford, London, where he has connections.

“If you have an immediate sighting of him, please call 999.”

Abdullah is described as

- black
- about 5ft 4ins tall
- of slim build
- with dark hair possibly in dreadlocks.

If you have any other information that could assist the police investigation, please contact them online. If you are unable to use this option, call 101.
