TWO farm vehicles have been destroyed by fire in a coastal North Yorkshire village.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service says that two of its units were sent out to Station Road in Cayton, near Scarborough, at 10.56am today (July 6).

Reports suggest that a tractor fire spread to adjacent grassland in the area.

“Hose reel jet, breathing apparatus and thermal camera used. 100% fire damage to tractor and hay turner,” said a spokesperson for the service.

They added that the cause is unknown.
