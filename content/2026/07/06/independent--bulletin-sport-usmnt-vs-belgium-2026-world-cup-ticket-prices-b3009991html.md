# World Cup ticket prices drop significantly before crucial USMNT match

- Ticket prices for the USA vs. Belgium World Cup Round of 16 match in Seattle experienced a significant drop, with the "get-in" price falling to $1,508 by 1 pm ET on Monday, representing a 27% decrease over three days.
- The "get-in" price for the USA vs. Belgium game had initially peaked at $ 3,115 after the matchup was confirmed on 1 July but reached a low of $1,283 on Sunday morning.
- This decline in ticket prices mirrors a broader trend across other Round of 16 fixtures, where the average "get-in" price for all games has decreased by 3% in the last three days and 8% over the past week.
- Specific examples include Argentina's match against Egypt, which saw its "get-in" price drop from $1,599 to $1,378, and Switzerland vs. Colombia, which now has the lowest "get-in" price after falling from $972 to $719 over the past two days.
- The winner of the USA vs Belgium contest will advance to face the victor of the Portugal vs. Spain match in Los Angeles on Friday, with the Portugal-Spain game experiencing a more modest price dip from $1,367 to $1,311.
