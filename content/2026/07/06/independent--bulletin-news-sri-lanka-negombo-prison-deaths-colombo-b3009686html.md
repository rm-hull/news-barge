# Sri Lanka prison clashes leave dozens dead and many more injured

- Clashes between prisoners in Sri Lanka have resulted in 25 fatalities and approximately 100 injuries, according to police and hospital sources.
- The incidents commenced on Sunday at a prison in Negombo, a coastal town situated about 35 km north of the commercial capital city of Colombo.
- The conflict involved convicted prisoners and those held under detention, though the specific cause of the clashes has not yet been determined.
- Authorities are currently clearing areas within the prison to account for all casualties, with the military placed on standby to provide support to the police if required.
- Television visuals depicted a significant police deployment outside the prison gates and a police bus transporting injured inmates from the facility.
