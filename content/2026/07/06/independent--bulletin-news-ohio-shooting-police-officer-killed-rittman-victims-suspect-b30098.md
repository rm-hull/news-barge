# Police officer among three dead after responding to 911 call in Ohio

![Two other officers and a police dog were hurt, Wayne County Sheriff Tom Ballinger said](https://static.independent.co.uk/2025/01/10/17/iStock-1169071737.jpg)

- A police officer was fatally shot while responding to a 911 call about gunshots and a disturbance at an Ohio home on Sunday night.
- The incident also resulted in the deaths of the suspect and two other people at the scene, though no further details about these fatalities were provided.
- Two additional officers and a police dog suffered injuries during the confrontation, with their conditions not immediately known.
- The shootings occurred in Rittman, Ohio, approximately 40 miles south of Cleveland.
- “Officers responded to the area and immediately started taking fire,” Wayne County Sheriff Tom Ballinger said. “At this point, we have lost an officer in the line of duty.”
