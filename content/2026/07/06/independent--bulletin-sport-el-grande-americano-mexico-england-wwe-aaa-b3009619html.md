# Who is El Grande Americano? All you need to know about the wrestling star spotted at England v Mexico

![](https://cdn.jwplayer.com/v2/media/UxcVtFXB/poster.jpg?width=720)

- WWE and AAA wrestler El Grande Americano was spotted at the dramatic World Cup match between Mexico and England at the Azteca Stadium on Sunday, with footage shared by WWE’s Espanol social media channel.
- He was accompanied by his girlfriend, Andrea Bazarte, who has previously worked as an announcer for the Mexican promotion AAA.
- Americano, who is actually a German wrestler formerly known as Ludwig Kaiser (real name Marcel Barthel), has rapidly gained popularity in AAA, becoming one of the promotion's top talents.
- He recently won a critically acclaimed mask vs. mask match against the Original El Grande Americano, who was unmasked to reveal Chad Gable, now a babyface in WWE.
- The match itself saw England ultimately triumph over Mexico by three goals to two to set-up a quarter-final clash with Norway on Saturday.
