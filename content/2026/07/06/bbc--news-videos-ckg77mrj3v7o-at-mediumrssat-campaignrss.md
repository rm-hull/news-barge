# Watch: Does Trump’s Fifa intervention undermine football’s integrity?

President Donald Trump confirmed on Monday that he asked Fifa to review USA striker Folarin Balogun's one-match ban at the World Cup. Fifa made the shock decision to suspend the one-match ban for 12 months, leading to widespread criticism.

The BBC's Daniel Bush looks at the US president's comments over his request and what critics are saying about Fifa's reversal.
