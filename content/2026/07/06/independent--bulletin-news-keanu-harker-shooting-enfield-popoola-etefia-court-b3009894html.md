# Drill rappers guilty of gunning down teenager on streets of London

![Keanu Harker, 18, was fatally struck by gunshot as he rode from his home in Enfield, London](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/06/16/01KWVZZMR8JSEBTM9ER1PWND71.jpg)

- Drill rappers Emmanuel Popoola and Teyvon Etefia, both 18, have been found guilty of murdering 18-year-old Keanu Harker in Enfield, north London, following a social media gang dispute.
- On the evening of June 26 last year, Popoola shot Harker in the head and chest with a pistol while chasing him on the back of a high-powered electric dirt bike ridden by Etefia.
- The killing was motivated by a Snapchat row and rivalry between two Enfield gangs, with gang members later bragging about the murder in drill rap videos.
- Following the shooting, Popoola fled to France via Eurostar but was later arrested and extradited, while Etefia was apprehended on a train attempting to escape.
- Eliezer Mbaki, Anais King, and a 17-year-old youth were also convicted of perverting the course of justice for their roles in assisting the killers, with sentencing for Popoola and Etefia scheduled for later this week.
