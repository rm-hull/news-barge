# North Yorkshire Police launch investigation following exposure incidents in Harrogate and Knaresborough

## North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information and witnesses following a number of exposure offences in Harrogate and Knaresborough.

The incident happened at around 1pm and 3pm on Saturday (July 4), close to the Bilton Viaduct and Nidd Gorge area along the River Nidd.

It is believed that two exposure offences were committed on the same day by the same suspect.

The suspect has been described as a white man, between 25 and 35-years-old, around 5f t10inch of a stocky muscular build.

![The police are appealing for information and witnesses following a number of exposure offences in Harrogate and Knaresborough](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOmY1ZWZmNTVhLWRjODQtNGE4MS1iMzQ0LTE2ZjQ4YzdhMjkzYTpiNTU3NWI2NS1kZWU0LTQ5ZjAtYThiNy1iMGNmMWM4NTIxNGU=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



The man has been reported as wearing a black baseball cap and carrying a black rucksack.

A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: “We’re particularly appealing for information about any witnesses that may have been present in the area and have yet to come forward to police.”

If you can help, you should call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

You should quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12260125733 when providing any details regarding the incident.
