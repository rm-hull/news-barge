A North Yorkshire traffic blackspot is closed this afternoon after a vehicle shed its load.

Sutton Bank on the A170 east of Thirsk is shut to clear up a concrete spillage.

North Yorkshire Council said: “We will provide an update when the road reopens. Please use an alternative route.”

#### Sutton Bank, North Yorkshire

Traffic at the moment is coping well in the area.
