Two people were seriously hurt in a crash on the A19 in North Yorkshire.

It was on the southbound carriageway near the junction for Knayton, north of Thirsk, at about 11pm on Wednesday 1 July.

A grey Ford Fiesta was involved.

#### Crash location

Both passengers remain in a serious condition in hospital.

The driver was arrested and has been released under investigation at this stage.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “The road was closed for eight hours while we investigated the scene and to allow the vehicle involved to be recovered.

“We’re appealing for any witnesses to the collision, anyone who may have seen the vehicle prior to the collision, or anyone with relevant dashcam footage, to contact us.

“If you have any information that could assist our investigation, please contact us online in the first instance.”
