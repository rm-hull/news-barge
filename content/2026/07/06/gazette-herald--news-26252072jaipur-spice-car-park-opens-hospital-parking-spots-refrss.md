A SMALL business is offering York Hospital patients, staff and visitors an alternative parking option amid growing concerns about costs and congestion.

Jaipur Spice has opened up a parking space adjacent to its curry house in Haxby Road to NHS patients, staff and visitors to help ease demand during peak times.

Car Park York owner Niyamul Islam Khan said his facility– less than 200 metres from York Hospital’s grounds – is open 24 hours a day.

He is offering stays at his car park at reduced rates for patients and visitors of the hospital, with further discounts for NHS staff.

  


It comes after York Hospital’s car parking facilities were revealed as the most expensive in Yorkshire.

  The car park is open for 24 hours a day, is CCTV operated and less than 200 metres from York Hospital 

*(Image: Newsquest)*

Users have criticised this, among with the site’s limited parking availability and the heavy congestion associated with the car parks.

Speaking about this, a representative for Car Park York said: “The recent debate surrounding congestion in Wigginton Road has highlighted the increasing pressure placed on hospital parking, with delays affecting patients, visitors, NHS staff and local residents alike.

“While long-term transport solutions are being explored, Car Park York believes better awareness of existing nearby parking options can provide an immediate and practical part-solution.”

The company representative added that while they appreciated there was no “single answer” to York Hospital’s parking and congestion challenges, the company hopes to play a “small part” in relieving some pressure.

  “Hospital visits are often stressful enough without spending valuable time sitting in traffic or searching for a parking space" 

*(Image: Newsquest)*

They said: “Hospital visits are often stressful enough without spending valuable time sitting in traffic or searching for a parking space.

“Our aim is simply to offer another option that is convenient, competitively priced and within easy walking distance of the hospital."

Currently, Car Park York visitors are charged £4.00 for parking up to two hours and £6 for stays up to four hours.

All-day passes are priced at £9 for visitors, with registered NHS staff able to park for just £6.

Monthly permits are available to £70 for NHS staff.
