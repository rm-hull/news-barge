A PRIDE flag has been taken down in the heart of a North Yorkshire village.

North Yorkshire Police are investigating an incident of criminal damage that took place in Easingwold’s Market Place last month and saw a Pride flag removed from the flagpole.

The incident took place on Saturday, June 13, at around 10.10pm, with police asking if people had seen a blonde-haired woman by the flagpole.
