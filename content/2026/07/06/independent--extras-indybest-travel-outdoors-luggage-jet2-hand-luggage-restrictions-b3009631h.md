**Read more:Best check-in suitcases, testedWhat are the Jet2 hand luggage dimensions?**

If you’re flying with Jet2, taking hand luggage is free of charge. However, it must weigh no more than 10kg and no 56cm x 45cm x 25cm (including wheels and handles).

You can also bring a small, under-seat bag onboard such as a handbag, laptop bag or a shopping bag from duty-free as long as it doesn’t measure more than 40 x 30 x 20cm in size and can be placed underneath the seat in front of you.

**What is the Jet2 infant baggage allowance?**

If you're travelling with children, you can bring up to two items per child, plus an extra 10kg allowance for infants. These items include a collapsible pushchair, pram or buggy, a car seat or booster seat, a baby carrier and a travel cot.

Each item must weigh no more than 32kg, and any extra items (or weight beyond your allowance) will be charged as an excess cost.

**What hand luggage can you take on Jet2?**

You can bring two bags with you for no extra charge: one bag up to 10kg and a smaller one that fits under your seat.
