MORE than £100 worth of retro video game stock has been stolen from a York city centre shop.

North Yorkshire Police has released a CCTV picture of a man that its officers would like to speak to after a theft at Sore Thumb Retro Games in Gillygate.

It happened on Saturday, June 6, and saw £120 of stock stolen from the shop.

“Please contact us if you recognise the male pictured on CCTV, as he may have information that will assist our investigation,” said a spokesperson for the force.
