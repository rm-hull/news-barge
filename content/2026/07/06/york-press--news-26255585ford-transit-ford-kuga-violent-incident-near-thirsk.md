POLICE are investigating a fight between two drivers near a roundabout in a North Yorkshire town.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a violent incident on the roads in Thirsk last week involving a Black Ford Transit van and a Ford Kuga.

It happened at 10am last Wednesday (July 1) on the A170 near the roundabout joining onto the A61 and saw two drivers get into an argument, which ended up in a fight with minor injuries.
