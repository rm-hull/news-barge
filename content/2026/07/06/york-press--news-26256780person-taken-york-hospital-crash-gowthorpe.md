A PERSON has been taken to York Hospital as a result of a crash along a main road in North Yorkshire.

Yorkshire Ambulance Service joined North Yorkshire Police and North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service in Gowthorpe, Selby at 11.45am on Monday (July 6).

  


It came after two cars were reported to have been involved in a crash, resulting in the main town centre road's closure.
