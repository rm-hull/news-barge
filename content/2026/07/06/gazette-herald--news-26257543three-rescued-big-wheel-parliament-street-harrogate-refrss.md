THREE people have been ‘brought to earth safely’ after becoming stuck in a North Yorkshire fairground attraction.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service says that its Harrogate aerial ladder platform and its supporting appliance were called to Parliament Street in Harrogate at 4.15pm this afternoon (July 6).
