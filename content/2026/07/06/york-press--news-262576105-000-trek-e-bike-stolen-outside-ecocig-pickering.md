A BIKE worth thousands of pounds has been stolen from outside a North Yorkshire shop.

The Trek e-bike, valued at £5,000, was stolen from outside the Ecocig shop on Market Place, Pickering, on Sunday, July 5, at 3.40pm.

A spokesperson for North Yorkshire Police said: "Anyone who witnessed the theft or has any CCTV or dashcam footage is asked to please get in touch.

"If you have any information that could assist our investigation, please contact us online in the first instance by emailing PC 896 Ash Coning, [ash.coning@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:ash.coning@northyorkshire.police.uk)

Alternatively visit the North Yorkshire Police website, select ‘Tell us about’, then choose ‘An existing case or report’ - link below in the comments.

If you are unable to use these options, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Please quote reference 12260126483 when providing information.
