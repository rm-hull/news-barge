                                    The fine dining restaurant at The Old Deanery is set to close later this month.

The restaurant will serve its final customers on Friday 31st July, bringing to an end almost two and a half years of fine dining at the Ripon venue.

The Old Deanery has confirmed it will continue to operate as a hotel and wedding venue, with all booked weddings and events going ahead as planned.

![](https://mmo.aiircdn.com/409/6a4b9ec1406785615a2.jpg)


A spokesperson said:

"After almost two and a half years, the fine dining restaurant at The Old Deanery will be doing its last service on Friday 31st July 2026.

"We will continue to offer overnight stays, and host our lovely weddings and events - with weddings and events being where we started when we first took over the venue in 2021.

"It has been an incredible few years, but the time has come to go back to our roots and focus on different areas of the business.

"Thank you to all our guests, past, present and future - what an amazing ride it has been!"


Tables remain available to book until the restaurant closes at the end of the month.

Customers with dinner reservations from 1st August onwards will be contacted directly, with any deposits refunded.
