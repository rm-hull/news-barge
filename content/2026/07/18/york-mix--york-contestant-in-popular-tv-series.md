**Anna from the charity store, St Leonard’s hospice, in York is a contestant on Great British Sewing Bee. **

She is one of 12 people on the BBC series which airs on Tuesday evenings from 8pm.

It has returned for its 12th series. The new 10-week series premiered on BBC One and BBC iPlayer in July, with 12 new home sewers competing for the crown. 

Patrick Grant and Esme Young have returned as the judges.
