We've dug deep to create a new gallery that captures ordinary moments of life in the city dating back several decades.

We've unearthed 10 photos that are likely to stir memories of people and places in 1990s York.

From change of shifts at the York Carriageworks to people shopping on the high street - and some of the lost but much-loved shops like Woolworths - these archive images might jog a few memories of bygone days in the city.

  Shift change at the York Carriageworks in 1991 . 

*(Image: The Press)*

We've also found some pictures of once familiar sights - who remembers Merlins, on the corner of George Hudson Street and Tanner Row, or when C&A was a retail magnet in the Coppergate Centre?

  Coppergate Centre, including C&A in 1997. 

*(Image: The Press)*

You might even recognise some of the customers enjoying a cuppa in our snap of café culture on Parliament Street.

We hope you like our collection of historic photos of York.
