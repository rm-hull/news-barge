Cach Mercer revealed as Strictly Come Dancing 2026 contestant

The BBC has started revealing the celebrity line-up for Strictly Come Dancing 2026, with Emma Willis, Josh Widdicombe, and Johannes Radebe confirmed as new presenters.

EastEnders star Lacey Turner was the first contestant announced, followed by Dani Dyer, who is returning after withdrawing from last year's show due to injury.

Australian singer Delta Goodrem, celebrity hairstylist Chris Appleton, and Love Island's Cach Mercer are also confirmed, with Mercer's inclusion reportedly breaking a show rule against reality stars.

TV presenter Will Best and Paralympian cyclist Dame Sarah Storey, the most successful Paralympian of all time, have also joined the cast.

Former football referee Mark Clattenburg is currently rumoured to be among the remaining contestants for the upcoming series.
