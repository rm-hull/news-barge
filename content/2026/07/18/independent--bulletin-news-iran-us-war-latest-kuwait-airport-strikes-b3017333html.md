# Flights suspended at Kuwait Airport as Iran and US exchange strikes overnight

- Kuwait Airways rescheduled all flights after operations at Kuwait International Airport were suspended due to alleged "hostile missile and drone attacks" from Iran.
- The US and Iran exchanged fresh strikes for a seventh consecutive night, with Iran claiming to have hit US military assets in Bahrain.
- Jordan intercepted 10 missiles, while Saudi Arabia and Bahrain issued public alerts regarding potential threats in the region.
- Iran claimed to have launched operations targeting maritime traffic in the Strait of Hormuz, stopping four vessels and reporting two oil tankers exploded, an account rejected by the US military.
- Iranian authorities stated 10,000 people were left without water following a US strike on a desalination plant in Jask county, with the US reportedly targeting civilian infrastructure.
