## 
  **100 per cent of nothing is still nothing**

Morrell House has cost York taxpayers £145,000 just to stand empty since 2018. Every proposal to bring the place back into beneficial use has come to nothing because of a political ideology that is in denial of financial reality.

Local Councillor Danny Myers insists any residential redevelopment must be 100 per cent affordable housing. He would do well to look back on York council’s disastrous policy of demanding a 50 per cent affordable contribution from all new housing schemes.

The council had to change their policy when they discovered 50 per cent of nothing is nothing.

Unfortunately, they haven’t learnt from experience because 100 per cent of nothing is also nothing and while that demand is in place Morrell House has no prospect of being redeveloped.

It will remain a wasted asset and an unwelcome eyesore costing taxpayers year on year.

  **Matthew LaverackRetired Architect & HousebuilderLord Mayors WalkYork**
