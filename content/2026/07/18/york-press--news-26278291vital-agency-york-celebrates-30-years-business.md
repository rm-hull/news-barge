A digital marketing agency that started life in 1990s Yorkshire is now marking 30 years in business.

Vital Agency, now based in York, has expanded from a website design and internet services company into an award-winning full-service digital and design agency. It now delivers e-commerce, branding, SEO, and digital marketing services to clients nationwide.

The company relocated to its York headquarters in 2022 and is led by managing director Andy Topps.

Mr Topps said: "What a journey it's been.

  


- 
    **Claire's Accessories went under owing almost £20million**
- 
    **Mansley Serviced Apartments to open in York in Spring 2027**
- 
    **The Cat Gallery in Low Petergate celebrates 25 years in York**
- 
    **Bruberrie Café And Bar set to open second site in York**

"We’ve had a few iterations of business over the years but to reach this milestone as a small independent agency, proudly run from York, is an achievement we're delighted to celebrate.

"It's only been possible thanks to our fantastic team, loyal clients, and everyone who's been part of the Vital story along the way."

Recent projects include brand guidelines for Piglets Adventure Farm, a visitor attraction just outside York, along with a design report for LifeSearch, a financial protection firm.

The agency also created its own new website earlier this year.

Other standout work includes a bespoke WordPress website for Bolton Castle, a privately owned visitor attraction located in North Yorkshire supported with digital marketing, including social media.

Mr Topps said: "Working to improve the digital output of a 14th-century castle is a true juxtaposition but embracing this unique property in the heart of the Yorkshire Dales was a pleasure.

"Starting with initial design ideas but always bearing in mind what the visitor needs to see and how easily they can make bookings involved many conversations."

The agency used advanced custom fields for flexibility and prioritised security to deliver a “highly adjustable solution” for the client.

He added: "Early indications are superb with online bookings across the full range of ticket options for the castle.

"Enhanced SEO & GEO are in place and already picking up traction soon after launch."

Vital Agency was Winner of Prolific North Awards 'Web Development Project of the Year’ for Short Lifts, creating an award-winning WordPress brochure site with integrated Magento v2 eCommerce + Hyvä solution.

Other national clients include Pelsis, an industry leading provider of pest control products for which the agency built a bespoke Magento eCommerce with SAP B1 integration.

Despite working with firms across the UK, Vital Agency said it is keen to strengthen relationships closer to home.

The company also hopes to create opportunities for York-based developers and creatives, while helping clients navigate new technologies, including artificial intelligence.
