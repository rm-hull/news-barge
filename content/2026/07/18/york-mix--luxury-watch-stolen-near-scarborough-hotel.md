Police are appealing for Information following the theft of a TAG HEUER Watch in Scarborough.

It happened near to the Crescent Hotel between 06/07/2026 – 07/07/2026 and between 2200-0400 hrs.

Enquiries are ongoing to establish the circumstances surrounding the theft and identify those responsible. Relevant CCTV enquires have also been conducted in the area.
