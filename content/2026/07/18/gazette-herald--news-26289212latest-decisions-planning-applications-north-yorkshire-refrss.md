Search the Public Notice PortalHere are the latest planning applications submitted to North Yorkshire Council for Claxton, Sheriff Hutton, Swinton, Gilling East, and Sherburn.

In Claxton, a property on Main Street will have 9 metres of hedgerow removed to create an open frontage for new parking provision.

The application, relating to Wellfield, was received and validated on June 2 and has since been approved.

Ref. No: ZE26/00468/HN | Received: Tue 02 Jun 2026 | Validated: Tue 02 Jun 2026 | Status: Decided

Permission has been granted for the demolition of a former pig housing facility at Ash Tree House in Sheriff Hutton.

The application was received on May 28 and validated on June 11.

Ref. No: ZE26/00471/DNO | Received: Thu 28 May 2026 | Validated: Thu 11 Jun 2026 | Status: Unknown

In Swinton, permission has been granted for a single-storey side extension at 16 Cherry Avenue.

A certificate of lawfulness for the proposed development was received and validated on May 28.

Ref. No: ZE26/00457/CLOPUD | Received: Thu 28 May 2026 | Validated: Thu 28 May 2026 | Status: Approved

In Gilling East, the council has approved the removal of a dead branch from a sycamore tree subject to Tree Preservation Order No. 40/1966.

The tree is located at Sycamore Cottage, 1 Cawton Road.

Ref. No: ZE26/00447/TPO | Received: Fri 22 May 2026 | Validated: Fri 22 May 2026 | Status: Approved

In Sherburn, a single-storey rear extension has been approved at Lyncroft on Mill Field.

The application was received on May 7 and validated on May 21.
