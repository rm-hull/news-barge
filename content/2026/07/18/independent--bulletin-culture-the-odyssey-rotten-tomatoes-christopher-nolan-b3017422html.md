Christopher Nolan's new film, The Odyssey, has become his most acclaimed movie to date, achieving a career record.

The film currently holds a 95 per cent score on Rotten Tomatoes, making it his highest-ranked film on the review aggregate site, surpassing titles like The Dark Knight and Interstellar.

Initially debuting with 97 per cent, the score for The Odyssey has slightly dropped as more verified user reviews were added.

The Independent awarded The Odyssey a five-star review, with Clarisse Loughrey calling it Nolan's best film to date and praising its adaptation of the source text.

The movie is projected to earn over $117m (£87m) in its opening weekend in the US alone, positioning it as the biggest debut for a live-action film released in 2026.
