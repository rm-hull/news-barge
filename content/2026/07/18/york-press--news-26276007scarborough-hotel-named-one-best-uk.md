A hotel on the North Yorkshire coast has been named one of the best beach hotels in the UK.

Which? has released a list of the best beach hotels to visit this summer, with the Bike and Boot in Scarborough making it onto the list.

The Bike and Boot is dog-friendly and is popular with both families and those wanting to go hiking around the area.

The hotel has a mixture of rooms available with doubles and king-size beds, as well as sofa beds, and some come with sea views if you don’t mind paying a little bit extra.


## What is the Bike and Boot in Scarborough like?

The Bike and Boot can be found just a short walk away from Scarborough Beach if you want to take the whole family, including the dog, on a seaside trip.

On the hotel, Which? said: "The starchily traditional Georgian building at the posh end of Scarborough’s long seafront conceals what’s probably the most dog-friendly hotel we’ve ever visited.

"Most of the canine guests are impeccably behaved, but one or two greet each other like lads on a raucous stag do.

"No one seems to mind, though, at this outdoorsy cross between a chic boutique and a youth hostel.

"In the basement, there’s space to leave a bike or surfboard, and wash your dog, then pop next door to a mini-cinema with velvet seats.

"Upstairs has a trendy coffee-shop vibe, with sea-green and grey corridors, and bikes hanging on the lounge wall."


The hotel’s café, Bareca, could be a great place to stop off and relax for a quick coffee and cake before heading out on a hike, or to treat yourself to a cocktail after a long day exploring.

You can even visit for an evening meal with plenty of seafood dishes, pizzas, small plates, and desserts to enjoy.

The Which? inspector also recommends trying the breakfast available for the DIY pancake making, which is described as a "delight".

Which? added that for the Bike and Boot’s target audience of hikers and surfers, the hotel is "ideal".

### What do visitors of the Bike and Boot in Scarborough think?

On Tripadvisor, one visitor said: "The hotel has the feel of a boutique hotel without being particularly small.

"We found the room cosy and comfortable and warm with a nice sea view.

"There is a coffee making machine in the room and a kettle but there is also free coffee in a lounge downstairs with free cake at 4 pm.

"The restaurant has sea views and breakfast was fine.

"The hotel is in an ideal location with a lot of attractions nearby.

"We plan to go back sometime in the future."
