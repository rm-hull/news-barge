# Andy Burnham set to unveil big energy plan as rumours swirl over Cabinet

- Andy Burnham, the new Labour leader, is set to announce a cost of living package as one of his initial acts upon becoming Prime Minister.
- His plans reportedly include measures to reduce energy bills, such as making heat pumps cheaper than gas boilers and cutting VAT on energy.
- Mr Burnham is expected to become Prime Minister on Monday, with anticipation of a rapid introduction of new policies.
- Speculation is widespread regarding his Cabinet appointments, with Shabana Mahmood, Wes Streeting, and Angela Rayner frequently mentioned for significant roles.
- However, sources close to Angela Rayner have dismissed reports suggesting she will take on the position of Health Secretary.
