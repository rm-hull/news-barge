A school has raised nearly £6,000 in just a few hours at its summer fair.

Amotherby School's Parent Teacher Association (PTA) annual summer fair netted a record-breaking £5,799, drawing on community spirit and local business support.

The fair, featuring traditional stalls and activities, saw a range of popular events including a teddy tombola with 201 teddies, jolly jars, and a welly-wanging competition.

There were also 184 tombola prizes, 79 faces painted, and 66 competition entries.

Since its formation in 2022, the PTA has raised £39,369.

Funds have been used for a new playground, an eco-garden, school trips, Year 6 leavers' hoodies, theatre trips, and more.

The PTA was formed after a hiatus in fundraising activities due to Covid-19.

A spokesperson for the PTA said: "Amotherby School PTA is incredibly thankful for all the support of local businesses and everyone who attends their events because without them the PTA wouldn’t be as successful as they are."
