## Investigation launched after attack at popular North Yorkshire venue

North Yorkshire Police believe this man may have information that will help their investigation *(Image: North Yorkshire Police)*

AN INVESTIGATION has been launched after an attack at a busy York venue.

North Yorkshire Police have released this image of a man that they'd like to speak to after a person was assaulted at a popular York event last month.

It happened on June 27 at York Racecourse.
