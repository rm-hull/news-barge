The scores are in — the latest batch of food hygiene ratings for York restaurants, cafes, takeaways and businesses has been revealed.

Environmental health officers have been visiting businesses across the city to see if they are meeting hygiene requirements.

Based on guidelines set by the Food Standards Agency, inspectors look to see how clean the facilities are; what food safety management procedures are in place and how hygienic food handling is.

Ratings are then dished out from five to zero — with the higher score the better the result.

  **Five - Very Good**

- Nelsons Bakery at 32 Fishergate, York, rated 5 on July 1, 2026.
- Holiday Inn Express at Malton Road, Huntington, York, rated 5 on July 3, 2026.
- B&B York at 15 St Peters Grove, York, rated 5 on July 7, 2026.
- Dhillon Bridge Fisheries at 4 Intake Avenue, York, rated 5 on July 8, 2026.
- YHA York at 42 Water End, York, rated 5 on July 9, 2026.

Businesses may have been reinspected since, and this will be reflected in our next round-up.
