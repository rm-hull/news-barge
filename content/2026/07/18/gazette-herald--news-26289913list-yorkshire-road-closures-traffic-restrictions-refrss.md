### What is the Public Notice Portal?

The Public Notice Portal carries statutory public notices published in local newspapers and is the fastest and most effective way of finding out what is happening in YOUR neighbourhood.

Search the Public Notice PortalCity of York Council has issued a series of temporary traffic orders for various works taking place across the city.

The affected roads include Glebe Avenue, Mansfield Street, Cemetery Road, Park Grove, South Bank Avenue, Abelton Grove, Tudor Road, Outgang Lane, Moor Lane, Low Mill Close, Millfield Lane, and Rawcliffe Drive/Rawcliffe Lane.

Full details for each closure are as follows:

- Glebe Avenue, Acomb: A section between property No 8 and its junction with Malvern Avenue will be closed from July 20 to July 22, from 8am to 6pm each day, for water mains works. A diversion will be in place via Carr Lane, Boroughbridge Road, and Malvern Avenue. Resident and emergency vehicle access will be maintained.
- Mansfield Street, York: Closed for resurfacing between Foss Islands Road and a point 101 metres north-east from July 20 to July 22, from 8am to 5pm each day. There will be no signed diversion, but resident and emergency vehicle access will be maintained.
- Cemetery Road, York: Closed for carriageway patching between Heslington Road and the southern boundary of property No 26 from July 20 to July 26, between 7pm and 6am each day. Traffic will be diverted via Fulford Road, Fishergate, Fawcett Street, Paragon Street, and Barbican Road. Resident and emergency vehicle access will be maintained.
- Park Grove, York, and Emerald Street: Closed for gas mains works from July 22 to August 28, daily from 8am to 5pm. The closure affects Park Grove between properties 24 and 62, and Emerald Street between the boundary of property No 1 and its junction with Park Grove. The diversion route is via Huntington Road, Monkgate, Lord Mayors Walk, Clarence Street, Haxby Road, Stanley Street, Neville Street, Eldon Street, Amber Street, and Earle Street. Emergency access will be maintained. Parking bays on Park Grove and Neville Terrace will also be suspended.
- South Bank Avenue, York: Closed between Nunthorpe Grove and Trafalgar Street from July 22 to July 23, between 9.30am and 3pm each day for highway works. A diversion will be in place via Bishopthorpe Road, Balmoral Terrace, and Trafalgar Street. Resident and emergency vehicle access will be maintained.
- Abelton Grove, Haxby: Closed for resurfacing from July 22 to July 24, between 8am and 5pm each day, from South Lane to a point 141 metres south. There will be no signed diversion, but resident and emergency vehicle access will be maintained.

- Tudor Road, Acomb: Closed between Stuart Road and Gale Lane for maintenance from July 27, between 9.30am and 2.30pm. A diversion will be in place via Stuart Road, Danesfort Avenue, and Gale Lane. Emergency access will be maintained.
- Outgang Lane, Osbaldwick: Closed for telecoms works from July 27 to July 29, daily from 8am to 6pm, from a point 290 metres north-east of its junction with Murton Way to a point 183 metres further north-east. No diversion will be provided, but resident and emergency vehicle access will be maintained.
- Moor Lane, Woodthorpe: Closed to traffic for water mains works from July 27 to July 29, daily between 7pm and 6am, between its roundabout junction with Tadcaster Road and a point 52 metres north-west. A diversion will be signed via Moor Lane, Askham Lane, Gale Lane, Thanet Road, St Helen’s Road, and Tadcaster Road. Resident and emergency vehicle access will be maintained.
- Low Mill Close, York: Closed daily between 9am and 4pm for water mains works, from July 27 to August 14, between property Field View and a point 213 metres north-west. No diversion will be provided. Alternative on-street parking has been arranged within resident parking zone R39A. A footpath closure is also in place, with a diversion for pedestrians.
- Millfield Lane, Poppleton: Closed for electrical mains works from July 27 to August 30, between property No 114 and a point 16 metres south-east of Millfield Lane level crossing. A diversion will be in place via Low Poppleton Lane, Boroughbridge Road, the A1237, and Millfield Lane. Resident, business, and emergency vehicle access will be maintained. A footpath closure is also in place, with pedestrian and cycle diversions signed.
- Rawcliffe Drive and Rawcliffe Lane, Clifton: Closed for highway remedial works from July 28 to July 30, from Rawcliffe Drive’s junction with Rawcliffe Lane to property No 1A and Rawcliffe Lane’s junction with Fairway to property No 34. A diversion will be in place via Rawcliffe Lane, Clifton, Water Lane, and Green Lane. Resident and emergency vehicle access will be maintained.

The council has stated that all orders will not prohibit emergency access or access to premises, provided it is not prevented by ongoing works or safety concerns.

Traffic signs and barriers will indicate the extent of restrictions.
