TWO people have been trapped in a lift in a residential property in a coastal North Yorkshire town.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said crews from Whitby were called to Waterstead Lane in the town around 7pm on Tuesday, July 7.

  



Power to the building was shut off and restored, a service spokesperson said, adding that the two people managed to free themselves.
