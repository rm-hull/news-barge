                                    Organisers of the Harrogate Food and Drink Festival have confirmed the complete live music and entertainment programme for their seventh annual celebration beside the historic grounds of Ripley Castle.

Taking place on the 22nd-23rd August, ticket holders can anticipate fourteen live performances through the weekend.

An eclectic mix of local soloists, community groups and independent bands are scheduled to take centre stage, while two critically-acclaimed tribute acts headline each evening.

The recently-announced programme further details a diverse selection of brand-new family activities and attractions, including the debut of a fourth live stage.

**Saturday 22nd August**

Headlining Saturday evening are *Fleetwood Shack*, an established six-piece ensemble dedicated to celebrating the music of Fleetwood Mac.

The full Saturday line-up includes:

- 11:00 Will Forrester Smooth Saxophone
- 12:00 Harrogate Spa Town Ukes
- 13:15 The Broken Flowers Band
- 14:30 Leeds City Stompers
- 16:00 Viva Vinyl
- 17:45 Fleetwood Shack
- 19:15 Ibiza Sax

**Sunday 23rd August**

Headlining Sunday evening are *The Freddie and Queen Experience,* a celebrated tribute to Queen and the legacy of Freddie Mercury.

The full Sunday line-up includes:

- 11:00 Will Forrester Smooth Saxophone
- 11:45 Howlin Mat
- 13:00 Hannah Goodall
- 14:15 Tempo Feliz
- 15:30 Danny Charles
- 17:15 The Freddie & Queen Experience
- 18:30 Ibiza Sax Classics

*Assistant Festival Manager Izaak Shuttleworth* added:


“One of our key priorities is to provide a platform for some of the region’s brightest emerging talent.

"From rock, pop and indie to jazz, swing, funk and soul, this year’s line-up has been carefully curated to ensure there’s a little bit of something for everyone.”

**New Free Family Attractions**

Organisers have further unveiled their most vibrant, diverse entertainment programme to date.

Most prominently, the *Sizzling Showdown Arena* makes its debut as the event’s fourth live stage.

The arena, hosted by renowned comedian Neil Frost, brings together four hourly, free-to-play live shows that are accessible to all ticket holders:

- 12:30 The Foodie Olympics
- 14:00 Wally Woof’s Family Dog Show
- 15:00 The Chilli Challenge
- 16:00 Pies Down, Eyes Down Bingo with Barry Bingo

Further new family attractions include:

- Inflatable Banana Jousting - Putting balance, strength and determination to the test
- Viking Heritage Kitchen - Demonstrating authentic Viking cooking techniques and cuisines

Early bird tickets are still available, priced at £6.95 for an adult, £4.95 for Under 16’s and Free for Under 5’s.

*For more information, Early Bird Tickets, and updates, visit www.harrogatefoodfestival.com*
