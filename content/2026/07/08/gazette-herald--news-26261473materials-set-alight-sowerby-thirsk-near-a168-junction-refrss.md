BUILDING materials have been set alight in the open in a North Yorkshire town, close to a major road network.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said crews from Thirsk were called to Bankside Close in Sowerby, near Thirsk around 12.30am (Wednesday, July 8).

  


The location is close to the A168 at its junction with the A19, two main roads in the county.


A service spokesperson said: "Crews used 1 Hose Reel Jet to extinguish the fire and no further action required."
