## VetPartners CEO takes on Ambleside ultra marathon for two charities

Jo Malone, founder and CEO of VetPartners, is set to take on a gruelling ultra-marathon to raise funds for Vetlife and Macmillan Cancer Support *(Image: VetPartners)*

A veterinary CEO is set to take on a gruelling ultra marathon for two charities close to her heart.

Jo Malone, founder and CEO of VetPartners, will run the 58.4km 5 Valleys Ultra in Ambleside on September 26 to raise funds for Vetlife and Macmillan Cancer Support.

The race includes 2,303 metres of ascent across challenging, technical terrain.

Ms Malone said: "Having seen so many loved ones, and colleagues within the business affected by cancer, I wanted to do something tangible to help raise funds for Macmillan.

  The race includes 2,303 metres of ascent across challenging, technical terrain 

*(Image: VetPartners)*

"For someone who wasn’t particularly sporty at school, taking on this challenge on the other side of 50 has definitely pushed me out of my comfort zone.

"I’m hoping we can raise a significant amount of money for two fantastic causes."

The challenge builds on her fundraising earlier this year when she completed a half marathon at the Lakeland Trails in Coniston, raising £3,750 for Vetlife.

Michelle Gratton, Vetlife Donations Relations Manager, said: "We are incredibly inspired by the commitment and enthusiasm shown by VetPartners colleagues in supporting Vetlife this year.

"We are grateful for the support of VetPartners and its colleagues, whose fundraising efforts help us continue providing vital support to members of the veterinary community."
