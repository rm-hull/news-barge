# Harrogate Town AFC launches first-ever girls-only Summer Soccer Camp this summer

## Harrogate Town AFC is launching its first-ever girls-only Summer Soccer Camp this summer, giving girls the opportunity to play, learn, and grow.

The new initiative will welcome girls in school years one to nine, providing a dedicated environment where young players can develop their football skills, build confidence and enjoy the game alongside other aspiring players.

Led by Harrogate Town AFC’s experienced FA-qualified coaching team, each session will combine expert coaching with fun activities and football-focused sessions suitable for players of all abilities.

The club says the camp has been designed to create a safe, supportive and inclusive space centred around girls’ football, allowing participants to improve their skills while enjoying a positive team environment.

Sessions will take place on selected Fridays throughout the summer holidays at St John Fisher Catholic High School, with families able to choose the dates that best suit them.

The Girls Summer Soccer Camp will run on:

- Friday, July 31

- Friday, August 7

- Friday, August 21

- Friday, August 28

Each session will run from 10am until 3pm.

Places cost £28 per day for non-members and £25 per day for Player Pathway members.

Current members of Harrogate Town AFC’s Player Pathway, including those involved with the ETC and COE programmes, can also benefit from a discounted daily rate.

With spaces limited, families are being encouraged to book early to secure a place.
