# When will Arthur Fery play next after racing into Wimbledon semi-finals?

![Arthur Fery stormed into the Wimbledon semi-finals (Mike Egerton/PA)](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/08/17/a9ce6ecc17b25d515e576c0da546887eY29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzgzNjEzNTE5-2.85237174.jpg)

- Arthur Fery delivered the performance of his career to beat Flavio Cobolli in straight sets in their Wimbledon quarter-final on Wednesday afternoon.
- Fery was taking on the Italian ninth seed who reached the French Open final last month, but he produced almost flawless tennis to secure a 6-4, 7-6, 6-0 victory.
- Fery, 23, was given a wildcard entry into the tournament and has made the most of his opportunity, with the Brit now dreaming of grand slam glory.
- Fery will be back on Centre Court for his semi-final on Friday afternoon, when he will take on German second seed Alexander Zverev, who beat US star Taylor Fritz in his own quarter-final.
- The other men’s semi-final will also take place on Friday, with Jannik Sinner due to face Novak Djokovic in a blockbuster showdown.
