# People really paid for trash from Taylor and Travis’ wedding

- Trash collected near the wedding venue of Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce in New York City has been sold online.
- Justin Gignac, known for selling NYC street trash, hand-picked items including cigarette butts, bottle caps, and an ovulation test kit.
- The items were sold under the title "NYC Pocket Garbage: Not Invited Edition (Taylor & Travis’ Wedding)" for up to $25.
- Gignac's website indicated that the collected trash, which also featured a lone AirPod and a ring pop, has now sold out.
- Separately, a bag of air reportedly collected at the wedding was listed on eBay for $49,999.99.
