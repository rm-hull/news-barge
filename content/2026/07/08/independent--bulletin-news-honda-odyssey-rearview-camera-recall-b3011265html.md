# Honda recalls over 300,000 vehicles over rearview image display issues

![Honda is recalling 325,588 Odyssey vehicles in the U.S.](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/08/14/04/GettyImages-2094530804-(1).jpg)

- Honda Motor America is recalling 325,588 Odyssey vehicles in the U.S. due to a defect affecting the rearview image display, as reported by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration.
- The recall affects certain 2018-2020 model year Odyssey vehicles.
- The issue involves the potential for water to enter the rearview camera, which can cause it to fail and not display an image when the vehicle is in reverse.
- This malfunction significantly increases the risk of a crash, posing a serious safety concern for vehicle occupants and others.
- As a remedy, Honda dealers will replace the faulty rearview camera in all affected vehicles at no cost to the owners.
