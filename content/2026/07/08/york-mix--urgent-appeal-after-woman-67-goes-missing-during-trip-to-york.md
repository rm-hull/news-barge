Police are searching for a woman who’s gone missing during a visit to York.

Judith Bell, 67, from the Bingley area in West Yorkshire, was last seen on Petergate at 3.15pm today (Wednesday).

Judith is described as

- white
- about 5ft 6in tall
- of slim build
- with grey/white shoulder-length hair in a bob.

She may be wearing a pink T-shirt with ‘Happy Campers’ written on it, blue denim trousers, and carrying a ‘Savers’ carrier bag.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “If you see Judith, please call 999 so we can quickly make sure she is safe.

“If you have any other information that could help us find her, please call us on 101.”
