*I’m a Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here!* might be four months away – but there are already plenty of names rumoured to be appearing on the next series.

ITV is once again facing renewed pressure to pull together an impressive list of famous faces due to the high calibre of stars the BBC has managed to recruit for *The Celebrity Traitors*– season two of which will be arriving later this year.

*I’m a Celebrity*, which started in 2002, is now on its 26th series, and Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly will return to host proceedings from the Australian jungle. It’ll be the first series since a second run of the all-stars show aired.

The series made headlines thanks to the biggest showdown in reality TV history after a fallout between Adam Thomas and Jimmy Bullard, which even put Ant and Dec through their paces.

If Ant and Dec are hoping for an easier go of it later this year, ITV might be secretly hoping the show will generate as many headlines – and if speculation is to be believed, the lineup will feature the usual array of TV presenters, soap stars and reality show personalities.

Below is a list of the biggest rumours so far:

**Christopher Dean**

![Christopher Dean](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/08/9/32/Screenshot-2026-07-08-at-10-13-50.png)

Christopher Dean – one-half of Olympic champions Torvill & Dean – might be skating to another ITV series following the cancellation of *Dancing on Ice* in 2025. 

While producers have reportedly been asking him for years, the 67-year-old’s recent retirement puts him in the spotlight as a likely contender, with the skater believed to be “looking for a new challenge”.

**Nadiya Bychkova**

![Ex-’Strictly’ dancer Nadiya Bychkova](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/06/03/13/37/Screenshot-2026-06-03-at-14-23-09.png)

Former *Strictly Come Dancing* professional Nadiya Bychkova, 36, might be swapping the dancefloor for the jungle, with __ The Sun__ reporting that her axing from the BBC series has freed her up to appear on this year’s series. However, Bychkova herself inadvertently distanced herself from

*I’m a Celeb* by claiming she’ll still be involved with

*Strictly*, albeit behind the scenes.

**Su-Elise Nash**

![Mis’Teeq Su-Elise Nash](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/08/9/13/Screenshot-2026-07-08-at-10-22-08.png)

Bringing a flash of Noughties nostalgia to the jungle could be Su-Elise Nash, best known as a member of girl group Mis-Teeq. Nash, 45, will be reuniting with Alesha Dixon and Sabrina Washington for a one-off reunion show in September. But months after, as reported by *The Sun*, she could be jetting to Australia for* I’m a Celebrity*, with an insider stating: “The timing couldn’t be better.”

**Ben Stokes**

![Retired cricketer Ben Stokes](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/08/9/43/Ben-Stokes.jpg)

There’s not much to the rumour that cricketer Ben Stokes, 35, will be this year’s sportsman on*I’m a Celebrity*, other than that he retired from the sport in June. He’s now one of the favourites to make an appearance on the reality TV juggernaut.

**Perri Kiely**

![Diversity star Peri Kiely](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/08/9/21/Screenshot-2026-07-08-at-10-18-49.png)

The Diversity star and former KISS radio presenter, 30, has had to turn down multiple offers to appear on *I’m a Celebrity*– but speaking on podcast* Brothers Uncovered* earlier this year, he said that he would accept if his schedule permitted. Whether producers will call upon his services again remains to be seen.

**Olivia Attwood**

![TV personality Olivia Attwood](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/08/9/34/Olivia-Attwood.jpg)

The former *Love Island* star, 35, is going to be on*I’m a Celeb* rumours lists forevermore after being forced to quit the series after just one day in 2022. 

She left the show “as a precautionary measure” in order to “undergo some medical checks”, and has since suggested she’d return to the show if her busy schedule permitted. Could this be her year to have a second attempt?
