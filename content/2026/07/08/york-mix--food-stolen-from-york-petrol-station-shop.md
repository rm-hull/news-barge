Police are investigating a theft at a York petrol station.

Food was stolen from Shell on Hull Road on Friday, 19 June.

Today detectives issued this CCTV picture. “Please contact us if you recognise the male pictured on CCTV, as he may have information that will assist our investigation,” a force spokesperson said.
