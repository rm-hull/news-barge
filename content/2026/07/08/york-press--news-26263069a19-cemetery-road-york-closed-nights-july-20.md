A BUSY York road is set to be closed for several nights later this month.

Parts of the A19 – Cemetery Road in Fishergate – are set to close for five nights in July for resurfacing works.

The road will be shut from 7pm on Monday, July 20, and is expected to be completed by 6am on the morning of Saturday, July 25 – reopening during the day where possible.

It will be closed between Winterscale Court and the crossroads with Kent Street and Heslington Lane.

Access to all vehicles – aside from emergency services – will be restricted and a diversion will be in place via Fishergate and Paragon Street

In a letter to residents, City of York Council said that works will be carried out by an external contractor on their behalf who will be working overnight between 7pm and 6am.
