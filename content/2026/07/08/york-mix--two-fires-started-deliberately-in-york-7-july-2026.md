Firefighters were called to two fires started deliberately in York last night (Tuesday).

The York crew were called to an incident on **Heworth Green** at**6.41pm**.

#### Heworth Green, York

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “On arrival the fire was found to be 3m by 4m and well alight.

“Crews used knapsack sprayers and a bucket to extinguish the fire.”

Then, at **11.30pm**, an Acomb crew was called to** Pulleyn Drive**, next to Knavesmire and Little Hob Moor, to a small fire in the open.

#### Map location

The spokesperson said: “The fire had been extinguished before the arrival of fire crews by the police on scene.”
