## Why these York twin sisters are having a DOUBLE celebration at their school prom

Twin sisters Ellie and Maygen Mchale from Vale of York Academy ahead of their school prom

  **York twins win special school prom prize for hard work**

IT was a double dose of summer celebration for these York twins who won a special prize ahead of their prom

Twin sisters Ellie and Maygen Mchale from Vale of York academy won a pre-prom photoshoot at their leavers' assembly for good attendance and hard work throughout their GCSEs.

They had a photoshoot at Clifford's Tower in York before heading into their prom at the Hilton York Hotel on Thursday, June 25.

  Twin sisters Ellie and Maygen Mchale from Vale of York Academy ahead of their school prom


Ellie and Maygen are just two of the hundreds of teenagers across the York area who have been dressing up for their end-of-school-year celebrations.
