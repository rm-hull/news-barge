Thieves broke into a shed in North Yorkshire and stole a high-value mountain bike in a late-night raid, police said.

The black carbon Santa Cruz bike was taken from Low Pasture Close in Pickering at around 1.05am on Tuesday (July 8).

Hours later, the bike was found in the beck on Mill Lane and has since been returned to its owner.

North Yorkshire Police said it is believed the suspects also attended nearby Hugden Close where attempts were made into garden sheds.

The force is urging anyone with information about the theft, including CCTV or doorbell footage, to get in touch.

"We’re particularly appealing for information and any footage in and around Low Pasture Close, Hugden Close, and Mill Lane," a police spokesperson said.

"Or for anyone who may know or have information that could assist us in identifying the individuals responsible."

Anyone with information is asked to email [nate.mills@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:nate.mills@northyorkshire.police.uk) or phone 101 quoting reference 12260127571.
