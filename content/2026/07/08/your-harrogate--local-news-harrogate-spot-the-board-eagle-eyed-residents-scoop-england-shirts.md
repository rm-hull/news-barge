                                    Three eagle-eyed Harrogate residents have won new England shirts thanks to Your Harrogate and Verity Frearson Estate Agents.

You’ve probably seen lots of Verity Frearson ‘For Sale’ and ‘Sold’ signs around town - but there’s a new style in particular that you should be on the lookout for…

They've put a football spin on a few of their signs in the Harrogate area for our 'Spot the Board' competition.

And Steven Wood, Rachel Robson and Monique Hemingway snapped a photo with one of the boards, uploaded to social media and tagged Your Harrogate and Verity Frearson before being selected at random as our first three winners.

But it doesn't stop there...

To celebrate England's progress to the World Cup quarter final vs Norway this weekend, we're giving away ONE MORE new England shirt in time for the match!

Just follow those three simple steps:

- Find one of Verity Frearson's football-themed boards in the Harrogate area.
- Snap a photo and upload the image to social media
- Tag Your Harrogate Radio and Verity Frearson in the post

Could you be our final winner?
