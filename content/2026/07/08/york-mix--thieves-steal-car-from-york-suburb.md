Thieves have stolen a car from a street in York.

They took a silver Kia Ceed from the road on Bedale Avenue, Osbaldwick.

It happened between 9.40am and 9.55am on Wednesday 1 July.

#### Bedale Avenue, Osbaldwick

North Yorkshire Police asked:

- Do you have any information which would help find those responsible?
- Did you hear or see anything around the above time and date?
- Do you have any CCTV coverage capturing the offence, or anything happening in the area before or after?
- Do you have a vehicle with a dash-cam parked in the area?

If so, they ask you contact them – see below.
