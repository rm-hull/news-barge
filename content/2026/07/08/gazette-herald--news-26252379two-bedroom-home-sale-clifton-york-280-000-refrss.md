A “beautifully presented” home has the market in York.

The two-bedroom house is for sale in Baker Street, Clifton, for £280,000.

  The two-bedroom house for sale in Baker Street, Clifton, York, for £280,000 

*(Image: Ashtons/Zoopla)*

Ashtons, which is marketing the end-of-terrace property, described it as “a York gem not to be missed”.

A spokesperson for the estate agent said it is “full of character, sitting just a short stroll from the boutique shops and cafés of Bootham”.

Inside, a carpeted entrance hall leads to a living room with a bay window.

Also on the ground floor is a reception room flowing into the modern kitchen, as well as a three-piece family bathroom.

Upstairs, the home has two double bedrooms with built-in wardrobes.

The main bedroom has an adjoining room, currently a dressing room, which Ashtons said has “excellent potential” to be converted into a “stunning en-suite, workshop or study space”.
