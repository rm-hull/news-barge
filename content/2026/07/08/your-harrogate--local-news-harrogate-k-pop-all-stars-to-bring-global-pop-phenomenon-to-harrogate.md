                                    Fans of K-pop are in for a treat later this month as K-Pop All Stars heads to Harrogate for a high-energy live show.

Celebrating one of the world's biggest music movements, the production comes to the Main Auditorium at the Harrogate Convention Centre on Sunday 26th July, promising an evening packed with chart-topping hits, spectacular choreography and stunning visuals.

The show features performances inspired by some of K-pop's biggest names, including BLACKPINK, NewJeans, KATSEYE and BTS, alongside numbers inspired by the hit film *K-pop Demon Hunters*.

![](https://mmo.aiircdn.com/409/6a4e01bf254376d34e2.jpg)


Audiences can expect a celebration of the genre's biggest anthems, with synchronised dance routines, dazzling stage production and an atmosphere designed to recreate the excitement of a live concert in Seoul.

*The performance starts at 7:30pm, with tickets available now via  Harrogate Theatre's website.*
