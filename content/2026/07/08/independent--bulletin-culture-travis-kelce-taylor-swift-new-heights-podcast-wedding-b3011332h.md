# Travis Kelce drops New Heights podcast finale days after fairytale wedding

- The Kelce brothers dropped their New Heights podcast series finale, which featured guest Tom Brady, on Wednesday, just days after Travis Kelce married Taylor Swift.
- The Kansas City tight end married Swift on Friday, July 3, at NYC’s Madison Square Garden with 1,000 star-studded guests in attendance.
- On the season finale of his New Heights podcast, Kelce reflected on his milestone year and his proposal to Swift, which he confirmed happened shortly after Swift's appearance on the New Heights season premiere. Travis called the episode “one I’ll remember forever.”
- There was no wedding talk during the episode, which People Magazine confirmed was pre-recorded.
- The huge event is said to have been planned with the help of elusive celebrity wedding planner Mark Seed, who was behind the celebrations for Swift’s frequent collaborator Jack Antonoff and his wife, actor Margaret Qualley.
