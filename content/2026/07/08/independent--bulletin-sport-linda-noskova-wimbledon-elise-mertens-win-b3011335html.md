# Linda Noskova reaches Wimbledon semi-finals in grand slam breakthrough

- Linda Noskova has reached the Wimbledon semi-finals, defeating Elise Mertens of Belgium.
- The ninth seed secured her victory with a 6-3 7-5 win on Court One.
- This achievement marks the 21-year-old's first appearance in a grand slam semi-final.
- Noskova is the second Czech woman to advance to this stage of the tournament, alongside compatriot Karolina Muchova.
- She is scheduled to face Ukraine's Marta Kostyuk in the semi-final for a place in Saturday's final.
