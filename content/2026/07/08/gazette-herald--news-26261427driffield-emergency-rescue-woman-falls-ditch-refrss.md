A WOMAN has fallen into a ditch in the suburbs of an East Riding of Yorkshire town and emergency services were called in to carry out a rescue.

Humberside Fire and Rescue Service said a crew was sent to New Walk in Driffield around 6.15pm yesterday (Tuesday, July 7).

  



A service spokesperson said firefighters helped rescue an adult woman from the ditch using throw lines and an ambulance scoop stretcher.

The incident was handed over to paramedics, the spokesperson said.
