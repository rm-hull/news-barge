A five-star hotel in Yorkshire is set to welcome a unique champagne picnic experience.

Grantley Hall is set to host Veuve Clicquot's Le Picnic experience in its grounds this summer.

The seasonal outdoor event is open to both hotel guests and visitors, whose ticket will grant them access to curated light bites from Grantley Hall's culinary team, a bottle of Veuve Clicquot Yellow Label Champagne, and a relaxed croquet game on the estate's manicured lawns.

Le Picnic has built a reputation for fusing Veuve Clicquot's signature joie de vivre with gourmet outdoor dining and social interaction.

With its launch at Grantley Hall, it is tipped to be one of the most desirable al fresco experiences of the summer.

Visitors can expect a blend of the hotel's tranquil countryside setting, first-class hospitality, and the distinctive chic style that Veuve Clicquot brings to the experience.

Renowned for its hospitality, Grantley Hall is a member of The Leading Hotels of the World and PoB Hotels.

It offers 47 luxury bedrooms, five restaurants including the Michelin-starred Shaun Rankin at Grantley Hall, three bars, a country pub, the Three Graces Spa, and the state-of-the-art ELITE Luxury Gym.
