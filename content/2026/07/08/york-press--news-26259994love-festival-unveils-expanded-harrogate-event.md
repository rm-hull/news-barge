A popular house music festival is returning to Harrogate.

Love To Be... will take place at The Stray on Saturday, September 5, featuring artists such as Claptone, Sam Divine, Paul Woolford, and Todd Terry.

The 2026 festival will include an expanded main stage, a new chillout zone, and an upgraded VIP area.

The Ilkley-based business, The Hair & Makeup Project, will introduce the Glam Van, a mobile beauty space offering treatments like festival braids and biodegradable glitter.

VIP ticket holders will enjoy a redesigned area featuring interactive games, exclusive DJ sets, an upgraded stage, and a magician.

  Claptone, Sam Divine, and Gok Wan will all appear at Love to be... 

*(Image: @zak_watson)*

The main stage, with a 3,000-capacity, has been enlarged and equipped with improved sound and production systems.

The festival's layout has been redesigned for better flow, with more room between stages.

A new chillout area will provide a break from the dance floor, featuring bean bags and soft lighting.

The festival will switch to a traditional bar system for faster service, and an expanded food village will offer more choices.

Tickets for Love To Be... Festival 2026 start at £51.50 plus a booking fee and are available on Skiddle.

The festival, which began in Sheffield in 1994, has become a significant event in UK house culture.
