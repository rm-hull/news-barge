A NEW shop is set to bring a touch of childhood nostalgia to York city centre.

Rocket’s Hideout – a trading cards shop focusing on popular Japanese game and TV series Pokémon – is set to open its doors in 11 Micklegate next weekend.

Owned by Lee Glenn and named after a villain’s liar from the first Pokémon game, this will be the brand’s second location with their other shop opening its doors in Selby in 2023 – offering fans a chance to play, trade, and open packs together.

It will also stock other popular trading card games (TCGs) including Magic: The Gathering, Lorcana, Star Wars, Yu-Gi-Oh, Dungeons and Dragons, and Warhammer.

Lee said: “We're really excited about opening in York. Rocket's Hideout started in Selby in 2023 and we've built something we're really proud of there, so expanding to York feels like the natural next step.

“It's a city that loves its independents and we think York is a great fit for what we offer. 11 Micklegate will be our second location and we want it to be more than just a shop- a proper community hub for Pokemon collectors, players and anyone who's TCG-curious.

“We're predominantly a Pokémon TCG retailer, though we'll also be stocking other trading card games, alongside accessories, collectibles and hosting regular community events, leagues and trading days.”

Rocket’s Hideout will open its doors at 10am next Saturday (July 18)
