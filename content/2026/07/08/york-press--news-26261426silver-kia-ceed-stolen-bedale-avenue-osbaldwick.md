CAR THIEVES have targeted a suburb of York in broad daylight just after rush hour.

North Yorkshire Police said a silver-coloured Kia Ceed was stolen from the roadside in Bedale Avenue, Osbaldwick, close to the A1079, between 9.40am and 9.55am on Wednesday, July 1.

  



North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information.

Officers are asking the public to come forward if they saw or heard anything in the area at the time or have CCTV or dash-cam footage.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Constable 1222 David Fulford at [david.fulford@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:david.fulford@northyorkshire.police.uk), quoting reference 12260123779.
