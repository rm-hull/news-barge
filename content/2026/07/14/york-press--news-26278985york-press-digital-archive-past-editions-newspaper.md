## What to do if you missed a copy of The Press you wanted

Sign up to a digital subscription of The Press to access e-editions from our archive and other benefits. *(Image: Newsquest)*

Have you missed a photo of your child in the newspaper and can't get hold of a copy?

The Press is offering readers a way to access past editions of the newspaper through its 'e-edition' function on its website.

This tool grants online subscribers the ability to flick through pages of the latest edition without needing to purchase a physical copy.

Furthermore, readers can download and print PDFs, save clippings of text or pictures, and search through a digital archive of editions dating back to July 2020. A separate archive which provides scanned pages from edition before this date can also be accessed here.

  The Press 

*(Image: Newsquest)*

This service can be accessed by signing up for a monthly digital subscription on The Press's website at www.yorkpress.co.uk/subscribe/

Digital packs, including access to the e-edition of the newspaper and a digital app, are available for £4.99 per month.

Alternatively web-only subscription option provides unlimited access to The Press, along with puzzles, for £3.99 per month or £39 per year.

This subscription can be cancelled at any time.

As an added benefit, subscribers can take advantage of the newspaper's Readers' Reward scheme.

This programme offers exclusive access to hundreds of offers, deals, and discounts.

Each week, subscribers also get to enjoy 50 new puzzles and crosswords online.

This development provides a multiple-avenue access to The Press from the comfort of your own home.
