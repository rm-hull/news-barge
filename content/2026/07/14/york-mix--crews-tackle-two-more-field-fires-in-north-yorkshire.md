It’s been another very busy night for North Yorkshire firefighters as they tackled more field blazes.

Last night, crews from Acomb, York, Huntington and Tadcaster tackled a crop field fire between **Rufforth** and**Long Marston**.

It sent large clouds of smoke over the area.

By **6.28pm**, Acomb, York and Tadcaster remained on scene damping down hotspots after extinguishing the fire with beaters and hose reel jets.

At **9.01pm**, firefighters were called to a combine harvester on fire in a field of crops** between Kirkbymoorside and Pickering**.

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “The fire, confined to the harvester only, was extinguished by crews in breathing apparatus using hose reel jets with the water supplied in a relay between the two fire engines from the closest hydrant available.”
