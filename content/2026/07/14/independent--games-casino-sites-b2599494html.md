## Payment methods for online casinos UK

The top UK casinos should offer a range of different deposit and fast casino withdrawal options, giving you the choice of how you manage your casino funds.

Here are the most secure payment methods available to casino users.

**Debit cards:** Nearly all UK casino sites will accept some kind of debit card, offering a secure and instant method for making deposits. Withdrawals via debit cards will take a little longer. Any credit card transactions have been prohibited in the UK since April 2020.

**Mobile wallet:** As more users opt for mobile casinos, the number of sites accepting instant deposits and withdrawals from mobile wallets has increased. Apple Pay casinos, Google Pay, and Samsung Wallet are fast becoming readily available payment methods for casino sites.

**E-wallets:** PayPal, Neteller, Skrill and Paysafecard offer instant deposits, like debit cards, but are often far quicker when it comes to making withdraws. They also offer a means to make transactions with a casino without revealing any financial details.

**Pay by phone:** Pay by phone casinos are another convenient way for mobile players to manage their funds. Unlike mobile wallets, transactions are funded via a user’s phone bill or prepaid balance. However, withdrawals are not possible by this method.

**Bank transfers:** Bank transfer casinos offer one of the most secure methods to make deposits and withdrawals and support higher transaction limits. Unfortunately, they can incur additional fees with certain banks or casinos and take longer to process.
