A MAN has suffered a fractured nose in a late-night assault at a pub.

North Yorkshire Police has released a CCTV image of a man it wishes to speak to in connection with an attack at a Scarborough pub last month.

The incident took place at the Sun Inn in the town's centre around 1.45am on June 21.

They said the man sustained facial injuries, including a fractured nose, during the attack.

A spokesperson for the force said: "Following extensive CCTV enquiries, we are now asking for the public's assistance to identify the man shown on the right of the the image, wearing a black t-shirt and white trainers.
