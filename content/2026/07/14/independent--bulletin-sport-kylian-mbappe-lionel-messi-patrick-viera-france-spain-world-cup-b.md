# Kylian Mbappe or Lionel Messi? ITV pundit issues verdict on who is the better player

![](https://cdn.jwplayer.com/v2/media/wb4wj3d9/poster.jpg?width=720)

- Patrick Vieira has declared Kylian Mbappe to be a better player than Lionel Messi.
- Mbappe is currently in exceptional form for France at World Cup 2026, leading the Golden Boot race with eight goals.
- His strong performance for the national team follows a challenging season with Real Madrid.
- Vieira compared Mbappe to legendary French forward Thierry Henry, noting Mbappe's superior speed and ability to score in tight spaces.
- Mbappe himself has stated his focus is on France's success in the tournament rather than individual goal tallies or comparisons with Messi.
