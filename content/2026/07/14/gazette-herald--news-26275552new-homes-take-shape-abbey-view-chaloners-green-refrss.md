A new community in Whitby is taking shape with a fresh wave of housing.

Barratt and David Wilson Homes are developing the site at Abbey View and Chaloners Green, offering three- and four-bedroom properties.

The North Yorkshire town combines beaches, harbour views, and access to the North York Moors, and is also known for its historic charm.

Steven Ball, sales director at Barratt and David Wilson Homes North East, said: "Abbey View and Chaloners Green allow us to deliver high-quality homes in a location that offers real lifestyle benefits for a variety of buyers.

  Maddie Hugill, Sales Adviser at Barratt and David Wilson Homes 

*(Image: Barratt and David Wilson Homes)*

"From its stunning coastline and countryside surroundings to its range of everyday amenities, residents can enjoy everything they need right on their doorstep."

The homebuilder has highlighted the appeal of Whitby through the eyes of a local sales adviser, Maddie Hugill, who described the town as offering "incredible views, beautiful walks and plenty to enjoy all year round".

She said: "For house hunters seeking a location that truly has it all, Whitby stands out.

"Its coastal setting, strong sense of community and access to local amenities make it a great choice for modern living."

Abbey View and Chaloners Green offer buyers a choice of homes in a scenic coastal setting.
