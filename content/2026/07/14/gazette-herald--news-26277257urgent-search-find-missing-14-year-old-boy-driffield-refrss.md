POLICE are searching for a teenager missing from an East Yorkshire town.

Coby, 14, from Driffield, was last seen in Beverley around 1.45pm yesterday (Monday, July 13).

  


He was reported missing soon after.

Humberside Police has issued a description of the missing teenager.

The force describes him as being 5ft 8ins tall, with blonde hair, and is believed to be wearing black joggers and a black hoodie.

Anyone with information regarding Coby's whereabouts is urged to call the Humberside Police non-emergency number 101, quoting log 275 of July 13.
