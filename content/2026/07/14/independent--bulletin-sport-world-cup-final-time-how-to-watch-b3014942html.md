Madrid erupts as Spain score first goal against France in World Cup semi-final

The World Cup 2026 final is scheduled for Sunday, July 19, at 3 p.m. ET at the New York New Jersey Stadium.

The final will be contested by the winners of the semi-finals: France versus Spain, and England versus Argentina.

This World Cup final will feature the tournament's first official half-time show, curated by Chris Martin and including performers such as Madonna, Shakira and Justin Bieber.

A Closing Ceremony, featuring acts like Tom Cruise and Laura Pausini, will precede the match, starting 90 minutes before kick-off.

The third-place play-off will occur on Saturday, July 18, at 5 p.m. ET at the Miami Stadium in Florida.
