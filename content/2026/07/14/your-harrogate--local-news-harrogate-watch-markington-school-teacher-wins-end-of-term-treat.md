Another teacher has received a special end of term treat thanks to Harrogate BID.

We asked listeners to nominate a teacher that deserves a treat as thanks for their hard work over the course of the academic year.

It's part of our 'End of Term Treat' campaign with Harrogate BID, with five teachers set to receive a £50 Harrogate gift card.

And the second winner is Mrs Robinson at Markington CE Primary School.

The nomination read:

“Mrs Robinson has been so great helping our daughter with her extra needs at school, now pushing her to being comfortable with both social and learning.

“She helps in her spare time with sporting events to get the girls into some team building, which has really helped our daughter who struggled so much a couple of years ago.”
