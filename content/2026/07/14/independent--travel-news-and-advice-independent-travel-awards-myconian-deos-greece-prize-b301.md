With just weeks left to enter The Independent Travel Awards, now is the time to tell us about your most exceptional getaways for the chance to win one of 12 incredible prizes.

Among them is this three-night Greek getaway for two in the whitewashed walls of Mykonos, just minutes on foot from the Old Port of Mykonos Town.

The lucky winner and a guest will have the chance to experience the height of Cycladic luxury with this stay at Myconian Deos, a member of The Set, during the summer 2027 season.

The hotel, a hilltop sanctuary offering panoramic views of Mykonos Town and the Aegean horizon, combines contemporary design with timeless hospitality and promises an unforgettable immersion in the refined lifestyle of Mykonos.

The prize includes three nights at Myconian Deos, for two people sharing a double guest room with a sea view on a bed and breakfast basis, subject to availability across the summer season 2027. T&Cs apply.

All rooms feature luminous interiors, semi-open marble bathrooms, king-sized or queen beds, a pillow menu, block-out blinds and “silent” aircon.

Guests will also be treated to daily breakfasts with Greek flavours as part of the prize.
