A York venue is opening its doors to the public tomorrow for the hotly anticipated World Cup semi final, where England will battle it out with Argentina for a spot in the final.

Patch inside The Bonding Warehouse, a co-working hub on Terry Avenue, by the River Ouse, has announced it’ll be ‘following England on their route to glory’, and opening Patch to the public for the semi-final **tomorrow, Wednesday 15 July**.

The venue will be showcasing all the highs, lows, and every memorable goal on the big screen, in this family-friendly World Cup watch party.

The venue will open at 6pm, two hours before kick off, so there’ll be plenty of time to grab a seat, refreshments and calm nerves before the big game.

Patch York will be serving up soft drinks, alcohol and snacks, so you can stay fuelled throughout the game (or cry into your snacks…)

Want the atmosphere of the match that avoids the floor covered in sticky beer? Head on down to Patch at The Bonding Warehouse for an evening that’s fun for all the family.

Tickets are free but must be booked via Eventbrite.
