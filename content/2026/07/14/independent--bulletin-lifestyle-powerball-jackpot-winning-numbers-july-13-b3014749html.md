# Powerball players urged to check tickets as jackpot rises to $498 million

![A ticket with five numbers, but missing the Powerball number, was sold in Virginia](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/15/05/GettyImages-2252768467-(2).jpg)

- Monday night’s Powerball drawing concluded without a grand prize winner, causing the estimated jackpot for Wednesday's drawing to climb to $498 million.
- This marks the 31st consecutive drawing without a jackpot winner, with the previous estimated jackpot standing at $480 million.
- A single ticket sold in Virginia matched five numbers but missed the Powerball, securing a $1 million prize.
- The winning numbers drawn on Monday were 5, 25, 36, 40, 48, and the Powerball number was 3.
- The odds of matching all six numbers to win the Powerball jackpot are 1 in 292.2 million, with the game played in 45 states, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, and the U.S. Virgin Islands.
