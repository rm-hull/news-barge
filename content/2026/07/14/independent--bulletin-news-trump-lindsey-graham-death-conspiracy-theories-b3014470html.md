# Trump breaks silence on Lindsey Graham death rumors

![Pressure mounting on Donald Trump ally Lindsay Graham over Georgia ballots phone call](https://static.independent.co.uk/2020/11/18/00/GettyImages-1061513362.jpg)

- Donald Trump addressed conspiracy theories surrounding the sudden death of Senator Lindsey Graham, who passed away on July 11 from a ruptured aorta after returning from Ukraine.
- During an interview on Newsmax, Trump initially quipped he would 'love' to confirm foul play but then dismissed the idea of anything untoward.
- Trump attributed Graham's death to a hereditary condition, referencing his 'racehorse theory' and noting Graham's father died at a similar age from heart complications.
- He stated that 'great doctors' had examined the case, confirming an aortic rupture, a condition he believed was hard to see and fix.
- Graham's office later released a preliminary report from the Medical Examiner of the District of Columbia, confirming death from an aortic rupture caused by hardened arteries.
