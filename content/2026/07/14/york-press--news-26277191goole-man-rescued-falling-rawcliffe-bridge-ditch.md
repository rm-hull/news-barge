A man has been rescued after falling into a ditch at the side of railway lines near an East Yorkshire town.

Humberside Fire and Rescue Service said crews were called to Moor Road in Rawcliffe Bridge, near Goole, at around 11.30pm yesterday (Monday, July 13).

  



The man suffered a suspected broken ankle, the service said.

He was rescued from the fall by firefighters.

A Network Rail van was deployed to transport the man to the side of the road.

He was then moved to an ambulance and left in the care of paramedics.
