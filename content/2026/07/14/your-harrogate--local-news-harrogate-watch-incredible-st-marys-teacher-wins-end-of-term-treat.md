                                    Another amazing teacher has received an end of term treat thanks to Harrogate BID.

We asked listeners to nominate a teacher that deserves a treat as thanks for their hard work over the course of the academic year.

It's part of our 'End of Term Treat' campaign with Harrogate BID, with five teachers set to receive a £50 Harrogate gift card.

And our fourth winner is Miss Millie Corcoran from St Mary’s Catholic Primary School.

The nomination read:

“As a mum, I simply cannot thank her enough for the endless support, dedication, and love she has shown to my daughter, during what has been a very tough year.

“Whenever my daughter has a little worry, Miss Corcoran is always the first to pick up on it. She resolves the issue immediately, stopping the anxiety in its tracks before it can progress into a migraine.”
