Police have issued an urgent appeal to find a man missing from his home in North Yorkshire.

Paul Brown, 62, from Scarborough, was last seen at 7am on Tuesday (July 14) in the Longwestgate area of the coastal town, North Yorkshire Police said.

The force said “extensive enquiries” are ongoing to locate Mr Brown, as it urged anyone with information about his whereabouts to “contact us immediately”.

Mr Brown is described as white, 5ft 10in tall, large build and bald, and wears a hearing aid in his left ear. He may be wearing black trousers, a white t-shirt and a grey body warmer.

Police said Mr Brown is known to travel by bus and may have travelled to the Scalby Road area.

“If you have an immediate sighting, please call 999,” a police spokesperson said.

“If you have any other information that could assist our investigation, please contact us online.”

Those with information are asked to contact North Yorkshire Police via its website or by phoning 101 quoting reference 12260133657.
