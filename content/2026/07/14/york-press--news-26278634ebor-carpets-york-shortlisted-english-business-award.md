A well-known York carpet company run by a father and son has been shortlisted for an England Business Award.

Adam and Kirk Jackson operate Ebor Carpets, which is based in Farndale Avenue, Osbaldwick.

Adam says the pair are 'incredibly proud' to have been shortlisted.

He told the Press: "We started Ebor Carpets four years ago with the aim of providing honest advice, high-quality flooring and outstanding customer service.

"Since then, we've helped almost 2,000 customers transform their homes and businesses, and together we have 68 years of experience in the carpet and flooring trade.

Adam continued: "Although the business is relatively young, our combined experience of 68 years in the industry means we bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every job.

"We supply and professionally fit carpets, vinyl flooring, laminate, luxury vinyl tiles (LVT) and other hard flooring for both residential and commercial customers.

"From helping customers choose the right flooring to expert installation, we provide a complete service from start to finish."

In addition to the award recognition, the business is highly raised by customers. It has 4.9 stars out of five on Google.

Adam explained: "Being a family-run business means every customer receives a personal service. We take great pride in our workmanship, professionalism and customer care.

"We never believe in cutting corners and always treat every home as if it were our own. Much of our work comes through recommendations and repeat customers, which we believe says everything about the service we provide."

It certainly seems a recipe for success.

Adam said: "Business is going really well, and we're grateful for the continued support from our customers and the local community. We believe this is down to our reputation for quality work, reliable service and honest advice. Word-of-mouth recommendations have played a huge part in our growth over the last four years."

The pair find out if they are a winner on Sunday at a black tie event at the Queens Hotel in Leeds.

Adam added: "We'd like to thank every customer who has supported us over the years. Reaching nearly 2,000 customers in just four years is something we're incredibly proud of, and being shortlisted for an England Business Award is a fantastic achievement for our family business.

"We'll continue to work hard to provide the highest standards of workmanship and customer service, and we look forward to helping even more people with their flooring in the years ahead."
