# Pedestrian fatally struck by three hit-and-run drivers in Long Beach

![A pedestrian has died after being struck by three vehicles](https://static.independent.co.uk/2025/01/10/17/iStock-1169071737.jpg)

- A pedestrian was killed in Long Beach late Monday night after being struck by three different vehicles, and all of the drivers left the scene without stopping, police say.
- The man, whose name has not been released, was hit around 11:40 p.m. at the intersection of Ocean Boulevard and Golden Shore, where he was pronounced dead by Long Beach Fire Department firefighters.
- "The preliminary investigation indicates an unknown black vehicle was traveling eastbound in the number two lane on Ocean Boulevard just west of Golden Avenue when it struck a pedestrian who was crossing northbound outside of a crosswalk," police said.
- "The pedestrian was struck again by a white truck and a third time by a red sedan," police said. "Neither vehicle stayed on scene."
- Anyone with information about the deadly crash is urged to contact Long Beach police or LA Crime Stoppers.
