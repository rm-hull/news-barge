                                    A popular teacher at Grove Road Community Primary School in Harrogate is the latest to receive an end of term treat thanks to Harrogate BID.

We asked listeners to nominate a teacher that deserves a treat as thanks for their hard work over the course of the academic year.

It's part of our 'End of Term Treat' campaign with Harrogate BID, with five teachers set to receive a £50 Harrogate gift card.

After winners from Ripley and Markington, our third winner has been revealed as Mr Trayling, Year 6 teacher at Grove Road.

The nomination read:

“He has continued to be steadfast and unwavering in his support of all the children in a turbulent time at the school.

“My daughter has thrived with him as her teacher, he is the kindest, most incredible soul, and anyone who is lucky enough to meet him is immediately drawn in by him.”
