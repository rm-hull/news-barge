AN INVESTIGATION has been launched after food was stolen from a North Yorkshire supermarket

North Yorkshire Police have released a CCTV image of an individual that its officers wish to speak to in connection with theft from a Harrogate shop.

The incident took place at the Co-op in Bilton Lane on June 24 and saw food stolen.
