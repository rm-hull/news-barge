# Trump’s ‘big news’ speech set to revive 2020 election claims

- President Donald Trump plans a primetime speech on Thursday to revive his unsupported claims of fraud regarding the 2020 election.
- Trump stated the speech would concern his grievances about the 2020 election and other matters, calling it “really, really big news.”
- He is expected to be joined by top intelligence officials, including CIA Director John Ratcliffe and acting Director of National Intelligence Bill Pulte.
- Pulte, appointed despite lacking relevant experience, has been reviewing documents for declassification to provide ammunition for Trump's claims.
- An ODNI report from March 2021 previously concluded that no foreign adversary attempted to alter or hack the technical aspects of the 2020 voting process.
