# Are Jude Bellingham’s leg veins normal? An expert on when to seek medical attention

- Jude Bellingham's prominent leg veins have become an internet talking point during England's World Cup campaign.
- Visible veins are common in elite athletes due to low body fat, powerful leg muscles developed through intense training, and increased blood flow during exercise.
- Playing in high temperatures and the use of blood-flow restriction (BFR) shorts, which temporarily make veins fuller, can further enhance their visibility.
- BFR shorts are used by athletes for training and recovery to increase muscle strength and size, but they do not permanently enlarge veins.
- While visible veins are typically harmless for athletes, any veins that become painful, swollen, warm, or hard warrant medical attention, as they could indicate conditions like varicose veins or deep vein thrombosis.
