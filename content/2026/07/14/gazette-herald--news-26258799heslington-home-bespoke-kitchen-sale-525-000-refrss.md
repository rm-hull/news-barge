A four-bedroom detached house with a bespoke kitchen is on the market in York for £525,000.

Located in Sails Drive, Heslington, the home has an attractive walled garden, off-road parking, and an integral garage.

Hudson Moody, the estate agents, said the home has undergone an extensive programme of refurbishment by the current owners in recent years.

  Living room with bio-ethanol fireplace, dual-aspect windows and French doors 

*(Image: Hudson Moody)*

The house has a welcoming reception hallway with a guest WC and stairs leading to the first floor.

The living room at the rear of the property has a feature fireplace with a bio-ethanol fire, dual aspect windows, and French doors opening to the rear garden.

The modern bespoke kitchen is a highlight of the property, featuring French doors opening to the rear garden, an inset four-burner gas hob with an extractor hood, and an electric oven.

  Bespoke kitchen with integrated appliances and French doors to the garden 

*(Image: Hudson Moody)*

It also includes integrated appliances such as a washing machine, slimline dishwasher, and microwave, with space for a freestanding tall fridge/freezer.

A bay-fronted dining room with wood flooring is another feature of the ground floor.

Upstairs, a first-floor landing with a storage cupboard leads to four generous bedrooms.

  Walled garden with patio seating area and summer house 

*(Image: Hudson Moody)*

The main bedroom benefits from a modern en-suite shower room with a heated USB mirror, and the house bathroom has a shower over the bath and a heated USB mirror with a Bluetooth speaker.

Outside, the house is approached by a double driveway providing off-road parking for two to three cars, leading to an integral garage.

There is also side access to the walled garden, with a patio seating area and summer house.

For more information, or to book a viewing, contact Hudson Moody on 01904 595181.
