The National League will aim to claim back-to-back wins over the American League for the first time since 2012 in the 2026 MLB All-Star Game at Citizens Bank Park, Philadelphia. Below we have all the start times and how to watch the **2026 MLB All-Star Game** live streams online, on TV and from anywhere.

After the American League won nine successive All-Star Games between 2013 and 2022, the National League has turned the tables in recent years with victories in 2023 and 2025. Their triumph 12 months ago in Atlanta came in dramatic circumstances as they won the first tiebreaker in the history of the All-Star Game, with the Philadelphia Phillies' Kyle Schwarber hitting three home runs and being named MVP.

Schwarber is one of six Phillies players ready to shine on home turf at Citizens Bank Park, including National League starting pitcher Cristopher Sánchez, but there are many more who are playing in their first Midsummer Classic. In total, 29 players will make their All-Star Game debuts in Philadelphia, including Toronto Blue Jays starting pitcher Dylan Cease and New York Yankees pitcher Cam Schlittler, both representing the American League.

There will also be emotional appearances from more experienced stars, including the American League's Mike Trout of the Los Angeles Angels, who will appear in his 12th All-Star Game less than 50 miles from his hometown of Millville, New Jersey. Several of his teammates and opponents are from much further afield, with 22 internationally born players set to be involved.
