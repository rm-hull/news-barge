# The World Cup superstar who ghosted Tom Holland

- Tom Holland revealed he was ghosted by footballer Erling Haaland after sending him a direct message.
- The *Spider-Man* star told Jimmy Kimmel on his US talk show that he messaged the Norway player after spotting him in a nearby hospitality suite at the Monaco Grand Prix.
- Holland said: “That is the exact type of humbling experience that is important for actors.”
- He then joked Haaland might not want to dine with him after England's recent World Cup quarter-final victory over Norway.
- Haaland previously explained his side of the story on the Norwegian version of *The Assembly,* adding: “I don’t watch movies much, so I have no idea who people are. There was one asking if we could go out for dinner, but I’d never seen him, so I didn’t bother to answer.”
