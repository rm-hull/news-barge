# The touching meaning behind the latest royal baby’s name

![A photo posted by Flora Vesterberg of her baby daughter, Isabel Marina](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/7/18/746041563_18563733205068480_1440510633989667503_n.jpeg)

- King Charles' third cousin, Flora Vesterberg, and her husband Timothy have announced the birth of their first child, a daughter named Isabel Marina Vesterberg.
- Isabel was born on 8 July, and her name pays tribute to Flora's paternal great-grandmother, Princess Marina, Duchess of Kent.
- Princess Marina, of Greece and Denmark, became a British princess upon her marriage to Prince George, Duke of Kent, in 1934.
- The baby is the first great-grandchild of Princess Alexandra, a cousin to the late Queen Elizabeth, and is expected to be 62nd in the line of succession to the British throne.
- Flora, an art historian, first announced her pregnancy in January; she married Timothy in a private ceremony in 2020, followed by a church blessing in 2021.
