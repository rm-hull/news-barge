## Fire crews, police and ambulances to descend on major York venue - here's why

Emergency services are set to descend on York's LNER Community Stadium for a major training drill. <i>(Image: North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue)

Emergency services will be conducting a training exercise at York's LNER Community Stadium tomorrow (Wednesday, July 15).

The operation will unfold between 9am and 2pm, involving several fire engines and partner agencies including Yorkshire Ambulance Service, and police.

  **Recommended reads:**

Crews will be practising their response to an incident where members of the public require decontamination.

  Emergency services drill at York Community Stadium tomorrow. 

*(Image: North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service)*

A spokesperson for North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service said: "If you are in the area of the Vangarde Shopping Park please do not be alarmed if you see several fire engines or partner agencies from Yorkshire Ambulance Service and the Police in attendance."

The training exercise aims to "keep our communities safe," added the spokesperson.
