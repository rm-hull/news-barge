# EuroMillions results: Winning lottery and Thunderball numbers for Tuesday 14 July

- Tonight’s EuroMillions draw has offered players the chance to win a jackpot of £32million.
- To win the prize, a player had to get five main numbers plus two lucky stars.
- In the UK, every EuroMillions entry automatically includes a unique "UK Millionaire Maker" code, entering you into an additional draw with a guaranteed £1 million winner in every draw.
- The winning EuroMillions numbers were **10, 19, 37, 42** and**47**. The Lucky Stars were** 9**and** 12**.
- The winning Thunderball numbers were **1, 15, 24, 25 and 36**. The Thunderball was 7.
