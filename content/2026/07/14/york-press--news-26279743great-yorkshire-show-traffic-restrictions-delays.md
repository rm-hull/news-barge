TRAVELLERS are warned of restrictions, detours, and delays after a major farming event has started at a popular North Yorkshire venue.

North Yorkshire Council and Great Yorkshire Show organisers have warned that major traffic restrictions will be in place for this year’s Great Yorkshire Show and visitors are warned to expect busy roads.

This follows major congestion between Harrogate and Knaresborough this morning.

The council have introduced a series of measures on roads in Harrogate, Knaresborough, and surrounding areas to manage the high volumes of traffic expected between July 14 and 17.

An extended one-way system will be in operation from 6.30am each day on show days.

A spokesperson for the Great Yorkshire Show said: "Please follow the signed routes to the Show on your approach to Harrogate and do not rely on satellite navigation systems as these may direct you onto a route with limited access or temporary restrictions."

The A661 Wetherby Road will operate one-way from the Kestrel Roundabout to the Rudding Lane junction towards the showground on a morning.

On the A658, the system runs towards Harrogate from the Thistle Hill junction.

This will be reversed on Wetherby Road at 2.00pm between Sainsbury’s traffic lights and The Kestrel Roundabout to accommodate traffic leaving Harrogate.

The reversed system will remain until 9pm, although it may be lifted earlier depending on traffic conditions.

The council has issued 15 temporary restrictions in total, including closures, banned turns, and suspended weight limits.

  **The restrictions are as follows:**

- One-way systems and banned turns on the A661 Wetherby Road, A658 John Metcalf Way, B6163 Thistle Hill, and Forest Lane.
- Parking restrictions on Hookstone Drive, Hookstone Wood Road, and residential roads north of Hookstone Drive.
- Suspended 7.5-tonne weight limits on Forest Moor Road and Thistle Hill for the duration of the event.
- Route changes will be signed, and alternative routes will be in place to help drivers avoid congestion and reach the showground efficiently.
