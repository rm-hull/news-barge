                                    Police have issued CCTV of a man following a shop theft in Harrogate.

It happened on the 24t June at Co-op on Bilton Lane and involved the theft of food items.

A police spokesperson said:

“Please contact us if you recognise the male pictured on CCTV, as he may have information that will assist our investigation.”


Anyone with information is asked to email PC1488 Bell on [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk), call North Yorkshire Police on 101 or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

Please quote reference 12260117921 when providing information.
