A man reported missing from his home in North Yorkshire has been found safe.

North Yorkshire Police confirmed on Tuesday afternoon (July 14) it had called off the search for the missing Scarborough man reported missing earlier that day.

The force had issued an urgent appeal to trace the man's whereabouts after he was last seen at 7am on Tuesday in the Longwestgate area of Scarborough.

In an update on Tuesday afternoon, a police spokesperson said: "Earlier today we issued an appeal to find a missing 62-year-old man from the Scarborough area.

"We are very pleased to report that he has been found safe.

"Thank you to everyone who shared our appeal."
