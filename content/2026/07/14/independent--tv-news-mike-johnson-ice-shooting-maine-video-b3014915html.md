# Mike Johnson says he knows nothing about ICE killing man in Maine

Mike Johnson said that he knows nothing about the Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) shooting in Maine as he’s “been a little busy”.

On Monday (13 July), an agent fatally shot Johan Sebastián Guerrero, a Colombian national, during an immigration enforcement operation - the second time in a week that the agency has used deadly force against a migrant.

Asked about the fatal shooting of Mr Guerrero, a father to a three-year-old, the House speaker said he would reserve judgement until he learnt more about the case.

“I’m not going to comment because I don’t know. I’ve worked about 22 hours the last two days.”
