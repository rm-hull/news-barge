# What Harry Kane said about the media in aftermath of Bellingham and Tuchel interviews

- Harry Kane stated that the media is attempting to create division within the England squad following interviews with Thomas Tuchel and Jude Bellingham.
- After England's 2-1 victory over Norway, Tuchel expressed dissatisfaction with the team's performance, while Bellingham dismissed his manager's comments, citing the tough playing conditions.
- Kane defended Bellingham, suggesting the midfielder was unaware of Tuchel's exact words and criticising what he called an an 'English mentality' to foster rifts at major tournaments.
- The England captain emphasised the squad's strong togetherness and praised Tuchel's direct communication style, calling him 'one of the best managers in the world'.
- England is preparing for their semi-final match against Argentina, which will kick off on Wednesday at 8pm BST.
