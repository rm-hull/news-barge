# Trump hits out at ‘sleazebags’ who he claims damaged Reflecting Pool

- President Donald Trump stated that "vandals" and "sleazebags" who allegedly damaged the Lincoln Memorial Reflecting Pool should face severe consequences.
- The pool was recently drained again for repairs to fix extensive damage, including 300-yard long slashes and an uplifted floor, which Trump attributed to "thugs".
- This follows a previous $16 million refurbishment where the new sealant peeled and algae bloomed, which Trump blamed on "Democrat-aligned saboteurs".
- Six individuals were arrested in connection with the damage, with former Olympic canoeist David Hearn pleading not guilty; his lawyers argue he is being scapegoated for poor quality repair work.
- The Reflecting Pool remained closed for the Independence Day weekend and has since undergone further repairs with increased security, prompting lawmakers to demand answers regarding the situation.
