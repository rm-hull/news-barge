                                    A fifth and final teacher has received an end of term treat thanks to Harrogate BID.

We asked listeners to nominate a teacher that deserves a treat as thanks for their hard work over the course of the academic year.

It's part of our 'End of Term Treat' campaign with Harrogate BID, with five teachers set to receive a £50 Harrogate gift card.

And our fifth and final winner has been revealed as Mrs West from Bishop Monkton CE Primary School.

The nomination read:

“Mrs West has been my rock for 7 years now. She has supported me and helped me navigate life as a parent with 3 children with additional needs and helped me through the lengthy processes of diagnosis and getting extra support.

“Nothing is ever too much to ask and she always wants to help.”
