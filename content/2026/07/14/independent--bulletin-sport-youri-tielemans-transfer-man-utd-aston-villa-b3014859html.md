# Youri Tielemans describes ‘special’ Man Utd move after Old Trafford transfer confirmed

- Manchester United have completed the signing of Youri Tielemans from Aston Villa for £35m.
- The Belgium captain has penned a four-year contract at Old Trafford after United activated his release clause.
- Tielemans expressed immense pride in joining the club, describing it as a "special" move and the culmination of his career.
- He becomes United's third summer arrival, following Andrey Santos and goalkeeper Karl Darlow.
- Director of football Jason Wilcox highlighted Tielemans' consistent performance, technical qualities, and leadership as key reasons for his acquisition.
