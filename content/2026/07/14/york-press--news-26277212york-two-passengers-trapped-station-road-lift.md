TWO women have been rescued from a lift they were trapped in close to York city centre.

  



North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service said a crew from York was called to Station Road around 2.20am this morning (Tuesday, July 14).

The service confirmed the lift was trapped between two floors and the women were trapped for 15 minutes.

A service spokesperson said crews manually lowered the lift using the machine's gearing.

The lift was lowered to the ground floor where keys were used in the release of the two women.
