A MISSING man, believed to be walking to the bottom of England, has been found in Rotherham.

North Yorkshire Police has confirmed that a missing 34-year-old man from York has been found in South Yorkshire after a nationwide search that involved various police forces.

The alarm was first raised for the man after he was last seen at around 11pm on Sunday, July 5, in Hob Moor, Acomb.

North Yorkshire Police said that they had alerted Greater Manchester, West Midlands, Northamptonshire, Bedfordshire, Cambridgeshire, Suffolk, the Metropolitan Police, the City of London, Devon and Cornwall, Hampshire, Kent, and British Transport Police to look out for him.

“We believed he was planning on walking to the south coast of England. Thank you for supporting the appeal which has now been cancelled,” said a spokesperson for the force.

They added a ‘many thanks’ for all those who had helped.
