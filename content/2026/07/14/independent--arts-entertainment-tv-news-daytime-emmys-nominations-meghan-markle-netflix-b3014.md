The 2026 Daytime Emmy nominations were released today, days after the Primetime Emmy nominations.

*The Young and the Restless* led all nominees with 18 nominations, followed by*Beyond the Gates* with 15 and*General Hospital* with 13. CBS led all networks and platforms, ahead of PBS and ABC.

Winners will be announced during the 53rd annual Daytime Emmy Awards ceremony on October 30 at the Hollywood Palladium. A host is yet to be announced.
