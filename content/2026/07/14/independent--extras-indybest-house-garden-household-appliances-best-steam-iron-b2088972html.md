If the prospect of tackling a towering laundry pile leaves you in a cold sweat, it could be time to invest in one of the best steam irons. Today’s top-notch models promise powerful steam and crease-busting technology to help you power through piles of clothes in record time. 

With four children in primary school and nursery, I’ve always got stacks of uniforms waiting to be ironed, alongside my own dresses and skirts that seem to crease the moment I sit down. From premium models to budget buys, I’ve tested myriad steam irons (as well as clothes steamers) to find out which ones are worth your money and actually live up to their claims.

Experts say reliability and efficiency are key when choosing an iron. Deyan Dimitrov, CEO and founder of Laundryheap, explains: “Buying a workhorse iron that you can rely on to get the job done consistently means you’re less likely to run into issues down the line. An iron that heats up quickly and can adjust temperature fast will help you get through the pile without wasting time changing temperature for different materials.”
