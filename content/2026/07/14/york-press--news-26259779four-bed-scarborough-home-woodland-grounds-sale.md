A bespoke timber-framed house with its own woodland gardens is up for sale in Scarborough for £1.1 million.

The four-bedroom detached house, known as Oak Tree Lodge, is located on High Mill Drive and is on the market with estate agents Tipple Underwood.

The property was individually designed and built and sits on a secluded plot with woodland gardens.

  Separate lounge overlooking the surrounding gardens 

*(Image: Tipple Underwood)*

While the listing states it is down a private drive, it is also just a short walk from North Bay.

Tipple Underwood says the property offers size and splendour on a secluded plot with woodland surroundings, dramatic features, two large garages, and enjoyable grounds.

  Dining area beside the drawing room 

*(Image: Tipple Underwood)*

The home has a stunning vaulted entrance hall with galleried landing, drawing room with inglenook fireplace, and stunning dining area.

It also has a lounge, sitting room, kitchen, large rear hall, utility room, office, and cloakroom on the ground floor.

  Kitchen with access to the adjoining rear hall and utility room 

*(Image: Tipple Underwood)*

On the first floor are four large bedrooms, with the master bedroom having an en-suite.

The family bathroom is also on the first floor.

  Woodland gardens with paths, lawn, summer house, potting shed and mature trees 

*(Image: Tipple Underwood)*

The property sits in beautiful grounds with woodland paths, lawn, summer house, potting shed, and two very large garages, one of which will house a boat or motor-home.

The two garages are 19' 3" x 18' 9" and 26' 4" x 15' 2", and the second garage has an 11'4'' height.

For more information, or to book a viewing, contact Tipple Underwood on 01723 266840.
