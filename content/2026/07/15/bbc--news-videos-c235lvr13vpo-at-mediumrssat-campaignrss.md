# Argentina fans celebrate World Cup win over England

Argentina fans at home and in the US have celebrated their progression to the World Cup final after beating England in a semi-final match.

The current World Champions overcame England in their match in Atlanta 2-1.

Fans have been dancing and celebrating in the streets, as they look ahead to the final against Spain on 19 July.
