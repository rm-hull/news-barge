### What is the Public Notice Portal?

The Public Notice Portal carries statutory public notices published in local newspapers and is the fastest and most effective way of finding out what is happening in YOUR neighbourhood.

Search the Public Notice PortalA series of road closures is set to affect York residents due to upcoming maintenance works.

Overnight and daytime road closures have also been confirmed.

The scheduled closures and restrictions are:

  **Mansfield Street, York:** Carriageway resurfacing will take place from July 20 to July 22, closing the section between Foss Islands Road and a point 101 metres north-east each day between 8am and 5pm.

No diversion route will be signed.

Access for residents and emergency services will be maintained.

  **Cemetery Road, York: ** Carriageway patching will take place from July 20 to July 26, with closures between 7pm and 6am each day, between Heslington Lane and the projected southern boundary of property No. 26.

A signed diversion will be in place via Fulford Road, Fishergate, Fawcett Street, Paragon Street, and Barbican Road.

Access for residents and emergency services will be maintained.

    **For more notices affecting YOUR area, visit our Public Notice Portal at publicnoticeportal.uk/york-pressPark Grove, York:** Gas mains works will be carried out from 8am on July 22 to 5pm on August 28.

Closures will affect Park Grove between the projected south-eastern boundaries of property Nos. 24 and 62, and Emerald Street between the north-western boundary of property No. 1 and its junction with Park Grove.

A signed diversion will be in place via Huntington Road, Monkgate, Lord Mayor’s Walk, Clarence Street, Haxby Road, Stanley Street, Neville Street, Eldon Street, Amber Street, and Earle Street.

Access for emergency vehicles will be maintained.

On-street parking will be suspended on the north side of Park Grove between Huntington Road and Brownlow Street, and on the south side of Neville Terrace between the western boundaries of property Nos. 7 and 30.

  **South Bank Avenue, York:** Highway works will take place from July 22 to July 23, with closures between 9.30am and 3pm each day, between Nunthorpe Grove and Trafalgar Street.

A signed diversion will be in place via Bishopthorpe Road, Balmoral Terrace, and Trafalgar Street.

Residents requiring access should contact site traffic management operatives.

Access for emergency vehicles will be maintained.

  **Abelton Grove, Haxby:** Carriageway resurfacing works will be carried out between July 22 and July 24, closing the section between South Lane and a point 141 metres south, between 8am and 5pm each day.

No diversion route will be signed.

Access for residents and emergency services will be maintained.

The full public notice with detailed information is available on the City of York Council website.
