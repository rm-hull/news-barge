A group of Yorkshire businesses will mark this year’s Yorkshire Day by doing what the county is best known for - offering straightforward advice, sharing knowledge and helping others.

York-based Partners PR is bringing together legal, tax, HR, telecoms, technology and communications experts for a free business drop-in the day before Yorkshire Day, on Friday July 31.

The event will give businesses the chance to access expert guidance in an informal setting.

Attendees will be able to access expert advice from Switalskis on commercial property matters, Clive Owen LLP on tax, McMillan & Associates on HR, Apollo Cloud on telecoms, Bellingham IT on technology, and Partners PR on PR and communications.

Karen Tinkler, Partners PR director, said: "Yorkshire businesses have always been known for their straight-talking approach, helping each other and telling it as it is. We always celebrate Yorkshire Day and this year wanted to bring together a fantastic group of businesses that all share those qualities.

“Everyone involved has generously volunteered their time because they believe in giving practical, honest advice. Between us, we can help with everything from legal and tax questions to HR, technology, telecoms and communications, so it's a brilliant opportunity for businesses to get expert guidance without any pressure.

“Whether people have a specific business challenge, want a second opinion or simply fancy meeting others from the Yorkshire business community over a brew – Yorkshire Tea, of course - we'd love them to drop in. They can stay for five minutes or an hour, the door is open.”

The Yorkshire Day business drop-in takes place from 9.30am to 11.30am on Friday July 31 at Partners PR, Independence House, Millfield Lane, Nether Poppleton, York YO26 6PH.

The event is free to attend and people are encouraged to pop in throughout the morning.

Advance booking is required via [https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/yorkshire-day-business-drop-in-tickets-1992351013514](https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/yorkshire-day-business-drop-in-tickets-1992351013514)
