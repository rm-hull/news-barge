## Popular restaurant and cocktail bar in York closes with immediate effect

Staff are understood to have been told about the news on the day and said they were "shocked" *(Image: Newsquest)*

A RESTAURANT and cocktail bar in a busy York street has closed with immediate effect.

Turtle Bay announced its sudden closure in Little Stonegate on Tuesday (July 14).

Staff are understood to have been told about the news on the day and said they were “shocked”.

  


The business was one of 44 Turtle Bay restaurants in the UK, serving dishes influenced by Caribbean cuisine, including jerk chicken, curries, burgers, rum cocktails and a tropical bottomless brunch menu.

  Turtle Bay, in Little Stonegate, has closed its doors with immediate effect 

*(Image: Newsquest)*

The venue has been a popular spot for casual drinks and dining since 2015.

The Press has reached out to Turtle Bay for comment.
