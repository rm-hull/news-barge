The bulk of a North Yorkshire country mansion has been put up for sale after 50 years in business.

Bellfield Country Suites, located in Masham, is now on the market with a freehold asking price of £850,000 through property specialists Christie & Co.

The sale marks the retirement of long-time owner Rita Dinsdale, who ran the property as a holiday accommodation business for five decades.

Mrs Dinsdale said: "It has been a pleasure and privilege to own such a magnificent mansion for the past 50 years in the Yorkshire countryside, which has given pleasure to thousands of guests from all over the world.

"I hope the new owner will love and cherish this beautiful one-off building as I have, being set in this wonderful location in this historic market town."

Bellfield Suites occupies two thirds of Bellfield House, an ornate 1830s mansion originally built by Colonel Thomas Theakston of Theakston Brewery as a family home.

The property was converted into holiday suites in the 1970s and now comprises 12 self-contained units, offering a total of 19 bedrooms, each with its own lounge and bathroom.

Period features include marble fireplaces, an elegant hall, a pine staircase, and a galleried landing.

The estate also includes private grounds, a walled courtyard, and a large car park.

There is potential for the new owner to convert existing rooms into separate owner’s accommodation if desired.

Mark Worley, director – hotels at Christie & Co, said: "We’re seeing increasing demand for holiday accommodation that can cater for multi-generational groups, or couples wanting a quiet relaxing stay.

"Bellfield is ideal, having spacious lounges, bathrooms, and multiple bedrooms depending on the requirements of the guests.

"At the same time, travellers are increasingly favouring shorter, more spontaneous breaks of two to three nights, reflecting a growing preference for flexibility and value."

The property is located on the edge of Masham, conveniently positioned for visitors to the Yorkshire Dales National Park and attractions such as the Masham Steam Fair and Sheep Fair.

Nearby destinations include Leyburn, Ripon, Richmond, Pateley Bridge, and Harrogate.

For more information about the property, visit the Christie.com website.
