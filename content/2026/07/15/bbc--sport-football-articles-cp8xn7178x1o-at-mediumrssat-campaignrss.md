Spain into World Cup final after dominant victory over France

**Saturday's World Cup third-place play-off between England and France and Sunday's World Cup final will both be shown live on BBC TV.**

England lost 2-1 to Argentina in dramatic fashion in Wednesday's semi-final a day after France were beaten 2-0 by Spain in their last-four tie.

Saturday's match, which is now known as the bronze final, will kick off at 22:00 BST.

Argentina will face Spain in the final a day later, with a 20:00 BST kick-off.

Both matches will be shown live on BBC One and BBC iPlayer - and there will be live commentary on BBC Radio 5 Live. ITV will also broadcast the final live.

The BBC Sport website will have the TV and radio coverage for both matches, plus live text commentary and in-game highlights.

Both games will also be available in ultra high definition (UHD).

The BBC Sport website will have its Fifa World Cup 3D experience available too.

Viewers can take total control of the angles and can relive goals as your favourite player - plus watch tactics unfold in real time - with side-by-side stats and player data.
