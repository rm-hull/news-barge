A THIEF has targeted a national supermarket chain in a North Yorkshire coastal town.

The county's police force said food was stolen from Sainsbury's in Ramshill Road on Thursday, July 2.

  


North Yorkshire Police have released CCTV of a woman they wish to speak to in connection with the incident, saying she may have information which could assist its investigation.

A police spokesman said: "Anyone who recognises the woman is asked to contact PC1488 Bell at [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk.](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk.)

"Or visit us online at the North Yorkshire Police website, select 'Tell us about', then choose 'An existing case or report'.

"Please quote reference 12260124686 when providing information."
