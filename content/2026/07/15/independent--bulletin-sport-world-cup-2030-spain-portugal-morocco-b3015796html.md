# Where will the 2030 World Cup be held?

- The World Cup 2030 will take place across three countries: Spain, Portugal and Morocco.
- The tournament will span across three continents as Argentina, Paraguay and Uruguay will each host an opening match marking the tournament’s 100th anniversary.
- Each of the three South American nations was chosen for its unique historical connection to the tournament’s origins.
- The tournament will run from June 8 to July 21, accommodating a 48-team structure.
- The World Cup 2034 will take place in Saudi Arabia, as confirmed by FIFA.
