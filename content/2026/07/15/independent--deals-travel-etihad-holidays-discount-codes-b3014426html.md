### Can I use an Etihad discount code on holiday packages?

Yes, Etihad discount codes can be used on eligible Etihad Holidays package bookings. Always check the terms and conditions, as some codes only apply to specific destinations, travel dates or minimum-spend thresholds.

### What's included in an Etihad Holidays package?

Most Etihad Holidays packages include your flights, hotel and transfers. You can often get free room and board upgrades, too (subject to availability).

### Can I pay for my holiday in instalments?

Etihad Holidays offers flexible payment options on most packages, enabling you to secure your booking with a deposit and pay the remaining balance at least eight weeks before you travel. The deposit amount and payment schedule depend on your booking.

### Can I earn Etihad guest miles on Etihad Holidays?

Eligible Etihad Holidays bookings can earn ‘Etihad guest miles’ to use towards future flights, upgrades and other travel benefits. The number of miles you'll receive depends on your booking and membership status.

### Can I change or cancel my Etihad Holidays booking?

Yes but change and cancellation policies vary, depending on your booking and supplier terms. For changes, a £50 per person fee applies. Date changes are considered a full cancellation, so you will need to check the cancellation policy in your terms and conditions.

### When is the best time to book an Etihad holiday?

Keep an eye out for major sales events such as Black Friday, the January sales and seasonal promotions. Booking well in advance or travelling outside peak school holiday periods can also help you secure lower prices on Etihad Holidays packages.
