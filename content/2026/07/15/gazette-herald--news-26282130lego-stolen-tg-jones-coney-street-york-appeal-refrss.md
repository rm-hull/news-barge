A thief has struck at a York shop.

North Yorkshire Police has issued a CCTV image of a person officers want to speak to after Lego was stolen from TG Jones in Coney Street on Tuesday, May 19, at around 2.45pm.

The force on Wednesday (July 15) issued the photograph, saying the person pictured may have information about the theft.

Anyone who recognises the man is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police online, by emailing [scott.nixon@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:scott.nixon@northyorkshire.police.uk) or phoning 101 and quoting reference 12260090728.
