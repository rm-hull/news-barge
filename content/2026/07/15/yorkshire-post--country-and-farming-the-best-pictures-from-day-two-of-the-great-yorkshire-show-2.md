The Great Yorkshire Show continued to draw huge crowds on day two as visitors flocked to Harrogate for another packed programme celebrating farming, food and the countryside on Wednesday (Jul 15).

Livestock competitions remained at the heart of the event, with sheep, cattle and pigs taking to the show rings, while judges assessed some of the country's finest animals.

Elsewhere, sheep shearing demonstrations, equestrian showjumping and carriage driving entertained spectators throughout the day.

Behind the scenes, handlers prepared prize-winning animals for competition, while families explored the bustling showground, browsing trade stands, food stalls and agricultural displays.

Our pictures capture some of the best moments from another busy day at one of Yorkshire's biggest and best-loved annual events.
