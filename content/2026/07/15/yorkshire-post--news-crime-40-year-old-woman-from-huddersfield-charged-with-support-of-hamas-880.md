# 40-year-old woman from Huddersfield charged with support of Hamas

## A 40-year-old woman has been charged with supporting banned terror group Hamas.

Marian Milenovic, of Huddersfield, West Yorkshire, is accused of one count of expressing an opinion or belief that is supportive of a proscribed organisation.

The British national faces the charge under Section 12 of the Terrorism Act 2000 following an investigation by Counter Terrorism Policing North East.

Milenovic, of Haigh Street, will appear at Westminster Magistrates’ Court on Thursday July 16.
