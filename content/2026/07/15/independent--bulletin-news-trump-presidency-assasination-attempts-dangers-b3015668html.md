# Trump jokes about assassination attempts behind closed doors, report says

- President Donald Trump has reportedly survived multiple assassination attempts during the past few years.
- He is said to joke about the dangers of the presidency, both publicly and privately, suggesting he would not have run had he known the risks, a White House official told *Semafor.*
- Incidents include a shooting at the White House Correspondents’ Dinner, a thwarted attack at a UFC event and attempts in Pennsylvania and Florida.
- Israel reportedly informed U.S. intelligence of a new Iranian plot to assassinate Trump.
- Despite these threats, Trump maintains a relaxed attitude, often brushing off security concerns and stating he cannot let them hinder his ability to function.
