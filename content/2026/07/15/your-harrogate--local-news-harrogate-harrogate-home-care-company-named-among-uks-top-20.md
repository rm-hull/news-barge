                                    A Harrogate home care company has been recognised as one of the UK's top 20 home care groups for 2026.

Carefound Home Care has received the national award from reviews website homecare.co.uk for the eighth year, with the accolade based on independent reviews from clients and their families.

The award recognises the UK's highest-rated home care groups, with more than 13,000 home care providers and over 1,000 home care groups operating across the country.

Carefound Home Care's Harrogate branch supports people across the local area with hourly home care, full-time live-in care and short-term respite care, helping older people remain independent in their own homes.

Amanda Hopkins, Reviews Manager at homecare.co.uk, said:

"As more people choose to receive care and support in their own homes, it is increasingly important that they can identify providers delivering exceptional standards of care.

"Carefound Home Care has demonstrated an outstanding commitment to supporting people to live independently while maintaining their dignity, well-being and quality of life.

"We are delighted to congratulate them on being recognised as one of the Top 20 Home Care Groups in the UK for 2026.

"Our reviews and awards provide a valuable source of information for people seeking trusted, high-quality home care and offer genuine insight into the experiences of those receiving care and support."


The recognition comes as demand for home care services is expected to continue growing as the UK's population ages.

Laura Hill, Registered Branch Manager at Carefound Home Care Harrogate, said:

"We are incredibly proud to be named one of homecare.co.uk's Top 20 Home Care Providers, particularly as the award is based on feedback from our clients and their loved ones.

"This achievement reflects the kindness, dedication and professionalism of our care professionals, who go above and beyond to help people remain safe and independent at home.

"It is a fantastic achievement for our Harrogate branch and reinforces our commitment to delivering the highest standards of person-centred care across the local community. We are grateful to our clients, their families and our entire team for making it possible."
