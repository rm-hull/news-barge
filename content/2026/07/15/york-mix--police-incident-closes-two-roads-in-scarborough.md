Two roads in Scarborough are closed by a police incident this morning.

Ramshill Road is closed from the junction of St Martin’s Place up to and including the junction of Royal Avenue.

And Northway is shut from the traffic lights to the police station.

#### Incident locations

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Please avoid these areas if you can, and use alternative routes where possible while emergency services work at the scene.”
