When finding space in the car for everything you need for your trip feels like a game of luggage Tetris, the best car roof box can expand your vehicle's storage space, ensuring you aren’t forced into a terrible choice about which child to leave behind.

The best car roof box depends on how you travel, your cargo, and how your car is already set up. If you already have something on the car roof when you go away, such as a bike or paddleboard, then a narrow or medium-width box will be the way to go. If the box doesn’t have to share roof space, a full-width box will allow you plenty of storage.

There are lots of practicalities to consider from how easy the initial mounting is, to whether the lid is sturdy and the locking mechanism is solid. It should also be easy to close, even when it’s nearing full capacity. Think about what would make it easiest to access the contents, too, and whether the box would open from a single side, from both sides, or hinge at one end.

With all this in mind, I tested a range of car roof boxes in real travel scenarios. I opened and closed these boxes dozens of times, to check for any issues with the locking mechanisms, ensuring the contents remained in the box, rather than all over the A303.

This 510l box opens from either the left or the right-hand side, and is supplemented with hydraulic struts so you can easily open and close with one hand. The box itself is long enough for skis and high enough for golf clubs, while the nice wide opening makes getting your gear in and out effortless. In fact, thanks to its design I felt that the box held more than the advertised 510l.

The husky’s security credentials lived up to expectations too (the box is multi-award winning) because of the manufacturer’s locking system that operates centrally without the need for turning a key – there are even further hinged catches for more security. The key can’t be removed until the box is properly shut, so you know there’ll be no nasty surprises as you’re driving. The box isn’t quite full-width either, so you would be able to port something else on the roof bar, like a bike, if needed. Finally, the box is nicely styled in matt black while its low profile is aerodynamic once you’re on the road.
