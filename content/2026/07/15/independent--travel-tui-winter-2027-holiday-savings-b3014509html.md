Planning ahead and booking your upcoming holidays? With TUI’s winter 2027 sale now on, it’s the perfect time to grab a getaway to one of many covetable destinations for less, from Zanzibar to Greece.

TUI is offering deals on holidays between November 2027 and April 2028. So, whether you’re dreaming of Christmas Day in the Maldives, seek a slice of paradise in Barbados, or are simply chasing the sun, there are hundreds of pounds to be saved in the sale. Plus, Zanzibar – a group of islands off the coast of Tanzania that boast tropical weather, powder-white beaches and a rich culture – is a new TUI holiday destination for 2027.

And, thanks to TUI’s convenient package deals, flights and accommodation are bundled into one – so all you need to do is book, and get packing nearer the time.

Unsure where to start? From adult-only peace and quiet in a beautiful location to family-friendly hotels with everything you could need to keep everyone entertained, keep scrolling for holiday inspiration. There are 50 destinations to explore, and we’ve rounded up a few options.

**Zanzibar**

Located off the coast of Tanzania, East Africa, this group of islands serves up pristine powder-white beaches with warm, crystal-clear waters, and cultural heritage to explore. From the narrow alleys and bazaars of Stone Town to the spice farm tours for a taste of local spice plantations – there’s plenty to see and do. Beyond relaxing at the beach and enjoying watersports, you could explore gems like the open-air Forodhani Gardens night market on the waterfront.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/11/44/TUI-BLUE-Bahari-Zanzibar.png)

At TUI BLUE Bahari Zanzibar, Zanzibar, you’ll have everything you might want for a family poolside holiday. In a peaceful location by an ice-white sandy beach, it boasts five restaurants, four bars and three pools. Plus, there’s a paddling pool and playroom to keep little ones entertained while the adults relax.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/11/41/The-Residence-Zanzibar.png)

With a tranquil ocean view, these secluded luxury villas provide a picture-perfect destination for couples. For somewhere to relax and reset, you’ll find a spa nestled in a tropical garden, with a whirlpool, sauna and steam room. There are two restaurants to dine in where you’ll find international dishes with a taste of Zanzibar.

**Greece**

For Mediterranean cuisine and world-class white-sand beaches, unwind on a Grecian getaway next winter.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/11/50/Atlantica-Aegean-Blue.png)

A stay at Atlantica Aegean Blue caters for families with children, as there’s a waterpark and kids’ clubs providing hours of fun for little ones, plus, there are food options for fussy eaters. Located by Kolymbia’s seafront, the hotel offers ocean views, which can be taken in from the la carte restaurant. Enjoy its activities, from relaxing in the spa to downward dog in a yoga session, or, practice your swing at the nearby golf course.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/11/42/Palazzino-Di-Corina.png)

For a boutique hotel with a friendly feel, the Palazzino Di Corina is a charming offering. There’s a pretty cobbled courtyard and vintage decor throughout, including wood-beamed ceilings to exposed stone walls. Situated in Rethymnon’s old town, the hotel isn’t far from a choice of restaurants, shops and bars. Cool off in the small plunge pool or if you’re getting itchy feet then you can head to the beach, which is also close by.

**Spain**

With reliable weather, vibrant cities, relatively short travel times and delicious local fare, it’s no wonder Spain draws so many holidaymakers to its shores. You might want to book a beach holiday on the Canary Islands, a laidback city break in Valencia or a jam-packed weekend in Barcelona.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/11/46/HG-Lomo-Blanco.png)

For a low-cost beachside holiday where you can easily escape to local bars, clubs and restaurants, a seven-night stay at HG Lomo Blanco, Lanzarote, costs £502 per person. It’s right in the centre of lively Puerto del Carmen, where evenings can be spent at the venues along the waterside promenade. Days can be spent lounging by the pools with a drink from the bar. Or, head to the beach, which is a 10-minute walk away.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/11/54/Cala-Lanzarote-Suite-Hotel.png)

If you’re looking for a quiet, adult-only retreat, consider the Cala Lanzarote Suite Hotel. With a contemporary paper-white design, it’s a minimalist’s dream. Take a dip in the inviting turquoise pool and take in the panoramic sea views. If you step out of the hotel, you’re located in Playa Blanca, with cocktails, upscale dining and more.

**Barbados**

Around a nine-hour flight from the UK, Barbados offers a tropical retreat, and is arguably one of the most paradiscal holiday locations, with calm, crystal-clear waters, lush greenery and a relaxed atmosphere.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/11/00/Southern-Palms.png)

Pretty in pink, the Southern Palms is characterised by its colourful facade which sits among tall palms. Situated by a powdery white-sand beach, it’s in St Lawrence, where Bajan cuisine, rum shacks and dancehalls can be found. At the hotel, there are two pools, a minigolf and a restaurant where a steel band plays on Sundays.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/11/59/The-Club-Barbados.png)

Looking for peace and quiet? This adult-only hotel, which has rooms with a garden, pool or sea view, offers a quiet retreat. Surrounded by multiple pools and waterfalls, there are four bars and two restaurants to enjoy while you’re there. Or, you can step out to see the local restaurants and nightlife or to do a spot of shopping.

**Indonesia**

If you’re happy to travel further afield, Bali blends lively and laidback elements that will appeal to a broad range of travellers, from families to solo partyseekers.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/11/54/Tijili-Seminyak.png)

Not shying away from colour, this small hotel in the beachside suburb of Seminyak, Bali, has a friendly feel and keeps things simple. Expect to find beanbags to sink into, multi-coloured lights and plenty of colour throughout. There’s a courtyard pool, a bar and a restaurant, and you’ll be a five-minute walk from Seminyak Beach.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/11/41/Puri-Santrian-Beach-Resort-And-Spa.png)

In a tranquil tropical setting with sea views and lush greenery, four pools, a luxury spa and a swim-up bar, the Puri Santrian Beach Resort And Spa is also just a stone’s throw from a powder-white sand beach. The hotel offers different activities allowing guests to delve into Balinese culture, including an Indonesian cooking class.
