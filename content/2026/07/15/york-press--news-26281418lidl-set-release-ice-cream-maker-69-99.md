Lidl has announced it is releasing its own ice cream maker as a new Middle Aisle item.

Arriving in stores from Thursday, July 16, the Premium Freezy Ice Cream Maker will be priced at £69.99 amid the UK facing hot temperatures.

The supermarket also highlights the ice cream maker as a cheaper option than some well-known brands.

In particular, it cites the Ninja CREAMi Deluxe 10-in-1 Ice Cream and Frozen Drink Maker for comparison, which is listed for £249.99.

  The Premium Freezy Ice Cream Maker features seven preset programmes 

*(Image: Lidl)*

Lidl adds: "The cherry on top? A £130 saving compared to the viral branded alternative, proving frozen fun doesn’t need to melt the budget."

With social media trends showing a surge in homemade gelato creations, Lidl expects high demand for its latest summer gadget.

## What can the Lidl Premium Freezy Ice Cream Maker do?

The machine is designed for convenience, allowing users to simply fill the tank with ingredients, freeze for 24 hours, and then spin for a frozen dessert in minutes.

It features seven preset programmes, including options for ice cream, light ice cream, gelato, sorbet, Smoothie Bowl, and Extras.


Each purchase comes with a recipe booklet to inspire homemade creations, and all necessary ingredients can be found in-store.

Lidl adds: "Customers who purchase the Premium Freezy Ice Cream Maker will also receive a recipe booklet and can purchase everything they need to get started in store.

The ice cream maker will be available in stores from Thursday, July 16, while stocks last.
