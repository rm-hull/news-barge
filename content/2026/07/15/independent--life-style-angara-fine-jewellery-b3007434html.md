Navigating the world of fine jewellery can be daunting, which is where luxury brand Angara comes in. With expertise and a passion for jewellery grounded in a 400-year legacy, it takes a down-to-earth approach to high-end pieces, while also bearing in mind the importance of getting these purchases right. Think quality and heritage craftsmanship, without pretentiousness.

Whether you’re looking for jewellery to celebrate a milestone, are searching for a special piece to elevate summer occasionwear, or seek everyday staples to refresh your ring stack, Angara makes everything in-house. And with an understanding that jewellery is hugely personal, its designs are bespoke. From the metal to the sustainably sourced gem stone, each piece is shaped by you.

![](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/10/14/27/Angara-gemstone-necklace.png)

**Birthstone jewellery that’s truly bespoke**

To celebrate a birth month, Angara offers the option to personalise your jewellery with your birthstone. Whether that’s a fiery red gemstone for the month of January, or the electric blue of tanzanite for December – your birthstone can become the focal point of a necklace, earrings or a bracelet. Should this pick be a forever piece for yourself or a thoughtful gift for someone else, birthstones make for a beautifully personal jewellery option.

**Elevated everyday pieces**

What makes a piece of jewellery something to be worn on repeat comes down to personal preference. If you're looking for a pared-back staple to wear on repeat, then the men’s twisted cuff bracelet offers a minimalist, contemporary look, but with sculptural detail for interest. Handcrafted in 14ct white gold, its simple design would look just as smart solo as it would with a watch.

![](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/09/12/18/Angara.png)

Equally, for a basic piece that feels just as elevated, the men’s tag pendant is a twist on a classic chain. With a checkerboard grid adorned with pavé lab-grown diamonds and polished metal, it comes in silver, white gold or yellow gold.

When it comes to earrings, it’s fair to say hoops have earned themselves everyday staple status. If you want something that feels a little more special, the diamond inside-out hoop earrings offer subtle elegance, while being elegant in their simplicity. Designed to snugly hug your ears, they’re studded with round lab-grown diamonds.

**Gifts to cherish**

The most joyful jewellery gifts to receive usually manage to walk a fine line between wow factor and real-world wearability. If you’re shopping for a giftee who tends to lean more into classic design, then the solitaire diamond twist bale pendant is an understated, elegant piece that still dazzles. A thoughtful, refined gift, the pendant features an infinity symbol-inspired housing the diamond, as a nod to endless love – perfect for anyone who wants something a little quieter.

![](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/15/14/22/Angara-pendant.png)

A forever piece to surprise them on a special occasion, the men’s rectangular signet ring offers a striking, sharp design with two lab-grown diamonds for the final flourish.

**A dazzling summer refresh**

Summer calls for a wardrobe refresh, and the same can be said for our jewellery rotation, especially when special occasions, such as weddings and garden parties, are filling up the calendar.

For those looking for an intricate piece to share with a loved one, the interlocking emerald open circle pendant offers a subtle pop of colour and is also available with blue sapphire, ruby or diamond. To complete the look, the prong-set solitaire oval emerald stud earrings offer a pop of matching colour.

Meanwhile, bold, playful or oversized pieces afford character, whether layered or worn as a focal point, such as the peridot flower cluster pendant, characterised by its soft earthy green floral design. Speaking of adding colour, for clean lines and a bright pop, the east-west emerald-cut blue sapphire men’s cuff bracelet offers a dose of understated luxury and unique charm.

![](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/15/14/32/Angara-bracelet.png)

When it comes to jewellery for summer weddings, necklaces can sit neatly above the neckline or hang over it for an undone, layered finish. For a statement piece with character, the lab-grown emerald-cut ruby pendant with lab diamond trio is both bold and timeless. Alternatively, the three-stone graduated bezel-set diamond lariat necklace will be perfectly complemented with a plunging neckline.
