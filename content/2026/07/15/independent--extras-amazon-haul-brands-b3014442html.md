If you’re shopping on a budget, Amazon Haul could be worth a look. By shipping products directly from the manufacturer, the retailer’s most affordable shopping destination is able to keep prices at £20 or less – with a trade-off of delivery taking up to two weeks.

Ranging from everyday home essentials to sportswear and beauty buys, a quick scroll of the Amazon Haul store will show that many products are unbranded. However, Amazon Haul does also stock big-name brands, from the likes of Adidas and Mattel to Levi’s and Lego, and there are deals and discounts worth keeping an eye out for, too.

To streamline your search, scroll down to find some of the most popular brands that are available at Amazon Haul right now, from tech and toys to fashion and more.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/16/38/Lego.png)

Encouraging screen-free fun and creativity, Lego remains a favourite for kids and adults alike. While Amazon Haul doesn’t tend to discount very large Lego sets, it isn’t uncommon to see a deal on smaller builds, such as minifigures and Botanicals sets. Whether you’re after a Harry Potter-themed build, a Technic set, or something else, it could be worth checking the range at Amazon Haul before shopping elsewhere – as you might get a better deal.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/16/54/Levis.png)

American fashion brand Levi’s is known for its denim, and on Amazon Haul, there are pieces at prices that warrant a double take. For instance, it’s not unheard of to find a pair of jeans priced at around £12, although they may only be available in one size. Beyond jeans, and jorts, Amazon Haul also houses clothing basics, from smart button downs to casual T-shirts and jumpers.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/16/45/Phillips.png)

Whether you need to replace a lightbulb or stock up on batteries, Amazon Haul offers everyday household essentials from Philips. The brand specialises in long-lasting, energy-saving LED bulbs, which are designed to help keep electricity costs down. Similarly, the Philips range includes solar lighting and charging, such as outdoor, weather-proof security lights.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/16/45/Adidas.png)

A sportswear name that needs no introduction, Adidas is famous for its athletic wear, accessories and shoes, from sliders for lounging around the house to everyday trainers. Renowned for blending streetwear with high-performance materials, Adidas is a go-to for its instantly recognisable branding. Amazon Haul’s offering is extensive, and there are often deals on.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/16/30/Playmobil.png)

Shopping for a little one? Toy brand Playmobil is listed on Amazon Haul, where there are minifigures and playsets for kids aged two and upwards. Unlike Lego, Playmobil uses pre-assembled or click-together figures and interactive features, designed to spark young imaginations. Sets on Amazon Haul include those themed around fantasy and action heroes.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/16/34/Clarks.png)

A household name for footwear, Clarks offers comfy, support-focused shoes. Whether it’s school shoes or everyday styles you need, the brand has a plethora of picks. With offerings like the trending wallabee shoes regularly seen on social media feeds, Clarks is now known for its fashion-forward styles as much as the classics.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/16/30/Puma.png)

Blending technological features with everyday style, German sportswear brand Puma offers workout gear and everyday staples. From gym bags to basics such as sports bras, underwear and socks, the range of Puma pieces at Amazon Haul spans options for kids and adults and covers everything from football boots to swimwear.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/16/08/Pokemon.png)

Calling all Pokémon fans: spanning collectible cards, branded clothing and so much more, the selection available at Amazon Haul is worth a browse for anyone wanting to build their collection for less. From adorable plush toys to action figures, stationery and even pyjamas, there’s something for every Pokémon enthusiast who ‘gotta catch ‘em all’.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/16/17/Under-Armour.png)

A popular choice for fitness pursuits, Under Armour is favoured by everyone from runners and golfers to tennis players and gym-goers. Its pieces cater to different scenarios and weather conditions – with ranges to keep you dry during wet training sessions, and comfortably cool no matter the season. As for the sports shoes it offers, there are trainers that feature elements designed to enhance overall performance, such as responsive cushioning for an easier ride.

![](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/14/16/23/Mattel.png)

Founded in 1945, Mattel has been behind some of the most iconic toys over the years, from Barbie to Hot Wheels. While the range available at Amazon Haul isn’t very extensive, products span the likes of card games from Uno and Disney collaborations – with low prices that are worth checking out.
