R&B singer R. Kelly has officially asked President Donald Trump to end his 30-year prison sentence for sex crimes.

The disgraced music artist, whose full name is Robert Sylvester Kelly, was convicted of federal sex trafficking and racketeering charges in New York in 2021.

After years in prison, Kelly’s attorneys have submitted a request for a commutation of his sentence to the Justice Department, which was made public this week.
