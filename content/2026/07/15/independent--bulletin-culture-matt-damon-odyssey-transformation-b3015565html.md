# Matt Damon: ‘The Odyssey is the most challenging movie I’ve ever been part of’

- Matt Damon has declared he will no longer undergo extreme physical transformations, specifically gaining weight, for film roles.
- For his role as King Odysseus in Christopher Nolan's *The Odyssey*, Damon lost weight, reaching 167 pounds through dietary and lifestyle adjustments.
- Damon described the film as the “most challenging movie” he has ever been a part of, though he “loved every minute of it”.
- The film follows Odysseus's arduous journey home to Ithaca after the Trojan War, battling various mythical foes.
- This marks Damon's third collaboration with director Christopher Nolan, who lauded the actor's ability to connect with audiences and portray complex characters.
