# Man and woman sleeping rough near beach attacked with irritant thrown at them

## Police in Scarborough are investigating after a man and a woman who were sleeping rough in Scarborough had what is believed to be an irritant thrown at them.

The attack happened at around 2.30am in the early hours of Wednesday July 15, police said.

The victims were taken to hospital for treatment to facial injuries that are not believed to be serious.

One woman has been arrested in connection with the incident and remains in custody at this time, North Yorkshire Police said.

An extensive investigation is ongoing to trace any other suspects believed to be involved.

All three emergency services were involved in the incident which led to two areas of the town - Ramshill Road and Northway - being cordoned off and road closures put in place.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information that could assist the investigation, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12260134286 when passing on information.
