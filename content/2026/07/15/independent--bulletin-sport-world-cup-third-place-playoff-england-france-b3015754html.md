# Is there a World Cup third place play-off after England lose semi-final against Argentina?

- The 2026 World Cup will include a third-place playoff, officially designated as the 'Match for third place', scheduled for Saturday, July 18, the day before the final.
- This match will be hosted at the Hard Rock Stadium in Miami, a venue with a 65,000-capacity that serves as the home ground for the NFL's Miami Dolphins.
- England will play France after the Three Lions lost 2-1 to Argentina in the second World Cup semi-final and France lost Spain on the previous night.
- The third-place playoff is set to be the 103rd fixture of the newly expanded tournament, with the k**ick-off time 10pm BST (5pm ET).**
- The third-place prize for the Fifa World Cup is $30 million.
