## Our live feed has now finished.

- The A1(M) has reopened in Yorkshire after a crash involving multiple vehicles closed part of the road.
- Two lanes of the road were closed southbound between junctions 45 and 44 near the A64 junction, close to Hazelwood, today (Wednesday, July 15).
