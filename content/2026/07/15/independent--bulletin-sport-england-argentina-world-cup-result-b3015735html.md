# Heartbreak as Argentina beat England in World Cup semi-final

- Argentina beat England as Lautaro Martínez’s stoppage-time goal won the game the for world champions.
- England took the lead in the World Cup semi-final as Anthony Gordon made it 1-0 before Enzo Fernandez equalised.
- England winger Gordon finished a well-executed play involving Harry Kane, Morgan Rogers and Declan Rice.
- Djed Spence then denied Giovanni Simeone with the 'tackle of the tournament' shortly after Gordon's goal.
- Before Chelsea midfielder Fernandez equalised for Argentina with a superb strike from outside the box, before Martínez’s goal.
