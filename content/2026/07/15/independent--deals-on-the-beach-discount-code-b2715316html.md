## Why book with On the Beach?

### Low deposits

You can secure most holidays from £30 per person, which is one of the lowest booking deposits offered among travel providers. Last-minute bookings of less than 45 days before departure do not qualify for a low deposit option.

### Price guarantee

Another hack to know is On the Beach's price guarantee, which means that if the price of your holiday drops at least 60 days before you depart, you can claim back the difference in the form of a voucher. You can check if your holiday is eligible through the On the Beach app, in which you’ll see exactly how much you can claim and how to get the credit straight to your account.

### Cancellation policy

The On the Beach cancellation policy for holiday packages varies depending on the time before departure; however, it’s stricter than other travel companies such as Tui and easyJet holidays. Here’s a breakdown of fees:

- 60 days or more before departure: a £50 fee per person, plus supplier charges.
- Less than 60 days before departure: £100 fee per person, plus supplier charges.

Supplier charges can be as much as 100 per cent of the total cost, meaning that cancellation charges vary by booking. Flights are also non-refundable from the point of booking, so it’s worth bearing this in mind before confirming your holiday.
