# King Charles meets 104-year-old war veteran during historic visit

- King Charles visited the Isle of Man, where he met 104-year-old Second World War veteran James Fenton.
- During their meeting, the King joked about ageing, remarking, “It's terrible because bits keep dropping off.”
- This marked Charles's first visit to the self-governing British Crown Dependency.
- He addressed the island's parliament, Tynwald, in the ancient Manx language.
- The King's visit also included a ride on a horse-drawn tram and attendance at a garden party.
