A WELL-KNOWN name from the world of motorsport is set to visit York for one night only.

Christian Horner - formerly one of Formula 1’s most influential team principals - is set to appear at York Barbican on Saturday, October 17, at 7.30pm for a special live event.

He will reflect on key moments from his career as part of a candid conversation about his new memoir, Drive - giving Formula 1 fans a behind the scenes look at what it takes to compete at the pinnacle of racing.

A spokesperson said: "In an evening of candid conversation, Horner will reflect on the defining moments of his career, the leadership lessons forged under relentless pressure, and the highs and lows of competing at the very top of world motorsport.

"From championship-winning campaigns to the decisions made behind closed doors, Drive offers a rare insight into what it takes to build and lead a team in one of the most demanding sports on the planet."

   

*(Image: Bradley Collyer / PA)*

Following the conversation, Christian will answer questions from the audience in a live Q&A session; every ticket includes a copy of Drive, offering fans a memento of the evening and a deeper dive into his story.

Tickets are available now on the Barbican website: [https://www.yorkbarbican.co.uk/whats-on/an-audience-with-christian-horner/](https://www.yorkbarbican.co.uk/whats-on/an-audience-with-christian-horner/)
