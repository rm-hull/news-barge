# When is the next World Cup and European Championship?

- World Cup 2026 is nearing its conclusion, with only two games remaining: The third-place play-off (Bronze final) and the final.
- England will face France in the Bronze final on Saturday, before Argentina face Spain in the final on Sunday.
- After that, attention with turn to the next European Championship and World Cup.
- The next European Championship will be in 2028 and will be co-hosted by England, Scotland, Wales, and the Republic of Ireland.
- The next edition of the World Cup will be held in Morocco, Portugal and Spain in 2030.
