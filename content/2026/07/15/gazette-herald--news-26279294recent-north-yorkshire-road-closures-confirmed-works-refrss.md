Search the Public Notice PortalA series of road closures across North Yorkshire have been announced.

North Yorkshire Council is implementing temporary traffic restrictions for essential maintenance.

Feetham to Gunnerside will be shut from July 27 to August 24 for drainage improvements.

Back Lane in Easingwold will be closed from July 27 to October 23 for unspecified works.

Both orders allow for access to premises, and alternative routes will be signposted on site.

The Feetham to Gunnerside order has a maximum duration until December 20, 2027, while the Back Lane order is valid until January 27, 2027.

A separate order will restrict heavy commercial vehicles over 7.5 tonnes in East Layton.

The affected roads are Main Village Street, from Limekiln Lane to B6274, and Moor Road, from Main Village Street to A66.

This restriction will apply from July 27 until January 21, 2028.

The closure will only be enforced when indicated by traffic signs.

North Yorkshire Council's customer service centre can be contacted for more information on the Feetham to Gunnerside and East Layton orders at 0300 131 2 131.

The Back Lane closure is managed by J N Bentley Limited, who can be reached at 0113 426 9757.

Real-time updates can also be found on the North Yorkshire Council's roadworks map at northyorks.gov.uk/roadworks-map.

Residents and motorists are advised to plan their journeys accordingly to avoid inconvenience.
