## Crowds turn out for music festival with 144 performances

Ilkley Live Community Music Festival has been hailed as a great success. *(Image: Dan Hammond)*

A community music festival is being hailed as a growing success in its sixth year.

Ilkley Live Community Music Festival took place across 20 venues in Ilkley during the weekend, featuring 144 performances and drawing larger crowds.

Dan Hammond, founder of Ilkley Live, said: "With 20 venues and 144 performances, it’s as big as last year but the audience has grown massively over the years."

  Ilkley Live Community Music Festival has been hailed as a great success. 

*(Image: Dan Hammond)*

The festival is organised by volunteers.

Mr Hammond said: "The festival is believed to be unique as it is 'by the community, for the community.'

  Ilkley Live Community Music Festival has been hailed as a great success. 

*(Image: Dan Hammond)*

"That means that it is set up so that venues, performers and sound engineers all work together to put on each stage, rather than it being centrally organised.

  Ilkley Live Community Music Festival has been hailed as a great success. 

*(Image: Dan Hammond)*

"The impact of that is that people get to know each other and build relationships that will mean the town gets more music throughout the year."

Funds raised go to the Ilkley Live charity, which aims to build community cohesion through music.

The charity has supported other music events, contributed to Outside the Box's programme, and is in the process of purchasing a Public Address (PA) system for use by anyone in the town.
