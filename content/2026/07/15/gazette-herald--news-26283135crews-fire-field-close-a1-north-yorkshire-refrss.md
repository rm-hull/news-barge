FIREFIGHTERS have been called to a blaze close to a major road - the A1(M) in North Yorkshire.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service confirmed that a number of crews have been called to a field in Shambles Lane, Hutton Conyers, after it caught on fire earlier this afternoon.

A spokesperson for the service said: “Crews are working alongside farmers to create fire breaks and dampen down the perimeter of the fire. In use are 4 hose reel jets and 8 beaters.

“If you are travelling on the A1(M), please be aware that smoke may be visible in the area. Please avoid the area where possible.”

In a post made to Facebook, one person called out for farmers who may have tankers of water to help extinguish the fire.
