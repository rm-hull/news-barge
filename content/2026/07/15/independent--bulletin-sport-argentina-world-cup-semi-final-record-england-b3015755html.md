# How Argentina extended their flawless World Cup semi-final record after beating England

- Argentina have beaten England 2-1 to reach the World Cup final.
- The win in Atlanta has seen Argentina extend their flawless World Cup semi-final record.
- Going into this evening’s match, Argentina had won all five of their previous World Cup semi-finals, in 1978, 1986, 1990, 2014 and 2022 respectively. Argentina also reached the final in the inaugural World Cup in 1930, although there was no official semi-final for that tournament.
- Argentina have gone on to win the World Cup three times in 1978, 1986 and 2022, while they finished runners-up in 1930, 1990 and 2014.
- Argentina will face Spain in the 2026 final on Sunday.
