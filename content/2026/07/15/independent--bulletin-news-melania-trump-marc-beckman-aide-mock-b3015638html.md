# Internet reacts to Melania Trump aide calling her ‘single most consequential’ first lady

- Marc Beckman, an aide to Melania Trump, declared her the "single most consequential" presidential spouse in American history during a Newsmax interview.
- Beckman claimed Melania Trump had a substantive impact on the Ukraine-Russia peace process by single-handedly executing four reunifications of Ukrainian and Russian children.
- Melania Trump previously revealed she opened back-channel negotiations with Vladimir Putin and has been credited with helping reunite 26 Ukrainian children with their families.
- Beckman's comments were widely ridiculed online by pundits and commentators, who suggested other first ladies were more deserving of such acclaim. “Wow, he actually said this with a straight face!” said *Atlantic* contributing editor Norman Ornstein on X.
- “F***in HILARIOUS,” added national security expert Malcolm Nance.
