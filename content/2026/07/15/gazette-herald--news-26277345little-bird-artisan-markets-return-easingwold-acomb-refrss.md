Handcrafted gifts and treats across an array of artisan stalls are set to take centre stage this weekend.

Little Bird Artisan Markets will return to Easingwold’s Market Square from 9am and Acomb’s Front Street from 10am on Saturday, July 18 to celebrate independent makers, food, and drink.

Jackie Crozier, managing director of Little Bird Made, said: "We love our summer markets, and while it’s definitely going to be a hot one this weekend, we’ve got everything in place to keep everyone cool and comfortable, from iced drinks to fantastic high streets with small independent coffee shops where you can relax in the shade once you have browsed the stalls.

"It’s the perfect excuse to support your local independent businesses, and enjoy the sunshine with the whole family."

  Visitors can expect handmade crafts, summer foods, home décor, and clothing from traders 

*(Image: Little Bird Artisan Markets)*

Visitors can expect handmade crafts, summer foods, home décor, and clothing from traders.

Gill Mallon, owner of Commune Homes and Gardens, said: "I love trading at Easingwold Artisan Market with Little Bird Markets every month.

"A lovely location in Easingwold Market Square with some gorgeous stalls offering a wonderful selection of handmade crafts."

Marketgoers are encouraged to arrive early to enjoy the cooler part of the day and explore the stalls.

For more information, visit: www.littlebirdmade.com
