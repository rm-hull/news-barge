If you’re looking to get your hands on an England shirt ahead of the remaining fixtures, here are the kits you can buy right now, from official England and Scotland World Cup shirts to budget-friendly options.

## Where to buy the England and Scotland World Cup kits 2026

England’s World Cup kit is manufactured by Nike, with the full range available to buy through Nike’s official store, as well as other retailers including Sports Direct, JD Sports, and the official Fifa store. Scotland’s kit, produced by Adidas, is also available through Adidas’ official store and selected retailers, alongside Fifa’s online store.
