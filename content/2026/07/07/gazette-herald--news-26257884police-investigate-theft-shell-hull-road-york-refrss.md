North Yorkshire Police have launched a CCTV appeal after a theft in a York petrol station.

The incident happened on June 18 at Shell in Hull Road, and involved the theft of £16.19 worth of stock.

Police are urging anyone who recognises the man to come forward as he may have information that will assist the force's investigation.

  


To get in touch, please email [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk) or alternatively call North Yorkshire Police on 101 and ask for PC 1488 Bell.

For anonymous tip offs, please contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

Please quote reference 12260113295 when passing on information.
