A crash has closed a stretch of the A1(M) in North Yorkshire today (Tuesday).

It’s shut in both directions between J52 (Catterick) and J51 (Leeming Bar) due to a collision.

One of the vehicles involved is a trailer carrying livestock.

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/a1m-crash-7-july-2026-google-map.webp)

Emergency services are at the scene.

At 11.58pm, National Highways Yorkshire said there were delays of up to 45 minutes on the approach, with four miles of congestion.

## Diversion Route

#### Southbound traffic is advised to follow the solid square diversion symbol:

- Exit the A1(M) southbound at J52 and take the 2nd exit at the roundabout onto the A6055 signposted Catterick
- Continue on the A6055 for 6 miles to the next roundabout
- At the roundabout take the 2nd exit signposted Leeming Bar Industrial Estate
- Continue to the next roundabout and take the 2nd exit to then re-join the A1(M) southbound at J51

#### Northbound traffic is advised to follow the hollow circle diversion symbol:

- Exit the A1(M) southbound at J51 and take the 2nd exit at the roundabout onto the A6055 signposted Catterick
- Continue on the A6055 for 6 miles
- Re-join the A1(M) southbound at J52
