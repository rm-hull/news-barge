## Pizza party brings families together at Stamford Bridge care home

Stamford Bridge care home recently enjoyed a special afternoon of pizza-making and family time *(Image: Barchester)*

Care home residents in Stamford Bridge recently enjoyed a special afternoon of pizza-making and family time.

The picnic pizza event took place at Stamford Bridge Beaumont Care Home to welcome in the beginning of the month.

Residents made the pizzas before enjoying a picnic-style lunch in the garden, with fizz and with their loved ones and visitors.

Emma Smith, general manager at Stamford Bridge Beaumont, said: "This has been a huge amount of fun and sparked a lot of smiles and wonderful conversations among our residents and guests."

  Residents made the pizzas before enjoying a picnic-style lunch in the garden 

*(Image: Barchester)*

One resident, Joyce, added: "Making our own pizzas was brilliant fun, and sharing them in the sunny garden with a glass of fizz and my family made it a truly special afternoon."

Stamford Bridge Beaumont is part of Barchester Healthcare, one of the UK’s largest care providers.

Stamford Bridge Beaumont provides residential care, nursing care and dementia care.

The home cares for 89 residents, offering respite care to long-term stays.
