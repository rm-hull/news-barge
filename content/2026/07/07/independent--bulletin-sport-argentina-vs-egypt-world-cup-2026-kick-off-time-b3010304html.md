# Argentina vs Egypt: What time is kick-off – and how can I watch the match?

- Argentina are set to play Egypt in the World Cup last-16 on Tuesday 7 July at **5pm BST** at the Atlanta Stadium.
- Argentina advanced after a 3-2 extra-time victory over Cape Verde, while Egypt secured their first-ever knockout win by beating Australia in a penalty shootout.
- Lionel Messi, with seven goals, will need to keep up his stellar scoring form in the race for the golden boot, with Kylian Mbappe matching his pace.
- Viewers in the UK can watch the match live on ITV1 or stream it for free on ITVX.
- The victor of this match will proceed to face either Switzerland or Colombia in the quarter-finals on Sunday 11 July.
