# Anne Hathaway cradles baby bump at The Odyssey world premiere

- Anne Hathaway displayed her baby bump at the world premiere of Christopher Nolan's *The Odyssey* in London.
- The 43-year-old actor attended the event at Odeon Luxe Leicester Square, wearing a baby-blue Dior Haute Couture gown.
- She posed for photographs alongside her castmates, including Matt Damon, Tom Holland, and Zendaya.
- Hathaway had previously announced her third pregnancy on 19 June via an Instagram video.
- She is married to actor and businessman Adam Shulman, and they share two sons, Jonathan, 10, and Jack, six.
