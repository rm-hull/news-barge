                                    Police have issued a CCTV image following a shop theft in Harrogate.

It happened on 15th June at Weetons on Leeds Road and involved the theft of £129.90 worth of stock.

A police spokesperson said:

“Please contact us if you recognise the male pictured on CCTV, as he may have information that will assist our investigation. Email [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk) if you can help.

“Alternatively, you can call North Yorkshire Police on 101 and ask for PC 1488 BELL or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.”
