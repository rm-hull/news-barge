The T9 SSD is the performance option. It delivers sustained read and write speeds of up to 2,000MB/s, making it ideal for 4K video editing, large creative projects, and transferring huge files in seconds.

The T7 SSD is the everyday pick. It reaches read speeds of up to 1,050MB/s and write speeds of up to 1,000MB/s, making it an excellent choice for backing up photos, documents, games, and everyday creative projects.

## Drop proof

Dynamic Thermal Guard helps keep the drive cool during extended workloads, while 256-bit AES hardware encryption protects sensitive files.

It's also built to withstand drops of up to 9.8 feet and works with PCs, Macs, tablets, cameras, gaming consoles, and the latest USB-C iPhones.

The Samsung T7 Portable SSD is aimed at people who don't need extreme performance but still want fast, dependable storage.

Its compact aluminum design is shock resistant, supports hardware encryption, and works with a wide range of PCs, Macs, smartphones, tablets, and gaming consoles.

The compact design of the two models makes them easy to carry in a pocket or backpack, while support for recording 4K 60fps video from compatible cameras and smartphones adds extra flexibility for creators.

The T9 Portable SSD is the clear choice if your workflow demands the fastest possible transfer speeds and you regularly edit large media files. If your needs are more focused on everyday backups, portable storage, and general productivity, the Samsung T7 Portable SSD offers excellent real-world performance at a slower speed.

Both drives featured here offer 1TB of storage, but the T9 and T7 are also available in 2TB and 4TB capacities.

Whichever model suits your needs best, both are dependable portable SSDs from one of the most trusted names in flash storage.
