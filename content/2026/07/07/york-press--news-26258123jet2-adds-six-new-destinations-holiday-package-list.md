Jet2 has confirmed a major expansion to their holiday package lists giving jetsetters six new locations to travel to.

Jet2 is expanding its wedding package service, Jet2Weddings, to offer ceremonies in Turkey, Portugal, Lanzarote, Italy, Malta and Santorini for summer 2027.

This brings the service’s total offering to 19 venues across these newly added destinations.

The packages include flights, accommodation, and transfers, with on-the-ground wedding arrangements handled by Jet2’s partner Perfect Weddings Abroad.

Steve Heapy, chief executive of Jet2holidays, said: "The launch of Jet2Weddings has been extremely successful, so we're pleased to be expanding our offering to five breathtaking new countries from Summer 2027.


"With even more stunning locations to select from for the perfect wedding abroad, we are giving customers even more choice and flexibility when it comes to tying the knot.

"We've no doubt that the appeal of these new destinations, along with the industry-leading benefits of Jet2holidays and Perfect Weddings Abroad, will prove popular with couples looking to say 'I do.'"

Couples can now book weddings in resort areas such as The Algarve in Portugal, Puerto Del Carmen, Playa Blanca and Puerto Calero in Lanzarote, Antalya and Dalaman in Turkey, Sorrento in Italy, St Julians in Malta and Megalochori in Santorini.

The expansion adds to Jet2Weddings’ existing portfolio in Cyprus, Rhodes, Zante and Crete.

Jet2Weddings launched in 2024 and has already seen strong demand.

The service advertises venues rated from three to five stars, and most hotels offer instant wedding day confirmation.

Helen Doyle, managing director of Perfect Weddings Abroad, said: "Following the success of our Greece and Cyprus weddings programme, I'm delighted to expand the Jet2Weddings portfolio by introducing a collection of stunning wedding locations across Turkey, Portugal, Lanzarote, Italy and Malta, giving couples more choice and a variety of styles for their wedding abroad.

"From beach weddings, garden settings and seafront locations, we really do have each couples' perfect location.

"The Jet2Weddings team at Perfect Weddings Abroad looks forward to guiding each couple on their exciting journey."

Jet2 say the package allows couples to sort their flights, hotels and transfers for their wedding all in one place, and Perfect Weddings Abroad will then take care of all the wedding arrangements.

Bookings for the expanded destinations became available on July 1.
