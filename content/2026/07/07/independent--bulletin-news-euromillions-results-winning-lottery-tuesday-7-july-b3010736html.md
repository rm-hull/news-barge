# EuroMillions results: Winning lottery and Thunderball numbers for Tuesday 7 July

- Tonight’s EuroMillions draw offered players the chance to win an estimated jackpot of £14million.
- To win the prize, a player had to get five main numbers plus two lucky stars.
- In the UK, every EuroMillions entry automatically includes a unique "UK Millionaire Maker" code, entering you into an additional draw with a guaranteed £1 million winner in every draw.
- The winning EuroMillions numbers were **5, 29, 33, 45**, and** 47**. The two Lucky Star numbers were** 5**and** 8**.
- The winning Thunderball numbers were **11, 12, 21, 33**, and** 38**. The Thunderball was** 10**.
