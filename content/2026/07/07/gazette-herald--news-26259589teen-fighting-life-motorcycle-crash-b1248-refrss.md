A 19-year-old is fighting for his life in hospital after his motorcycle was involved in a crash in East Yorkshire.

Humberside Police is urging the public to come forward with information following the crash on the B1248 near the North Road junction in Lund on Monday (July 6).

The incident happened at around 4pm and involved a white Volkswagen van and a black Yamaha motorcycle, which the teenager was riding.

  


His injuries are considered to be life-threatening, a police spokesperson has confirmed.

They added: “The crash happened for reasons that are still unknown.

“Anyone who witnessed the collision, has dash camera footage, or has any other information about the incident, is urged to please call us on our non-emergency number 101 quoting log 349 of the 6 July.”
