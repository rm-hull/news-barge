### What is the Public Notice Portal?

The Public Notice Portal carries statutory public notices published in local newspapers and is the fastest and most effective way of finding out what is happening in YOUR neighbourhood.

Search the Public Notice PortalA two-week road closure is planned for works to the footpath as part of multiple public notices announced.

The full closure will be in place between July 20 and December 31 in Newgate, Malton, as part of works to the footpath, kerbing and road markings.

During the closure, access to premises will be maintained subject to access.

Traffic signs will indicate when the restriction is active, and an alternative local route will be signposted where appropriate.

The order allows for the closure to be implemented at any point during the specified period, with further details to be provided via on-site signage.

A prohibition on parking will also be introduced in Newgate.

This will also be in place for up to two weeks, at some point during the period from July 14 to December 31.

This restriction is to allow for kerbing works.

The prohibition on parking will also be in place only when indicated by traffic signs.

Further information on both the traffic and parking orders can be obtained by contacting the North Yorkshire Customer Service Centre on: 0300 131 2 131 or by visiting northyorks.gov.uk/roadworks-map

In Thornton-le-Clay, Moor Lane will be closed for duct installation by Quickline Communications.

The works are scheduled to take place between July 20 and July 31.

As with the Newgate order, access to premises will be maintained where possible, and a local diversion will be in place.

Quickline Communications, the works promoter, can be contacted via Spectrum Communications on: 07856 910564.
