The cast of a popular TV crime drama has been left "devastated" after the series was axed after just three series.

AcornTV has confirmed the cosy crime drama Whitstable Pearl will not be returning for a fourth season.

Many fans online have been discussing whether the show will return since its third season aired in 2024 on Channel 4.

The series’ ending left many questions unanswered for fans, with many saying the storylines "deserve closure."


## Why has Whitstable Pearl been axed?

AcornTV has decided to concentrate on other shows, leaving many plotlines in the popular drama open-ended.

A TV insider for The Sun said: "It’s absolutely devastating for everybody involved as they feel the series will be left incomplete.

"Viewers are also going to be gutted as they’ll never know if the two main characters ever get together romantically, as the ‘will they, won’t they’ storyline has been a huge part of the drama."

The show followed Pearl Nolan, a single mother and ex-police officer played by Kerry Godliman, who ran a seafood restaurant called the Whitstable Pearl with her mum.

After her friend dies in suspicious circumstances, Pearl ends up in a conflict with the new cop in town, DCI Mike McGuire, played by Howard Charles.

While the pair start off by clashing, they have grown closer over the series, with viewers waiting to see if they will get together romantically.


As the series has been axed, fans’ questions will not be answered.

After the third series was released, fans on Reddit had been discussing its ending, with one user saying: "But given the note the season ended on (not really a cliff-hanger, but still rather open-ended in a way that seems to deserve closure), it certainly seemed as if the writers expected another season.

"Of course, that doesn't guarantee another season (I see you there Queens of Mystery), but I think the show is pretty popular, and I certainly hope and want more."

If you are upset about Whitstable Pearl ending, you might want to check out the ITV reboot of Dalziel and Pascoe when it comes out, which will feature Kerry as one of the main characters.

Kerry will be starring in the show as Inspector Andrea Dalziel alongside Nina Singh.
