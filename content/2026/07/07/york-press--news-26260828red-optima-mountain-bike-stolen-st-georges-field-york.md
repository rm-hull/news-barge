A mountain bike has been stolen in York.

The red Optima Amulet bicycle was taken from the bike racks near St Georges Field and Tower Gardens on Monday, June 29, between 1.45pm and 4pm, North Yorkshire Police said.

A force spokesperson urged people to “remain vigilant and keep an eye out for it”.

“Please be particularly mindful of anyone attempting to sell the bicycle online, through social media marketplaces, or at local car boot sales,” they said.

“If you have seen this bicycle, have CCTV, dashcam, or doorbell footage that may assist our enquiries, or have any information regarding its whereabouts, please get in touch quoting the reference number below.

“We would also ask residents to report any suspicious activity involving this bicycle to the police immediately.”

Anyone who sees the bike is asked to email [nabiel.galab@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:nabiel.galab@northyorkshire.police.uk) or phone 101 and ask for PC 319.

Information can be passed anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or visit its website.

Quote reference number 12260122049 when passing information to police or to Crimestoppers.
