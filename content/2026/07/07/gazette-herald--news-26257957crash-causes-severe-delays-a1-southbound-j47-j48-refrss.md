  **UPDATE: All lanes are now open, a spokesperson for National Highways has confirmed.**

MOTORISTS are being warned of a crash which has partially blocked a main road in North Yorkshire.

The incident was reported at 7.07am on Tuesday (July 7).

  


Traffic is heavy, with “severe” delays on the A1 southbound from Junction 48 A6055 in Boroughbridge to Junction 47 on the A59 (Allerton Park).

At the time of writing at 9.20am, the delays are now easing up.

A spokesperson for National Highways said at 8.45am: "All lanes are now open on the A1 southbound in North Yorkshire between J48 Aldborough and J47 Hopperton.

"Current delays of 40 minutes with three miles of congestion on approach."
