An insurance broker has appointed a new branch manager at its York office.

Howden, based in Feasegate, has named Frank Abbott to lead the team, bringing with him a strong focus on customer experience and people-first values.

Mr Abbott said: "I’m really excited to step into this role in York.

"I’m passionate about leading a team that’s approachable, engaging and genuinely enjoys helping others.

"What drew me to Howden is the chance to build something truly focused on people; both in terms of our clients and our team."

He emphasised the importance of listening to clients, adding: "For me, it’s about putting people first, making sure every client feels heard, supported and valued."

Howden provides insurance advice for individuals and businesses, with a focus on building relationships rather than simply selling policies.

The York branch offers face-to-face support and personalised insurance solutions to clients in the community.

For more information, visit: www.howdeninsurance.co.uk/york
