# Switzerland vs Colombia: What time is kick-off – and how can I watch the match?

- Colombia and Switzerland are scheduled to meet in a World Cup last-16 tie, with both teams vying for a place in the quarter-finals.
- The match will kick off at **9pm BST** on Tuesday 7 July at BC Place Vancouver.
- Viewers in the UK can watch the game live on ITV1 or stream it on ITVX.
- Colombia progressed by topping their group and defeating Ghana, while Switzerland recovered from an initial draw to win three consecutive matches.
- The winner of this fixture will advance to face either Argentina or Egypt in the quarter-finals on Sunday 11 July.
