A THIEF has targeted a popular food hall in North Yorkshire.

The incident took place in Weetons, in Leeds Road, Harrogate on June 15, and involved £129.90 worth of stock being taken.

  


A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Please contact us if you recognise the male pictured on CCTV, as he may have information that will assist our investigation.

“Email [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk) if you can help.

“Alternatively, you can call North Yorkshire Police on 101 and ask for PC 1488 BELL or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

“Please quote reference 12260110937 when passing on information.”
