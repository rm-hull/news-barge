A MAN in his 20s has been taken to hospital for head and facial injuries after a serious assault in York.

The incident took place in Davygate on Saturday (May 9) and North Yorkshire Police have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to.

  


A force spokesperson said: "If you have any information that could assist our investigation, please contact us online in the first instance by emailing [peter.wilson@northyorkshire.police.uk.](mailto:peter.wilson@northyorkshire.police.uk.)

"Alternatively, you can visit the police website, select 'Tell us about', then choose 'An existing case or report' or our live chat on our website.

"If you are unable to use these options, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

"Please quote reference 12260083784 when passing on information."
