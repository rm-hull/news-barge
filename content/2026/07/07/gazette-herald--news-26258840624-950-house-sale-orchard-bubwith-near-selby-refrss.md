ONE house worth over £600k is the last property remaining at a new housing estate close to Selby.

The Orchard - a 10-home project by Mulgrave Properties in Bubwith, near Selby - which offers a mix of two, three, four, and five-bedroom houses has completed work on its site, with just one house left for buyers.

Eight of the homes are already occupied, with just one property, the four-bedroom Letchworth, still available for sale at £624,950.

Debbie Foster, sales director at Mulgrave Properties, said: "The Orchard has proved to be an extremely popular development, thanks largely to the attractive village location and the variety of homes that it has provided.

"We only have one house, the 4-bedroom Letchworth, remaining for sale."

The homes are traditional in style but feature modern energy-efficient elements, including air source heat pumps and electric vehicle charging points.

Debbie added: "We pride ourselves on building homes which combine character with functionality.

"The properties at The Orchard are traditional in design but offer high levels of energy efficiency along with modern green technologies such as air source heat pumps and charging points for electric vehicles."

Mulgrave Properties has also made a £23,800 contribution towards off-site recreational facilities as part of a planning agreement, and a green space has been created within the development itself.

For more information, visit www.mulgraveproperties.co.uk/developments/the-orchard.
