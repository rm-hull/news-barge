## Thieves target North Yorkshire value store in early morning raid

The theft took place at Boyes Department Store in Whitby *(Image: North Yorkshire Police)*

THIEVES have targeted a value store in North Yorkshire.

The incident took place at Boyes Department Store in Whitby on May 19 at 9.55am, and involved the theft of numerous items, North Yorkshire Police said.

Police are asking anyone who recognises the people in the CCTV appeal to contact them.
