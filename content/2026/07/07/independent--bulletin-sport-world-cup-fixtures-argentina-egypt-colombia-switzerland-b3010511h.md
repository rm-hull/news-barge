# World Cup today: Messi v Salah as final last-16 games take place

- The final World Cup round of 16 matches take place on Tuesday as the quarter-final line-up is almost complete.
- Colombia and Switzerland are scheduled to meet in a World Cup last-16 tie, with both teams vying for a place in the quarter-finals.
- The match will kick off at 9pm BST on Tuesday 7 July at BC Place Vancouver.
- Argentina are set to play Egypt in the World Cup last-16 on Tuesday 7 July at 5pm BST at the Atlanta Stadium.
- The holders advanced after a 3-2 extra-time victory over Cape Verde, while Egypt secured their first-ever knockout win by beating Australia in a penalty shootout.
