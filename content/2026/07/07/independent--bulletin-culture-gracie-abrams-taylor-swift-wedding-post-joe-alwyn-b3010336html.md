# Why Gracie Abrams has raised eyebrows with Taylor Swift wedding post

- Pop singer Gracie Abrams has raised eyebrows after including a film still from *The Favourite* featuring Joe Alwyn in a recent Instagram post.
- The carousel also showcased the sparkling red dress Abrams wore to Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's wedding, which she attended over the weekend in New York City.
- Joe Alwyn is Taylor Swift's ex-boyfriend, and the scene depicted his character tackling Emma Stone's.
- Fans commented on Abrams’ post questioning the motive behind the image, with one saying: “Babes I love you but this is weird asf”.
- Abrams opened for Swift on her Eras Tour, and the two later collaborated on the 2024 song “us”.
