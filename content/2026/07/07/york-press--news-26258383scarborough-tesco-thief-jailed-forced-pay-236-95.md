A THIEF has been jailed after a spate of incidents in North Yorkshire.

Catalin Ionut Baran, 24, of Marlborough Street in Scarborough pleaded guilty to the thefts at Scarborough Magistrates Court on Monday (July 6).

Baran was convicted for stealing washing products and alcohol from Tesco and Tesco Express stores in Scarborough earlier in 2026.

  


The court was told that Baran had a “flagrant disregard” for people and their property.

He was ordered to pay £236.95 in compensation and was sentenced to prison for 12 weeks.
