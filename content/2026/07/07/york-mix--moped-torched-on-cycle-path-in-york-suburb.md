A moped was torched in York early this morning (Tuesday).

Firefighters were called to the incident in **Arran Place**, Heworth, at** 4.57am**.

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “A crew from York attended a fire to a moped that had been abandoned on a cycle path and believed to have been deliberately ignited.

#### Arran Place, York

“The crew used one hose reel jet to extinguish the fire.”
