Police are investigating two indecent exposure offences near a North Yorkshire beauty spot.

They happened at around 1pm and 3pm on Saturday 4 July, close to the Bilton Viaduct and Nidd Gorge area along the River Nidd near Harrogate and Knaresborough.

Police believe that two offences have been committed on the same day by the same suspect.

The suspect is described as

- a white man
- between the ages of 25 and 35
- around 5ft 10in tall
- of a stocky muscular build.

He was wearing a black baseball cap and carrying a black rucksack.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “We’re particularly appealing for information about any witnesses that may have been present in the area and have yet to come forward to police.”
