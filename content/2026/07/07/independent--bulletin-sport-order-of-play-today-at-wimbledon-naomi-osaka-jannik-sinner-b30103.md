# Wimbledon 2026: What is the order of play on day nine?

- The Wimbledon quarter-finals are getting underway, featuring key matches in both the men's and women's singles draws.
- Coco Gauff is scheduled to play an all-American quarter-final match against fourth seed Jessica Pegula in Centre Court’s first match of the day at 13:30pm.
- Meanwhile, defending champion Jannik Sinner will go head-to-head with Jan-Lennard Struff on Court No.1 at 1:00pm.
- Naomi Osaka will then compete against Karolina Muchova, who previously knocked out former champion Barbora Krejcikova, on Court No.1.
- Novak Djokovic will then take on Felix Auger-Aliassime in Centre Court’s final match of the day, after Alexander Zverev and Jiri Lehecka complete their suspended match.
