From frilly and lace sets, to practical and comfortable underwear, lingerie is a universal wardrobe essential – it is, after all, your first layer each day. Whatever your relationship status, there is something undeniably indulgent about a brand-new set, whether you’re buying it as a treat to yourself or as a present for a partner. Thankfully, the best lingerie brands cater for everyone.

From wired to non-wired, cupped to mesh or cotton, the best set for you is entirely down to personal preference. Some prefer skimpy silhouettes, while others look to elasticated waistbands and generous brief styles that still offer some support. Classic red pieces are perfect for special occasions, while black, beige and white lend to everyday wear.

The lingerie world is brimming with brands for every taste, budget, shape and size – from fuller-bust bras to flattering small-cup silhouettes. Beyond reliable favourites such as Boux Avenue, Pour Moi and M&S, the high street has upped its game with more size-inclusive options that don’t scrimp on style (Asos, New Look and Next are all pleasingly affordable).

Others, such as Dora Larsen, Fruity Booty, Stripe & Stare and Nudea, have embraced the need for more functional lingerie, growing a loyal following for their flattering sets that are still supportive and comfortable.

Here, I’ve rounded up all the best lingerie shops and retailers to know, from affordable favourites to luxury labels.
