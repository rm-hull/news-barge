# Coco Gauff wins all-American clash with Jessica Pegula to continue best-ever Wimbledon run

- Coco Gauff advanced to her first Wimbledon semi-final after defeating fellow American Jessica Pegula in a three-set match on Centre Court.
- This victory marked Gauff's fourth consecutive match at this year's tournament that she won in a deciding set, demonstrating her tenacious performance, as she secured a 4-6, 6-3, 6-3 win.
- Pegula, who was the highest-remaining seed in the women's draw, was overcome by Gauff, who had never previously progressed beyond the last-16 at Wimbledon.
- Gauff is now set to face either Naomi Osaka or Karolina Muchova in the semi-final for a chance to reach her inaugural Wimbledon final.
- In other Wimbledon news, Novak Djokovic is scheduled to compete against Felix Auger-Aliassime later on Tuesday, while Jannik Sinner moved closer to the semi-finals, and British wildcard Arthur Fery made history by reaching the quarter-finals on Monday.
