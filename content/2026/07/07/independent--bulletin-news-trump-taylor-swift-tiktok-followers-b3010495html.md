# Trump wrongly states facts when insulting Taylor Swift just days after her NYC wedding

- Donald Trump claimed to be the most popular person on TikTok, stating he had surpassed Taylor Swift on the platform.
- He made this assertion during a White House press conference on Monday, July 6, while discussing the social media app's purported dangers.
- Trump misnamed the platform as “Tic Tac” during his statement.
- He specifically claimed, “Taylor Swift was No 11. I’m No. 1 in TikTok by far.”
- Official figures indicate Trump has approximately 16.6 million followers on TikTok, while Taylor Swift has about 33.5 million.
