# The Odyssey stars wow red carpet at world premiere in London

The stars of The Odyssey have posed on the red carpet in London ahead of the film's world premiere.

Christopher Nolan's adaptation of Homer's Ancient Greek epic poem, starring Anne Hathaway, Matt Damon, Zendaya and many more, is one of the most anticipated film releases of 2026.

The story depicts Odysseus (played by Matt Damon), as he heads home from the Trojan War, in a film with a budget rumoured to be $250,000,000 (£187,000,000).
