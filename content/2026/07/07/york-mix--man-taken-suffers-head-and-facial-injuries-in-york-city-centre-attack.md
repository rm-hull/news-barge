A man was taken to hospital after an attack in York city centre.

It happened on **Davygate** at**4.35am** on**Saturday 9 May**. 

Now police have released this image of a man they want to trace as part of their investigation.

#### Davygate, York

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/cctv-york-9-may-2026-davygate-york-full.webp)

The victim, a man in his 20s, was assaulted by a male suspect. He was taken to hospital for treatment for head and facial injuries.
