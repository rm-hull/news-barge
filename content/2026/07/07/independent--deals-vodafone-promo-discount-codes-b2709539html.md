## Helpful FAQs

### How do I use a Vodafone promo code?

You can redeem your Vodafone promotional code at checkout. Once you’ve reviewed your items, look just below your basket and above the total price for a “Promotional code” dropdown. Click to expand it, enter your code and apply it to see the discount reflected in your order total before you complete your purchase.

### How can I switch to Vodafone?

Switching to Vodafone is straightforward. If you’re switching broadband, Vodafone will contact your current provider for you. Simply choose your plan and installation date.

For mobile switches, you can move your number online or by text. To keep your existing number, you’ll need a PAC code, which you can get for free by texting ‘PAC’ to 65075. Vodafone also provides step-by-step guides and customer support to make the process easy.
