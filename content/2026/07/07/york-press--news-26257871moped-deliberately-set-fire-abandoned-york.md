A FIRE is thought to have been deliberately started in a York suburb.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service were called to reports of a moped on fire, which had been abandoned on a cycle in Arran Place in Heworth at 4.57am on Tuesday (July 7).

  


Fire crews said the moped is believed to have been deliberately ignited.
