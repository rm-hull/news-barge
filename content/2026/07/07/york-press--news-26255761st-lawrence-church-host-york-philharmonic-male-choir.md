The York Philharmonic Male Voice Choir will perform at an upcoming concert.

With a broadening repertoire, the choir is eager to display its polished choral music, maintaining the enthusiasm and excitement from its centenary year.

The concert is set to take place at St Lawrence Church, in Lawrence Street, and will feature music deemed "well suited to a summer evening".

Doors will open at 7pm, with the choral performance beginning at 7.30pm.

A spokesperson for the choir said: "This is a golden opportunity to hear how the choir is moving on and to enjoy the kind of music that is well suited to a summer evening."

Refreshments, including wine, will also be available.
