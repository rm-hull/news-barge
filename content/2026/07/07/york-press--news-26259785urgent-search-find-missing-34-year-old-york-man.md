POLICE are searching for a missing 34-year-old from York.

Jamie Foster, from the Holgate area, was last seen at around 11pm on Sunday, July 5, in the Hob Moor area of Acomb.

He is described as a white male, 1.85m tall and of a heavy build.

He has shoulder-length brown hair and a fair complexion, North Yorkshire Police has said.

  


When Jamie was last seen, he was wearing a red long-sleeved top, blue jeans, and trainers, and had a bandanna on his head.

He was carrying a backpack with clothing, so he may have changed what he is wearing.

A force spokesperson said: "Jamie is a keen walker and may have travelled some distance from where he was last seen.

"If you see Jamie, please do not approach him directly, but call 999 immediately.

  "If you have any other information that could help us find him, please call us on 101 and select option 1 to speak to our control room.

  

  "Please quote 12260126753 when sharing information."
