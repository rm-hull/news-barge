Police are searching for a York man who’s missing from home.

He’s 34 year-old Jamie Foster from the Holgate area.

was last seen at around 11pm on Sunday 5 July in the Hob Moor area of Acomb.

Jamie is:

- white
- 6ft tall
- of a heavy build
- with shoulder length brown hair
- and a fair complexion.

When Jamie was last seen he was wearing a red long sleeved top, blue jeans and trainers and had a bandanna on his head.

He was carrying a backpack with clothing so may have changed what he is wearing.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Jamie is a keen walker and may have travelled some distance from where he was last seen.

“If you see this missing person please do not approach him directly, but call 999 immediately.

“If you have any other information that could help us find them, please call us on 101 and select option 1 to speak to our control room.

“Please quote 12260126753 when sharing information.”
