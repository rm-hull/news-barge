An under-construction high-rise building near New York City’s Grand Central Terminal was evacuated over fears that it would collapse after construction workers noticed cracks inside.

Emergency responders were called to 235 East 42nd Street around 8 a.m. Tuesday after workers noticed structural support beams beginning to buckle on the 21st and 22nd floors, ABC 7 reported.

As people began evacuating, the 21st to 26th floors started to cave under the stress, officials said.
