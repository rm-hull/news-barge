A period property with a long local history has hit the market in York’s Bishophill Conservation Area.

Located in Hampden Street, the four-bedroom freehold is listed at £725,000.

The property extends across four floors and is currently arranged as two separately accessed maisonettes.

According to information from the listing on Zoopla, via estate agents Carter Jonas, Hampden House was formerly a corner shop for more than a century and is well known in the local area.

  Reception room with cast iron fireplaces and bespoke cabinetry 

*(Image: Carter Jonas)*

The maisonettes can be kept as they are or potentially combined into a four- or five-bedroom family home, subject to any necessary consents.

Architectural drawings are available to demonstrate alternative residential layouts, and these can be viewed online.

The upper maisonette takes up the first and second floors and is described as having bright accommodation, which benefits from open views across the conservation area.

The principal reception room features original cast iron fireplaces and bespoke cabinetry.

  Fitted kitchen with integrated appliances 

*(Image: Carter Jonas)*

There is a fitted kitchen with integrated appliances, a bathroom, and two double bedrooms, with the option to reinstate a third bedroom if needed.

The ground and lower ground floors form the lower maisonette, which was once the building’s shop.

This floor includes a reception room, two bedrooms, and a bathroom. The kitchen and dining area are situated on the lower ground floor.

Another feature highlighted in the property listing is a private landscaped rear courtyard, which includes off-street parking.

  Landscaped rear courtyard with off-street parking 

*(Image: Carter Jonas)*

The listing notes that off-street parking within York's historic city walls is a rarely available feature.

Hampden House is described as offering various possibilities for future owners.

These options include maintaining the existing arrangement, reconfiguring as a family home, or exploring possible commercial or mixed-use uses, depending on any necessary approvals.

For more information, or to book a viewing, contact estate agents Carter Jonas on 01904 409467.
