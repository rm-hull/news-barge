Bank cards from a man’s wallet lost in York have been used in “numerous locations” since they went missing, police said.

Officers have issued a photograph of a man they want to speak to after the bank cards were fraudulently used.

North Yorkshire Police said the man may have information about the case as he has been photographed on CCTV “in numerous locations at the relevant times around York” where the bank cards have been used.

“We are appealing for the public to come forward if you can identify him,” a force spokesperson said.

Those who recognise the man are asked to email [peter.henderson@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:peter.henderson@northyorkshire.police.uk) or phone 101 and ask for PC1668 Henderson

Information can be passed anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Quote reference 12260099820 when passing on information to police or to Crimestoppers.
