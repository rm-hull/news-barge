Switzerland and Colombia both have the chance to equal their best performance at a FIFA World Cup as they meet in the last 16 in Vancouver — and you can live stream the game around the world for free.

Murat Yakin's Swiss side eased to a 2-0 win over Algeria in the last 32 to claim their first World Cup knockout victory in 88 years and now stand on the cusp of a first quarter-final appearance since 1954. Led by inspirational captain Granit Xhaka, who won his 150th cap against Algeria, Switzerland have a useful blend of experience and youth, with attacking midfielder Johan Manzambi emerging as one of the stars of the tournament. The 20-year-old has scored three goals and provided two assists as part of a dangerous front four that also includes Dan Ndoye, Ruben Vargas and Breel Embolo.

Colombia won't fear Switzerland's attacking quartet following three successive clean sheets, most recently in the 1-0 win over Ghana in the last 32. Midfielder Jhon Arias scored the winner against the Black Stars, but manager Néstor Lorenzo will have been equally pleased with the performance of Luis Suárez. The striker claimed the assist after coming on for the injured Jhon Córdoba, who will miss the rest of the tournament. Suárez is expected to get the nod from the start in Vancouver as Colombia aim to reach the quarter-finals for only the second time.
