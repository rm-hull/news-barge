Firefighters were called to help a trapped child last night (Monday).

They went to the incident off **Cornlands Road** at**7.09pm**.

A North Yorkshire Fire & Rescue Service spokesperson said: “A crew from Acomb assisted a child who had climbed in to a baby swing and become stuck.

#### Cornlands Road, York

“The crew used small tools to dismantle the swing and release the child.”
