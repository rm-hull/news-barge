### Up to 80 per cent off in the sale

Unlike some sports retailers where discounts only appear during major shopping events, the SportsShoes sale has stock available year-round. Discounts regularly reach up to 80 per cent off, with new products added weekly across running shoes, hiking gear, gym clothing and accessories.

SportsShoes also tends to run multiple overlapping promotions at once, so it’s fairly common to find sale pricing alongside voucher codes or brand-specific discounts. Previous offers have included an extra 10 per cent off top brands such as Nike, Saucony and Asics.

### Join the SportsShoes running club

If you’re part of a running club already, or maybe even thinking about joining one, the SportsShoes running club scheme is worth looking into. Members can currently get 10 per cent off orders over £50, alongside access to additional promotions and exclusive club offers.

### Use the price match promise

SportsShoes also offers a price match service if you spot the same product cheaper elsewhere. According to the retailer, it will aim to beat competitor pricing by at least £1, provided the item matches in size, colour and specification.

You can either complete the online price match form or contact the team directly before ordering. If approved, SportsShoes will also include free delivery with your order.

Once your student status has been verified, you’ll receive a unique code to apply during checkout.

### NHS staff can save with Blue Light Card discounts

SportsShoes also offers discounts for NHS staff and keyworkers through Blue Light Card. The exact offer changes throughout the year but, at the time of writing, Blue Light Card holders could get 12 per cent off new items.

### Use the free online gait analysis tool

One of the more useful features on the SportsShoes website is the free online gait analysis service. Instead of visiting a specialist running store in person, you can upload a short treadmill running video and receive recommendations on suitable shoe support and running styles.

It’s genuinely helpful if you are unsure whether you need neutral, stability or cushioned running shoes, and it could save you from buying the wrong pair the first time round.

### Can I get an app discount code for SportsShoes?

SportsShoes does occasionally run app-exclusive offers and early-access promotions, although there isn’t always a permanent app discount code available. It’s still worth downloading the app if you shop there regularly, as limited-time discounts, sale alerts and member offers sometimes appear there before the main website.

### Are SportsShoes returns free?

SportsShoes currently charges a £3.99 fee for UK returns through Royal Mail, InPost and Asda to You services. The retailer does offer a generous 100-day returns window, though, which is longer than many competitors.

### How do I track my SportsShoes order?

Customers can track their order through the shipping confirmation email or via the order tracking section on the SportsShoes website. Tracking updates usually become available once the parcel has been dispatched.

### Is SportsShoes.com legitimate?

Yes. SportsShoes is a well-established UK sports retailer that has been operating since the 1980s. The company also has thousands of positive Trustpilot reviews, particularly around pricing, delivery speed and customer service.
