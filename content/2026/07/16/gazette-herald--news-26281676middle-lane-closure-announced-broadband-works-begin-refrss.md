Search the Public Notice PortalA five-day road closure is planned in Skirpenbeck.

Middle Lane will close to through traffic between August 3 and August 7.

The closure will allow for the installation of ducting and chambers as part of the new BDUK fibre network, being carried out by Quickline Communications.

Works will be limited to this five-day period from 8am to 5pm each day, although the order allows for a closure period of up to 18 months should there be any delays.

While the road will be closed to all through traffic, access will be maintained for residents, emergency vehicles and pedestrians.

A diversion will be in place, routing traffic from the junction with Main Street, Skirpenbeck, right onto Brickyards Lane, Bugthorpe, right onto A166 Bridlington Road, Full Sutton, right onto Dolegate, Skirpenbeck and finally right onto Main Street, Skirpenbeck.

The closure forms part of a wider project to deliver ultrafast broadband to the region.

East Riding of Yorkshire Council advises that further information regarding the closure can be obtained from Streetscene Services, Highways Maintenance on (01482) 393939.
