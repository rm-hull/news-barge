York Theatre Royal have launched a new free membership scheme for 16-30 year-olds.

The scheme aims to give young people the opportunity to get the best seats in the house for just £15.

16-30 year-olds can book two tickets (for themselves and a friend or family member of any age) for a variety of York Theatre Royal’s Main House productions.

Shows on offer include drama such as YTR’s in-house productions of* The Penelopiad * and* Death of A Salesman*, world-class dance performances with London City Ballet’s* Firebird * and Acosta Danza’s *Cuban Baroque*, musical offerings from York Light Opera including their 2027 show* 9 to 5 The Musical* and much more.

Members will also get access to invites to exclusive membership events and monthly £15 Club newsletters to keep up to date on new shows added.

For each production included in the discount scheme there will be 100 £15 tickets released available on a first come first served basis.

Sign up for the 16-30 membership is free – find out more and how to sign up at the York Theatre Royal website here.
