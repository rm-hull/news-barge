# Liverpool star close to signing new contract in welcome boost to Andoni Iraola

- Liverpool are nearing an agreement on a new contract with midfielder Dominik Szoboszlai, a significant development for new head coach Andoni Iraola.
- The 25-year-old Hungary captain's current deal is entering its final two years, which puts Liverpool at risk of losing him on a free transfer or being forced to sell him for a reduced fee.
- Szoboszlai, who joined Liverpool from RB Leipzig in 2023 for £60m, was a standout performer last season, contributing 13 goals and 12 assists, and even filling in at right-back.
- Securing Szoboszlai's future is crucial for Liverpool, particularly after recent contract issues led to the departures of Ibrahima Konate and Trent Alexander-Arnold to Real Madrid.
- The club views Szoboszlai as a potential future captain, and his retention will aid Iraola's rebuilding efforts, alongside the recent signing of Victor Munoz and the arrival of Jeremy Jacquet.
