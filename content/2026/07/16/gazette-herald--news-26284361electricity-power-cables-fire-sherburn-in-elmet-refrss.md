A FIRE involving high-voltage power cables has sparked an emergency response in a North Yorkshire town.

  


Firefighters from York and Selby were called to the incident in Sheburn-in-Elmet at 11.46pm last night (Wednesday, July 15).

Two high-voltage electrical cables were reported to be 'well alight'.

Crews used two breathing apparatus, one hose reel jet, and powder fire extinguishers to bring the fire under control.

Electricity engineers attended to isolate the power and make the area safe.
