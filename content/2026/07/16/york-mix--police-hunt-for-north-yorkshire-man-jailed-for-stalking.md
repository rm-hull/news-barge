Police are hunting for this man.

He’s 48-year-old Scott Bradley from Knaresborough.

Bradley is wanted for recall to prison. He was jailed for stalking.

Bradley has blonde hair, blue eyes and is 6ft tall.

Police ask anyone with an immediate sighting of Bradley to please call 999.

If you have seen him recently or have information that could help locate him, you’re asked to call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Alternatively, you can pass on information anonymously through independent charity Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or via their website.

Please quote reference number 12260124900.
