## Sam Smith's brewery dumps Gumtree as it changes methods in seeking managers

Samuel Smith's Old Brewery in the Centre of Tadcaster *(Image: Darren Greenwood)*

Sam Smith’s Brewery has stopped using the website Gumtree as it continues to seek management couples for its many empty pubs.

The Tadcaster-based brewery used to regularly use the site, with the Press reporting last December it placed more than 120 vacancies on the website.

Last month, a Facebook group which reports on matters concerning the brewery said there were just 17 Sam Smith’s pubs advertised on the site, with the vacancies expiring on June 30.

The Press this week checked Gumtree and could not find any adverts seeking management couples for the brewery.

Instead, the brewery continues to recruit through its owns runapub.co.uk website.

This currently lists 11 vacancies, including the Angel and White Horse in Tadcaster, Agar Arms at Warthill, Crown Inn at Bolton Percy, and the Shoulder of Mutton at Appleton Roebuck.

However, the website says other vacancies are available.

A recent list compiled by Tom Taverner of Malton said last month that out of 315 Sam Smith’s pubs, 152 were identified as open, 145 closed and 17 of undetermined status.

In recent days, Sam Smith’s brewery has started placing banners on pubs, advertising that management couples are being sought for them. Pubs include the Brown Cow and Trafalgar Bay in York, as previously reported by The Press.

This is despite the brewery in May removing such banners from empty pubs in May following a reported spate of burglaries.

  The Brown Cow in York, with a banner advertising that it seeks a management couple. 

*(Image: NQ)*

The move comes amid reports of workmen being reported at several Sam Smith’s pubs in the area, suggesting a re-opening may be imminent for them.

Sadly, the brewery continues its policy of not commenting to the media, making verification of any re-openings difficult.

However, as previously reported, Paula Dawson will be re-opening The Tankard in Rufforth on Wednesday July 22. The Tankard has been closed since September 2023.

The Wellington Inn on Alma Terrace, Fishergate, is also said to be re-opening by the end of the month, with a management couple coming up from Pontefract to take it on.
