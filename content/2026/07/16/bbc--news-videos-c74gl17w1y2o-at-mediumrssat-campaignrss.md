# 'Good day to drown a few sorrows', says King as he pours pint

King Charles III has hinted at England's World Cup semi-final loss to Argentina as he poured a pint in a pub.

"Maybe it's a good day to drown a few sorrows," he said to laughter, while Queen Camilla smiled as she stood next to him.
