# Police confirm person charged over Ann Widdecombe social media post

- A 50-year-old has been arrested and charged by Police Scotland in connection with a social media post.
- The arrest follows a report made to Police Scotland on Saturday, July 11, regarding content published online.
- The social media post in question concerned the death of former MP Ann Widdecombe.
- Ms Widdecombe, aged 78, was found deceased at her home in Haytor on Dartmoor, Devon, last Thursday.
- Police Scotland confirmed that a report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal regarding the incident.
