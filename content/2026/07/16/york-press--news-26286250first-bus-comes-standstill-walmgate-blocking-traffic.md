## Bus comes to standstill in major York road, blocking traffic from both directions

A First bus carrying passengers has come to standstill in Walmgate, with traffic mounting *(Image: Newsquest)*

  **The road has since reopened.The Press has contacted the police and will update the story once we have received a statement.**

A First bus has come to standstill on a major York road, blocking traffic from both directions.

The bus, carrying passengers, was seen blocking a section of Walmgate at around 2pm on Thursday (July 16).

  


It appeared to be pulling into St Denys Road.

The bus driver said he had spoken with North Yorkshire Police following an incident.
