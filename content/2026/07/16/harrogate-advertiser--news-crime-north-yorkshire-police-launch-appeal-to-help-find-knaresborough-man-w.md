# North Yorkshire Police launch appeal to help find Knaresborough man wanted on recall to prison

## North Yorkshire Police has released an image and launched an appeal to help find a wanted 48-year-old man from Knaresborough.

Scott Bradley, 48, is wanted on prison recall after being jailed for stalking.

Bradley has blonde hair, blue eyes and is 6ft tall.

If you see Mr Bradley, or have any information about his current whereabouts, you should call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

![Scott Bradley, 48, from Knaresborough, is wanted on prison recall after being jailed for stalking](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOjZmNWM0OTI4LTcwYmItNGQ3Ny04YTZmLTdhYWY3MDk3OTY4MzoyMGZkNTdlMy0yN2Q1LTQzZDEtODk1NC01YjJkZThjMzE3Yzg=.png?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



If the sighting is immediate, you should call 999.

Alternatively if you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or by visiting [https://crimestoppers-uk.org/](https://crimestoppers-uk.org/)

You should quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12260124900 when providing any details regarding his whereabouts.
