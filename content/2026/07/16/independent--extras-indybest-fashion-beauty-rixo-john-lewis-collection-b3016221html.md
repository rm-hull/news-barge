Renowned for its eccentric prints and bohemian designs, Rixo has garnered the favour of celebrities and fashion editors alike, with everyone from Margot Robbie to Kylie Minogue donning the brand since its launch more than 10 years ago. Of course, with a star-studded clientele often comes an equally A-list price tag – as is the case with Rixo.

With most of the brand’s dresses costing between £300 to £500, they tend to fall into the aspirational – versus accessible – category of fashion. That is, however, until Rixo announced its upcoming collaboration with John Lewis, where the luxe designer will see its prices starting at just £65.

Set to launch on 22 October 2026, the exclusive line will feature 44 pieces spanning all four corners of the textile realm, from knitwear and blouses, to dresses, jackets, skirts, scarves, shoes and bags. Here’s everything we know so far.

![The collection is vintage-inspired, with motifs drawn from John Lewis' archival textiles](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/12/55/john-lewis-x-rixo-collection-indybest.png)

With more than three months between now and the collection launch, both John Lewis and Rixo are keeping their lips sealed regarding the full breadth of the range and the pieces we can expect to see. That said, they have revealed a few juicy details.

Nodding to John Lewis’s long-standing British heritage, the range will showcase a floral motif that takes inspiration from an archival John Lewis block-printed textile from 1849. While this specific design won’t feature on all the pieces, the brand has revealed that it’s set to be splashed across “two dresses, a blouse, skirt and scarf.” One of the dresses in question will be titled ‘the vesper’ and has been described as “a full-length dress finished with delicate cap sleeves and a ruffle neckline.”

The broader range will be vintage-inspired and aims to marry together Rixo’s signature silhouettes with John Lewis fashion’s everyday versatility. According to Rixo co-founder Orlagh McCloskey, “This collaboration feels like a true extension of Rixo – taking the heritage of John Lewis, leaning into its incredible archive, and bringing it to life with that Rixo magic. We can’t wait to see women across the UK, whether they’re longtime Rixo lovers or discovering us for the first time through John Lewis, style it in their own way and make it theirs.”

With prices starting at £65 and the pieces available in all John Lewis fashion stores and via John Lewis online, I’ve no doubt this collection will fly off shelves.
