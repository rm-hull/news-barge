THIEVES have targeted one of York's largest shopping centres and made off with over £600 of clothing.

North Yorkshire Police said it happened in June when the items were stolen from the McArthurGlen York Designer Outlet in Fulford.

  


A spokesperson for the force said it's possible that the thieves travelled to or from West Yorkshire.

North Yorkshire Police released images of two people it wishes to speak to, saying they may have information which could assist its investigation and asked the public for its assistance.

The spokesperson said: "Please get in touch if you recognise them - if you can help, please email [james.tomsett@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:james.tomsett@northyorkshire.police.uk) or visit the "Tell us about" section of our website.

"Anyone with information is asked to quote reference 12260108696."
