From a breath of fresh air in the form of a beguiling bouquet to a sensory journey to one of your favourite holiday spots, scented candles and diffusers can spark countless emotions and evoke lasting memories.

Moreover, with a surge in popularity, home fragrance and scentscaping has evolved into a luxe lifestyle choice – and depending on your budget – worth the splurge.

They are a firm favourite in the world of interiors thanks to the multi-sensory benefits and decorative aesthetic which can elevate a room with the flicker of a flame, or perfumed reed.

Cue these fragrant vessels and scented oils to create a heavenly ambience…

**1. Firma Mini Scented Candles, £14.50, (was £24.50, 70g,) Oliver Bonas**

![(Oliver Bonas/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/16/07/14093125-200690d3-3986-4b4f-8a83-185a76e120c9.jpg)

A tantalising trio to capture the Mediterranean scent of figs, the juicy, fruity aromas of pomelo, and delicate aroma of sweet rose petals; Fig & Blackcurrant, Pomelo & Mandarin, Rose & Sandalwood will leave a trail of romantic florals balanced by earthy cedarwood and tangy citrus.

**2. NEOM Wellbeing London, Great Day Reed Diffuser, £55, (100ml), John Lewis**

![(John Lewis/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/16/07/14093221-e9d0eaf0-1e98-4339-a00c-b3513757ba27.jpg)

Tailored towards wellbeing, NEOM is a hub for aromatherapy with essential oils to elevate your wellness and boost your mood. This limited edition features seven essential oils including wild mint and mandarin to promote a natural freshness in the air – and give you a little lift every time you pass by.

**3. Simon James Home Italian Kitchen Garden Candle, £24, (220g), Simon James Home**

![(Simon James Home/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/16/07/14093306-7fee248b-b8e3-4b13-b439-4b00fff11840.jpg)

A harmonious combination of fresh oregano, tomato leaves and earthy goodness of herbs; with top notes of Sicilian lemon groves and sun-ripened oranges picked at their peak of sweetness, this botanical candle’s the closest thing to an outdoor kitchen.

**4. Habitat Ceramic Singlewick Candle – Santorini, £8, (250g), Habitat**

![(Habitat/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/16/07/14093404-762cfee2-ae0f-4f50-9e50-bcfa3d2f96c2.jpg)

Inspired by the Greek island of Santorini with its whitewashed buildings, blue-domed roofs and piercing blue Aegean Sea, this picture-postcard holder brings the scents of island living home; a stress busting blend of bergamot, jasmine, violet, sea salt and cedarwood calms and soothe the senses.

**5. Floris Hyacinth & Bluebell Reed Diffuser, £100 (200ml), Floris London**

![(Floris/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/16/07/14093536-0dcc2e0e-d34f-43f2-85b0-77adbe15d2df.jpg)

Rich and heady, a bouquet of scented hyacinths and sweet bluebells lend depth and weight with thoughts turning to a spring woodland of violet blue flowers. Hints of carnation and ylang ylang come into play with fresh, leafy notes and soft balsamic complimenting the floral accord. Intoxicating and joyous.

**6. Baobab Collection: Scented Candle Vony Kopelika, Signature, £165 (right, 4 wicks, 1.1kg); Grande, £325, (5 wicks, 3kg) Baobab Collection**

![B](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/16/07/14093712-3791d1da-cadc-494f-9b31-900b17143536.jpg)

Pretty as a picture, these raffia baskets woven with hand-embroidered blooms by a woman’s cooperative in Madagascar make a wonderful keepsake – and perfect plant pot when the candle has burnt its last flame. A work of art and famed for their high-end, oversized candles, Belgian brand Baobab’s Kopelika is inspired by the bud in Malagasy with hypnotic notes of orchid, intoxicating ylang ylang and flower of salt lending a mineral freshness. Floral and exotic.

**7. Jo Loves Jardin de Citrus, £90, (250g), Jo Loves**

![(Jo Loves/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/16/07/14094507-f923829d-978a-46e1-aa8f-f04c25ab0651.jpg)

Cited as capturing the spirit of spring, imagine the invigorating freshness of fragrant blossom, evergreen foliage and pots of lemon trees and oranges bordering a pretty patio. With deeper notes of lively green orange and warm, spicy coriander, its flame fuses nature with energy, and full of cheer.

**8. Anatomē London Apothercary Rose, Petitgrain and Black Spruce Diffuser, £68, (200ml), Anatomē**

![(Anatome/PA)](https://static.the-independent.com/2026/07/16/07/14094605-2e2b38d0-d563-490f-bf91-e0cff0781d34.jpg)

Think of a favourite spot in a rose garden, a sensory experience no matter the season, this rose diffuser puts sweet florals in the driving seat. Balanced by signature botanicals, including earthy and woody cypress, citrus and zesty may chang, along with spicy and warm saffron. With woody black spruce and damask rose at its heart and a base of cedarwood, it’s a must-have floral accent for a positive sensory perception.
