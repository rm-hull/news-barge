## Free networking event for York businesses

Steve Lowe, sales director of York Press/ LOCALiQ *(Image: NQ)*

Business leaders entrepreneurs and professionals from across York and North Yorkshire are invited to a free networking event designed to encourage collaboration and spark new opportunities.

Organised by the York Press and York St John University in partnership with York College, the event will take place at York St John University on Thursday, July 30, from 5pm to 7pm.

It aims to connect organisations from across the region in a relaxed setting.

The event welcomes start-ups, established companies, charities and sole traders, offering an opportunity to connect with like-minded professionals and expand their networks.

  Paul and Antonia Chasney

Speakers will include Paul Chasney of Goose Beck Vineyard at Bilton-in-Ainsty and Phillipa Symington, a partner at Clive Owen accountancy.

York Press sales director Steve Lowe said: "York has an incredibly strong and supportive business community, and these networking events continue to create opportunities for organisations of all sizes to connect, collaborate and grow.

"Whether you're looking to generate new business, learn from other local leaders, or simply meet like-minded professionals, this event provides the perfect platform."

  Phillipa Symington of Clive Owen LLP 

*(Image: Pic supplied)*

Previous events run by The York Press and York St John University have drawn attendees from across the region, helping to build partnerships, exchange ideas and identify commercial opportunities.

Organisers are encouraging businesses to reserve their free place early, with demand expected to be high.

The event will be held at York St John University on Lord Mayor's Walk, York.

The event is free to attend.
