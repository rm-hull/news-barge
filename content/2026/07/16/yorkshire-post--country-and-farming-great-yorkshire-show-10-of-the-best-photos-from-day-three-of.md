As ever, our photographer was also down at the showground nice and early to capture everything that was going on.

The Great Yorkshire Show is known as one of the best agricultural shows in the UK.

This year the show takes place July 14-17, celebrating the best of British agriculture, including 8,500 animals across livestock classes, sheep-shearing, heavy horses, cattle parades and agricultural machinery on display.

The event also offers visitors main ring entertainment, refreshments, and shopping.
