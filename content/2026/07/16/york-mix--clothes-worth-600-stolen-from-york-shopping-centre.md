Police are investigating a theft from York Designer Outlet.

More than £600 of clothing was stolen from a store in the centre at Naburn in June.

Detectives have today issued these pictures of two people they want to speak to about the incident.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “It’s possible that the two have travelled to or from the West Yorkshire area.

“Please get in touch if you recognise them as we believe they will have information that may assist our investigation.”
