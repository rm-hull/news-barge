Going by leaks and what Samsung has teased so far, we're pretty confident that the Samsung Galaxy Watch 9 is about to be announced. Seeing that this is coming, Amazon is attempting to shift its remaining stock of the Samsung Galaxy Watch 8 by slashing the price to an incredible low of £154.79 (was £319).

With less than a dozen devices available at the time of writing, though, there's no way this offer is going to last very long. So, I'd move fast if you want to get the wearable we called "the best mainline Galaxy Watch in ages" in our 4.5-star review.

## Clearance deal on the Samsung Galaxy Watch 8

![Samsung Galaxy Watch 8 Samsung Galaxy Watch 8](https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/kuzTPgTR2qnCv27LofqqoL-200-80.jpg.webp)

The Samsung Galaxy Watch 8 is about to be superseded by a brand-new model, so Amazon is shifting all of its remaining stock for a ridiculously low price. We were incredibly impressed with the smartwatch in our review, as it felt like a big step forward for the wearable. There's the stylish design, as well as advanced health and fitness tracking features, and a smart running coach. This offer will disappear in a flash with such limited stock, so I'd grab it while you can if you want one of the best smartwatch deals we'll see all year and you're happy with slightly older tech.

## Is the Galaxy Watch 8 worth it?

At this clearance price, it's very hard to argue against. Sure, the new Galaxy Watch 9 is almost certainly about to be announced next week at Samsung Unpacked, but it will cost at least twice as much as this model at its discounted price.

Previous offers on the Galaxy Watch 8 haven't come anywhere near to this low, so chances are it will never be beaten and never be seen again once stock runs out.

For such an advanced and well-rated smartwatch, it's easily one of the best deals of the year. If you're happy picking up some slightly older but still incredibly capable tech, especially if you're a fitness and health tracking enthusiast, I geninuely don't think you'll find a better bargain than this all year.
