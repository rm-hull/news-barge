Downton Abbey star Jim Carter OBE will host a special fundraising event.

The Harrogate-born actor is supporting an event at Rudding Park on Tuesday (August 11) from 2pm till 5pm to raise funds for Women in Sport - an organisation working to create a future where every girl feels she belongs in sport.

Jim, who played Mr Carson in the hit ITV drama, will be joined by Olympic champion Tessa Sanderson and Women in Sport CEO Stephanie Hilborne OBE for a special afternoon tea.

  


The event aims to bring together supporters from across Yorkshire to address the significant gender gap in team sport.

  Jim Carter OBE will raise vital funds for Women in Sport with Olympic champion Tessa Sanderson and charity CEO Stephanie Hilborne OBE 

*(Image: PA Media)*

Nearly one million fewer girls than boys play team sport in England, according to recent research.

Organisers said: "We're hoping to welcome local businesses, community leaders and residents who want to support this local cause."

Tables of eight are available for £650, with individual tickets priced at £90.
