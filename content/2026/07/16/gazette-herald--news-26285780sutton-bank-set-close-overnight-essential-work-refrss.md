A MAIN road in North Yorkshire is set to close for essential overnight work for two days.

Sutton Bank on the A170 is due to close between 7pm and 4am on Thursday (July 23) till Saturday (July 25).

North Yorkshire Council said the works on the road – a hot spot for traffic incidents – will ensure that the “strategic route remains in a structurally sound condition long term”.

  


A spokesperson said: “During the closure of Sutton Bank, access to premises on the bank will be maintained whenever possible, however due to the nature of the works, there may be times when access will be restricted.”

For enquiries on the road closure, please contact North Yorkshire Council’s highway department on 0300 131 2 131.
