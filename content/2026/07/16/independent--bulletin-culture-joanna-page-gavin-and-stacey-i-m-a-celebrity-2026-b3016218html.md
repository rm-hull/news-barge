Joanna Page, best known as Stacey Shipman from Gavin & Stacey, is rumoured to be in talks to join the upcoming series of I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here!.

According to The Sun, the 49-year-old Welsh actor is said to be “really keen” to participate, with sources suggesting she would be “a coup” for ITV.

A spokesperson for ITV told The Independent that any names suggested are just speculation.

Page has previous experience in reality television, having appeared on programmes such as The Masked Dancer, Cooking with the Stars, and Taskmaster.

Other celebrities rumoured to be in contention for the series, which typically airs in November, include Christopher Dean, Nadiya Bychkova, and Olivia Attwood.
