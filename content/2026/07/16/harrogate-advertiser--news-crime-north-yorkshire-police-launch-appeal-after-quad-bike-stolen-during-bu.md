# North Yorkshire Police launch appeal after quad bike stolen during burglary at property in Ripon

## North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information and witnesses after a quad bike was stolen from a property in Ripon.

The incident happened between 11pm on Thursday 9 and 4am on Friday 10 July at a property near Grantley.

It involved the theft of a Honda 500 quad bike, as well as damage to the property.

A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: “We’re particularly appealing for any sightings of the quad bike or any suspicious activity in the area at this time.

![The police are appealing for information and witnesses after a quad bike was stolen during a burglary at a property near Grantley in Ripon](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOjY2NWFmOWY4LTRkMTAtNGQ5Ni05OWI0LTE2NzhkZDQ3MDc4NTo4ZjNjYWRlNC02YTNlLTRhYTAtOTIyYy1lNTI4NGZiZTc0YmY=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



“If you have seen the quad bike or know where it is, please get in touch.”

You can also call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

You should quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12260130125 when providing any details regarding the incident.
