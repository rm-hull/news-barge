## How to save on Thorpe Park tickets in 2026

### Advanced booking discounts

By booking in advance, you can get your Thorpe Park tickets for less than half the price. You won’t need to worry about the fluctuating British weather, either, as Thorpe Park offers a weather and return guarantee. So if you happen to visit on a day when the weather forces closure or ride availability is affected for an hour or more continuously, you can return for free on selected dates**.How to get 2-for-1 Thorpe Park tickets**

Thorpe Park is currently running a 2-for-1 promotion on theme park tickets where you can unlock a second theme park of your choice, completely free. This includes entry to Alton Towers, Chessington, Legoland or another day at Thorpe Park.

These offers enable you to get two tickets for the price of one when you pre-book online, making them one of the best ways to save during the summer holidays. Both tickets must be used by the same person, but for families who are planning multiple days out, this is a great way to save on a second theme park visit.

### Thorpe Park short break deals

Outside of term-time, overnight stays at Thorpe Park start from £77 per person. As well as enjoying a night in the on-site hotel, you get two days of theme park entry, an hour of fast-track entry to rides, a buffet breakfast, a discount in the gift shops, and more. You can even choose to upgrade to a ride-themed room, including Nemesis Inferno and The Swarm. If anyone in your party is under 1.2m, they’ll get a free ticket, too.

### £22.75 tickets with the Thorpe Park student discount

Students can take advantage of exclusive online prices, with day tickets currently starting at just £22.75 when booked in advance – that’s about 65 per cent cheaper than standard on-the-gate prices. Overnight stays are also discounted for students, starting from just £99 for two people.

To redeem this discounted rate, a valid student ID must be presented when collecting your tickets or checking in to the hotel.

### Save extra with a Thorpe Park annual pass

If you’re planning more than one trip this year, a Thorpe Park annual pass could be the best way to save. Prices start from £64, enabling you to enjoy the park as many times as you like, including its award-winning Halloween fright nights.

Alternatively, a Merlin annual pass unlocks access to 32 of the UK’s top attractions, including Thorpe Park, Chessington World of Adventures, Sea Life Centres, The London Eye and more. This is a great option for families who like to explore a variety of days out.

### Thorpe Park group discounts

Groups of 10 or more will benefit from booking as a group. Rather than the on-the-door price of £64 per person, your party will get a preferential rate from just £23.63 per person, saving you more than 50 per cent on the price.

Children under 1.2m tall can get free entry to Thorpe Park. That means younger children, who aren’t quite tall enough for the bigger rides, can still join in the fun.

The cheapest Thorpe Park tickets start from £29.75 per person when you book in advance online. That’s over half the standard on-the-day price, making it well worth planning ahead.

### How many discount codes can I use on a Thorpe Park booking?

Thorpe Park only allows one discount code or deal per booking. That means coupons can’t be stacked, but choosing the right one can still give you a big saving.

### Are there any Thorpe Park coupons for fast track?
