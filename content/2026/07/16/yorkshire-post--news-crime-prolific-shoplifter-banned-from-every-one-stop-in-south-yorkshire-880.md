# Prolific shoplifter banned from every One Stop in South Yorkshire

## A prolific shoplifter has been banned from every One Stop store in South Yorkshire.

Katie Hudson, 30, has been handed a three year criminal behaviour order following a spate of offending.

Hudson stole relentlessly from One Stop stores in Doncaster between June 21 and July 7.

She pleaded guilty to 11 counts of theft.

![Doncaster's Safer Neighbourhood Services (SNS) team, who work to tackle local issues in communities, including different types of anti-social behaviour and neighbourhood crime, secured the CBO against Katie Hudson following a spate of offending](https://www.yorkshirepost.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOmY1Y2VlNWMwLWIwODUtNGIyMy1iYWEzLTg5ZGZlN2NmYTM5ZjpmMzEwN2Q3Zi02YTFiLTQ0NjctYWM1Yy1mZmI1Y2ZhNmIzMmE=.png?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



She also admitted two thefts that took place in May as well as a charge of assault by beating after Hudson attacked a shop worker who challenged her when he spotted her stealing from a store.

Hudson, of no fixed abode, was jailed for a total of nine months in a sentencing hearing at Doncaster Magistrates' Court on July 9.

To further protect shoppers, shop workers and retail businesses in Doncaster, the SNS team applied for a CBO which was accepted in court.

It means Hudson must not enter any One Stop shop in South Yorkshire nor the Asda store in Springwell Lane, Balby.

This order will remain in place until July 9, 2029.

If Hudson breaches the terms of her CBO, she could be sent back to prison.

PC Vicky Green is an anti-social behaviour officer within Doncaster's SNS team who helped to build the case against Hudson and secure the CBO.

She said: "Retail workers do not come to work to be abused, harassed or assaulted by shoplifters.

"When the offending is relentless like in this case, we will always seek to obtain CBOs to protect workers, businesses and shoppers from offenders like Hudson and we hope this order reaffirms our commitment to tackling retail crime.

"If anyone sees Hudson entering a One Stop store or the Asda in Springwell Lane upon her release from prison, please report it to us as she is breaching the terms of her order.

"Only by reporting this information and shop thefts as a whole can we take appropriate and proportionate action to continue tackling retail crime and we are grateful for the support and cooperation of One Stop and other stores in Doncaster in this regard."
