# Jude Bellingham explains heated exchange with Lionel Messi after being accused of ‘waking the beast’

- England's Jude Bellingham downplayed a heated on-pitch conversation with Argentina captain Lionel Messi during their World Cup semi-final, stating they were merely "discussing a foul" and that it was "nothing big really".
- The incident occurred in the first-half of the match in Atlanta, with Bellingham explaining he challenged Messi about a foul, to which Messi retorted about an earlier one, leading to Bellingham's comment about Messi's strength.
- Argentina secured a 2-1 victory over England, with Messi setting up both late goals, breaking English hopes in the semi-final contest.
- Following the match, Thierry Henry criticised Bellingham on *FOX* for confronting Messi, insisting the England midfielder should have been careful to not “wake up the beast”.
- England manager Thomas Tuchel faced criticism for his tactical decisions and substitutions in the second-half, particularly after England took a 1-0 lead, but maintained he had "no regrets" about his approach.
