Claudia Winkleman has quit her BBC talk show, The Claudia Winkleman Show, after just one series.

She said she was "too nervous to enjoy" the programme, which launched earlier this year.

The show featured Winkleman interviewing celebrity guests in front of a studio audience, including Jeff Goldblum and Ralph Fiennes.

Winkleman expressed gratitude to the BBC, her guests, and the production team for the opportunity.

Winkleman, who stepped down from presenting Strictly Come Dancing after 15 years last December, presents The Traitors and spin-off The Celebrity Traitors.
