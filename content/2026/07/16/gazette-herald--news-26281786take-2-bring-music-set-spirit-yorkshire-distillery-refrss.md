An acoustic duo is set to make their debut at a whisky distillery in Scarborough.

Take 2, made up of Grace Tattersall and Josh Giddings, will perform at Spirit of Yorkshire’s Distillery Session in Hunmanby on August 29.

Earlier this year, the group was recognised as one of the most valued and recommended vendors by couples at the Hitched Wedding Awards.

Steve Marson, marketing manager at Spirit of Yorkshire, said: "Grace and Josh bring over a decade of professional performance experience to our intimate stage, having spent years entertaining audiences both internationally and across the UK.

"Expect a full-band sound created live on stage through Josh’s clever live-looping, paired with Grace’s incredible lead vocals.

"It promises to be a fantastic night."

Tickets are £15, or £25 including a Ploughman’s supper or vegetarian option.

Tickets can be booked online at bit.ly/distillery-sessions-2026-take-2 or by calling 01723 891758.
