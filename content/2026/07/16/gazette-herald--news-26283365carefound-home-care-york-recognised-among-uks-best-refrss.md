A care provider has been named among the top 20 home care groups in the UK.

Carefound Home Care, which has a branch in York, was recognised in the Homecare.co.uk Top 20 Home Care Awards 2026.

The awards are based on reviews from clients and their families, marking the eighth time the company has received this honour.

Lorna Miles, registered branch manager at Carefound Home Care York, said: "Winning this homecare.co.uk Top 20 Award is something we're incredibly proud of at our York branch.

"It means so much because it reflects the hard work, compassion and commitment our care professionals show every single day.

"We'd like to thank our clients and their families for their trust and kind feedback, as this award truly belongs to them as much as it does to our team."

Carefound Home Care provides hourly home care, full-time live-in care and short-term respite care for older people.

Amanda Hopkins, reviews manager of homecare.co.uk, said: "Carefound Home Care has demonstrated an outstanding commitment to supporting people to live independently while maintaining their dignity, well-being and quality of life.

"We are delighted to congratulate them on being recognised as one of the Top 20 Home Care Groups in the UK for 2026."
