THIEVES have struck at the entrance to a gym in York and made off with a distinctive £900 bike.

North Yorkshire Police said a charcoal grey Bickerton Docklands 1824 folding bike was taken from outside The Gym Group on Foss Islands Road between 4.40pm and 5.30pm on July 9, 2026.

  


Officers say they are keen to speak to anyone who saw the theft, witnessed suspicious activity in the area, or knows where the bike might be now.

They are also appealing for anyone with CCTV, dashcam, doorbell, or phone footage from the area that could help the investigation.

A police spokesperson added: "We would also ask members of the public to remain vigilant for this bicycle being offered for sale online, through social media marketplaces, local selling groups, or at car boot sales."

Anyone with information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police by email at [nabiel.galab@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:nabiel.galab@northyorkshire.police.uk) or through the force’s website by selecting 'Tell us about'.

A live chat service is also available on the website Monday to Friday from 9am to 5pm.

Those unable to use the online options can call 101.

The reference number to quote is 12260130429.
