                                    Police are appealing for information about a hate crime that occurred on Walworth Avenue in Harrogate.

It happened at around 7:30pm on Thursday 2nd July, between Walworth Avenue and a ginnel that runs towards Knaresborough Road.

It involved a woman described as being in her late 40s or early 50s with blonde hair, wearing sunglasses and a short-sleeve white top with sand-colour shorts and carrying a shopping bag.

The victim, who walks with a crutch due to a disability, was approached by the woman who verbally abused him, specifically targeting this disability, causing significant distress.

A police spokesperson said:

“We’re particularly appealing for information for anyone who witnessed anything, or may have CCTV or video doorbell coverage in the area.”


*If you have any information that could assist the investigation, email: PC 724 Furnass on**[jodie.furnass@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:jodie.furnass@northyorkshire.police.uk)** or call North Yorkshire Police on 101.*

*Please quote reference 12260124395 when providing information.*
