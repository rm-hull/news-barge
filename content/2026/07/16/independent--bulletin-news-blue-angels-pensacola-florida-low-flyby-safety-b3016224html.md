# People were sent running after a Blue Angels jet screamed over a Florida beach

- A U.S. Navy Blue Angels jet flew exceptionally low over beachgoers in Pensacola Beach, Florida, during the annual "Breakfast with the Blues" event.
- The low-level flypast caused umbrellas, chairs, and hats to tumble, sending spectators scrambling; an incident captured in viral phone footage.
- Naval Air Station Pensacola officials are conducting a review of the maneuver, stating that the aircraft flew below standard profiles.
- The statement emphasized that the safety of the community, spectators, and pilots is their highest priority, and a thorough safety review will ensure adherence to Navy and FAA standards.
- Attendees described the experience as both chaotic and "amazing," with some saying they would remember it for the rest of their lives.
