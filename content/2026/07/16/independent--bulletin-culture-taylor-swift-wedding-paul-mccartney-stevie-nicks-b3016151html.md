Pop star Taylor Swift and NFL player Travis Kelce married at Madison Square Garden in New York on 3 July, with details now emerging from attendees.

ESPN presenter Pat McAfee revealed that the wedding featured musical performances from Stevie Nicks, Paul McCartney and Avril Lavigne.

McCartney reportedly played The Beatles' “I Want To Hold Your Hand” for the first time in over 60 years, while Lavigne performed her hit “Sk8r Boi”.

“And to be clear, those musicians... one would just call out the other, and then they would come up and they would just do their s***,” McAfee said.

Swift walked down the aisle to her song “Love Story”, and guests received lace handkerchiefs embroidered with a lyric from her song “Blank Space” as wedding favours.
