The high street has always been a great place to shop for more affordable takes on cult beauty favourites, and M&S has built a name for it’s impressive fragrance dupes. The retailer's Apothecary range, in particular, has become a favourite among shoppers looking for luxurious, long-lasting scents that capture the feel of designer scents without the premium price tag.

In 2022, M&S's Warmth eau de parfum went viral on TikTok after beauty fans compared it to Le Labo's cult-favourite Santal 33. Since then, M&S has expanded its fragrance collection, with several scents drawing comparisons to iconic perfumes, including Jo Malone's wood sage & sea salt and Sol de Janeiro's cheirosa 62.

While designer perfumes can easily cost well over £100, M&S perfumes start from just £8, making them an accessible way to refresh your fragrance wardrobe without overspending.

Whether you prefer fresh, woody, floral or fruity scents, these are the best M&S fragrances to shop now – especially if you're a fan of Le Labo, Jo Malone or Carolina Herrera.

![M&S apothecary warmth eau de parfum, 50ml]([https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/46/MS-apothecary-warmth-eau-de-parfum-50ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/46/MS-apothecary-warmth-eau-de-parfum-50ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp) 296w)

M&S’s Le Labo alternative has fast become a cult classic. Hailing from the high street stalwart’s apothecary range, M&S’s warmth parfum blends aromatic notes of cardamom, cinnamon, cedarwood and sandalwood to evoke a scent that rivals santal 33. Infused with essential oils, the warming fragrance comes in a 50ml bottle – the same size as Le Labo’s, but costs £160 less.

![M&S sea salt and neroli eau de toilette, 30ml]([https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/37/MS-sea-salt-and-neroli-eau-de-toilette-30ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/37/MS-sea-salt-and-neroli-eau-de-toilette-30ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp) 296w)

Fans have compared this scent to Jo Malone’s signature wood sage and sea salt scent (from £56, Johnlewis.com) and at £8, M&S’s sea salt and neroli eau de toilette is a steal. While we are yet to test this for ourselves, it features notes of neroli, mandarin and musky sea salt, lending it similar salty, fresh and earthy aromas to Jo Malone’s fragrance.

![M&S black pepper eau de toilette, 100ml]([https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/26/MS-black-pepper-eau-de-toilette-100ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/26/MS-black-pepper-eau-de-toilette-100ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp) 296w)

If you’re looking for an inexpensive alternative to a popular eau de toilette for him, this black pepper scent has been compared to Davidoff adventure (£30, Thefragranceshop.co.uk) on TikTok. M&S has noted that its eau de toilette marries notes of vanilla with amber, resulting in a warm, sweet and spicy scent.

![M&S sweet pistachio eau de toilette, 30ml]([https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/09/MS-sweet-pistachio-eau-de-toilette-30ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/09/MS-sweet-pistachio-eau-de-toilette-30ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp) 296w)

If you’re a Sol de Janeiro devotee, in particular the brand’s Brazilian crush cheirosa 62 (£19.20, Lookfantastic.com), this eau de toilette is said to be similar. The fresh pistachio essence combines with subtle hints of tuberose and tonka bean, resulting in a fragrance that M&S describes as reminiscent of warm pastries and delicate blooms.

![M&S midnight blossom eau de toilette]([https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/15/MS-midnight-blossom-eau-de-toilette.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/15/MS-midnight-blossom-eau-de-toilette.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp) 296w)

An alternative to the YSL black opium eau de parfum (£102, Boots.com), which is well-known for its black coffee and vanilla scent, this midnight blossom eau de toilette blends notes of sweet jasmine, tuberose and amber for “soft florals and warm, musky undertones”, according to M&S.

![M&S velvet amber eau de toilette, 100ml]([https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/14/MS-velvet-amber-eau-de-toilette-100ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/14/MS-velvet-amber-eau-de-toilette-100ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp) 296w)

The grown-up sister to M&S’s now-discontinued velvet rose perfume, the velvet amber scent is being hailed an even better rival to Chanel’s iconic no5 scent (£156, Boots.com). Offering a high street alternative, you could add M&S’s velvet amber to your online basket 12 times before it matches the price of the designer offering. Described as a “rich amber scent” by the retailer, expect fragrant top notes of neroli blended with bold white flowers.

![M&S soft iris eau de toilette, 100ml]([https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/57/MS-soft-iris-eau-de-toilette-100ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/57/MS-soft-iris-eau-de-toilette-100ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp) 296w)

With TikTok claiming this eau de toilette strikes a strong resemblance to Chanel’s coco mademoiselle scent (£126, Chanel.com), M&S describes it as a “delicate floral fusion”, with iris, jasmine and bergamot alongside orange blossom and vetiver, amber and patchouli as the base. Plump for this over the designer fragrance and you will save more than £100.

![M&S warm neroli eau de toilette, 100ml]([https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/08/MS-warm-neroli-eau-de-toilette-100ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/08/MS-warm-neroli-eau-de-toilette-100ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp) 296w)

TikTok believes the store’s warm neroli is a rival of Paco Rabanne’s lady million scent (£89, Boots.com). The cheaper alternative is characterised by similar notes of neroli and citrus. Touted as being a fresh fragrance with earthy tones, the budget 100ml buy is described as elegant and warming.

![M&S pink pepper eau de toilette, 100ml]([https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/11/MS-pink-pepper-eau-de-toilette-100ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/11/MS-pink-pepper-eau-de-toilette-100ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp) 296w)

Calling to Lancome’s la vie est belle perfume (£97, Lookfantastic.com), M&S’s pink pepper scent is considerably cheaper. The designer scent is loved for its rich, fruity fragrance, with M&S’s cheaper option balancing similar orange flower blossom and jasmine notes, layered over a sweet base of vanilla, amber musks and patchouli.

![M&S red berries and rose eau de toilette, 30ml]([https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/53/MS-red-berries-and-rose-eau-de-toilette-30ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp](https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/07/16/8/53/MS-red-berries-and-rose-eau-de-toilette-30ml.png?quality=75&width=296&auto=webp) 296w)

This red berries and rose eau de toilette from M&S has been likened to Giorgio Armani’s Sì (£69, Lookfantastic.com), owing to its sweet scent. With red berries and rose teamed with warm base notes of vanilla and amber, this high street scent is an absolute steal at just £8.
