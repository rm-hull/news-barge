FIRE crews have been called to an area outside The Shambles in York.

Two appliances from North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service were seen outside the entrance to the historic street at 5.30pm on Thursday (July 16).

  


No other emergency service presence was seen in the area at the time.

The fire service has been contacted for comment on the incident.
