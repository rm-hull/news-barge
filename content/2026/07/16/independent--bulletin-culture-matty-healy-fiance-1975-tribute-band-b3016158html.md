# Matty Healy joins The 1975 tribute band for wild stag do performance

![Matty Healy, frontman of The 1975, celebrated his stag do by performing with a tribute band called The 9075](https://static.independent.co.uk/2025/03/06/08/6b5b20df672e99f9aa66741137f1cfa8Y29udGVudHNlYXJjaGFwaSwxNzQxMzM1NjI0-2.79099870.jpg)

- Matty Healy, frontman of The 1975, celebrated his stag do by performing with a tribute band called The 9075.
- Videos circulating on social media show Healy singing “The Sound” and crowd surfing during the celebration.
- Healy is set to marry American model Gabbriette (Gabriella Leigh Bechtel) later this month.
- Gabbriette recently had her Las Vegas hen do, which included being handcuffed, posing with burlesque star Dita Von Teese, and an Elvis impersonator.
- Her hen do also featured balloons spelling “Mrs Healy” and a sign indicating “I survived Gabbriette’s bachelorette Las Vegas 2026”.
