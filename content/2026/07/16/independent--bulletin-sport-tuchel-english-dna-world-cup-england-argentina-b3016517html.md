Copa Independent | England are out - the same story but at a greater expense?

England were eliminated from the World Cup after a 2-1 semi-final loss to Argentina, despite taking a 55th-minute lead.

Manager Thomas Tuchel highlighted a recurring issue where England become passive and lose momentum after scoring, a pattern seen in previous major tournaments.

Tuchel faced criticism for his defensive substitutions during the match, including replacing goalscorer Anthony Gordon with Ezri Konsa.

He stated that the 'DNA' of English football needs to change, specifically regarding ball possession and controlling the game.

Despite the defeat and criticism, Tuchel reaffirmed his '100 per cent' commitment to remain as England manager until Euro 2028.
