North Yorkshire Police is hunting for a man wanted for prison recall.

Scott Bradley, 48, from Knaresborough, was jailed for stalking.

He has blonde hair, blue eyes and is 6ft tall.

  


To report an immediate sighting of Bradley, please call 999.

If you have seen him recently or have information that could help locate him, call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Alternatively, you can pass on information anonymously through independent charity Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or via their website.

Quote the reference number 12260124900 when providing any information.
