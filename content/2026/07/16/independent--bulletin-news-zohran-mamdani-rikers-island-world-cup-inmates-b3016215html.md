# The FBI is hunting for the people who leaked concerns about Trump’s new Air Force One

- The FBI is investigating leaks concerning alleged security issues with a Qatari-gifted jet used by President Trump as Air Force One during a recent trip.
- Investigators are seeking to interview individuals who flew with President Trump, including Secret Service members, to identify those who shared information about the alleged security concerns.
- Reports from The New York Times indicated that the Qatari-gifted Boeing 747-8 lacked some defensive capabilities present in the older Air Force One model.
- President Trump reportedly switched planes during his trip, using the older Air Force One for a segment from Turkey to the UK before reboarding the Qatari jet for the flight back to the U.S.
- The investigation, led by FBI Director Kash Patel and White House Chief of Staff Susie Wiles, has also involved subpoenas served to reporters at The New York Times.
