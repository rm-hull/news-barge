# Trump says Iran released US citizen in ‘gesture of goodwill’ amid conflict

- President Donald Trump announced that Iran released a U.S. citizen, whom he claimed was wrongfully detained in December 2024, as a "gesture of goodwill.”
- Vice President JD Vance alleged that some Israeli officials are attempting to prolong the war with Iran indefinitely and are hindering negotiation efforts.
- The U.S. conducted multiple airstrikes against Iran on Wednesday, aiming to degrade the country's ability to attack commercial ships in the Strait of Hormuz.
- Trump convened an emergency meeting on Tuesday to discuss a significant offensive aimed at reopening the Strait of Hormuz, *Axios’* sources said.
- American forces reinstated a blockade of Iranian ports in the Strait of Hormuz on Tuesday, though Trump reversed a plan to levy a 20 percent fee on maritime traffic.
