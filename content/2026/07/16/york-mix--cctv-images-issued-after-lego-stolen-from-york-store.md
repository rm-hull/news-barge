Do you know this man?

Police want to talk to him after a theft from a shop on Coney Street in York.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “We’ve issued this CCTV of a person we would like to speak to following a theft in York.

“It happened at 2.45pm on Tuesday 19 May at TG Jones and involved the theft of Lego.

“If you have any information that could assist our investigation, please contact us online in the first instance.”
