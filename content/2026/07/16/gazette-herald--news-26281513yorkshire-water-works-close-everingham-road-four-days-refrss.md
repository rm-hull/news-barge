Search the Public Notice PortalA four-day road closure in Everingham will be in place to install a new water supply.

East Riding of Yorkshire Council has issued a notice closing Thorpe Le Street Road, outside Field House.

The work will take place from July 28 to July 31, between 8am and 5pm each day, and will be carried out by Yorkshire Water.

A diversion route will be in place, guiding traffic via Thorpe Le Street, Southfield Lane, Bull Lane, Harswell Lane, A614 Market Weighton Road, A614 Holme Road, and A1079 York Road.

All affected roads will be clearly signed throughout the closure, and access will be maintained for emergency vehicles at all times.

Full details of the closure and diversion route are available on the East Riding of Yorkshire Council website under public notices.

Anyone seeking further information is advised to contact the council and ask for Highways Customer Care.

The telephone number for the council is 01482 393939. Reference number: RJH.
