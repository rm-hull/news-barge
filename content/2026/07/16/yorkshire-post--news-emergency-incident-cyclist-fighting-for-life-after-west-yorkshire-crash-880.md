# Cyclist fighting for life after West Yorkshire crash

## A cyclist has been critically injured in a West Yorkshire crash, police said.

The collision, which involved a grey Omoda 5 SUV and a pedal cycle, occurred at around 10:05pm yesterday on the southbound A636 Denby Dale Road near to the traffic light junction with Thornes Road.

The rider of the pedal cycle, a 66-year old-man, received life threatening injuries and was taken to hospital by ambulance where he remains in a critical condition, police confirmed.

The driver remained at the scene and has been spoken to by officers.

Officers from the Roads Policing Unit are carrying out enquiries into the collision and are appealing for witnesses to the incident or the circumstances leading up to it.

Anyone with information, particularly anyone with relevant dashcam footage, is asked to contact RPU officers via 101 quoting reference 13260403435 or online via www.westyorkshire.police.uk/livechat
