## Bus operator looking to recruit new drivers at York open day this month

Transdev is looking for the next generation of talent in York to drive its business forward *(Image: Transdev)*

A bus operator in York is looking for the next generation of talent to drive its business forward.

Transdev is hosting a recruitment open day at its York depot on July 25, with opportunities for both qualified and trainee bus drivers.

The company operates more than 500 buses and employs 1,400 people across the north of England, operating the Coastliner, York and Country, and York CitySightseeing brands.

  A Yorkshire Coastliner bus on the 843 route to Leeds, operated by Transdev 

*(Image: Transdev)*

Dale French, training manager at Transdev, said: "From driving the most scenic routes along our stunning Coastliner and guiding our open-top CitySightseeing buses around York itself, we have a variety of roles currently available.

"And you can learn more about them all at the Summer Open Day, where you can get behind the wheel of our training bus for an on-site driving experience, and start the application process with us on the very same day."

No previous experience is required to attend the open day.

  A CitySightseeing bus in the streets of York, operated by Transdev 

*(Image: Transdev)*

Trained drivers in York can earn up to £16.90 per hour and benefit from free travel for themselves, their partner, and up to two children.

The open day will run from 10am to 2pm at the York depot, located next to Rawcliffe Bar Park and Ride.

Full details are available at: [https://www.transdevbus.co.uk/careers-pages/work-with-us/](https://www.transdevbus.co.uk/careers-pages/work-with-us/)
