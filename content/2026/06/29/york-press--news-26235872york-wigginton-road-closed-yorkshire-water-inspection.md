A MAIN road in York is closed this morning after a water pipe burst.

Yorkshire Water said a burst main in Wigginton Road outside York Hospital has forced its closure there whilst work is carried out on the repair.

A member of staff at the hospital said the road is closed close to the bridge before Crichton Avenue.

He said he saw water gushing out onto Wigginton Road and pooled at entrance number two to the hospital, with pedestrians walking around it and vehicles slowing on both sides of the road.
