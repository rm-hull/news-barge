# North Yorkshire Police launch investigation after Cartier watch stolen from hotel in Harrogate

## North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a distinctive luxury watch was stolen from a hotel in Harrogate.

The Cartier Tank Must XL watch was stolen from the Cairn Hotel, on Ripon Road, during the week commencing June 15.

The stolen watch is stamped with the serial number 4324322048AX.

If you have any information that could assist the police with their investigation, you should get in touch.
