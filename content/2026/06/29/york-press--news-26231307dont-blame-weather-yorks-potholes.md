**Don’t blame the weather for York's potholes – here's why**

REGARDING pothole repairs in York.

Is there no quality control? Is there no guarantee for the work done?

Six months later and the holes are crumbling away again.

This problem wasn't around when I first started driving 50 years ago, so what has happened to the standards?

If someone repaired my drive and it crumbled within a year they would come and sort it out.

All the excuses like, it's the hot weather or it's the cold weather or there's too much traffic are nonsense.

I've just come from Mexico, no potholes. I was in Norway last year, no potholes.

So why do we have all this trouble considering how much the government must take in road tax and fuel tax?
