A BURST pipe is pouring water out onto a main road in a York congestion hotspot.

An area of Wigginton Road outside York Hospital is has a covering of water on both sides this morning (Monday, June 29).

  **Recommended reading:**

The road appears open to vehicles and the footpaths appear untouched by the spill.

A Yorkshire Water spokesperson said: “We are aware of a burst main on Wigginton Road and have a team on the way to complete a repair.

"For the safety of residents and road users, the road is temporarily closed whilst we complete the work.

"We apologise for the inconvenience and thank everyone for their patience.”
