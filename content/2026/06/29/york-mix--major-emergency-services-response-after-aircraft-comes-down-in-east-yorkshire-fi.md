There’s a major emergency services response to an incident involving an aircraft in East Yorkshire tonight.

The aircraft – which appears to be a glider – has come down in a field in **Howden**.

Police, fire and ambulance crews are on the scene.

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/glider-crash-howden-29-jun-2026-5.jpg)

![](https://wp.theyorkmix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/glider-crash-howden-29-jun-2026-6.jpg)

The glider has come to rest in a field close to the M62 near the Ouse Bridge.

A witness said that there were a large number of emergency service vehicles still arriving.

The aircraft appears to still be in one piece.

We have asked the authorities for more details and will update when we have them.
