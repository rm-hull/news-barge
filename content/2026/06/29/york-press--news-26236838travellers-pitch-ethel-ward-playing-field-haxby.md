  **UPDATE: Neighbourhood Enforcement Officers have been called to the playing field in Haxby and served the travellers with Direction to Leave Notices.**

Travellers who have pitched up at a playing field in a York suburb will be moved on “as quickly as possible”, a town council spokesperson has said.

Haxby Town Council said it would continue to work with City of York Council and North Yorkshire Police to move Travellers on after they have been camping in Ethel Ward.

In a statement on Monday (June 29) a council spokesperson said: “We are aware that there have been a number of incidents on Ethel Ward.

“We advise all to report any incident to the police as soon as possible.”

It comes three days after the council said it was aware of “developments” on the playing field.

The Press has contacted City of York Council and North Yorkshire Police for further comment.
