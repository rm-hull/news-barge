A man has been arrested following an incident of indecent exposure at York Racecourse.

Police are now asking for help with their investigation from racegoers.

It happened between 6.30pm and 6.45pm on Saturday (27 June) near the clock tower.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Members of the public informed officers on patrol that a man had indecently exposed himself to their group.

“The suspect was identified and arrested. He has since been released on bail pending further enquiries.

“We’re particularly appealing for information from any witnesses who may also have seen a man exposing himself.”
