# North Yorkshire Police launch appeal to help find Knaresborough man wanted on recall to prison

## North Yorkshire Police has released an image and launched an appeal to help find a wanted 33-year-old man from Knaresborough.

Robert Johnson, 33, is wanted on recall to prison and in connection with an assault.

Officers believe Johnson is likely to be in the Knaresborough, Harrogate or York area.

Johnson is described as around 6ft tall, skinny build, with short and dark receding hair.

![Robert Johnson, 33, from Knaresborough, is wanted on recall to prison and in connection with an assault](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOjg0MWQ5YTEyLWEzYWUtNDA5YS1hMTNhLTY1ODAzMzYyNWM1NzpkMWJmZWZmNS1lMTkzLTQ3N2QtYjlhZS1kM2MwZTBlNGE1MGE=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



If you see him, or have any information about his current whereabouts, you should call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

If the sighting is immediate, you should call 999.

Alternatively if you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or by visiting [https://crimestoppers-uk.org/](https://crimestoppers-uk.org/)

You should quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12260118291 when providing any details regarding his whereabouts.
