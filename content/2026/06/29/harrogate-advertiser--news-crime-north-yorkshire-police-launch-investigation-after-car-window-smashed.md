# North Yorkshire Police launch investigation after car window smashed in Pateley Bridge

## North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information and witnesses after the window of a car was damaged in Pateley Bridge.

The incident happened in the early hours of Sunday, June 21 on Greenwood Road, when the rear windscreen of a car was smashed.

If you saw anything, have any CCTV or dashcam footage, or have any information that could help, you should get in contact with the police.

![The police are appealing for information and witnesses after the window of a car was smashed on Greenwood Road in Pateley Bridge](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOmY0MDMxMjc2LTA4OTQtNGFkMC1iNmZlLTYzZmVmZDU1NjEwMTo2YmEyZTgwMy04MWI2LTQ2NTUtYTVmMS1kODE2M2NkOWFlYWM=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



You can also call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Alternatively, if you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or by visiting [https://crimestoppers-uk.org/](https://crimestoppers-uk.org/)

You should quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12260115032 when providing any details regarding the incident.
