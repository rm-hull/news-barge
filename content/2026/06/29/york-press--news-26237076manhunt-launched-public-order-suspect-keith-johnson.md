A manhunt is underway for a man wanted in connection with public order offences and threats to commit arson.

Keith Johnson, 44, who is also wanted on suspicion of threatening to commit criminal damage, has been the subject of extensive police enquiries in the Richmondshire and Durham areas.

He is described as white, with brown hair and a dark beard turning grey, and speaks with a northern accent.

Anyone who sees Mr Johnson should call 999 immediately.

If you have any other information, contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting reference 12260078909.
