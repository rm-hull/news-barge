## Plaudits for York pub, which reopened at weekend after month-long renovations

The pub interior *(Image: Pic supplied)*

A popular York city centre pub is receiving plaudits after it reopened following a month of renovations.

The Windmill Inn on Blossom Street reopened its doors on Saturday after a six-figure renovation.

The Greene King pub has had a full makeover inside and out; including new planting and furniture in the courtyard beer garden.

The brewery giant says the pub, which was built in the late 17th Century, has a high-quality feel inside with new décor and furnishings and an improved sports zone with Sky Sports and TNT Sports, along with a pool table for sports enthusiasts to enjoy.

The updated layout has allowed the creation of an area where train times are on show, a moved fuelled by its closeness to York railway station.

Johnny Want, general manager of The Windmill Inn, said: “We are so pleased to be open again and serving customers.

“We have a great location and are popular with our regulars, neighbours and workers in the area as well as commuters and people visiting the city.

“Our new look alongside The Windmill Inn’s warm welcome and great atmosphere makes us a great choice on the York pub scene.

*(Image: Pic supplied)*

“We really are a pub for all occasions with great sports viewing, we are perfect for colleague catch ups, leisurely lunches, and works drinks at the end of the day. People find it difficult to leave! Come in and see us today.”

The Windmill Inn is popular with drinkers and diners, receiving 4.1 stars out of five on Google, based on 1,749 Google reviews.

The latest awarded five stars and said: “Great service, staff are a good laugh, pub looks great after the refurb, would definitely recommend and I will definitely be returning.”

TripAdvisor awards The Windmill 3.9 stars out of five, based on 1,122 reviews, but ranks it number one out of 53 bars and pubs in York.

The most recent TripAdvisor Review awarded five stars and said: “Visited the Windmill Inn recently and was really impressed.

“The new layout works brilliantly — the space feels well thought-out, making it a lot easier to find a good spot whether you’re after a quiet drink or somewhere to watch the football.

“The staff are fantastic — genuinely friendly and welcoming. A great local pub in York. Highly recommended."

Details of the pub can be found on the Greene King app and on the Windmill Inn’s website.
