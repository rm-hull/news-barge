Police are appealing for witnesses after an incident in a North Yorkshire supermarket.

A silver car has been badly damaged in the car park behind Sainsbury’s in the Market Place, Ripon.

It happened between 11pm on Wednesday, 17 June, and 7pm on Thursday, 18 June.

#### Incident location

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: “Did you see anything? Were you driving in the area with an active dashcam? Do you have any information to give?”

Anyone with information can contact police on the numbers below.
