Commuters have been facing major delays today on a key train route.

The disruption has been affecting the East Coast Mainline due to the theft of signalling cables between Grantham and Newark North Gate, which has led to fewer trains and speed restrictions.

Commenting on Monday morning, an LNER spokesman said: "Network Rail have been working hard to restore normal working.

"Over 100m of cable has been stolen resulting in the major disruption to our services.

"A power generator is now on site to power the signalling system with a permanent fix to the cabling taking place during the day."

Network Rail has been working to restore normal working as soon as possible.

Journey planning is available on the LNER travel alerts page and the National Rail website.
