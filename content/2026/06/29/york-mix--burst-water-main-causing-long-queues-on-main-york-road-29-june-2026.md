There are long queues on Wigginton Road in York this morning (Monday).

It’s due to a burst water main close to York Hospital.

Yorkshire Water engineers are on the scene working to fix it.

#### Incident location

Traffic is queuing both ways near the hospital.

Inbound traffic is slow back to the railway bridge.

The water mains have burst and flooded Wigginton Road on multiple occasions in the past few years.
