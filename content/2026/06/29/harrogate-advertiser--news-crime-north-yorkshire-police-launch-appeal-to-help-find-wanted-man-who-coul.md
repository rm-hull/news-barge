# North Yorkshire Police launch appeal to help find wanted man who could be in Harrogate

## North Yorkshire Police has released an image and launched an appeal to help find a wanted 37-year-old man who could be in Harrogate.

Benjamin Steel, 37, is wanted by police for multiple offences, including failing to attend Harrogate Magistrates’ Court having been charged with exposure and outraging public decency.

He also failed to attend the same court on Thursday (June 25) for theft from a shop in Knaresborough.

Steel is described as white, 5ft 7inch tall, dark curly hair, facial hair, and blue eyes.

![Benjamin Steel, 37, is wanted by police after failing to attend Harrogate Magistrates' Court, where he was due to appear on charges of exposure, outraging public decency and shop theft](https://www.harrogateadvertiser.co.uk/webimg/b25lY21zOjk1MjIwOWQxLTQwMzUtNGJjYS04ZWE3LTM1ODI1NDU0MDdmYjo5NTM0NTliYS1hYWUyLTQwZmMtYjAzZC0xNzBiNmU3MzI0MWM=.jpg?crop=3:2,smart&trim=&width=640&quality=65)



A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: “Extensive enquiries are ongoing to locate Steel and we’re now appealing for your help to help find him.

“We believe he could still be in Harrogate.”

If you see him, or have any information about his current whereabouts, you should call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

If the sighting is immediate, you should call 999.

Alternatively if you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or by visiting [https://crimestoppers-uk.org/](https://crimestoppers-uk.org/)

You should quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12260118291 when providing any details regarding his whereabouts.
