## Thieves target woman in her 90s in York city centre

North Yorkshire Police has launched a CCTV appeal after a woman in her 90s was followed and stolen from *(Image: North Yorkshire Police)*

THIEVES have stolen from a shopper in her 90s in York city centre.

North Yorkshire Police said the victim was followed from Lloyds Bank into Marks & Spencer in Parliament Street on Friday (June 12).

A force spokesperson said: “Once inside, she was approached by a group of people, who took cash from her.

“The victim only realised the money was missing when she went to pay for the items she had picked up.

"She was understandably left very distressed and upset.”
