Celebrity chef James Martin showcased his impressive weight loss transformation at an awards ceremony last week.

James, who hails from Yorkshire, stepped out with his fiancée Kim Johnson at the TRIC Awards on Tuesday, 23 June, and showed off his three-stone weight loss.

The chef showed up to the awards show wearing a tailored grey suit and is noticeably slimmer.

The couple were photographed together on the red carpet at the Grosvenor House Hotel in London.


## James Martin’s weight loss journey

He spoke about his decision to start losing weight on the Spooning with Mark Wogan podcast.

He said: “It comes down to the fact that I race cars, or I still try and race a few cars and I actually struggle to get out of them now.

“Getting in them, you kind of fall in them, but then you've got to get out of them and it just doesn't look very good.

“I've decided that I maybe need to stay clear away from these [picks up a Twix bar] and stuff like that and maybe eat fish, and it's worked.”


In the past, James has shared meals that he has been eating during his weight loss journey, including a two-ingredient breakfast that he describes as “simple and very tasty”.

He told Hello! Magazine that grilled tomatoes on toast was one of his favourite dishes to eat during his weight loss journey, alongside his barbecued leeks with lardons and hazelnuts.

### James Martin’s skin cancer diagnosis

In 2018, the chef was diagnosed with facial skin cancer that left him in “debilitating pain”.

He went through multiple surgeries and said the cancer had returned on “several occasions” meaning he had to go through further treatments.
