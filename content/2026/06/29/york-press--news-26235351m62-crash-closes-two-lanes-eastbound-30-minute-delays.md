A CRASH on a major North Yorkshire motorway caused delays of up to 30 minutes this morning.

Highways England said traffic on the eastbound M62 between junctions 34 and 45 was being held after the incident which was first reported around 7am (Monday, June 29).

The lane closures were between the junctions for the A19 and Selby, and the southbound M18.

The government agency's traffic cameras appeared to show a vehicle in lane three of the motorway.

Two lanes were reported as closed - Highways England has since confirmed traffic is moving freely in both directions in the area.

The Press has contacted North Yorkshire's fire and ambulance services and will update this breaking story here when we receive more information.
