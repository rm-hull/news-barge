A 33-year-old man from Knaresborough is wanted on recall to prison and in connection with an assault.

Robert Johnson, who is being sought by North Yorkshire Police, is believed to be in the York, Harrogate, or Knaresborough area.

  **Recommended reading:**

He is described by police as around 6ft tall, of skinny build, with short, dark, receding hair.

Anyone who sees him or knows where he is should call North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference 12260109566.

For immediate sightings, call 999.

Information can also be given anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
