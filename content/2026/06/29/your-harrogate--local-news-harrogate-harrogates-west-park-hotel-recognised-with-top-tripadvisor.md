                                    Harrogate's West Park Hotel has been recognised among the world's top-rated hospitality businesses after receiving a Tripadvisor Travellers' Choice Award.

The annual award places the hotel in the top 10% of listings worldwide, based on consistently positive reviews and ratings from guests on Tripadvisor.

The boutique hotel, overlooking the Stray, has welcomed guests since 2013 and is part of the Provenance Collection, which also operates nearby venues including The Coach & Horses in Harrogate, alongside a number of hotels and inns across North Yorkshire.

A spokesperson said:


"We're honoured to have been recognised with a Tripadvisor Travellers' Choice Award.

"The West Park Hotel has been the heart of Harrogate stays since 2013, and moments like this remind us why we love what we do.

"Thank you to every guest who took the time to share their experience."


To celebrate the success, Provenance Inns is offering 26% off for the first 26 guests to book a Stray Suite, Penthouse or Apartment using the code: TRIPADVISOR26.
