# North Yorkshire Police launch investigation after car damaged in supermarket car park in Ripon

## North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information and witnesses after a car was damaged in the car park of a supermarket in Ripon.

The incident happened between 11pm on Wednesday 17 and 7pm on Thursday 18 June, when a silver car was damaged in the car park to the rear of Sainsbury’s on Market Place.

If you saw anything, have any CCTV or dashcam footage, or have any information that could help, you should get in contact with the police.

You can also call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Alternatively, if you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or by visiting [https://crimestoppers-uk.org/](https://crimestoppers-uk.org/)

You should quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12260113154 when providing any details regarding the incident.
