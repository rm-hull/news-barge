## Fast food chain confirms fan-favourite US item hits UK menus this week

Taco Bell is adding Baja Blast to its UK menus from June 30 *(Image: Getty)>*

A popular US drink is finally launching in the UK after years of fans campaigning.

Taco Bell UK has confirmed that Mountain Dew Baja Blast, a tropical lime-flavoured soft drink that has gained a loyal following in the US, will be launched nationally from June 30.

It comes after it launched in the fast food chain's Baker Street restaurant in London between June 26 and 28.

## Taco Bell fans finally get US Baja Blast drink after years of campaigning

Fans in the UK have shown growing interest in the beverage, with Google searches for Baja Blast rising by 40% over the past five years, reports Creatorzine.

More than six years ago, supporters even launched a Change.org petition urging Taco Bell to introduce Baja Blast to the UK market.

The petition reads: "As Taco Bell continues to open stores across the UK, one thing has been mentioned by many Baja Blast fans, yet still, we are unable to get the sweet and delicious Baja Blast here in the UK."

It adds: "If you have not yet had the chance to try Baja Blast, I promise you this petition is worth signing, and you will understand upon tasting the sweet nectar that is Baja Blast."

One supporter commented: "My friend who lives in Europe really wants to try it and its way too good not to try at least once so yeah."


The drink has built up a huge following in the US, with fans regularly sharing their love for it on social media.

One person wrote: "My go to drink."

Another said: "My favourite summer drink!"

A third added: "Yum! Tastes like air conditioning after swimming on a hot summer day."

Lucy Dee, marketing director at Taco Bell UK, said: "Baja Blast has built an incredible following over the years, and we've consistently heard from UK fans asking us to bring it here.
