# Harrogate hospitality venue offers 26% off after being named among world's top-rated hospitality businesses

## A popular Harrogate location for locals and visitors has been named in the world's top 10% of hotels by Tripadvisor in its annual awards.

The West Park Hotel has received a Tripadvisor Travellers' Choice Award based on consistently positive reviews and ratings from guests on Tripadvisor.

Located just across from the Stray in Harrogate's Conservation Area, the boutique hotel, AA Rosette-awarded restaurant and bar has flourished since becoming part of the North Yorkshire-based Provenance Collection in 2012.

A major redevelopment led by an award-winning team of architects from Bowman Riley in 2021 proved a hit, in particular for its new all weather terrace, including a retractable roof, and an outdoor bar.

“We're honoured to have been recognised with a Tripadvisor Travellers' Choice Award,” said a spokesperson for the Provenance Collection, which owns a collection of gastro pubs and boutique hotels in the North, including The Coach & Horses in Harrogate

"Moments like this remind us just how much we love what we do.

"A heartfelt thank you to every guest who took the time to share their experiences online."

Originally opened as a coaching inn in the 19th century, these days the West Park Hotel boasts 25 spacious rooms with an air of indulgence and Hypnos beds wrapped in Egyptian linen.

To say ‘thank you’ for winning the Tripadvisor award, Provenance Inns is offering 26% off for the first 26 guests to book a Stray Suite, Penthouse or Apartment at the West Park by using the code: TRIPADVISOR26
