# Sustainability Win 

            *Published: 29 June 2026*

            Stockwell Community Wellbeing Hub has achieved a big sustainability win - the recent building improvements work and installation of new solar panels led by Knaresborough Town Council’s Climate Change Emergency Committee (funded through North Yorkshire Council’s SPF funding) have boosted the building up to a Grade B efficiency rating. 

  This upgrade means lower energy use, reduced carbon emissions, lower bills, greener energy, and a brighter future for everyone who uses the hub. Proud to see this brilliant community space becoming more sustainable every day.
