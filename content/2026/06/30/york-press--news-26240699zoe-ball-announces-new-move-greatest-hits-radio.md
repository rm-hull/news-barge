Zoe Ball has announced she will be joining Greatest Hits Radio to launch a brand-new show.

The move marks Zoe’s first daily radio show since her Radio 2 Breakfast Show, which she left in 2025.

It will see her host afternoons, following Ken Bruce’s mid-morning show and ahead of Simon Mayo at Drivetime.

Discussing the announcement, Zoe said: “There’s something truly special about radio – the relationship you build with listeners, the energy, the music and the moments of joy and laughter you share together.


"I’ve missed it and this felt like the right time to begin a new chapter with Greatest Hits Radio.

"As a station, Greatest Hits Radio has built something really brilliant – epic music, big personalities and a genuine connection with listeners. I’m excited to be joining the team and getting started.”

Gary Stein, director of audio for Bauer Media Audio UK, said: “Zoe is one of the UK’s most talented and best-loved broadcasters.

"She has an extraordinary ability to connect with audiences, a real passion for great music and we’re delighted she’s chosen to join Greatest Hits Radio."

Greatest Hits Radio Content Director, Andy Ashton, said: “I could not be more thrilled to welcome Zoe to the Greatest Hits Radio family.


"The whole team are buzzing that another world class broadcaster will be part of our daytime line-up every weekday.

"Zoe and the team are already working on the new show, and we can’t wait to share it with everyone."

## When will Zoe Ball's new radio show be starting?

Zoe Ball in the Afternoon will air weekdays 1pm-4pm, starting from Monday, September 7.
