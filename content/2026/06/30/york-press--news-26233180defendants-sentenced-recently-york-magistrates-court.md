Nathan Harris, 25, of Carr Lane, York, pleaded guilty to assaulting and strangling a man, strangulation and using threatening or insulting words or behaviour towards another person.

He was given an 18-month community order with 20 days’ rehabilitative activities and 160 hours’ unpaid work and was ordered to pay £200 compensation to the man.

Matthew Hotchin, 22, of Windmill Way, Haxby, pleaded guilty to harassment of a woman and criminal damage to her car.

He was given a 12-month community order with 10 days’ rehabilitative activities and 100 hours’ unpaid work and ordered to pay £80 compensation, £85 prosecution costs and a £114 statutory surcharge.

Frederick Wymark-Hoar, 38, of Fenwick Street, off Bishopthorpe Road, York, pleaded guilty to causing actual bodily harm to a man.

He was given a 12-month prison sentence suspended for 12 months on condition he does 200 hours’ unpaid work. He was ordered to pay £500 compensation.

Dylan Norman, 30, of Wreyfield Drive, Newby, Scarborough, pleaded guilty to assaulting a police officer and failure to attend court.

He was given an 18-month community order with 26 days’ rehabilitative activities and 120 hours’ unpaid work and ordered to pay £85 prosecution costs.
