MOTORISTS have complained of heavy congestion on key roads in and around York this morning.

Traffic is slow on Wigginton Road, heading into York before Haxby Road, which is adding extra time to journeys and affecting surrounding routes.

As reported, one lane was closed for Yorkshire Water repairs to fractured water pipe very close to one of the entrances of York Hospital. Diversion signs are in place to steer traffic away from the closed area where there is a deep hole in the road surface.

There are also queues on the A1237 York Outer Ring Road at Haxby Road roundabout which is delaying motorists.

The A19 Fulford Road and Main Street in York has the usual level of slow traffic while motorists are also crawling on Clifton and Bootham, heading into York city centre and Gillygate.

Drivers are being warned to expect journeys to take longer.
