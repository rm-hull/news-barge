                                    Harrogate's popular 32-metre observation wheel is returning to the town centre this weekend.

The big wheel, operated by WM Attractions Limited, will again be situated on Cambridge Crescent, offering views across the town and surrounding district.

It will open at 11am on Saturday (4th July) and will run daily from 11am to 7pm until Sunday 26th July.

No advance booking is required, with tickets priced at £5 for those under 1.4 metres and £7 for those over 1.4 metres.

![](https://mmo.aiircdn.com/409/6745dd1b5c349.jpg)


The wheel is accessible to some wheelchairs and mobility scooters, depending on the model, and well-behaved dogs may join their owners for the ride.

The wheel will be assembled adjacent to the war memorial in the coming days.

Operations will be weather dependent, and the attraction may close at short notice in adverse conditions.

*The wheel has been co-ordinated by North Yorkshire Council.*
