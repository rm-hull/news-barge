                                    A popular independent interior design retailer is targeting Harrogate for its second location.

Upside Down Design, which has been based on Bootham in York since 2017, has announced plans to open a new store on West Park, next to Boho Chic.

It's expected to offer a range of homeware, furniture, lighting and interior design services.

The business is known for selling a mix of home accessories, gifts, soft furnishings and vintage-inspired pieces, alongside offering bespoke interior design advice.

The company announced plans earlier this month and is currently recruiting five members of staff for the new shop.

An official opening date has now yet been revealed.
