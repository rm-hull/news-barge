A THIEF has targeted a department store in a coastal North Yorkshire town.

The county's force said £50 of stock was stolen from Boyes department store in Station Square in Whitby on Saturday, June 6.

  


North Yorkshire Police issued a CCTV image of a man they wish to speak to in connection with the incident and said he may have information which will assist its investigation.

Members of the public are asked to contact police if they recognise the individual.

Information can be emailed to [joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:joanne.bell@northyorkshire.police.uk), or you can call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

Please quote 12260104382 when passing on information.
