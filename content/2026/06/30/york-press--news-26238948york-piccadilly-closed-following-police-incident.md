A POLICE incident in York city centre has led to the closure of a main road.

The county's force said an approximately 200-metre stretch of Piccadilly between Mill Street and Merchantgate was closed last night (Monday, June 29) over concerns for the welfare of a man.

  



Later in the evening, around 10pm, a North Yorkshire Police spokesperson confirmed a man had was brought to safety and it was arrangingfor him to receive support.

They thanked the public for their understanding and said support is being arranged.
