POLICE are appealing for help identifying a man following an attack in Whitby.

The incident took place in the early hours of Sunday, May 31 at The George Hotel in Baxtergate, Whitby, where a doorman was assaulted in what police described as an unprovoked attack.

  


Officers have released a CCTV image of a man they said they would like to speak to in connection with the incident as he may have information which could assist its investigation.

A North Yorkshire Police spokesperson said: "Please email [ed.chatland@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:ed.chatland@northyorkshire.police.uk) if you recognise him.

"Alternatively, you can call North Yorkshire Police on 101, and ask for PC 1824 Ed Chatland, or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their website."
