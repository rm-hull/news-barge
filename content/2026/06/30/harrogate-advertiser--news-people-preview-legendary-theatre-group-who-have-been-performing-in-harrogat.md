# Preview: Legendary theatre group who have been performing in Harrogate every summer for more than 30 years

## The much-anticipated return of popular favourites Oddsocks to Harrogate RHS Harlow Carr will take place today signalling that summer really is here.

Presented by HACS Harrogate Music Festival, the sumptuous grounds of RHS Harlow Carr will see one of the UK's best-loved touring theatre companies deliver a new take on Shakespeare’s classic tale Romeo & Juliet.

The travelling theatrical troubadours will bring their irresistible mixture of energetic mayhem and family fun today, Tuesday, June 30 and tomorrow, Wednesday, July 1.

Oddsocks’ creative producer Elli Mackenzie said: “Our audience in Harrogate is always so supportive and fun to play to and the beautiful surroundings at RHS Harlow Carr make the experience all the better.

![Oddsocks are here - Presented by HACS Harrogate Music Festival, RHS Harlow Carr will play host to one of the UK's best-loved touring theatre companies deliver a new take on Shakespeare’s classic tale Romeo & Juliet. “The future of our work and live theatre in general relies heavily on organisations such as Harrogate International Festivals who have booked our productions since the early 1990s.

"It’s always a highlight to return.”

In this family‑friendly adaptation of the Bard’s famous play, Oddsocks uncover humour and heartfelt pathos in the unlikeliest of places.

Expect larger‑than‑life characters, playful storytelling and joyful anarchy as the company transforms despair into uplifting comedy.

Formed in 1989 by Andy Barrow and Elli Mackenzie and praised by The Stage for their “great comic timing and superb slapstick”, Oddsocks have been performing as part of Harrogate International Festivals’ summer season for more than 30 years.

Sharon Canavar, Harrogate International Festivals’ Chief Executive, said: “Summer in Harrogate just wouldn’t be the same without an Oddsocks appearance and we’re delighted to welcome them back.

“They have been coming here as part of our summer programme for more than 30 years, bringing joy and laughter to generations of families.

“So pack your picnic, rugs and folding chairs, and get ready for an evening of laughter and mayhem that is suitable for all ages. Just remember to dress appropriately for the British weather and make sure you bring something warm to wear!”

Oddsocks: Romeo & Juliet, RHS Harlow Carr, Beckwithshaw, Harrogate, June 30 and July 1, 7.30pm.

To book tickets, visit the Harrogate International Festivals website at: [https://harrogateinternationalfestivals.com/event/oddsocks-romeo-juliet-2/#book](https://harrogateinternationalfestivals.com/event/oddsocks-romeo-juliet-2/#book)
