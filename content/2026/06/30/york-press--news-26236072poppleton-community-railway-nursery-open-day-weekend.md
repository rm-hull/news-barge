A community railway nursery is opening its doors this weekend.

Poppleton Community Railway Nursery (PCRN) is inviting visitors to its much-anticipated Open Day on Saturday, July 4.

From 10am to 4pm, a variety of plants, herbs, vegetables, shrubs, perennials, alpines, and succulents will be on offer.

Additionally, the nursery's narrow gauge railway will be on display, and there will be an offer of Buy-One-Get-One-Free on all bedding plants.

For those with a penchant for culinary delights, an array of home-made refreshments will be available.

Stalls selling books, bric-a-brac, and a range of homemade crafts promise plenty of shopping opportunities.

The Open Day is not just about leisurely activities, as the nursery, which is managed entirely by volunteers, also welcomes new helpers.

It provides a haven for people with mental health difficulties, working in partnership with local health agencies to deliver therapeutic sessions.

PCRN is open on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays from 10am to 3pm.

Visit poppletonrailwaynursery.co.uk for more details.
