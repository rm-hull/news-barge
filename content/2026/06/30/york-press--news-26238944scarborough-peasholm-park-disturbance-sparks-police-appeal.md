A DISTURBANCE involving a group of youths and a man in a very popular park in the centre of a North Yorkshire town has sparked a police appeal for information.

The incident took place around 8.15pm on Saturday, June 27 in Peasholm Park, Scarborough, between Columbus Ravine and Peasholm Drive.

  



North Yorkshire Police believe the group was involved in the altercation.

Anyone with information is asked to email [daisy.ferguson@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:daisy.ferguson@northyorkshire.police.uk) or call 101.

Crimestoppers can also be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
