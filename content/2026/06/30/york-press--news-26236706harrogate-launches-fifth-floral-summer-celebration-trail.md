## Floral displays to brighten Harrogate town centre this summer

Eight vibrant floral displays are set to bring colour and creativity to Harrogate town centre this summer *(Image: Harrogate BID)*

Eight vibrant floral displays are set to bring colour and creativity to a town centre this summer.

Harrogate BID has announced the return of its Floral Summer of Celebration for a fifth year, running from July 3 to 17.

The project is delivered in partnership with Harrogate International Festivals, sponsored by the Harrogate District Chamber of Commerce and will feature a trail of eye-catching displays across the town centre.

  The project is delivered in partnership with Harrogate International Festivals 

*(Image: Harrogate BID)*

Martin Mann, chief executive of Harrogate District Chamber of Commerce, said: "We’re delighted to support the Harrogate Floral Summer of Celebration once again."

The displays, which aim to encourage people to explore Harrogate, will be installed at the Cenotaph War Memorial, The Yorkshire Hotel, Nicholls Tyreman, Montpellier Hill, Cross James Street, Victoria Shopping Centre, Library Gardens and the Harrogate International Festivals HQ.

  The Floral Summer of Celebration, in its fifth year, runs from July 3 to 17 

*(Image: Harrogate BID)*

Bethany Allen, operations manager at Harrogate BID, said: "We’re incredibly excited to see the Floral Summer of Celebration return to Harrogate town centre for its fifth year."

Sharon Canavar, chief executive of Harrogate International Festivals, added: "We’re delighted to be working alongside Harrogate BID on this year’s Floral Summer of Celebration as we mark 60 years of Harrogate International Festivals."

Harrogate BID plans to enter the project into this year’s Yorkshire in Bloom awards.
