After more than 25 years, the much-loved comedy classic Dinnerladies is returning, this time to the stage in York.

A brand-new adaptation of Victoria Wood’s hit sitcom will tour UK theatres in 2027, launching at York’s Grand Opera House from February 23 to 27.

The tour marks the first project commissioned by the Victoria Wood Estate.

Les Dennis, known for his work on Coronation Street and Only Fools and Horses The Musical, will star as maintenance man Stan.

He said: "I’m so thrilled to be on board with this new production of Victoria Wood’s Dinnerladies."

The Victoria Wood Estate added: "We are delighted to be bringing the joy of Victoria Wood’s much-loved classic Dinnerladies back to the nation after more than 25 years.

"We hope that this new stage adaptation by Beth and Emma Kilcoyne and directed by Sean Foley - featuring a story inspired by the original TV series - will take theatregoers across the UK straight back to the delights of the canteen and characters they grew to love."

Directed by double Olivier Award-winner Sean Foley, the show is adapted by Beth and Emma Kilcoyne and will feature all the beloved characters from the fictional Manchester canteen.

Tickets go on general sale from Friday, July 3.
