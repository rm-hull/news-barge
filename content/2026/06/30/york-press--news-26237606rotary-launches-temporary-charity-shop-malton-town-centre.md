## Pop-up charity shop opens in Malton for one month to support local causes

The Malton and Norton Rotary charity shop opened on June 29 for one month, raising money for local causes *(Image: Supplied)*

A charity shop has opened for one month only in Malton.

The Malton and Norton Rotary charity shop opened on June 29 on the corner of Newbiggin and Spittal Street.

  The shop has a large stock of clothing, accessories, and other goods for sale 

*(Image: Supplied)*

The shop, which was formerly the Malton Hospital shop, will be open every day except Sundays from 10am to 4pm.

The shop has a large stock of clothing, accessories, and other goods for sale, with all proceeds going to support local needs.
