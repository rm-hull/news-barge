George Thomson joined Harrogate from FC United of Manchester in July 2017

**Harrogate Town midfielder George Thomson has signed a new one-year deal.**

The 34-year-old's new contract will see him complete a 10th season with the club.

He has made 335 appearances for the Sulphurites and helped them win promotion from National League North up to League Two.

"Obviously the club means a great deal to me. You don't spend this long somewhere and don't care about it," he told the club website., external
