**'World has gone mad and we are getting more like America'**

THE world has gone mad. We have the free world led by the worst President of The United States of America in 80 years.

China is almost ready to take over as the worlds' top super power and we must keep in touch with them.

At long last, we have awoken to the threats from Russia.

In the UK, trust has almost disappeared. We have no leaders anywhere to respect and look up to in any area.

Without a doubt our best folk are the likes of Sir David Attenborough, Sir Kevin Sinfield, Sir Gareth Southgate, Dame Judi Dench, MP Kim Leadbeater, and Catherine the Princess of Wales.

We can have little or no faith in our church and religious leaders, in lawyers, judges and the British legal system. Scandal follows scandal. Justice is rarely carried out. There is support for the villains but virtually none for the many victims.

We have no meaningful deterrents. We have not enough prisons nor police officers. Our priorities are all upside down.

We have fantastic staff at the coal face of our NHS but it is badly led, underfunded, and has far too many middle managers.

Our armed forces have been cut and cut for years.

There is no incentive in Britain today to be an honest, hard working, decent tax paying citizen. The idle thrive.

We get more like America every week; shameful!

  **David Quarrie,**

  **Lynden Way,**

  **Holgate, York**
