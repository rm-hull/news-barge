## York family home of more than 50 years goes on sale for £675,000

A four-bed detached house in Heworth, York, is up for sale for £675,000 *(Image: Price Independent Estate Agents)*

A detached house in York that has remained in the same family for more than 50 years is up for sale.

Offers are invited over £675,000 for the freehold property in Stockton Lane, Heworth.

The house has four main bedrooms and sits on a plot extending to around 0.26 acres.

Set within a well-established residential area, the house is described in the listing as offering buyers an opportunity to update and personalise, while retaining original and period features.

  Living room with a fireplace and a bay window overlooking the front garden 

*(Image: Price Independent Estate Agents)*

The entrance hall features original tiled flooring and leads into three reception rooms.

The main living room contains a fireplace and a bay window overlooking the front garden and driveway.

There is a second reception room at the rear with sliding doors that open onto the rear garden, intended to provide a connection between indoor and outdoor spaces.

A dedicated dining area flows into the kitchen, which was originally installed in the 1970s, according to the listing.

The kitchen includes storage and worktop space, with a large window offering views over the garden.

  Dining area leading into the kitchen 

*(Image: Price Independent Estate Agents)*

Additional ground-floor features include a shower room and an area described as suitable for utility purposes.

Upstairs, the principal bedroom contains fitted wardrobes and a dressing area situated within a bay window.

Other bedrooms on this floor are described as providing accommodation for family members, guests or for use as a study.

There is an additional room, currently used as a games room, which has access to a balcony and could be utilised as a fifth bedroom, subject to planning.

A first-floor bathroom serves the bedrooms.

  Kitchen with storage, worktops, and garden-facing window 

*(Image: Price Independent Estate Agents)*

The listing highlights potential for further enhancement of this area to suit the needs of a new owner.

Outside, the rear garden is described as mature and includes established trees, planting, and lawned areas.

Parking is provided via a driveway with space for several vehicles.

The property is noted as being within walking distance of York city centre, with local amenities, supermarkets, cafés, and leisure facilities situated nearby.

  Rear garden with established trees, planting and lawn areas 

*(Image: Price Independent Estate Agents)*

Schools regarded as popular are also found in the vicinity, and regular public transport links are available.

According to the listing, homes on Stockton Lane offering this combination of character, outside space and location are considered a rare opportunity.

The sale is offered chain-free, giving prospective buyers potential for a straightforward purchase process.
