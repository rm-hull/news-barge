                                    More than a thousand vintage and classic cars are set to roll into Newby Hall near Ripon next month as the historic estate prepares to host the North's largest classic car show.

The Historic Vehicle Rally, organised by North of England Classics and Pre‑War Automobiles, will take place on Sunday 19 July, 10am–3pm, bringing together an impressive line‑up of vehicles from across the country.

From Aston Martins to Wolseleys, the rally showcases a full spectrum of motoring heritage in Newby’s award‑winning grounds.

Alongside the extensive car displays, visitors can browse a large autojumble, ideal for enthusiasts searching for parts, tools and memorabilia.

Real Markets will also be at the show, offering high‑quality Yorkshire produce, handmade crafts and speciality goods from local makers.

![](https://mmo.aiircdn.com/409/6a43be7120d067d33cb.jpg)


*Tickets include entry to Newby Hall’s renowned gardens and adventure playground - Adults: £16, Children: £10, Family (2 adults + 2 children): £47 and Under‑4s: Free*

*Tickets are available at www.newbyhall.com. Visitors wishing to tour the house can upgrade their ticket once inside the grounds.*
