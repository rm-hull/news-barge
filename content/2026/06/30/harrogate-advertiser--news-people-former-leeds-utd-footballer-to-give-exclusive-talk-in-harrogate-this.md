# Former Leeds Utd footballer to give exclusive talk in Harrogate this week

## Harrogate’s newest and most prominent workspace is to host an exclusive talk this week with a former England footballer to celebrate the FIFA World Cup 2026.

Premium serviced office provider WorkWell, which has enjoyed a strong first year at its Harrogate location, will welcome Leeds United footballer Danny Mills to its offices at Copthall Bridge this Thursday, July 2.

Mills, who earned 19 caps for the England national team and was starting right-back in the five games that England played in the 2002 FIFA World Cup, will share insights from his career playing extremely high-level professional football.

Also well known for being a football pundit on radio and TV, Mills will discuss his unique and personal experiences competing internationally at his sport, drawing on the leadership, mindset and team dynamics required to achieve such success in his field.

Attendees at the event at 4pm will gain valuable knowledge about how to overcome setbacks, work efficiently as a team and perform successfully under high pressure environments all while learning about football at the most exciting time to be a fan.

Following the talk, guests will have the opportunity to take part in an interactive Q&A session before networking with fellow attendees and business leaders over drinks and refreshments.

Places at this exclusive event are strictly limited.
