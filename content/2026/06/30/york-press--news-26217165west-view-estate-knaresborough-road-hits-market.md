A country estate made up of two restored period homes and a contemporary annexe is listed for sale in Little Ribston, North Yorkshire.

West View Estate, in Knaresborough Road, is on the market with a guide price of £1,150,000.

The estate includes two period residences and a separate annexe.

  Reception room with exposed beams and original fireplace 

*(Image: Preston Baker)*

All the properties share a private courtyard at the social heart of the estate.

Both houses are described as having period features, such as exposed beams and original fireplaces.

The principal residence, called West View, includes reception rooms, a kitchen, and two bedrooms.

A second house, West View Barn, offers fully independent living space for family, guests, or those seeking separate accommodation within single ownership.

  Kitchen within the principal residence 

*(Image: Preston Baker)*

A contemporary detached annexe stands within the estate.

Recently refurbished, it has open-plan living space, a modern kitchen, and bi-fold doors that open onto the courtyard.

According to the listing, the annexe may suit use as guest accommodation or for income generation.

  Main bathroom space at the property 

*(Image: Preston Baker)*

The estate’s outbuildings are described as adaptable, providing options for fitness, hobbies, storage, or creative purposes.

A garden room with shower facilities could be used as a home office, guest suite, or for holiday letting.

Paths lead from the main courtyard to a “secret garden”, which features a pond.

Additional garden areas include vegetable beds, asparagus plots, as well as mature fruit trees and soft fruit bushes.

  Garden with pond, vegetable beds, asparagus plots and fruit trees 

*(Image: Preston Baker)*

Parking is available off the road, and a roof has recently been installed, with completion stated as 2025 in the listing.

The description states the site may be well suited to multi-generational living, guest accommodation, holiday letting, or those seeking to generate income from letting or a home business.
