# North Yorkshire Police launch investigation after PlayStation 5 stolen from property in Harrogate

## North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information and witnesses after a games console was stolen during a burglary in Harrogate.

The incident happened at 9.30am on Sunday (June 28) on Woodfield Drive, where an unknown offender entered an insecure property and stole a PlayStation 5.

A spokesperson at North Yorkshire Police said: “We’re particularly appealing for information about the incident, including CCTV from the area, and from anyone who may have witnessed suspicious activity around the time of the incident.”

You should quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12260120795 when providing any details regarding the incident.
