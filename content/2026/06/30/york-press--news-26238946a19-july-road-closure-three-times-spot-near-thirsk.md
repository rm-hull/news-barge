A MAJOR road in North Yorkshire is set for daytime closures for roadworks twice in July.

The A19 will be shut for road surface dressing close at crossroads for the village of Bagby and for Moor End Lane, near Thirsk and the major junction with the A168 for the A1(M) and A170 for Pickering and Scarborough.

  


There will also be an overnight closure in the same month in the same area.


North Yorkshire Council provided the following information about the road closures:

Thursday, July 2 - closed from 9.30am to 3.30pm for surface dressing

Friday, July 10 - closed from 9.30am to 3.30pm for road surface treatment

Tuesday, July 21 - closed for stud reinstatement

It is not known if diversions will be put in place during the closure.
