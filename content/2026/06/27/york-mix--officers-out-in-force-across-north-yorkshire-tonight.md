For England’s game tonight, police officers will be increasing patrols across North Yorkshire to help keep everyone safe.

Officers will be covering busy town and city centres as well as rural routes. They say their focus is on preventing incidents before they happen through high-visibility patrols and targeted operations.

59 people have been arrested in connection with drink or drug driving offences since England’s first match.

They say they will take robust action against anyone found driving under the influence.
