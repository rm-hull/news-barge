It is a celebration to commemorate the service of the people within the British Armed Forces. Events will commence from 11am, ending at 5pm.

The South Bay area of the town will be filled with marching bands, parades, exhibitions and will also provide a perfect backdrop for the many air displays planned.

PCSO Jeni Loosley will be leading our Police Cadets.

If you are travelling in for the event at Scarborough Open Air Theatre tonight with Richard Ashcroft, plan for delays as there are several road closures as part of the Armed Forces Day.
