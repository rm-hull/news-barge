Officers were in the Haxby area last. night, following reports of Anti – Social Behaviour surrounding the CO-OP and the Ethel Ward Playing field.

They are asking people to report instances of such behaviour and any other offences that may have occurred via 999 or 101. Where possible try to obtain images of anyone causing ASB.

They say they value the communities we serve and by obtaining the identities of anyone causing Anti – Social Behaviour we can better tailor our response.
