A new funding scheme is offering Yorkshire SMEs the chance to secure a no-strings-attached cash boost.

One Stop Business Finance (OSBF) has launched the OSBF Opportunity Grant, which will award a single SME £5,000 to support business growth, tackle challenges, or unlock new opportunities.

The funding does not need to be repaid.

Andrew Mackenzie, managing director of OSBF, said: "It's about backing a business we believe in and demonstrating the difference that support can make.

"Sometimes the gap between standing still and moving forward is smaller than people think.

"It can be a piece of equipment, a new hire, a marketing campaign, or simply having the confidence to take the next step."

Designed to champion business potential over risk, the grant is intended to help recipients realise ambitions that might otherwise remain out of reach.

Applicants must explain how they would use the funding and share their future plans.

Mr Mackenzie added: "We spend a lot of time talking about risk in business.

"Not enough time talking about potential.

"If you spend time speaking to business owners, you'll find no shortage of ambition and determination.

"What many are struggling with is the environment they're operating in.

"We're not looking for the biggest business or the most polished application.

"We're looking for ambition, potential, and a clear sense of purpose.

"We want to hear from the people behind the business and understand what £5,000 could help them achieve."

The grant is open exclusively to SMEs based in Yorkshire, with applications open now until July 17, 2026.

Businesses can apply by emailing [grant@osbf.co.uk.](mailto:grant@osbf.co.uk.)

OSBF was founded in 2014 and has spent more than a decade supporting SMEs through periods of growth, change, and uncertainty.
