Due to the high temperatures running into the weekend, North Yorks Moor Railway have made the following changes for Saturday, 27th June and are subject to change on Sunday, 28th June too:

Most services between Pickering and Grosmont will be diesel-hauled and steam-hauled services will run on the lower-risk Grosmont to Whitby section.

This is to protect our natural surroundings and for the comfort and safety of our staff, volunteers and visitors.
