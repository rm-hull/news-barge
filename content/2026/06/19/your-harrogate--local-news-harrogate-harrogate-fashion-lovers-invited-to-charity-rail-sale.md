                                    Harrogate residents are being invited to enjoy an evening of pre-loved fashion at a special fundraising event supporting Artizan International.

Positive Influence Agency and Artizan International have joined forces to host a Rail Sale at Artizan Café on Wednesday 22nd July.

Guests will be able to browse high-quality pre-loved clothing and accessories from a range of local sellers, while enjoying drinks and light refreshments from the café.

All ticket sales from the event will be donated directly to Artizan International, helping to fund the charity's work supporting adults with learning disabilities through training, employment opportunities and meaningful community connection.

Chelsea Talbot, Founder of Positive Influence Agency, said:

"This event is about bringing people together while supporting an incredible local charity.

"Whether you're shopping for a wardrobe refresh, selling pieces you no longer wear, or simply coming along to enjoy the atmosphere, every ticket purchased helps support the life-changing work Artizan does with adults with learning disabilities.

"We're really looking forward to welcoming the community for a feel-good evening with purpose at its heart."

Artizan Café is a training café in the heart of Harrogate, supporting over 30 young adults with learning disabilities each week to develop workplace skills, confidence and independence through real-life experience in hospitality and customer service.

Liz Cluderay, Chief Executive Officer of Artizan International, said:

"We’re thrilled to be working alongside Positive Influence Agency on this exciting community event.

"At Artizan, we are committed to helping adults with learning disabilities and Neurodiversity to gain new skills, grow in confidence, and strengthen their sense of belonging within the local community.

"Events such as this play an important role in generating essential support for our programmes, while also creating opportunities for people to come together and share positive experiences.

"We look forward to welcoming guests to the café and making it a truly memorable evening.”

**Tickets**

The Rail Sale offers two ticket options. Shopping tickets are available for £5, while those wishing to sell their own pre-loved clothing and accessories can book a rail space for £15.

Due to limited venue capacity, organisers are encouraging people to book early.

The event takes place on Wednesday 22nd July at Artizan Café, Harrogate.

Rail sellers can set up from 6pm, with shopping ticket holders welcomed from 6.30pm. The event will conclude at approximately 9pm.

_Tickets are available via the Positive Influence Agency website, or by scanning the QR code displayed on event posters within Artizan Café._
