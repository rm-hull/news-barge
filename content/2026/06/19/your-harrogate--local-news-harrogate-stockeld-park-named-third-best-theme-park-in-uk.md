Stockeld Park has been named the third best theme park in the UK in Tripadvisor's latest Traveller Favourites rankings.

The attraction near Wetherby secured a place in the national top three based on visitor reviews and ratings left on Tripadvisor.

The park has more than 9,000 reviews on the platform and currently holds a 4.6-star rating.

Stockeld Park is home to its Enchanted Forest, adventure playgrounds, giant maze, go-karts and the Playhive indoor play centre, attracting families from across Yorkshire and beyond.

The park was ranked only behind Alton Towers Resort and Thorpe Park.

A spokesperson for Stockeld Park said:

"This is even more special to us as it's based on public reviews, so we'd like to say a massive thank you to everyone who chooses to spend their hard-earned money visiting us and everyone who takes the time to leave a review.

"If you haven't visited yet, come try it for yourself! We're open weekends and local school holidays for epic family days out."


The top 10 UK theme parks as revealed by Tripadvisor are:

- Alton Towers Resort
- Thorpe Park
- **Stockeld Park**
- Paultons Park
- Legoland Windsor Resort
- Blackpool Pleasure Beach
- Heights of Abraham
- Drayton Manor Theme Park
- Chessington World of Adventures Resort
- West Midlands Safari Park

Stockeld Park is currently open on weekends and during local school holidays.
