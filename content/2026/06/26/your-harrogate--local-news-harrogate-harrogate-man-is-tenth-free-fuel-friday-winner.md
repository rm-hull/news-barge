                                    Brandon from Harrogate is the tenth winner of Your Harrogate's Free Fuel Friday.

Our weekly competition continued this morning, giving residents the chance to have their tank filled for free.

Clues were given on the radio and on social media to hint at where the Your Harrogate team would be after 10am.

And Brandon was the first to find Ricky Durkin, AKA the Free Fuel Fairy, and utter the phrase that pays:


'Are you Your Harrogate's Free Fuel Fairy?'

Today’s secret location was the Shell petrol station on Ripon Road in Harrogate.

Brandon took advantage of an £80 tank full of fuel and said with the money he has saved, is off out to celebrate a friend's birthday.

Could you be next week's Free Fuel Friday winner? Just follow the clues next week and be the first to find our Free Fuel Fairy.
