Fire crews from Richmond and Reeth responded to a report of a chimney that had been struck by lightning.

It happened just after 4pm at****Marrick, near Richmond.

This caused the chimney to fill with smoke and filled the residential property with smoke as a result.

Crews used two breathing apparatus and 1 hose reel jet, premises taped off for safety precautions.
