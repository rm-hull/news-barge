                                    One of Harrogate's longest-established restaurants is marking a major milestone this year as The Orchid celebrates a quarter of a century in business.

The award-winning pan-Asian restaurant, based within the Studley Hotel on Swan Road, has become a familiar part of Harrogate’s hospitality scene since opening its doors in 2001.

Situated in one of the town’s most historic quarters, the restaurant sits close to several of Harrogate’s best-known landmarks, including the Mercer Art Gallery, Royal Pump Room Museum and the Old Swan Hotel.

Its menu draws inspiration from across Asia, featuring dishes influenced by the culinary traditions of Thailand, Vietnam, Singapore, Hong Kong and China.

![](https://mmo.aiircdn.com/409/6a329b2c03fd2c15ecb.jpg)


To celebrate reaching the quarter of a century milestone, The Orchid is offering diners 20% off its à la carte menu from Monday to Thursday throughout June.
