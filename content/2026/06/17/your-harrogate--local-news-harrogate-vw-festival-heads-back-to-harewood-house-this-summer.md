                                    One of the UK's biggest Volkswagen events is set to return to Harewood House this summer.

VW Festival will take place at Harewood House near Leeds from Friday 8th to Sunday 10th August, bringing together thousands of VW enthusiasts, families and visitors for a weekend packed with entertainment.

The festival is celebrating its 21st anniversary and remains one of the country's largest family-friendly Volkswagen shows.

Visitors can expect hundreds of display vehicles and VW club exhibits from across the UK, alongside live music, family activities, shopping stalls, street food and bars.

![](https://mmo.aiircdn.com/409/6a32c1be4b4f65687f1.jpg)


A popular fancy dress competition will also return, with festival-goers of all ages encouraged to get involved.

Day visitors will be able to enjoy access to Harewood House, its gardens and adventure playground as part of their ticket during normal opening hours.
