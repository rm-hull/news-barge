                                    A new Italian restaurant and deli is set to open in Knaresborough tomorrow (Friday).

Vomero will officially open its doors at 12pm in the town centre.

The new venue will operate as an Italian salumeria and deli during the day, serving fresh paninis and coffee, before transforming into an Italian trattoria in the evening, offering pizza, pasta and wine.

The restaurant will be based at Castle Courtyard within Knaresborough's market place.

Castle Courtyard is home to a range of independent businesses and eateries in the former Old Town Hall building.

A spokesperson for Vomero said:

"We can’t wait to welcome you and to become part of this amazing community!

"A daytime deli (perfect for a fresh panini and great coffee) and by night, a cozy Italian trattoria with pizza, pasta and wine!

"We're eagerly looking forward to seeing everyone and raising our glasses together!"


The restaurant will open from 12pm and will accept both advance bookings and walk-in customers.
