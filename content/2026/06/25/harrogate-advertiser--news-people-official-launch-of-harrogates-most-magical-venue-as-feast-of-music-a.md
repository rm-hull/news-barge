# Official launch of Harrogate's 'most magical venue' as feast of music and entertainment begins

## The magical Spiegeltent Festival has been launched today in Harrogate as a sizzling summer programme of rock, disco, burlesque and more gets under way.

The Spiegeltent Festival 2026 – in association with Ogden of Harrogate, one of the country’s best known family jewellers – will run from today to July 4 with an exciting line-up curated by award-winning jazz saxophonist and composer Camilla George.

Located in Crescent Gardens, the vintage handcrafted, mobile entertainment venue made of wood, canvas, bevelled mirrors, and stained glass has been brought to the town by Harrogate International Festivals, which is celebrating its 60th anniversary.

This extraordinary venue will play host to everything from crowdpleasing Leeds indie band Hope & Social to renowned sitar duo Ayan Sengupta and Srijanee Banerjee, not to forget the House of Burlesque, a Silent Disco and Jimmy’s Night of 1990s nostalgia.

Ogden of Harrogate, known for its stunning collection of fine jewellery and distinguished clients, is back as Spiegeltent sponsors.

The James Street shop, now run by Ben Ogden, has a long affiliation with Harrogate International Festivals.

The current owner of the famous jewellery business is a direct descendant of founder James Ogden, who first opened it in 1893.

Within a few years it moved into its present location going on to attract a global clientele including such iconic figures as Winston Churchill, Cary Grant, Audrey Hepburn and Madonna.

Ben Ogden said: “We are proud of our association with the Spiegeltent Festival.

"It’s a real gem that brings together so many talented and diverse performers to Harrogate.

“We’re looking forward to seeing some of the dazzling array of artists on stage and helping the Festivals celebrate its 60th anniversary”

Among the other exciting names appearing this summer at Harrogate’s Spiegeltent will be the brilliant Cut A Shine, with their rip-roaring ceilidh, and rising jazz sensation Lucy-Anne Daniels.

Sharon Canavar, Chief Executive of Harrogate International Festivals, said: “It’s fantastic to see the much-loved Spiegeltent back and we’re thrilled that Ogden of Harrogate is back as the sponsor.

“As an arts charity, we rely on support from fantastic businesses like Ogden’s.”
