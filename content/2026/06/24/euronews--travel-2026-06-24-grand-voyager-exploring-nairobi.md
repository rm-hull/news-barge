## From roaming wildlife to luxury hidden retreats, Nairobi is a destination on every traveller's bucket list. But what is it about Kenya that makes it so unique?

Kenya is a country known throughout the world for its incredible wildlife, rich culture and fertile farmland. It's also becoming an increasingly popular destination for travellers.

In this episode of Grand Voyager, Lilly Douse explores some of Nairobi’s most iconic locations, along with a few hidden gems.

Nairobi is one of the few places in the world where visitors can experience wildlife so close to a major city. The Nairobi National Park is one such place, famous for its big game animals, which roam freely throughout the area.

Lilly begins her journey here, exploring the rugged landscape and seeing the animals up close. Her adventure continues in the nearby Nairobi Animal Orphanage, where she learns about rescued wildlife and how the animals are cared for.

From there, she visits a local farm, discovering the skill and hard work behind Nairobi’s famous coffee industry

Along the way, Lilly experiences Nairobi’s creative side, meeting the people behind handmade crafts inspired by Keyan’s tribal traditions.

It's a journey filled with wildlife, creativity, and experiences that feel uniquely Kenyan.
