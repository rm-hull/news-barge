Looking for an exciting way to keep your children active, entertained and making new friends this summer?

We’ve teamed up with The Web Adventure to give one lucky family the chance to WIN TWO FREE DAYS for TWO children at The Web Adventure OFSTED Registered Holiday Club during the summer holidays!

Whether you need a little extra childcare support or simply want your children to enjoy two unforgettable days packed with fun, adventure and new experiences, this fantastic prize is the perfect summer treat.

**How does it work?**

The Web Adventure Holiday Club offers children a safe, engaging and action-packed environment where they can play, learn and explore. Designed to keep children active and entertained throughout the holidays, every day is filled with exciting activities and opportunities to make new friends.

**What makes The Web Adventure Holiday Club special?**

- OFSTED Registered Holiday Club
- DBS-checked staff with recommended child-to-staff ratios
- Dedicated nursery-based location away from public access
- Private Tipi and outdoor play area
- Access to Forest School and Art Shed activities
- Private indoor play sessions at The Web
- Private laser tag sessions
- Supervised access to play areas and fairground attractions
- Safe, fun and engaging experiences every day

Holiday Club Details:

Standard Day: 9am – 4pm (£45 per child)

Extended Hours Available:

8am – 9am and 4pm – 6pm (£2.50 per hour)

Sibling Discount:

5% per child (pre-paid bookings only)

Food:

Lunch and tea available when pre-booked (£10 for both meals)

Bookings are limited and demand is expected to be high, so early booking is recommended.
