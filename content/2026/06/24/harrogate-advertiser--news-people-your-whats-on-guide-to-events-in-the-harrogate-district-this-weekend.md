# Your what’s on guide to events in the Harrogate district this weekend and into July

## If you are planning to go out in the Harrogate district this weekend or in the next month or two, here are the shows, gigs and comedy not to miss:

Until Sept 6: Aesthetica Art Prize 20: Defining the Future of Contemporary Part II: Perception at Mercer Gallery, Harrogate.

Thursday, June 25-27, 7.45pm: Harrogate Dramatic Society presents The Dresser by Ronald Harwood in Harrogate Studio Theatre. Also 2pm.

Thursday, June 25, 7.30pm: Billy the Bard: An evening of Music, Muse, Myth & Mirth at Frazer Theatre, Knaresborough.

Thursday, June 25-27, 7,30pm: The Performance Ensemble present Sinfonia: Stories From Harrogate at Harrogate Theatre. Also 2.30pm.

Saturday, June 27, 7.30pm: Cuore Chamber Orchestra's Grand Season Finale concert at the Royal Hall, Harrogate.

Saturday, June 27, 3pm: Harrogate Choral Society present Voyage Spirituel at St Wilfrid's Church.

Saturday, June 27, 7pm: Ripley Live presents The Diamonds & The Doghouse Doctors in the garden at Ripley Town Hall. Plus England Wold Cup match.

Saturday, June 27, 7.30pm: Chequered Past at The Bilton Club, Skipton Road, Harrogate.

Saturday, June 27, 10am: Teas, lunches and homemade cakes until 4pm at Fewston Parochial Hall. All proceeds to Friends of Birstwith School. Also Sunday.

Sunday, June 28, 4pm: Generation Dance presents Lights, Camera, Dance – Generation Dance at the Royal Hall, Harrogate.

Sunday, June 28, 2pm: Band stand concert with The Cherry Tones at the Band Stand at the Recreation Ground, Pateley Bridge.

Thursday, July 2, 7.30pm: Uncle Frank presents This is Disco at Harrogate Theatre.

Thursday, July 2, 7pm: KS3 Performing Arts showcase presents Spotlight at the Royal Hall, Harrogate.

Thursday, June, 9pm: Live music with Hot Sauce at the Blues Bar, Harrogate.

Friday, July 3, 7.30pm: Copacabana Magic - The Barry Manilow Tribute Show at the Royal Hall, Harrogate.

Friday, July 3, 7.30pm: Buddy Holly and The Cricketers at Harrogate Theatre.

Friday, June 26, 9pm: Live music with Brain Fever at the Blues Bar, Harrogate.

Saturday, July 4, 4pm: Summer Sessions with Ted Key + VIPS at The Bilton Club, Skipton Road, Harrogate.

Saturday, July 4, 11.30am: Little Stages Festival series presents My First Concert at Harrogate Theatre.

Sunday, June 28, 9pm: Live music with Drew Neiren Hall at the Blues Bar, Harrogate.

Tuesday, July 7, 7pm: Harrogate Youth Theatre presents Monday Night Live, a brand-new sketch comedy show, at Harrogate Theatre. Also 8pm.

Wednesday, July 8, 7.30pm: Vinyl Sessions presents Thin Lizzy’s Greatest Hits for Harrogate Hospital Special Care Baby Unit at Starling Independent Bar, Café & Kitchen. Harrogate.

Thursday, July 9, 7.30pm: Bootleg Blondie the official number one tribute to Debbie Harry and Blondie at Harrogate Theatre.

Friday, July 10, 8pm: Alfie Moore: Fair Cop Radio Warm-Up at Frazer Theatre, Knaresborough.

Friday, July 10, 7pm: The Mercians live at The Bilton Club, Harrogate.

Saturday, July 11, 7.30pm: The Moondogs live at The Bilton Club, Harrogate.

Saturday, July 11, 8pm: Hyena Lounge Comedy Club presents Hal Cruttenden, Paul Thorne, Nick Doody and Jon Pearson at Harrogate Theatre.

Saturday, July 11, 7.30pm: Ripley Live presents Sarah Collins and Keep The Faith at Ripley Town Hall.

Saturday, July 11, 7.30pm: R.E.M. Songs by Candlelight at Frazer Theatre, Knaresborough.

Sunday, July 12, 4pm: Vocalis summer concert Deep River at St Wilfrid’s Church, Duchy Road, Harrogate.

Friday, July 17, 7.30pm: Back Out Of Hell - The Ultimate Meat Loaf tribute show featuring Peter Young with a full live band at Frazer Theatre, Knaresborough.
