# Award-winning Harrogate hotel is to offer a 'brand new way to play and enjoy golf' this Yorkshire Day

## An award-winning Harrogate hotel is to launch a ‘first for Yorkshire’ this summer with a brand new way to play and enjoy golf.

Rudding Park luxury hotel, spa, and golf resort, which holds an official AA 4-Star rating as a hotel and boasts a prestigious 1 MICHELIN Star for its Fifty Two restaurant, is to introduce an exciting new initiative for anyone who likes golf or, even, those who thought they didn’t.

Described as active, fun and very social, Gimme Golf will start at Rudding Park this Yorkshire Day on Saturday, August 1.

Like padel is to tennis, Gimme Golf offers the chance for a new whole experience beyond the normal angst of competitive sport.

Be the first to know, and be among first to play, by putting your details at: www.ruddingpark.com/golf/gimme

Rudding Park’s golf offer currently boasts the 18 hole Hawtree Course, the Trackman Driving Range and the six hole Repton Short Course.

Its fully inclusive Golf Academy is currently undergoing a ‘make-over’ to bring an ever better service for golfers and visitors.

As part of the project, certain areas of the Golf Academy have been closed temporarily, including the Golf Academy Shop which is expected to reopen on Friday, July 17.

Rudding Park’s owners, the Mackaness family, recently revealed long-term redevelopment plans to construct a five-star members country club and updated golf facilities aimed for completion by 2030.

A ‘gimme’ in golf is a short putt that a player's opponent or playing partners concede.
