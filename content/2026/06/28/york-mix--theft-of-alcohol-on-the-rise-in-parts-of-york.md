Police have issued a CCTV image of a woman they would like to speak to, following two shop thefts in York.

Both happened on the 16/05/26, at the SPAR store on Brockfield Park Drive, York and involved the theft of alcohol.

Please contact police if you recognise the woman pictured on CCTV, as she may have information that will assist our investigation.
