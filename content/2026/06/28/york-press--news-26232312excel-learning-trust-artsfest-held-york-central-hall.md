## Students wow audiences at York showcase of music, dance and drama

Students from schools across the Excel Learning Trust were celebrated in a showcase of dance, music, and drama at York's Central Hall *(Image: Excel Learning Trust)*

Young performers in York have been praised for lighting up the stage with their talent and creativity.

Students from schools across the Excel Learning Trust were celebrated for their talent and teamwork in a showcase of dance, music, and drama at York's Central Hall.

The University of York venue hosted the 2026 ArtsFest on Thursday, June 25.

  The University of York venue hosted the 2026 ArtsFest on Thursday, June 25 

*(Image: Excel Learning Trust)*

Clare Hardy, associate director of personal development at Excel Learning Trust, said: "I'm so proud of everyone who took part, not only for their amazing effort on the night, but also for their hard work and commitment in the lead-up to Arts Fest.

"Huge thanks go to those colleagues in all our schools who have worked so hard to get the children ready for show night, our supporters and partners who made the evening possible, and of course, our pupils who were the stars of the show."

Mark Hassack, CEO of the trust, called the event a defining moment for the community.

  ArtsFest aims to enrich the curriculum through creative experiences 

*(Image: Excel Learning Trust)*

He said: "Seeing our young people perform on such a grand stage with absolute confidence, talent, and passion was truly awe-inspiring.

"Moments like these are what make us so proud of our schools."

ArtsFest aims to enrich the curriculum through creative experiences.
