Bruce Falkingham, 88, of Newhay Lane, Cliffe, Selby, pleaded guilty to speeding in a 40mph speed limit on the A63 in Hemingbrough.

He was banned from driving for six months, fined £40 and ordered to pay £120 prosecution costs and a £16 statutory surcharge.

Simon Soumati, 50, of Amberley Street, Holgate, York, pleaded guilty to speeding in a van in a 50mph speed limit on the A19 Easingwold bypass.

He was banned from driving for six months, fined £115 and ordered to pay £120 prosecution costs and a £46 statutory surcharge.

Lee Fitton, 55, of Low Marishes, near Malton, pleaded guilty to speeding in a 60mph speed limit on the A64 eastbound near Castle Howard.

He was banned from driving for six months, fined £40 and ordered to pay £120 prosecution costs and a £16 statutory surcharge.

Lee Smith-Swallow, 52, of Town End Avenue, Carlton near Selby, failed to respond to a summons and was convicted in his absence of failure to inform police who was driving his car when it was allegedly committing a motoring offence.

He was banned from driving for six months, fined £660 and ordered to pay £120 prosecution costs and a £264 statutory surcharge.
