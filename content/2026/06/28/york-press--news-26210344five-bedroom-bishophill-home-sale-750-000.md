## Inside ‘truly remarkable’ home on the market in prime York location – for £750,000

Outside at the five-bedroom home for sale on the corner of Cromwell Road and Baile Hill Terrace for £750,000 *(Image: Churchills/Zoopla)*

A “truly remarkable” home has hit the market in a prime York location – for £750,000.

The five-bedroom end-of-terrace home is for sale on the corner of Cromwell Road and Baile Hill Terrace in Bishophill.

Churchills, which is marketing the property, said the sale is a “tremendous opportunity” to purchase a “truly remarkable property” with views of the York City Walls and Minster.

  Inside the five-bedroom home for sale on the corner of Cromwell Road and Baile Hill Terrace for £750,000 

*(Image: Churchills/Zoopla)*

A spokesperson for the estate agent said it is a “well cared for” family-sized home with an “abundance of period features throughout”.

Inside, an entrance hallway leads to a longue with a bay window, as well as a breakfast/dining room.

On the first floor are three double bedrooms and a three-piece bathroom.

Two more double bedrooms are on the second floor, along with a shower room.

Outside, the home has a traditional front and side forecourt, with a lawned courtyard and patio at the rear.
