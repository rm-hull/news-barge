*(Image: North Yorkshire Police)*

Police hunting a paint thief have released a picture of the man they want to speak to.

The image comes from CCTV taken on May 5 at Boyes on Queen Street, Scarborough.

Police believe the man pictured can assist them in their search for the man who stole paint from the store on that day.

If you know who he is email [Ben.Hepworth@northyorkshire.police.uk](mailto:Ben.Hepworth@northyorkshire.police.uk) if you can help.

Alternatively, you can call North Yorkshire Police on 101 and ask for PC 685 HEPWORTH or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

Please quote reference 12260103481 when passing on information.
