A Yorkshire Wolds rapeseed oil business is celebrating a recent triple win.

Based in Thixendale, Charlie & Ivy’s took home top honours for three of its products at the Farm Shop & Deli Product Awards.

The winning products were Truffle Mayonnaise, Truffle Oil, and Lemon, Garlic, Thyme & Balsamic Bread Dipper.

Jason Thompson, who looks after New Product Development at Charlie & Ivy’s, said: "Independent and specialist retailers are some of the main outlets for people to buy Charlie & Ivy’s products, so it’s great news that we have been awarded the highest accolade for products stocked by these retailers, and to get such fabulous feedback.

"We’re also incredibly proud that this is our fourth consecutive gold medal, and our products have only ever won gold in these awards."

The awards recognise and reward the best food and drink products sold in farm shops, delis and other specialist food retailers.
