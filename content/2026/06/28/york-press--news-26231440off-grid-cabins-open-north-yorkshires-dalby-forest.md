## PICTURES - Off-grid cabins open in the heart of popular North Yorkshire forest

The windows aim to make people feel at one with nature *(Image: Provided)*

OFF-GRID cabins have opened their doors in the heart of a popular North Yorkshire forest.

Unyoked, a global nature start-up, has announced that new cabins will be placed in Dalby Forest, following a 10-year agreement between nature-focused start-up Unyoked and Forestry England.

Under the scheme, up to five off-grid cabins will be installed at 40 sites across England, offering short stays in peaceful forest settings with the aim of improving access to nature and wellbeing, focusing on reducing stress and boosting mental clarity.

Cam Grant, co-founder of Unyoked, said: "This partnership with Forestry England is a significant milestone in Unyoked’s mission to make nature a habitual, accessible part of everyday life.

"It will expand our current off-grid cabin offering in the UK, giving nature-seekers the special opportunity to stay in the nation’s beautiful forests.

  The Briar Cabin in Dalby Forest 

*(Image: Provided)*

"England's forests are rich in history and biodiversity, and we’re excited to provide unique spaces where people can disconnect, recharge, and experience the profound wellness benefits of spending time in nature firsthand."

Each cabin is custom built for Dalby Forest and designed to accommodate one or two people. They are positioned away from pathways, facilities, and other guests to ensure privacy and immersion in the natural landscape.

  The interior of the Dalby Forest cabins 

*(Image: Provided)*

Mike Seddon, chief executive of Forestry England, said: "We are thrilled to partner with Unyoked on this exciting initiative that will help more people experience the nation’s forests, offering innovative and exciting ways for people to enjoy them.

"It’ll be a great addition to Forestry England’s strong track record of long-term, successful business partnerships.

  The cabins are all catered towards the off-grid experience 

*(Image: Provided)*

"Like us, Unyoked puts conservation and sustainability at the heart of their business.

"We’re confident Unyoked’s unique and low-impact off-grid cabins will allow people to enjoy a slower-paced, secluded and tranquil stay in our woodlands, to really connect with nature and restore their wellbeing."

The scheme is intended to give people access to restful stays in protected woodlands not typically available to the public, with the aim of promoting wellbeing and a deeper appreciation for nature.

All cabins are powered by solar energy, use composting systems, and are built with a minimalist, sustainable design to reduce their environmental footprint.

Rates start at £159 per night on weeknights and £219 on weekends, with more information on the Unyoked website.
