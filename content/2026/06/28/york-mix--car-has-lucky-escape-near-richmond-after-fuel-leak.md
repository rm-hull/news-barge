A crew from Reeth assisted with ensuring a car was safe following a single road traffic collision, near Richmond, which resulted in the vehicle leaking petrol.

It happened on the B6270 in Reeth just after 9 this morning, 28th June.

Firefighters used small tools to make it safe.
