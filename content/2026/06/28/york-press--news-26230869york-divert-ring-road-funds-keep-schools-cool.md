**York should divert ring road funds to keep schools cool**

ON March 11, 2019, City of York Council declared a climate emergency and now we are feeling effects of climate change.

This week has shown how poorly York is prepared for the extreme heat that will become the norm. Our houses, offices and schools are not equipped for this weather and illness due to extreme heat will overwhelm our hospital system.

Labour councillors supported declaring a climate emergency but now need to act and ask the Government to divert the funds allocated for the dualling of the ring road to retrofitting our schools and communal spaces so they can be kept cool during heatwaves for children and vulnerable people.

  *Henry Stevens*

  *Western Terrace,*

  *New Earswick,*

  *York*
