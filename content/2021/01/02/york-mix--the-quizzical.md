## WIN with YorkMix Radio weekday mornings

Make sure you don’t schedule a Zoom meeting at 11:30 or you’ll miss out on North Yorkshire’s daily general knowledge competition, put the kettle on and play along instead.

Each day our contestant will answer ten general knowledge questions, the highest score at the end of the week wins. Oh, did we mention there’s a twist? At the end of each round the contestant can choose whether or not to take the Killer Question, if they take the question and get it right their score doubles, get it wrong and it halves.

Each week’s winner receives an amazing prize.

Think you’ve got what it takes to take on the rest of North Yorkshire and win? Fill in our form below and we’ll add you to our list of contestants.
