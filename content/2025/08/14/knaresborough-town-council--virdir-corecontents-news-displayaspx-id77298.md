# New Illustrated Town Map

            *Published: 14 August 2025*

             Explore Knaresborough Like Never Before! 


We’re excited to unveil our brand new Knaresborough Town Map Leaflet – beautifully illustrated by local artist Helen Salmons.

“I was honoured to be asked to create these maps for Knaresborough, and I hope they bring enjoyment to locals and visitors for many years to come. It’s been wonderful to capture everything I love about our town in one place (and I still discover more every time I go exploring!). I’m delighted that, thanks to Knaresborough Town Council, so many people will get to enjoy them too.” (Helen Salmons)

This double-sided A3 map features:

![🔸](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/images/emoji.php/v9/t72/1/16/1f538.png)

A clear, functional map of the wider town centre to help you find your way

![🔸](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/images/emoji.php/v9/t72/1/16/1f538.png)

A richly detailed, illustrated view of the heart of Knaresborough – packed with historical imagery and local landmarks

Whether you’re a resident looking to rediscover hidden gems, or a visitor keen to explore our iconic streets, this map is your perfect companion.

The maps will be available at Knaresborough Library, Knaresborough Castle & Courthouse Museum, and the Knaresborough Heritage Centre – completely free to take home!

 

Come and pick yours up, and see our town in a whole new way.
