# Watch: Kate gives out tickets and meets public in surprise Wimbledon visit

The Princess of Wales greeted members of the public during a surprise visit to Wimbledon on Thursday.

She also handed out tickets to attendees and took card payments before heading to a seat on Court 18 to watch Briton Arthur Fery play his second-round match against Finland's Otto Virtanen.

The princess sat between All England Lawn Tennis Club chairwoman Deborah Jevans and former British tennis number one Tim Henman.
