# Bright Friday 2025 Christmas Lights Switch On

            *Published: 02 December 2025*

            Thank You! 

  A huge thank you to everyone involved in making our Bright Friday Christmas Lights Switch-On such a fantastic event tonight! 

  It truly was a massive team effort from so many individuals, groups, and organisations — your hard work, energy, and enthusiasm created an evening filled with community spirit and festive fun. Events like this only happen because of the brilliant people who come together to make them special. 

  Thank you to everyone who helped, supported, performed, volunteered, or simply came along to celebrate with us — you made it magical.
