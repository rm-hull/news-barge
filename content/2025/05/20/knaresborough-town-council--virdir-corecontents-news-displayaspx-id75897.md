# Knaresborough Market Update

            *Published: 20 May 2025*

            Update on Knaresborough’s Market

  20 May 2025

  Knaresborough Town Council (KTC) have submitted a business case to North Yorkshire Council (NYC) to facilitate the next steps towards the proposed transfer of Knaresborough’s Chartered Market. 

    If NYC accepts the business case, formal discussions between the two councils will continue to ensure the best outcome for Knaresborough. 

    This is a positive step towards bringing the market under local management. 

    All Town Council meeting agendas and minutes can be found on the Town Council website www.knaresboroughtowncouncil.gov.uk 

  KTC will continue to share updates as progress and decisions are made.
