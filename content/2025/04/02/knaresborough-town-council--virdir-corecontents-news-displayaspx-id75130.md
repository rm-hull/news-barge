# Knaresborough Spa Baths Charity 

            *Published: 02 April 2025*

            The Knaresborough Spa Baths Charity was established in 1975 using funds from the closure of the old Spa Baths in Starbeck.  These funds were deposited in a charitable account and the interest is available for small grants.
