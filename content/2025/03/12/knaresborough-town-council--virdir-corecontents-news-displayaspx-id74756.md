# Vacancy for position of self employed Maintenance Person / Town Ranger

            *Published: 12 March 2025*

            Knaresborough Town Council have an exciting opportunity for a self-employed Maintenance Person / Town Ranger. 

 Could this be you? 

  KTC are looking to engage the services of a self-employed Maintenance Person / Town Ranger to tackle a range of odd jobs across the whole town aimed at maintaining and improving the town’s appearance.  

  Cllr James Pickard said, *“**We hope the appointment of a self employed Town Ranger will be the first step towards tackling all the odd jobs that you often pass and think – ‘why does somebody not sort this?’ If you have a passion to see Knaresborough presented at its very best, and like what we do at the Town Council, then this job could just be for you”.*

  If you think this could be you or someone you know, please see the information below for more information about the role and anticipated tasks and hours to be worked.  All expressions of interest to be send to the Clerk of Knaresborough Town Council via email [clerk@knaresboroughtowncouncil.gov.uk](mailto:clerk@knaresboroughtowncouncil.gov.uk) 

  If you have any questions or wish to discuss this opportunity further, please email the Clerk or contact us on 01423 864 080.

The closing date is 28 March 2025.
