# Meet Your Town Councillors - Drop in Surgery 

            *Published: 26 March 2025*

            On Monday 31st March 10am – 12noon KTC are introducing a ‘Meet your Town Councillors’ - drop in surgery at Knaresborough Library. 

Our hope is that these drop-in surgeries will become a regular opportunity for Knaresborough residents to meet and have a chat with one of your local Councillors. This first session will be attended by Cllr Hannah Gostlow, Cllr Matt Walker and Cllr Kathryn Davies.

Look out for future dates when you’ll be able to drop in and meet with other Town 

Councillors in the coming months.
