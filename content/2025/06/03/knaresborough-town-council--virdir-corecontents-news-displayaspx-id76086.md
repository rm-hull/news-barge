# Trial of New Town Ranger Service Begins

            *Published: 03 June 2025*

            Knaresborough Town Council are pleased to share that OJW Services are starting their 6 month contract with Knaresborough Town Council this week providing a Town Ranger service. We are really excited to be working with Ollie and his team over the coming months. 

To start with they will be concentrating on improving the Town Centre in preparation for Bed Race and the summer months. Look out for Ollie and his team weeding and sweeping this week!
