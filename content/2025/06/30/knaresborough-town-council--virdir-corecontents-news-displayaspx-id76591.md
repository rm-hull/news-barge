# Knaresborough Market Update

            *Published: 30 June 2025*

            Knaresborough Town Council (KTC) have published the Business Case document that sets out the case for KTC to undertake the management and operation of the town's weekly charter market.  This document was presented to North Yorkshire Council on the 20 May 2025 after it was approved by resolution at an extraordinary meeting of KTC on Monday 19 May 2025.
