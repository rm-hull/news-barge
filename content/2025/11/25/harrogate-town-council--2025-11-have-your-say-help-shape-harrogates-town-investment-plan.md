## Take part in the Harrogate Town Investment Plan (TIP) survey

Everyone with an interest in Harrogate’s future is encouraged to complete the online survey. Your feedback will help identify what works well in the town, what could be improved and where investment should be focused over the coming years.

**Survey link: [https://online1.snapsurveys.com/interview/8e020d7b-6fb4-4814-8d87-cfddf182e1a6**](https://online1.snapsurveys.com/interview/8e020d7b-6fb4-4814-8d87-cfddf182e1a6**)

*(The survey is open until 21 December.)*

## Upcoming engagement events

As part of the consultation, North Yorkshire Council is organising in-person events where the community can meet the project team, learn more about the town investment plan, and discuss ideas in more detail. These sessions will take place in late November, with confirmed details to follow.

## Why your views matter

The tip aims to create a forward-looking and resilient vision for Harrogate, supporting economic growth, environmental sustainability and high-quality placemaking. This can only be achieved with strong community input.
