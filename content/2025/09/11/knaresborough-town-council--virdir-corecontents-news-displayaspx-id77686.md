# Town Ranger Update 

            *Published: 11 September 2025*

            **Town Ranger Update**

Oliver and his team have been hard at work this summer, tackling a wide range of Town Ranger tasks that have truly made a difference in brightening up our town. From clearing overgrown ginnels and pathways choked with nettles and weeds, to restoring our traditional fingerpost signs and scrubbing away unsightly graffiti, they've been busy breathing new life into neglected corners.

One standout achievement we’re especially excited about is the vibrant new flags now flying proudly at the Castle—funded by KTC to replace the worn and weathered ones.

A huge thank you to Oliver and the team for their dedication and efforts in making our town shine!
